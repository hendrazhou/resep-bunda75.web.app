The ingredients needed to make Pull Apart Garlic Cheesy Bread:
  1. Make ready 4 Cups Flour
  2. Prepare 2 Teaspoons yeast Instant
  3. Make ready 1 1/5 Cups Milk Water /
  4. Prepare 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  5. Make ready Cheddar Cheese
  6. Prepare Parsley
  7. Take 1/4 Cup Garlic , chopped
  8. Take 1 Egg

Steps to make Pull Apart Garlic Cheesy Bread:
  1. Mix the flour, yeast, milk and salt until the dough comes clean from the sides of the bowl. (You can use half milk, half water too)
  2. Place the dough into a greased bowl and leave to rest at room temperature until it doubles in volume.
  3. When risen divide it into 10-12 balls and flatten them.
  4. To make the filling crumble the cheese and chop the garlic and herbs. Mix them. Place about 1-2 tbsp of the cheese-herb mixture on the flatten dough.
  5. Seal the pocket with a fork or with your fingers and place it in a greased loaf pan. Do the same with the rest.
  6. Leave again to rest at room temperature. When risen brush with egg wash and bake in preheated oven, at 190C, for about 35-40 minutes.
