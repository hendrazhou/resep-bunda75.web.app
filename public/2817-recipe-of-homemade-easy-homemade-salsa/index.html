The ingredients needed to make Easy Homemade Salsa:
  1. Take 1 Jalapeño
  2. Get 1 clove Garlic
  3. Get 2 tbsp Lime Juice
  4. Get 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  5. Get 10 Cherry Tomatoes
  6. Prepare 1/4 Onion
  7. Make ready 1 bunch Cilantro
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Parsley
  9. Take 1 pinch Salt
  10. Prepare 1 pinch Sugar

Steps to make Easy Homemade Salsa:
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. I used my single sized ninja blender, so just fill with all ingredients. I put the tomatoes in last because that way I can fill it up with more or less.
  3. Pulse about 4 times in your blender. Pour into a bowl then serve. Mine was cold because all my ingredients came out of the fridge, but serve cold.
