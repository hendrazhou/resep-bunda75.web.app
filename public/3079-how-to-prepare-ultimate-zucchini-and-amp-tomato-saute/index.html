The ingredients needed to make Zucchini & Tomato Saute:
  1. Make ready 2 fresh zucchinis, cut into 1/4 inch rounds
  2. Get can diced tomatoes
  3. Get 1/4 cup finely chopped celery
  4. Prepare 2 yellow small sweet peppers, diced
  5. Get 1 roma tomato, cut into chunks
  6. Take to taste dill weed
  7. Prepare to taste Italian seasoning
  8. Make ready to taste fresh cracked black pepper
  9. Make ready to taste onion powder
  10. Prepare to taste salt
  11. Take 2 tablespoons butter
  12. Make ready vegetable broth for moisture

Instructions to make Zucchini & Tomato Saute:
  1. Add zucchini, peppers, celery, canned tomatoes and spices to saute pan, use vegetable broth to add moisture for saute
  2. Saute on medium low for about 20 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes, adding broth when necessary
  3. When the zucchini is tender, add butter and fresh chopped roma tomato, fold in till butter melts and tomato warms thoroughly, about 2 minutes
  4. Place in serving dish and top with grated parmesan cheese
  5. Serve and enjoy!
