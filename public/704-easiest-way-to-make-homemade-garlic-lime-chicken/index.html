The ingredients needed to make Garlic Lime Chicken:
  1. Take 4 oz Chicken breast
  2. Get 3 tbsp Lime juice
  3. Take 1 tbsp Garlic powder
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp Onion powder
  5. Prepare Salt and pepper
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Olive oil
  7. Get 2 tsp Garlic powder
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp Butter

Instructions to make Garlic Lime Chicken:
  1. Mix 1 tablespoon of garlic, 2 tablespoons of lime juice, onion powder, salt and pepper together in a small bowl.
  2. Make sure chicken breast are thawed.
  3. Rub seasoning onto both side of each chicken breast.
  4. Melt butter and olive oil in pan on stove.
  5. Cook chicken breast for 6 minutes on each side.
  6. Once done, sprinkle 2 teaspoons of garlic and 1 teaspoon of lime juice on top of chicken.
