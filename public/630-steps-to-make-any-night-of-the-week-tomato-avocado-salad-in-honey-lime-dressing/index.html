The ingredients needed to make Tomato Avocado Salad in honey lime dressing:
  1. Get 2 large roma tomatoes - diced
  2. Make ready 1 medium avocado - pitted, scooped out of skin and diced
  3. Make ready 1/2 medium onion - thinly sliced
  4. Prepare 1 large lime
  5. Prepare 2 tbs minced fresh cilantro
  6. Make ready 2 tsp honey
  7. Make ready 1/2-1 tsp sriracha type hot sauce
  8. Get 2 tbs olive oil
  9. Get Pinch salt and pepper

Instructions to make Tomato Avocado Salad in honey lime dressing:
  1. Place prepared tomatoes, avocado, and onion in a medium bowl with a tight fitting lid.
  2. Zest and juice lime into a separate medium bowl. Add cilantro, honey, sriracha, olive oil, and a pinch of salt and pepper to lime juice. Wisk to combine.
  3. Drizzle dressing over salad mixture. Gently toss to coat. Cover salad directly with plastic wrap, place lid on container, and refrigerate for 1 hr before serving. Enjoy!
