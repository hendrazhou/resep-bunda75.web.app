Instructions to make Fried Yam with Egg and Beverage Tea:
  1. Peel, slice, wash the yam. Add salt and mix together
  2. Heat the oil in the deep fryer and fry the yam
  3. Break the eggs into a plate, add salt, onions and beat together.
  4. Pour a little oil in the frying pan. Allow it to be hot. Pour the beaten eggs and fry.
  5. Add milk, milk and honey in a cup. Add hot water and stir together. Serve with the fried yam and egg.
