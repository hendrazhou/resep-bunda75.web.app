  10. Get 1 cup water
  11. Prepare Strawberry pineapple lemonade
  12. Get 1 cup chopped strawberry
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  14. Make ready 2 tsp -lemon juice
  15. Get 2 tsp -simple syrup
