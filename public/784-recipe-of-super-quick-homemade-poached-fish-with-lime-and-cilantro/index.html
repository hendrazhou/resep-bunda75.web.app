The ingredients needed to make Poached fish with Lime and Cilantro:
  1. Prepare 1 fish fillet of your choice
  2. Make ready 1 bunch cilantro
  3. Take 1 lime
  4. Get 2 tbsp water
  5. Take 1/3 tsp black pepper
  6. Get 1/3 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 2 clove garlic
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp white wine
  9. Make ready 1 some diced red,yellow pepper and onion
