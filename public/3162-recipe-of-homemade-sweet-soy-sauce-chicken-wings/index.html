Instructions to make Sweet soy sauce chicken wings:
  1. Put all the soy sauce and rock sugar into the pot. Low heat until all the sugar melted. (Not to boil!)
  2. Put the chicken wings into the pot for 5-6 minutes, with low heat. Turn around after 3-4 minutes, to make sure the color are even in both sides of the chicken wings.
  3. Close the fire and let the chicken wings keep in the sauce for 5 minutes. (To avoid over cook)
  4. The sauce could keep and reuse for next time! Keep in the fridge after cooling down.
