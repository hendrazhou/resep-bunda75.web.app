The ingredients needed to make Suji Tutti-frutti cake:
  1. Make ready 1 cup semolina
  2. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  3. Take 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  4. Prepare 2 teaspoons Tutti frutti
  5. Take 2 tablespoons milk
  6. Take 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  7. Prepare 2 teaspoon butter
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanilla essence

Steps to make Suji Tutti-frutti cake:
  1. Take a bowl and add semolina, baking powder, powdered sugar and sieve all together.
  2. Preheat oven at 180°.
  3. Mix baking soda, butter, tutti-frutti and milk to make smooth batter.
  4. Grease a mould and dust with refined flour. Remove extra flour by taping it from backside. Pour the batter in mould and place it oven for 30 mins.
  5. After 30 mins check with toothpick wheather it is cooked or not.
  6. If cooked then cut it in square pieces and serve.
