The ingredients needed to make White bread with guava jelly (goiabada):
  1. Get 3 cups flour
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 cup milk
  3. Take 3 table spoons of sugar
  4. Get 1 tea spoon salt
  5. Take 2 table spoons of quick bread riser
  6. Take 1 cup guava jelly softened

Instructions to make White bread with guava jelly (goiabada):
  1. Add all ingredients at once to the bread machine
  2. Let the circle run in * white bread
  3. Once the circle stops, open the dough on a floured counter
  4. Melt the guava with a few spoons of water in a warm pan until a soft mix is done
  5. Spread the guava softened over the opened sought and roll up
  6. Let it raise until doubles the size and put in the over pre heated 350 F. Let it bake until the top is brown
