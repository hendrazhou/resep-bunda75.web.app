The ingredients needed to make Cheese garlic bread:
  1. Get 4-6 slices garlic bread
  2. Make ready 1 cup chopped vegetables (onion, tomatoes, capcicum)
  3. Prepare 4 tbs pizza sauce
  4. Make ready 1 packet mozzarella cheese
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp oregano herbs
