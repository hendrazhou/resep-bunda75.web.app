The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Mango Lassi:
  1. Get 2 large strawberrys
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup plain greek yogurt
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup mango greek yogurt
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup or less of mango juice
  5. Prepare 2 ice cubes
  6. Get 2 tsp honey
