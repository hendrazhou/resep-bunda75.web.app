The ingredients needed to make Banana-Nutella Muffins:
  1. Take 3 medium sized browned-bananas
  2. Make ready 2 eggs
  3. Get 1/2 cup Nutella spread (choco-hazelnut spread)
  4. Take 1/2 cup water
  5. Get 1 cup self-rising flour (baking powder and salt included)
  6. Prepare 1 Nesquick (chocolate) syrup
  7. Prepare 1/3 cup unsalted butter

Steps to make Banana-Nutella Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F
  2. In a medium-large bowl mash bananas into lumpy paste, add both eggs, mix
  3. In a separate microwave safe bowl, add butter and Nutella, microwave on high for 30s, stir and pour into banana-egg mixture
  4. Gradually add flour to mixture, stir, gradually add water to mixture, stir
  5. Pour ~1/3-1/2 cups of mixture into each cup of ungreased muffin pan
  6. Drizzle ~1 tbsp of Nesquick onto each muffin, swirl chocolate syrup into mixture using knife or toothpick
  7. Place muffin pan in oven for ~30 mins, when toothpick or fork comes out of center of center muffin(s) clean: they're ready
