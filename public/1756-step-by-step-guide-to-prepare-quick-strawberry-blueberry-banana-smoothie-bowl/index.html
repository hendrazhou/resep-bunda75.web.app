The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Blueberry Banana Smoothie Bowl:
  1. Take 150 ml almond milk / soy milk / coconut milk
  2. Get 125 ml plain yoghurt / vanilla yoghurt
  3. Get 150 gr strawberry (fresh or frozen)
  4. Take 75 gr blueberry (fresh or frozen)
  5. Take 2 bananas frozen
  6. Take honey (optional)

Steps to make Strawberry Blueberry Banana Smoothie Bowl:
  1. Put everything (except honey) in a blender and process until smooth.
  2. Add some honey as desired (I didn’t because the bananas were sweet enough).
  3. Put it in a bowl. Garnish with sliced bananas, sliced strawberries, blueberries, some chia seeds, and some toasted almonds. Yummy! 😋
