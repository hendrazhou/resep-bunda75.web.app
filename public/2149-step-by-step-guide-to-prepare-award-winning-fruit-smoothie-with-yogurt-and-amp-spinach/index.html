The ingredients needed to make Fruit Smoothie with Yogurt & Spinach:
  1. Make ready 1 cup frozen fruit (strawberries, peaches, and mangos were used in mine.)
  2. Make ready 1 banana- peeled (sliced frozen or unfrozen both work)
  3. Make ready 1 handful of spinach-washed & stems removed
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup greek or low fat yogurt (plain or vanilla will work, for an extra kick use some with fruit flavor)
  5. Get 1 tbsp honey
  6. Get 1/4 cup favorite juice (orange, mango, apple or cranberry work well)
