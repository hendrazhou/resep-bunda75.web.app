The ingredients needed to make Ken's Soothing Ginger-Key Lime Tea:
  1. Make ready 2 cup water
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp ginger
  3. Get 2 key limes
  4. Get 2 key lime zest
  5. Get 2 tsp honey

Instructions to make Ken's Soothing Ginger-Key Lime Tea:
  1. Set water on stove to boil.
  2. Slice quite thinly, fresh ginger into water. Zest two key limes into water and bring to a boil.
  3. When water boils, juice key limes into water. Boil for 5-10 minutes
  4. Remove from heat. Add honey and mix thoroughly. Serve hot or warm, adding honey to taste.
