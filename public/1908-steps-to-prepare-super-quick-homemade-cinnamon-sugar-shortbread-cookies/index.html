Instructions to make Cinnamon Sugar Shortbread Cookies:

  2. In a bowl beat butter and sugar until well bleded
  3. Add flour salt and cinnamon and beat until if comes together
  4. Formonto ball
  5. Take portions of dough and roll into 1 inch balls
  6. Make cinnamon sugar
  7. Combine sugar and cinnamon in a shallow bowl
  8. Dip each dough ball in egg wash
  9. Roll I'm cinnamon sugar
