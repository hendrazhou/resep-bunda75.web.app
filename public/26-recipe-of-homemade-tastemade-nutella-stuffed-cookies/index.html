The ingredients needed to make Tastemade Nutella-Stuffed Cookies:
  1. Get 10 tablespoons Nutella
  2. Prepare 160 g flour
  3. Make ready 1/3 cup butter
  4. Prepare 1 egg
  5. Take 60 g cane sugar
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon baking powder

Instructions to make Tastemade Nutella-Stuffed Cookies:
  1. Cover a tray with parchment paper
  2. Scoop the Nutella onto the tray and freeze for 15 minutes
  3. Pour the flour and butter into a bowl an mix with an electric mixer (I lost the footage)
  4. Add the egg, sugar, and baking powder and mix again (I lost the footage)
  5. Knead the dough with your hands (I lost the footage)
  6. Cut the dough into pieces and roll them into a ball and flatten it out (repeat until there is no dough left)
  7. Place the frozen Nutella on top of the flattened dough and cover it completely (I made 4 large normal cookies and 1 large chocolate cookie, both are stuffed with Nutella of course)
  8. Bake at 350°F for 16 minutes
  9. Take the cookies out transfer them onto a plate and ✨Enjoy!✨
