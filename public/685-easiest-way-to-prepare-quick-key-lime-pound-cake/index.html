The ingredients needed to make Key Lime Pound Cake:
  1. Get 1 cup salted butter, softened
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup shortening
  3. Make ready 3 cups sugar
  4. Take 6 large eggs
  5. Make ready 3 cups all purpose flour
  6. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Get 1 cup milk
  8. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Take 1 tsp lime zest
  10. Get 1/2 cup fresh lime juice
  11. Take For Key lime glaze:
  12. Prepare 1 cup powdered sugar
  13. Take 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
  14. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions to make Key Lime Pound Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Cream together butter and shortening. Gradually add sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Add eggs one at a time beating until just blended.
  3. Stir flour, baking soda together in separate bowl and add alternatively with milk by starting with flour and ending with flour until just blended. Do not over mix. Stir in lime juice, vanilla, and lime zest.
  4. Pour batter into 10 inch greased and floured tube pan or fluted pan. Bake at 325°F for an hour and 15 minutes or until tooth pick inserted into center comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10-15 minutes. Remove from pan to wire rack.
  5. Mix together key lime glaze until smooth and pour over cake while warm.
