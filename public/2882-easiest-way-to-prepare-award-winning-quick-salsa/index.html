The ingredients needed to make Quick Salsa:
  1. Prepare 2 green tomatoes
  2. Prepare 2 red tomatoes
  3. Make ready 1 red bell pepper
  4. Get 1 green bell pepper
  5. Take 1 cucumber
  6. Prepare 1 onion
  7. Get 5 shallots
  8. Get 2 garlic, smashed and chopped well
  9. Prepare Juice from 1 lime
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  11. Get 1 1/2 tsp salt
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp freshly cracked black pepper

Instructions to make Quick Salsa:
  1. Chop tomatoes, bell peppers, cucumber, onion, shallots into small pieces.
  2. Add juice from lime, sugar, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Adjust the taste when necessary.
  5. Keep in the fridge for up to three days.
