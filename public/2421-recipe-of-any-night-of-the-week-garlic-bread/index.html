The ingredients needed to make Garlic Bread:
  1. Make ready 2 cup Mozzarella cheese
  2. Prepare 1 loaf French bread
  3. Prepare 120 grams Butter
  4. Take 1/4 cup Garlic
  5. Prepare 1 cup Salt
  6. Take 1/2 tbsp Pepper
  7. Take 3 tbsp Chopped parsley
