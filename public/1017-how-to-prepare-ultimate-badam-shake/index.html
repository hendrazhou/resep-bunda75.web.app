The ingredients needed to make Badam Shake:
  1. Get 1 ltr cream milk
  2. Get 20 almonds/badam
  3. Get 4 tbsp sugar
  4. Get 1 tbsp vanilla custard powder mix them with 2 tbsp milk for few minutes to bring the color out
  5. Take 1/4 cardamoms powdered
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp milk for gtind the badam in mixer to make badam paste
  7. Prepare 15 pc Tutti frutti
  8. Take 4 cashew
  9. Make ready 10 Raisin or kismis

Steps to make Badam Shake:
  1. To Make Badam Paste: - - 1st method: - Soak them in water overnight, next day peel the almonds, add the almonds into the blender, add a 2 tbsp of milk and blend into a paste. - - 2nd method: - If badam shake has to be made instantly, then blanch the almonds in water for few minutes, take them out, peel them, add into the blender, add some milk and make a paste.
  2. For the badam milkshake: - - Take a sauce pan, into it add milk and bring it to a boil.
  3. Add blended badam paste and cook for another ten minutes on low flame and keep stirring in between.
  4. Add cardamom powder into it and mix well.
  5. Add sugar as per requirement or add condensed milk.
  6. Take the vanilla custard powder infused milk and add into the badam shake.
  7. Put the flame on sim for few minutes. - - Switch off the flame.
  8. Chill the badam shake for some time in the refrigerator. - - Pour them into the glasses.
  9. Garnish with finely chopped almonds and kismis and cashew.,tutti frutti.
  10. Serve chilled with ice cubes or serve hot without refrigerating. - - Enjoy the milk badam shake.
