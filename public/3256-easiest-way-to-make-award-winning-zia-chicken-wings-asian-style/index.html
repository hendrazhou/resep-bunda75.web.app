The ingredients needed to make Zia Chicken Wings (Asian style):
  1. Take Chicken
  2. Get 4 large chicken wings
  3. Prepare 2 cup flour
  4. Take 2 tsp Salt
  5. Get 1 tsp blackpepper
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  7. Take 1/2 tsp paprika
  8. Take Sauce
  9. Get 4 clove garlic ( finely chopped)
  10. Get 2 tsp Sesame oil
  11. Get 1 tbsp Salted butter
  12. Take 4 tbsp Sweet Thick Soya Sauce

Instructions to make Zia Chicken Wings (Asian style):
  1. Wash the chicken
  2. Mix the chicken with the dry ingredients
  3. Deep fry the chicken until golden brown and crispy
  4. For the sauce, melt the butter in a hot pan.
  5. Stir in garlic then after you can smell the garlic put in the sesame oil and Soya sauce
  6. Stir ingredients until the sauce thicked and bubbled. Cool it down a bit.
  7. Then toss the chicken into the sauce. Make sure everything is cover.
  8. Ready to serve.
