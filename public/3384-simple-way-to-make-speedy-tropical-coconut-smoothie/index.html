The ingredients needed to make Tropical Coconut Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 1 large ripe banana
  2. Take 3 slice pineapple rings
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup pineapple juice
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp cream of coconut
  5. Make ready 2 cup tropical flavored yogart
  6. Take 1 cup milk
  7. Get 8 ice cubes
  8. Take 1/2 cup sugar if desired …i like mine sweet
