The ingredients needed to make Karachi biscuit:
  1. Take 1 cup refined flour
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup custard powder
  3. Get 1/4 TSP baking powder
  4. Get Pinch baking soda
  5. Make ready 75 gm butter
  6. Take 60 gm powdered sugar
  7. Take 2 tbsp milk
  8. Make ready 2 drops pineapple essence
  9. Get 2 tbsp tutti frutti
  10. Prepare 3 tbsp cashew nut

Steps to make Karachi biscuit:
  1. In a bowl beat butter and powdered sugar till whitish with electric beater
  2. Then add seived refined flour,custard powder baking powder baking soda to this mixture and mix with spatula
  3. To this mix tutti frutti and cashewnut,and milk
  4. Mix all well
  5. Add pineapple essence,mix
  6. Make a roll cylindrical and roll it in cling film
  7. Keep it in refrigerator for 1 hr
  8. Now cut the thick slices out of it
  9. Keep it in greased tray and bake for 170 degree at 15 minutes
  10. Serve it /store it in jar after it comes to room temp.
