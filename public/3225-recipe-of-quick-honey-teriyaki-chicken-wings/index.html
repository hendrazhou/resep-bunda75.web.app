The ingredients needed to make Honey Teriyaki Chicken Wings:
  1. Take 1/4 cup Soy Sauce
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp Ground Ginger
  3. Get 1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
  4. Prepare 5 tbsp Brown Sugar
  5. Get 2 tbsp Honey
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup Water
  7. Take 1 tbsp Corn Starch
  8. Prepare 1 packages Chicken wings

Instructions to make Honey Teriyaki Chicken Wings:
  1. Mix all ingredients (beside water and cornstarch ) in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
  2. Mix water and cornstarch together until it is dissolved. Add it to the mixture in the saucepan.
  3. Stir mixture until it thickens
  4. Place chicken wings in a baking dish and pour sauce over it
  5. Bake covered on 350°F for 1-1 1/2. Uncover then put back in the oven for 20 minutes.
