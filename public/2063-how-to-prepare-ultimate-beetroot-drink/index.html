  7. Make ready Lemon juice optional
  8. Make ready cubes Ice

Steps to make Beetroot Drink:
  1. Here is our ingredients.
  2. Wash and peel the beetroot,carrots,apple,orange, and Ginger.
  3. Cut and rinse them.
  4. In a blender pour in everything plus ice cube you can add water.
  5. Blend till smooth, using siever and sieve out the juice.
  6. Freeze it and enjoy.
