The ingredients needed to make Nutella Microwave Mud Cake:
  1. Get 4 tbsp Self-raising flour
  2. Get 4 tbsp Sugar
  3. Get 1 Egg
  4. Take 3 tbsp Coco powder
  5. Get 3 tbsp Nutella
  6. Take 3 tbsp Milk
  7. Get 3 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  8. Get Whipped Cream (Optional)
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup Heavy cream
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp Sugar

Steps to make Nutella Microwave Mud Cake:
  1. Combine all ingredients in a large coffee mug.
  2. Whisk well with a fork until smooth.
  3. Microwave on high for 2:10 minutes
  4. Add heavy cream to the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment and whip on medium-high speed until almost stiff.
  5. Add sugar and beat until heavy cream forms stiff peaks.
  6. Top Mug Cakes with whipped cream and a little chocolate sauce, if desired.
