The ingredients needed to make Honey garlic chicken wings:
  1. Take 3 lb chicken
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup honey
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup lemon juice
  4. Get 1/4 cup water
  5. Take 2 tbsp soy sauce
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp Apple cider vinegar
  7. Take 2 tsp garlic powder

Instructions to make Honey garlic chicken wings:

  3. Barbecue chicken wings turning turning once until cooked thoroughly.
