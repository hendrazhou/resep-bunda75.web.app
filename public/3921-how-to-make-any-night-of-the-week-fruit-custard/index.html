The ingredients needed to make Fruit Custard:
  1. Take 3 cup milk
  2. Make ready 4 tbsp sugar
  3. Take 3 tbsp custard powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 apple chopped
  5. Get As needed Grapes (Chopped)

Steps to make Fruit Custard:
  1. In a deep pan, boil the milk. Meanwhile, once the milk is boiling, add the sugar and keep stirring until the sugar melts.
  2. When the milk is boiling, meanwhile in a plate add the custard powder to normal milk.
  3. Now add this custard powder to the boiling milk and keep stirring so that there should be no lumps.
  4. Take chopped apples and chopped grapes and place them on the custard. The delicious fruit custard is ready to be served.
