Instructions to make Tiffany's mouth firing buffalo wings!:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. In a large mixing bowl pour the Franks RedHot then add both of the McCormick's packages. Stir until powder is mixed with the Frank's sauce.
  3. Once done, add thawed chicken drumsticks in the bowl of sauce. Coat the drumsticks with the sauce do this for all drumsticks
  4. Then cook them in the oven for 25-30 minutes until drumsticks or wings are thoroughly cooked all the way.
  5. Eat and enjoy ! :)
