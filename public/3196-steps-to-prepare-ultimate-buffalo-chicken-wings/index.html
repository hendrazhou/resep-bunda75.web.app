The ingredients needed to make Buffalo Chicken Wings:
  1. Make ready 1/2 c butter
  2. Make ready 1/2 c hot sauce
  3. Take 1/4 c water
  4. Prepare 2 T white or apple cider vinegar
  5. Prepare 1 tsp sea salt
  6. Make ready 2 1/2 lbs chicken wings, 16 pieces
  7. Prepare Bleu Cheese or Ranch dressing for dipping, optional
