  2. Make ready 2 LG Tomatoes
  3. Get 3 LG Mangos
  4. Prepare 10 Habenero Peppers
  5. Make ready 4 LG Jalapeños
  6. Take 1 LG White Onion
  7. Take 15 Cloves Whole Garlic Cloves

  9. Get 1 LG Green Bell Pepper
  10. Take 1 tbsp Ground Cumin
  11. Get 1 tbsp Mexican Oregano
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp Black Pepper
  13. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Salt
  14. Make ready Juice Of 1 Lime

Steps to make Mike's Mango Habenero Garlic Salsa:

  3. Jar/can while hot. Allow to cool in jars upside-down. Then chill. Enjoy!
