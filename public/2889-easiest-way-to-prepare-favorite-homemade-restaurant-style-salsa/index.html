The ingredients needed to make Homemade Restaurant Style Salsa:
  1. Get 1 pound plum (Roma) tomatoes
  2. Prepare 1 half medium white onion
  3. Make ready 2 serrano chiles
  4. Prepare 8 sprigs cilantro
  5. Make ready 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
