The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. Prepare 1 loaf french baguette
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup butter room temperature
  3. Make ready 4 cloves of freshly crushed garlic
  4. Get 1 tsp mix herbs
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp Salt (if butter is unsalted)
  6. Get 1/4 tsp chilli flakes
  7. Take 1/2 cup cheddar and mozarella cheese

Instructions to make Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. Preheate your oven at 180 degrees. - Slice baguette into 1/2" slices, cut on the diagonal.
  2. For garlic butter In a small-sized bowl, combine together the butter, garlic, herbs, chilli flakes and salt, mix ingredients together until well blended.
  3. Evenly smear the garlic butter mixture evenly over bread slices.
  4. Top with the cheeses and sprinkle with the dried parsley.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Serve warm. Enjoy!
