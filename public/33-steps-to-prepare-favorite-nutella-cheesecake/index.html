The ingredients needed to make Nutella Cheesecake:
  1. Make ready 400 grams Cream Cheese
  2. Take 400 grams Nutella
  3. Prepare 100 grams Icing Sugar
  4. Get 1/4 tsp Vanilla Crystal
  5. Take 150 grams Crushed Cookies
  6. Get 50 grams Melted Butter

Steps to make Nutella Cheesecake:
  1. Put crushed cookies into base of the pan. Mix it with melted butter until harden. Put in freezer for a while
  2. Beat cream cheese until soft
  3. Add sifted icing sugar. Mix well.
  4. Add nutella and vanilla. Mix well.
  5. Pour the cream cheese mixture into the pan. Refrigerate at least 4 hours
