The ingredients needed to make Oats guava parfait:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup oats
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup guava pulp
  3. Prepare 2 cups coconut milk
  4. Get 3 tbsp chia seeds

Steps to make Oats guava parfait:
  1. Soak chia seeds with 1/2 cup of water overnight.
  2. In a pan heat 1 cup coconut milk, add oats and cook for 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  3. In a bowl mix together 1 cup coconut milk and guava pulp(bought from store)
  4. Now in a serving glass place some oats porridge, then layer with some soaked chia seeds. Then again layer with oats and finally top with guava coconut mixture.
  5. Oats guava parfait is ready. Garnish as you like and serve it cold or at room temperature.
