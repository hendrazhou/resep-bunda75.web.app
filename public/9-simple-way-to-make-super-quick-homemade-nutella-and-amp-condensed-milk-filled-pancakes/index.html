The ingredients needed to make Nutella&condensed milk filled pancakes:
  1. Make ready 1/2 half cup flour
  2. Prepare 1 tablespoon baking powder
  3. Get 1 egg -3tablespoon sugar
  4. Get 1/2 cup powdered milk
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  6. Get 1 cup water 2 tablespoon oil
  7. Make ready For the fillings and garnishing:
  8. Get nutella
  9. Make ready condensed milk
  10. Prepare powdered milk
  11. Take banana
Steps to make Nutella&condensed milk filled pancakes:
  1. Mix all the ingredients together to form a thick and smooth batter.
  2. On a nonstick pan fry it on a low heat, when one side is done turn to the other side.
  3. When u are done with the frying you let it cool down..
  4. Take one pancake.. - Spread nutella on it, then spread powdered milk over it, then condensed milk.. Take another pancake and cover with it..
  5. Now get some round slices of banana.. Arrange two of them on the pancake to represent eyes. - And with your nutella make a moon - shape on the pancake to represent mouth.
  6. Take the filled pancake, cut them into cubic shape as in d pic below and drizzle with some nutella..
