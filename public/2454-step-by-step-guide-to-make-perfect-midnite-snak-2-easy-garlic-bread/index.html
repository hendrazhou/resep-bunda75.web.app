The ingredients needed to make Midnite snak #2 easy garlic bread:
  1. Make ready Ingredients
  2. Prepare 2 hamburger or hot dog buns

  4. Take 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp dried parsely
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp parmesan cheese

Steps to make Midnite snak #2 easy garlic bread:
  1. mix margarine and garlic powder
  2. spread mixture on buns
  3. sprinkle parsely on top of buns
  4. sprinkle 1/2 tablespoon cheese on each bun half
  5. toast in toaster oven at 375°F for 15 minutes or until golden
  6. enjoy
  7. can also be a side bread for pasta or soup dishes
