The ingredients needed to make Healthy Banana Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 1 banana
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Low-fat or fat-free yoghurt (Greek or French vanilla are my favourites)
  3. Take 1/2 cup Low-fat or skim milk
  4. Make ready 1 tsp honey (or more)

Instructions to make Healthy Banana Smoothie:
  1. Cut up banana and place in blender (or jug if using a hand blender)
  2. Add honey, yoghurt and milk
  3. Blend on medium speed or until smooth
  4. Serve with icecream or fruit and enjoy!
