The ingredients needed to make Milo Marble Cake:
  1. Prepare 1 cup All-purpose Flour
  2. Get 3 tsp Milo powder
  3. Make ready 180 gm Condensed Milk
  4. Get 2 Egg
  5. Take 2 tbsp Unsalted Butter
  6. Get 1 tsp Baking Flour
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  8. Make ready 1 tsp Vanilla Essence

Steps to make Milo Marble Cake:
  1. Measure and take all the ingredients before starts preparing.
  2. Beat the butter softly, then add egg, condensed milk and Vanilla Essence. Whisk all together.
  3. Now add flour & baking powder and whisk without lumps.
  4. Divide the mixture into 2 equal portion in separate bowls. Add Milo and cocoa powder in one bowl and whisk without lumps. Now both vanilla and Milo mix is ready.
