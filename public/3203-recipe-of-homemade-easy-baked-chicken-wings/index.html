The ingredients needed to make Easy baked chicken wings:
  1. Get 2 lb chicken wings
  2. Get 1 tsp kosher salt
  3. Make ready 1 tsp ground black pepper
  4. Make ready 1 tsp granulated garlic
  5. Get 1 tsp paprika
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  7. Take 2 tsp cornstarch
