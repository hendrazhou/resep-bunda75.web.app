The ingredients needed to make Eggless tutti frutti sponge cake:
  1. Get 100 gm melted butter/oil
  2. Make ready 100 gm sugar
  3. Make ready 100 ml milk
  4. Get 250 gm refined flour
  5. Get 50 gm curd
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking soda
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon baking powder
  8. Take 2 teaspoon vanilla essence

The tuti fruiti mix I have used also had the citrus peels so I have not added any extra flavorings. Eggless tutti frutti cake recipe without condensed milk. Baking would be a fun & breeze if you have the perfect recipe in hand. You don't have to look for any consistencies like sweets.

Steps to make Eggless tutti frutti sponge cake:
  1. Take curd and baking soda mix it well and keep aside.
  2. Take butter, sugar and milk mix 10-15 minutes or until sugar dissolve.put vanilla essence and mix it well.
  3. Put the curd into the mixture.
  4. Seive the flour and baking powder together.
  5. Put the flour in the milk batter and mix it well
  6. Grease the tray. Put batter in the tray garnish with tutti frutti. Put it in pre heated oven for 20-25 minutes In 120 degree temperature.
