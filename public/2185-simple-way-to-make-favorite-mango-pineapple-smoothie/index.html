The ingredients needed to make Mango Pineapple Smoothie:
  1. Get 1 cup Mango pieces (frozen)
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup Pineapple pieces (frozen)
  3. Take 1/3 cup Pineapple juice
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup Plain Yogurt
  5. Prepare Water to thin if needed
