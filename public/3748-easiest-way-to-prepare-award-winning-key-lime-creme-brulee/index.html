The ingredients needed to make Key Lime creme brulee:
  1. Prepare 9 egg yolks
  2. Get 9 Tbsp raw sugar.white sugar can be used
  3. Get 3 cups heavy cream
  4. Make ready 1 vanilla bean cut and scrapped.or 3tsp of extract
  5. Get 1 zest from 1 Lime
  6. Get 6 tbsp topping 6 tbsp of white sugar
  7. Make ready 3 Tbsp key lime juice

Steps to make Key Lime creme brulee:
  1. Whisk together eggs sugar vanilla bean scrapping and 2 tsp of lime zest. 3 Tbsp of key lime juice
  2. Heat 3cups heavy cream over medium heat until it begins to bubble. Strain into the egg mixture whisking constantly
  3. Fill your ramekins. Sprinkle remaining zest on top
  4. Bake at 325°for 45 minutes. Using a water bath about 1/2 way up the ramekins
  5. Cool and refrigerate for at least 2 hours
  6. Now take 1 tsp of sugar on top and torch your sugar or use the broiler to crisp the sugar your done serves 6
