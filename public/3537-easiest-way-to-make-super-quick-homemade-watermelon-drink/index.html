Steps to make Watermelon drink:
  1. Wash and cut watermelon. Peel the back and blend. Sieve the juice in a bowl.
  2. Add lemon juice, sliced lemon, cocumber and mint leaves to the drink.
  3. Allow to infuse for 1- 2 hour and remove the cocumber, lemon and mint leaves. Serve chilled.
