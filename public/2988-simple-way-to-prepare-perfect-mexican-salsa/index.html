The ingredients needed to make Mexican salsa:
  1. Make ready 2 1/2 can tomato sauce
  2. Prepare 1 packages red dried chillies
  3. Get 1 clove garlic
  4. Take 1 bunch cilantro
  5. Make ready dash salt
  6. Make ready 1 oz lemon juice
  7. Get 2 can water

Steps to make Mexican salsa:
  1. Put dried chillies in plastic bag microwave for 45 sec for light color or 46 sec twice for dark color
  2. Throw chillies , tomato sauce and water in blender then add chopped garlic, cilantro, salt, lemon juice in and blend.
