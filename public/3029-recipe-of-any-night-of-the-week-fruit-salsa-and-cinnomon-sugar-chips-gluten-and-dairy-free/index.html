The ingredients needed to make Fruit Salsa And Cinnomon Sugar Chips (gluten and dairy free):
  1. Take 1 Apple
  2. Make ready 1 Nectarine
  3. Take 1 cup Frozen Raspberries
  4. Make ready 1 cup Blueberries
  5. Make ready 1 can Pineapple juice
  6. Make ready 5 Flour or corn tortillas (depending if you want gluten free or not)
  7. Make ready 5 tbsp Cinnamon
  8. Make ready 5 tbsp Sugar
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp Vanilla Extract
  10. Make ready 1/3 cup Olive oil or Vegetable oil (whatever you prefer)

Cut into wedges and arrange in a single layer on a large baking sheet. This fresh fruit salsa with cinnamon chips is the perfect Summer dessert or appetizer! It's super easy to make and so delicious. Steps to make Fruit Salsa And Cinnomon Sugar Chips (gluten and dairy free):
  1. Creating your fruit salsa: Cut your apple and nectarine into chunks
  2. Put your raspberries, blueberries, pineapple juice, apples, and nectarine into a blender
  3. Use the pur?e setting to slowly blend the salsa. Leave the salsa chunky if you like
  4. Chill in the fridge until ready to serve. If it looks watery just stir it around or put in the freezer
  5. Making your Cinnamon Sugar chips: cut your tortillas (corn or flour) into triangles. You can make as many as you want. 5 tortillas should make about 30 chips.
  6. Make your cinnamon sugar mixture with equal parts cinnamon to sugar. Make as much as you need to sprinkle on your chips.
  7. Get a pan hot with your oil and vanilla extract. BE CAREFUL WHEN POURING THE VANILLA, it spits everywhere because of the alcohol inside it.
  8. Place your tortilla triangles into the pan and fry until a golden brown color.
  9. When they are done frying dash your cinnamon sugar topping on the back and the front of each chip.
  10. Add more of the oil and vanilla to the pan after every two times of frying the chips.
  11. Take your fruit salsa out of the fridge and enjoy!
