The ingredients needed to make Cucumber mango smoothie:
  1. Prepare 1 mango peeled
  2. Make ready 1 cucumber peeled
  3. Get 20-25 mint leaves
  4. Take 1 table spoon lemon juice
  5. Take 1 table spoon honey
  6. Make ready 1 tea spoon Cummin powder
  7. Prepare 1 cup crushed ice
  8. Get to taste Salt
