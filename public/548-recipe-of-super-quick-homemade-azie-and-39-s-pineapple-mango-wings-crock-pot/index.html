The ingredients needed to make Azie's Pineapple Mango Wings (crock pot):
  1. Take 6 large Chicken Wings
  2. Take 2 tbsp Cooking Oil
  3. Get 1 can Crushed Pineapples
  4. Take 1 cup Cubed Mango (fresh or frozen)
  5. Get 2 tbsp Honey
  6. Get 1 tbsp Red Pepper Flakes
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  8. Take 1 dash Salt and Pepper (to taste)

Instructions to make Azie's Pineapple Mango Wings (crock pot):
  1. Season chicken wings with salt, pepper, garlic powder and pepper flakes
  2. Brown in pan coated with cooking oil for 2 minutes on each side
  3. Turn crock pot on high and add can of pineapple, mangos, and honey top with browned chicken wings and cover
  4. Cook on high for 3-4 hours or on low for 7-8 during last hour you can remove cover to stir chicken and let the sauce cook down
