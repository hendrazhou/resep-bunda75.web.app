The ingredients needed to make Vitamin A-mazing Green Smoothie:
  1. Prepare 1 generous handful of baby kale
  2. Get 1/2 cup carrot juice(make sure its 100% juice and not a blend)
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup skim milk
  4. Get 1/2 cup water
  5. Prepare 1 cup frozen mixed unsweetened berries
  6. Take 1 small (preferably frozen) banana
  7. Prepare 1/2 of a greek yogurt (5.3 oz) (I chose mixed berry by Dannon light n fit)
  8. Get 1 tbsp ground flax seed
  9. Get 5 or six ice cubes
  10. Take 1 a stevia or splenda packet or a bit of honey(optional but it sweetens up your smoothie, honey will add calories)
