The ingredients needed to make JAZZYS bean salsa:
  1. Get 1 can pinto beans
  2. Prepare 1 can dark kidney
  3. Prepare 1 can yellow hominy
  4. Prepare 2 can Black Eyed Peas
  5. Get 1 can garbanzo beans
  6. Make ready 1 can black beans
  7. Make ready 1 can crowder peas
  8. Get 1/2 bunch celery
  9. Get 3 green bell peppers
  10. Prepare 2 onions
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp minced garlic
  12. Take 1 cup sugar
  13. Take 1/2 bunch cilantro
  14. Get 8 jalapeno peppers
  15. Get 24 oz Italian dressing
