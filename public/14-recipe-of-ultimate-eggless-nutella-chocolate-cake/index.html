The ingredients needed to make Eggless nutella chocolate cake:
  1. Get 10 tablespoon sugar
  2. Take 14 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  3. Make ready 6 1/2 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder (I use cocoa powder hersheys)
  4. Make ready 2 teaspoon baking powder
  5. Get 1 teaspoon baking soda
  6. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon salt
  7. Take 1 medium apple peeled and chopped
  8. Get 1/2 cup full cream milk
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  10. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup boiling water
  12. Take 2 tablespoon Nutella
