Instructions to make Avocado Strawberry Smoothie:
  1. Add milk to blender
  2. Add chopped avocado
  3. Add fresh/frozen strawberries
  4. Add lemon juice
  5. Add honey
  6. Cover blender
  7. Start blending the mixture
  8. Blend until smooth
  9. Pour into glass and enjoy
