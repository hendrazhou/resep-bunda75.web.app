Steps to make Lemon Pepper chicken wings:
  1. Preheat oven to 400° fahrenheit
  2. rinse chicken wings in cold water & drain them on paper towels
  3. dip chicken wings in melted butter or olive oil
  4. fry chicken wings until crispy
  5. remove chicken from fryer & drain on paper towel then dip in lemon pepper seasoning
  6. arrange wings in a single later on a foil lined baking sheet
  7. bake wings for 10-15 minutes
