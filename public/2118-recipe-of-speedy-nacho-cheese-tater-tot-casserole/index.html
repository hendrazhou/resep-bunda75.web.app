  4. Prepare 1 can cream of celery soup
  5. Prepare 15 oz El Mexicano Nacho cheese sauce

Steps to make Nacho cheese tater tot casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Cook hamburger in skillet and season with salt pepper and garlic powder
  3. Once hamburger is cooked stir in your cream of celery, nacho cheese sauce(half to full can) and sour cream to the consistency of personal preference.
  4. Add in some French fried onions to preference, I did about a half cup and mix together well.
  5. Pour your mixture into a greased baking dish and top with shredded cheese.
  6. Place your tater tots on top of your casserole and bake for 45-60mins
  7. Add more shredded cheese to the top and put back in oven 5 more mins or until cheese melts. Take out and serve.
