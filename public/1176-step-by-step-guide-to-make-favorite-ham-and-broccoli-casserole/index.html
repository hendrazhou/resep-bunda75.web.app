The ingredients needed to make Ham and broccoli casserole:
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. Take 1 small onion, diced
  3. Prepare 2 clove garlic, minced
  4. Prepare 1 head broccoli, chopped into 1/4 in florets, stems peeled and diced 1/4 in thick
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp water
  7. Take 1 cup ricotta cheese
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup sour cream or plain greek yogurt
  9. Make ready 2 eggs
  10. Prepare 3 cup cooked barley, rice, or orzo
  11. Take 1/2 lb cooked ham, diced
  12. Get 1/2 cup basil, julienned
  13. Make ready 1 1/2 cup monterey jack cheese, shredded
  14. Make ready 1 salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions to make Ham and broccoli casserole:
  1. Cook barley (or whatever grain you're using). Chop ham and broccoli.
  2. Preheat oven to 350.
  3. Heat oil over medium-high heat in a Dutch oven. Reduce heat and saute onion until softened. Add garlic and saute 1 minute more.
  4. Add broccoli and salt, and return pot to medium-high heat. Stir fry until broccoli begins to brown. Add water, cover and steam until broccoli is tender. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. In small bowl, whisk together ricotta, sour cream, and eggs.
  6. Add cooked barley, cooked ham, and basil to the pot. Add ricotta mixture and 1 cup of the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle top with remaining cheese.
  8. Cover and bake 20 minutes. Uncover and bake an additional 10 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
