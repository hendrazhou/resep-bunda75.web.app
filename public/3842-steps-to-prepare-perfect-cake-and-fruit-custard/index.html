The ingredients needed to make Cake and fruit custard:

  2. Take 3 tbsp custard powder
  3. Take 4 tbsp sugar or as your taste
  4. Prepare 1 sachet jelly
  5. Take 2 bananas chopped
  6. Get 1 apple chopped
  7. Prepare 5 pieces cake left over

Steps to make Cake and fruit custard:
  1. First boil the milk in a pan
  2. Add sugar in it mix well
  3. Take 1 bowl, add cold milk one fourth cup, mix custard powder in it and make paste
  4. Add in hot milk mix again and again with spatula till thick
  5. Now keep aside till cool
  6. Make jelly: add jelly powder in 1 cup boiled water, mix well till transparent
  7. Now pour in to the plate and cool it keep 20 min in fridge
  8. Cut pieces with knife
  9. Now take a dish and make first the layer of cake and then custard
  10. Then layer of fruits and jelly
  11. Serve it. Believe me the cake make super taste in custard
