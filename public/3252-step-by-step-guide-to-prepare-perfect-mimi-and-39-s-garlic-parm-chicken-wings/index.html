The ingredients needed to make Mimi's Garlic Parm Chicken wings:
  1. Prepare 1 dozen wings
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp baking powder
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp olive oil
  4. Take 2 tbsp basil
  5. Make ready 2 clove garlic minced
  6. Make ready 1 tsp thyme
  7. Get 1/2 tsp pepper
  8. Take 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (opt)
  9. Prepare 1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps to make Mimi's Garlic Parm Chicken wings:
  1. Coat the wings with baking powder and sprinkle with salt. Place in fridge uncovered..the longer the better!!
  2. Cook wings on grill until crispy, about 30 minutes mins.
  3. In bowl mix salt, oil, basil, garlic, thyme,pepper and red pepper.
  4. When wings are done throw in bowl and coat with mixture.
  5. Add parmesan cheese and toss!
