The ingredients needed to make Tutti Frutti:
  1. Take 1 cup Watermelon rings
  2. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 2 cup water
  4. Prepare as needed Food colours (green,red, orange)
Instructions to make Tutti Frutti:
  1. The white part of melon peel off gently. Cut it into small pieces.
  2. Boil those pieces in water for 5 mints. After boiling sieve them.
  3. Then in a pan, add 1 bowl of sugar and 2 bowls of water, make it to boil.
  4. Then put the melon pieces in sugar water and boil for 5 to 7 mints.
  5. After that divide those pieces into three equal parts in bowls.
  6. Then add any food colours of your choice in those bowls.
  7. Mix it well with the help of a spoon.
  8. Then leave them covered like this for 4 to 5 hours.
  9. Then sieve it with the help of siever.
  10. Put them separately in a tray and leave to dry.
  11. If it is not drying then you can dry it for some time even in the sun.
