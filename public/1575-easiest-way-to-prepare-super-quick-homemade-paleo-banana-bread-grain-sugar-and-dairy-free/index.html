The ingredients needed to make Paleo Banana Bread ~ Grain, Sugar and Dairy free:
  1. Take 3/4 cups Casava Flour
  2. Get 1 1/4 cup Cashew Flour
  3. Take 1/2 tsp xantham gum
  4. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Take 3 over ripe bananas
  6. Take 3 eggs
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup monkfruit brown sugar granuals
  8. Make ready 1 tsp maple extract
  9. Take 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  10. Get 1/4 full fat coconut milk

Steps to make Paleo Banana Bread ~ Grain, Sugar and Dairy free:
  1. Mix the first 4 ingredients in a small bowl and set aside
  2. In a large bowl combine last 6 ingredients and beat with an electric mixer till well blended
  3. Pour dry ingredients on top of liquid and blend for 3 minutes on med high speed.
  4. Use 2 small loaf pans, lone with parchment paper and cook at 350 for approximately 30 to 40 minutes, checking after 30 minutes with a toothpic.
