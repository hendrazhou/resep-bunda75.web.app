The ingredients needed to make Cheese garlic bread:
  1. Take 3 1/2 cups grated Cheddar
  2. Get 3/4 cup grated Pepper Jack
  3. Take 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  4. Take 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  5. Prepare 1 1/4 sticks softened butter
  6. Take 1 dash salt
  7. Get Freshly ground black pepper
  8. Take 4 cloves garlic, finely minced
  9. Make ready 1 loaf crusty French bread
  10. Take 1/2 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  11. Get Olive sliced

Steps to make Cheese garlic bread:
  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Mix the cheeses with the mayonnaise, 1/4 stick of the softened butter. Add the salt and pepper to taste and set aside or keep in the fridge until you need it.
  3. Melt the remaining 1 stick butter in a skillet over medium-low heat and add the garlic. Cook for a couple of minutes to release the garlic flavor.
  4. Cut the loaf of bread in half. Brush the garlic butter over each half, making sure it's evenly distributed. Spread the cheese mixture nd olive sliced on the loaves and bake until the cheese is hot and bubbly, about 10 minutes.
  5. Once out of the oven.sprinkle over the chopped parsley. and serve.
