The ingredients needed to make Picante salsa:
  1. Get 6 tomatoes
  2. Prepare 1 bunch cilantro
  3. Make ready 1 jalapeño
  4. Get 1 onion
  5. Take 2 tbsp minced garlic
  6. Get 1/8 cup lime juice
  7. Get coarse sea salt
