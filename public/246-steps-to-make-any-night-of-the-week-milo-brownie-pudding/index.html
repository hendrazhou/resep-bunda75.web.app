The ingredients needed to make Milo Brownie Pudding:
  1. Make ready 500 ml chocolate milk
  2. Take 81 grams powdered Milo or as desired
  3. Take 2 pieces white bread
  4. Take 1 pack agar powder (7grams)
  5. Get Sugar if needed

This brownie pudding recipe is easy and fast and only dirties up one bowl to make it. It's a perfect dessert for a cold night. Serve hot, before the pudding gets absorbed by the brownie! A "magical" dessert that separates during baking into a rich and fudgy brownie on top of a silky chocolate pudding.

Instructions to make Milo Brownie Pudding:
  1. Put everything in a blender.
  2. Don't forget to shred the brads first. Then blend.
  3. Put the mixture in a pan and heat it until boiling.
  4. Put the mixture in you fave container.
  5. Let the steam dissapear then put them in the fridge.
  6. Enjoy.
