The ingredients needed to make Key Lime Pie Bars:
  1. Get 1 roll pillsbury sugar cookie dough
  2. Take 1 large container cool whip
  3. Take 14 oz sweetened condensed milk
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup lime juice
  5. Make ready 2 cups key lime yogurt
  6. Take 1 lime

Instructions to make Key Lime Pie Bars:
  1. Start by slicing 1 inch thick pieces off the cookie dough roll (like your making a batch of cookies). Place on a cookie sheet & bake for 18-20 mins.
  2. Once the cookie dough is done baking. Place cookies into a large ziploc bag. Crush with a rolling pin until finely crushed. Pour into bowl with 1 cup cool whip. Mix thoroughly & then spread at the bottom of a 9x13 cake pan.
  3. In a seperate large bowl combine condensed milk & lime juice. Beat until smooth.
  4. Add yogurt & the rest of the cool whip. Whisk until smooth. Pour mixture over cookie spread in cake pan.
  5. For garnish or decoration shave lime peeling & slice lime to top the bars. Serve & enjoy!
