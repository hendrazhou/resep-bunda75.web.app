The ingredients needed to make Masala Guava icecream:
  1. Get 1 litter Milk
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Milk powder
  3. Get 1 cup Cream
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup Sugar
  5. Take 1/2 cup guava squish

Steps to make Masala Guava icecream:
  1. Boil milk till the quantity become half
  2. Take a 1/2 cup of milk and add milk powder and mix well Now add slowly milk powder and stir it continually.
  3. Now add sugar and cream and cook till sugar is disloved completely and Cool down.
  4. Now add guava squish and beat until it becomes smooth
  5. Now put ice cream in airtight container and chill in refrigerator for 1 hour
  6. After 1 hour again beat ice cream with the help of electric bitter for 8-10 minutes
  7. Again chill for 5-6 hour until the set up
  8. Now guava ice cream is ready sprinkle red chilli powder and salt mix powder
  9. Enjoy guava with spices as ice cream
