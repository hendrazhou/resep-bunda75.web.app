The ingredients needed to make Instant Coconut tutti frutti laddu:
  1. Take 1 bowl coconut powder
  2. Take 1 bowl rava
  3. Prepare 1/2 bowl dry fruits
  4. Get 1/2 bowl tutti frutti
  5. Take 1 bowl powdered sugar
  6. Make ready 6-8 tsp milk
  7. Prepare 3-4 tbsp ghee
  8. Get 2-3 green elaichi

Steps to make Instant Coconut tutti frutti laddu:
  1. Take a pan and roast the rava with ghee. Only 5 mins roast dont change the color of rava.
  2. Add coconut powder and green Elaichi in roasted Rava then turn off the flame. When mixture cool down then add dry fruits, tutti frouti,pisi sugar and milk.
  3. Then make small Laddu help of your palm..ready your tasty and instant coconut tutti frouti laddu.
