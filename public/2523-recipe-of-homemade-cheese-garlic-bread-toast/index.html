The ingredients needed to make Cheese Garlic Bread Toast:
  1. Take 4 slices Brown Bread
  2. Get 4 Cheese Slices
  3. Get 8-10 Garlic , crushed
  4. Make ready 100 gms Butter
  5. Prepare 2 Green chillies, finely chopped
  6. Take 1 tsp Chilli Flakes
  7. Get 4 pinch Salt
  8. Get as required Coriander

Instructions to make Cheese Garlic Bread Toast:
  1. Add 80 gms butter in pan.
  2. Let the butter melt.
  3. Add grated garlic.
  4. Saute for about 2 min on low flame.
  5. Add salt.
  6. Garlic butter is ready.
  7. Add butter in the same pan and roast the bread on one side.
  8. Spread the garlic butter on bread.
  9. Put cheese slice on bread.
  10. Sprinkle red chilli flakes, coriander and green chilli.
  11. Add some butter in the pan.
  12. Now put our garlic bread in pan and cook by covering the pan for minutes.
  13. Enjoy!
