The ingredients needed to make Mango lassi😊#cookpad #ramadanrecipe:
  1. Prepare 2 cups curd
  2. Prepare 2 tbsn sugar,pinch of salt
  3. Prepare 1 cup mango pulp
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup milk,fresh cream and some chopped mangoes to garnish
