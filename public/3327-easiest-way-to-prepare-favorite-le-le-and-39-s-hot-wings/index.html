Instructions to make Le-Le's Hot wings:
  1. Preheat deep fryer. Then preheat oven to 400°
  2. Wash chicken. Pat dry place in pan season be sure to cover all the wings.
  3. Allow to sit in the fridge for one hour before cooking.
  4. Remove from frige and allow to sit at room temperature for about 10 mins.
  5. Then drop into deep fryer until golden brown. Place back in pan until crispy turning while in oven. Once done transfer wings to a bowl.
  6. Pour hot wing sauce give a little shake and enjoy. If you prefer your sauce a little more baked into the wings transfer back to the oven for a few minutes and enjoy.
  7. I like to dip mine in franks red hot sauce ranch dressing.
