The ingredients needed to make Pandanus Drink:
  1. Prepare 6 Pandan leaf
  2. Prepare 500 ml Water
  3. Prepare 2 cup Ice
  4. Take 2/3 cup Sugar

Steps to make Pandanus Drink:
  1. Boil pandanus leaf with water for about an hour.
  2. Add some sugar and take the pandanus leaf out.
  3. Pour the drink into the jug and add some ice cubes.
  4. Bring into the fridge.
