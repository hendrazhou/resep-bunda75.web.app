The ingredients needed to make Mango-Chile Potato Salad:
  1. Make ready 3 lb red potatoes; quartered
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup dried mango; medium dice
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp onion powder
  4. Take 1 tbsp garlic powder
  5. Get 1 tbsp paprika
  6. Get 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  7. Get 8 oz Kraft Chipotle Mango dressing
  8. Take 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  9. Take 1 orange bell pepper; small dice
  10. Take 1 red bell pepper; small dice
  11. Make ready 3 shallots; minced
  12. Make ready 1 cilantro; chiffonade
  13. Get 1 bunch scallions; cut on bias
  14. Get 1 salt and pepper
