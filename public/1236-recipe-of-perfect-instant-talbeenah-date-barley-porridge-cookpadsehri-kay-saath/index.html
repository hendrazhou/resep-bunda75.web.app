Instructions to make Instant Talbeenah ( Date barley porridge) #CookpadSehri kay saath:
  1. Bring milk to boil
  2. Slow the heat, add barely powder gradually to avoid lumps and mix,
  3. Add 1 cup water and mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes on slow heat
  4. Remove seeds of dates and chop them in small pieces. add dates to the milk mixture
  5. Add elaichi powder (cardamom powder) mix, add saffron (optional)
  6. Cover the lid and cook for 5-10 minutes on slow heat. talbeenah is ready to serve
  7. Transfer it to a bowl
  8. Garnish with chopped cardamom
  9. Tip: can use honey instead of dates
