Instructions to make Vanilla flavoured cup cakes:
  1. Take a bowl add 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida),1/2 cup powder sugar,1 tsp baking powder,and 1/2 tsp baking soda mix all dry ingredients well.
  2. Add 1 cup condensed milk.
  3. Add 2 tbsp oil in it.
  4. Pour 2 cups milk.
  5. 2 tbsp vanilla essence.
  6. 2 tbsp tuti frutti.
  7. 3 drops lemon.
  8. Mix all dry and liquid ingredients well.
  9. Now grease the tea cups with oil.
  10. Pour 3 tbsp mixture in all cups and bake cup cakes on 180° for 35 minutes.
  11. After 35 minutes pour the Chopped almond and tutti fruitti over the cup cakes.
  12. Ready to serve.
