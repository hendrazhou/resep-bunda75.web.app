The ingredients needed to make Garlic Ginger Chicken Wings:
  1. Prepare 3 lb separated trimmed chicken wings
  2. Get 1 tsp 5 spice powder
  3. Get 3 tbsp light soy sauce
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp vinegar
  5. Take 2 tbsp brown sugar
  6. Make ready 4 tsp chopped garlic (3 cloves)
  7. Take 4 tsp ginger
  8. Make ready 4 tsp oil
  9. Take 1 small hot pepper chopped
  10. Get 3 green onions
  11. Make ready 1 black pepper

Steps to make Garlic Ginger Chicken Wings:
  1. Sprinkle chicken with 5 spice, toss with soy sauce
  2. Put on foiled pan and bake @ 425° turning once until crisp (40-45 min)
  3. Stir together remaining soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, garlic, ginger, oil, black pepper, hot pepper
  4. Add wings, toss to coat
  5. Sprinkle with green onions
