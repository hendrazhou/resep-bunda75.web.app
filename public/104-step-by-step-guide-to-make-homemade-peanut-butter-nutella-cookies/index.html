The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Nutella Cookies:
  1. Prepare Cookies
  2. Get 1 Egg
  3. Get 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  4. Make ready 3/4 cup Peanut Butter (creamy or chunky)
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup Sugar
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup Nutella

Steps to make Peanut Butter Nutella Cookies:
  1. Mix all ingredients very well.
  2. Spray cookie sheet with cooking spray. Oven should be pre-heated at 350°
  3. Make small balls out of BP batter and place on cookie sheet about 2in. apart.
  4. Using potato masher or fork, wet and dip in sugar and smash balls down…use a crisscross if using fork.
  5. Bake for about 10 to 12 mins. the longer time will create a slight crunch around the edge.
  6. suggestions: add chocolate chips to the batter. add chocolate kiss or bar of chocolate to top of cookie directly after baking…allow to cool and dip in almond bark…sprinkle lightly with cinnamon after baking, colored sprinkles, etc…
