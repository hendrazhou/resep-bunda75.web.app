The ingredients needed to make NUTELLA COQUITO (PUERTORRICAN HOLIDAY DRINK):
  1. Get 1 can evaporated milk
  2. Get 1 can condensed milk
  3. Prepare 1 can cream of coconut
  4. Make ready 1 can coconut milk
  5. Take 1 cup nutella
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Get 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  8. Take 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  9. Prepare 1 cup white rum (Bacardi) or you can leave it out
