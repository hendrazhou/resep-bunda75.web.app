Instructions to make Oreo and nutella stuffed cupcakes:
  1. Prepare cupcake batter as per instructions.
  2. Grease and line a cupcake tin. Preheat oven.
  3. Spoon half the cupcake batter into muffin tin.
  4. Place a single oreo and a teaspoon of nutella over the cookie into each cup.
  5. Spoon over remaining batter and bake for about 15 minutes.
  6. Serve on its own or with a peanut butter cream. Enjoy!
