The ingredients needed to make Watermelon juice:
  1. Take 2 cups watermelon pieces
  2. Take 2 tsp sugar
  3. Make ready 2 tsp roohafza syrup
  4. Take To taste salt
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp black salt
  6. Make ready 1 tsp cumin powder
  7. Take 2 cups Sparkling water or soda or cold drink

Instructions to make Watermelon juice:
  1. First of all, cut watermelon in pieces. Make very small pieces and keep them separate. Now add sugar and roohafza to the watermelon and grind it in mixture jar until it gets frothy.
  2. Now, add soda or sparkling water or cold drink. I used Sprite. Now add black salt, salt and cumin powder. Mix well
  3. Now, add small pieces of watermelon at the base of the glass. Pour the juice and add some more pieces of watermelon. Serve cold.
