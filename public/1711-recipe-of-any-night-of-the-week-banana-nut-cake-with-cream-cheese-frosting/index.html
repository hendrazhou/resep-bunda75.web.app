The ingredients needed to make Banana Nut Cake With Cream Cheese Frosting:
  1. Prepare Cake:
  2. Get 3 cups AP flour
  3. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Take 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  6. Make ready 10 tbs butter at room temperature
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar

  10. Make ready 3 eggs
  11. Take 5 medium to large bananas mashed

  13. Get 1/2 cup milk (I used nonfat)
  14. Take 1 cup crushed pecans or walnuts (I used pecans)
  15. Take Frosting:
  16. Get 8 oz cream cheese at room temperature
  17. Make ready 1/2 cup butter
  18. Take 2 tsp vanilla

Instructions to make Banana Nut Cake With Cream Cheese Frosting:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Combine flour, salt, baking soda and cinnamon in a medium mixing bowl.
  3. In a separate large mixing bowl, beat together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in vanilla and eggs to butter mixture.
  5. Stir in the mashed bananas in the butter mixture.
  6. Mix in the sour cream, milk and 1/2 of the flour mixture to butter mixture.
  7. When finished, mix in rest of the flour mixture to butter mixture.
  8. Fold in crushed pecans (or walnuts) to batter.
  9. Pour batter in a 9 by 13 inch lightly sprayed baking dish and bake 35 minutes or until done. Depending upon your elevation, it took me 35 minutes exactly.
  10. For the frosting, cream together the cream cheese, butter and vanilla.
  11. Add the powder sugar and if using an electric mixture, blend at medium speed until creamy and smooth texture.
  12. Add the powder sugar and if using an electric mixer, set to medium and blend away until texture is smooth and creamy.
  13. Enjoy :)
