The ingredients needed to make Strawberry-Mango Salsa:
  1. Prepare 10 strawberries; tops removed & small dice
  2. Take 3 jalapeños; seeded & minced
  3. Take 2 mangoes; small dice
  4. Get 1 avocado; small dice
  5. Get 1 small red onion; minced
  6. Get 1 lime; juiced
  7. Take 1/4 bundle cilantro; chiffonade
  8. Take large pinch salt
