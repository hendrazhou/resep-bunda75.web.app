The ingredients needed to make Key Lime Pie:
  1. Make ready pie
  2. Get 1 , 9" ready made frozen pie crust. You may make your own but ready made gets this on the table quicker
  3. Prepare 14 oz can of sweetened condensed milk
  4. Take 3 egg yolks plus one whole egg
  5. Take 1/2 cup key lime juice, about 16-20 key limes
  6. Prepare 2 tsp key lime zest
  7. Get 2 tbsp sugar

Steps to make Key Lime Pie:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Once heated bake pie shell for 10 minutes. While this bakes, prepare filling.
  3. In mixing bowl, mix sweetened condensed milk, eggs, lime juice and sugar. Mix well.
  4. Once shell is ready, add in pie filling.
  5. Bake an additional 15 minutes.
  6. Let cool and refrigerate for 2-4 hours.
  7. You may top with fresh whipped cream.
