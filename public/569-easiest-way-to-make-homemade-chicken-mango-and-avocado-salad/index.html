The ingredients needed to make Chicken, mango and avocado salad:
  1. Make ready 1 chicken breast, diced
  2. Get 1 tbsp skim milk
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp plain yogourt 2%
  4. Prepare 1/4 curry
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp rice vinegar
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp extra virgin oil
  7. Get 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  8. Make ready 1 mango, diced
  9. Take 1 avocado diced
  10. Prepare 1 lettuce
  11. Take 1 salt and pepper to taste

Steps to make Chicken, mango and avocado salad:
  1. Preheat the oven or grill
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, milk and curry and brush the chicken with it. Cook 10 minutes, add salt and pepper
  3. Dressing: mix rice vinegar, salt, pepper, olive oil, sesame oil. Whisk until well blended
  4. Mix everything together

Arrange salad mix on individual plates. Top with equal amounts of chicken mixture. Stir dressing to combine again, and drizzle dressing over salad.
