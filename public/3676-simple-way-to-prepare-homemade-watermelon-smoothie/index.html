The ingredients needed to make Watermelon Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 4 cups deseeded watermelon, cubed
  2. Take 1/4 cup sugar
  3. Take 4 tbsp mint mojito
  4. Prepare 1 cup ice
  5. Prepare 1/2 lemon, juiced
  6. Get 1 sprig mint

Steps to make Watermelon Smoothie:
  1. Blend the watermelon, sugar, mint mojito, ice and lemon juice together.
  2. Serve cold. Adjust sweetness as preferred.
  3. Top with cubed watermelon, ice cubes and mint.
