The ingredients needed to make Tender Coconut milk Barley croutons soup:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Barley cooked
  2. Get 1 cup Coconut milk
  3. Get 1/2 cup Tender coconut pieces
  4. Prepare to taste Salt
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp Pepper
  6. Take 20 gms Butter
  7. Take 2 tsp Croutons
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Cornstarch

Instructions to make Tender Coconut milk Barley croutons soup:
  1. Heat a pan put butter once it's melts add tender coconut pieces
  2. No need to fri more time just fri for while pour cooked Barley coconut milk
  3. I cup water keep boiling add cornstarch melt into another bowl just combine already boiling into this
  4. Keep boil once it's thick add salt,pepper mix well
  5. Take a serving bowl pour tender coconut milk Barley soup add croutons coconut pieces on top ready to serve hotly
