The ingredients needed to make Chilli Guava icecream:
  1. Get 1 cup chopped guava
  2. Take 1/2 cup condensed milk
  3. Prepare 1 cup fresh cream
  4. Prepare 1 cup powdered sugar
  5. Make ready to taste Salt and chilli powder

Steps to make Chilli Guava icecream:
  1. Grind all the ingredients together and keep it in an air tight container. Refrigerate for 8hrs.
  2. Serve it in bowl and sprinkle chilli powder and salt on it.
  3. You can use ripen guava too. Ripen guava tastes great without sprinkling chilli powder and salt even.
  4. Note : remove seeds part if you don't like crunchiness while eating.
