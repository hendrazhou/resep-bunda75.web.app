  2. Get 4 slices bread
  3. Take 2 spoons sugar
  4. Make ready 2 spoons cinnamon
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup milk (I think)
  6. Prepare 1 spoon butter
  7. Make ready 1 banana

Instructions to make Cinnamon French Toast:
  1. Mix 2 eggs, the milk, cinnamon and sugar together.
  2. Dip the bread in the mix.
  3. Spread butter on a pan and put the bread there.
  4. Take the bread out when it gets a little brown.
  5. Cook the eggs that's left. (I did mine sunny side up.)
  6. Cut the banana into pieces and spread it everywhere.
  7. Eat. (Tastes good with syrup too.)
