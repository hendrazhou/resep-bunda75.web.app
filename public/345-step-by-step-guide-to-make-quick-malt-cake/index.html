The ingredients needed to make Malt cake:
  1. Make ready 3 cups flour
  2. Make ready Half teaspoon salt
  3. Take 1/2 cup powdered milk
  4. Take 1/2 cup water to mix the milk
  5. Prepare 4 eggs
  6. Take 1/2 cup maltina
  7. Get 1/2 cup milo
  8. Take 1 cup butter
  9. Make ready 1 tbs vanilla extract
  10. Take 3 tsp baking powder

Instructions to make Malt cake:
  1. Heat the oven, coat pan with butter and flour and set aside.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt into a bowl. mix in the milo.
  3. Beat in butter one teaspoon at a time. Mix till the mixture looks like a damp sand
  4. Beat in egg one at a time. Add vanilla
  5. Mix the milk with water and add.
  6. Finally pour in the malt. Mix till batter is smooth.
  7. Pour in pan and bake for 35mins or until a skewer comes out smooth.
  8. Bring out and let to cool. Your cake is ready.
