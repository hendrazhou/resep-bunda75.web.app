The ingredients needed to make Mango Cake:
  1. Get 4 large eggs
  2. Take 1 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 cup flour
  4. Prepare 1 large mango
  5. Prepare 40 g olive oil
  6. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  7. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
  8. Take 1 tsp mango essence
  9. Make ready juice of 1 lemon
  10. Prepare zest of 1 lemon
  11. Take 1/2 cup chopped hazelnuts

Instructions to make Mango Cake:
  1. Separate the egg whites and use a mixer to whisk them until they become fluffy and form soft peaks.
  2. Gradually add the sugar, then fold in the egg yolks gently.
  3. Sift the flour and mix it in gradually.
  4. Roughly chop the mango and blend in a blender to form a puree, then add it to your bowl.
  5. Add all the remaining ingredients and mix well, then pour into an oiled cake pan.
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C and bake the cake for 40-50 minutes or until fully cooked.
  7. Allow to cool outside oven for atleast 15 minutes. garnish with whipped cream and roasted hazelnuts.
