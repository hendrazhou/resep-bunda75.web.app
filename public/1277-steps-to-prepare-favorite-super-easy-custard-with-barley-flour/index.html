Instructions to make Super Easy Custard With Barley Flour:
  1. Combine all the ingredients other than the ● ingredients together in a mixer or food processor. Mix or pulse for 3-5 seconds so as not to over mix it.
  2. Transfer what you made in Step 1 to a heatproof bowl and cook for 2 minutes in the microwave. Once it's done, return to the mixer and mix for 3-5 seconds.
  3. Cook it once again in the microwave for 1-1 minute and 30 seconds. Whilst still hot, add in the ● ingredients mixing well with a whisk.
  4. So that the surface doesn't dry out, cover it with plastic wrap. Once it has cooled, chill in the fridge and it's ready.
