The ingredients needed to make Easy Black Bean Salsa:
  1. Get 4 Roma Tomatoes
  2. Make ready 1 large Red Onion
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp Lime Zest
  4. Get 1 1/2 cup Cilantro
  5. Get 1 tbsp Ground Cumin
  6. Take 3 tbsp Lime Juice
  7. Take 1 1/2 tsp Kosher Salt
  8. Take 1 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  9. Prepare 1 large Can of Black Beans, Drained

Instructions to make Easy Black Bean Salsa:
  1. Drain and set aside the black beans. Chop red onions, tomatoes, and cilantro into small pieces.
  2. In a blender add the zest of the lime, juice of the lime, cumin, salt, and olive oil, blend for approximately 2 minutes.
  3. Now combine black beans, tomatoes, onions, and cilantro all together mixing in the mixture from the blender. Let salsa stand 20 minutes then serve with tortilla chips.
