  2. REMOVE THE SEEDS
  3. Peel the bananas and cut into small pieces
  4. Put all in a blender
  5. Pour ROOHAFZA sharbat/ Honey/ or any sweet syrup
  6. Blend all. Garnish with mint leaves and ENJOY!
