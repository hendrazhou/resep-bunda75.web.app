The ingredients needed to make Fresh Lime Chicken Wings:
  1. Prepare 1 packages Taco mix
  2. Prepare 2 Lime
  3. Get 8 Chicken wings

Mix chicken wings with taco mix. Place chicken wings into prepared pan. Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat. Add chipotle peppers with adobo sauce Stir in lime juice.

Instructions to make Fresh Lime Chicken Wings:
  1. Dry chicken wings
  2. Mix chicken wings with taco mix.
  3. Marinade chicken wings for 2 hours.
  4. Put chicken wings in the oven at 350°F for 15 mins.
  5. Flip chicken wing every 5 minutes.
  6. Cut 1 lime for decoration. Use 1 lime to make lime juice.
  7. Squeeze lime juice on the wings before eating.
