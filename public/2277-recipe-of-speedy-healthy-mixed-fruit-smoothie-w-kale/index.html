The ingredients needed to make Healthy Mixed-Fruit Smoothie w Kale:
  1. Take 2 cup Mixed fruit- strawberries, raspberries, mangoes, peaches
  2. Prepare 1 cup Kale (can substitute or add spinach as well)
  3. Take 8 ice cubes
  4. Take 1/2 cup milk
  5. Take 1 cup plain light frozen yogurt
  6. Prepare 1 scoop of vanilla protein powder (or plain)
  7. Get 1 tbsp ground flax seed

  2. Add in kale and ice cubes and stir in slightly, then blend
