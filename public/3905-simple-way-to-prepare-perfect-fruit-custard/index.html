Instructions to make Fruit Custard:
  1. Dissolve custard powder in 2 tbsp milk. Set aside.
  2. Boil milk and sugar. After sugar dissolves add the custard paste slowly into milk and continue stirring till it thickens. Cool a bit.
  3. In a bowl add all the fruits and mix them. Pour the custard over the fruits and allow to set. Refrigerate and serve chill.
