Instructions to make Salty Green Mango:
  1. Peel Mango
  2. Cut in small pieces
  3. Squeeze the lime on the mango
  4. Add Salt
  5. Add Black Pepper
  6. Add Ground Cummin
  7. Mix well
  8. Taste. Add more seasoning if needed.
  9. Enjoy!
