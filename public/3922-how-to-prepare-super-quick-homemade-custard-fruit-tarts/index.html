The ingredients needed to make Custard Fruit Tarts:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp Custard powder (I have used mixed fruit)
  2. Get 1 pinch Baking powder
  3. Get 3/4 cup Maida
  4. Get 2 tablespoons Butter
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon Sugar
  6. Take 1 tablespoon milk
  7. Take 2 cups Milk
  8. Take 2 tablespoons Custard powder
  9. Take 2 tablespoons Sugar
  10. Get 1 teaspoon Vanilla essence
  11. Make ready 1 cup mixed Fruit

Steps to make Custard Fruit Tarts:
  1. Mix the custard powder and baking powder together.
  2. Take butter and sugar together in a bowl and cream together. Prepare a dough by mixing maida, milk and custard powder.
  3. Knead and smooth the dough. Divide it into 8 equal portions and roll it into a 4-inch round shape.
  4. Make small holes in it and keep it in molds.Bake it for 15 minutes at 180 degrees c.
  5. Add custard powder to 1/2 cup milk and keep aside. Heat the remaining milk and sugar and mix well.
  6. Add custard powder and vanilla essence. Stir continuously on low heat.
  7. Cool it and now fill prepared custard in these 8 tarts. - Now cool it and refrigerate it for 4-5 hours so that the custard is set. Add fruits on the top before serving.
