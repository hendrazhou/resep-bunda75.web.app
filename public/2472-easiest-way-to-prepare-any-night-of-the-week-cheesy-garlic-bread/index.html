The ingredients needed to make Cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Get 4 Sliced bread (I used homemade)
  2. Prepare 1 cup Grated cheese
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup Chopped onions
  4. Get 1 tbsp Chopped green chillies
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Red chilli flakes
  6. Take 1 tbsp Oregano
  7. Make ready to taste Salt
  8. Get 1 tsp Pepper
  9. Get 4 tbsp Butter
  10. Get 2 tbsp Chopped garlic
  11. Prepare 1 tsp Garluc powder

Steps to make Cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Take a pan heat butter add garlic and cook for 2 to 3 minutes
  2. Take a bread slice spread the prepared garlic butter
  3. Then spread cheese, onion, chillies, red chilli flakes., salt, pepper and garlic powder
