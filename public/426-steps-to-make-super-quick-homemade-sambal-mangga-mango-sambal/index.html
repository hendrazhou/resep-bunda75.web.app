The ingredients needed to make Sambal Mangga | Mango Sambal:
  1. Get 4 bird eye chilli peppers
  2. Get 2 cayenne peppers
  3. Make ready 1 tsp fish/shrimp paste
  4. Take 1 small scallion
  5. Make ready Half tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1 tsp palm sugar
  7. Take 1 tsp lime juice
  8. Make ready 1 slice slightly underripe mango

Steps to make Sambal Mangga | Mango Sambal:
  1. Cut the mango into small pieces and put aside
  2. Blend all the other ingredients or use mortar
  3. Mix with mango evenly and serve fresh.
  4. I used dried peppers because fresh peppers are out of season now.
