The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Sour Chicken Wings:
  1. Take 5 chicken wing drums
  2. Get 1 tsp vegetable oil
  3. Get 3 1/2 Tbsp vinegar
  4. Make ready 3 Tbsp each soy sauce, sake
  5. Take 2 Tbsp each sugar, mirin
  6. Make ready 1 Tbsp honey for glazing

Steps to make Sweet & Sour Chicken Wings:
  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan and sear the chicken on both sides until lightly browned.
  2. Put all ingredients (except honey) in a pan and simmer. Lower the heat, shake the pan from time to time and baste the chicken to glaze with the sauce and cook until the liquid is almost gone.
  3. Add honey and simmer until the sauce thickened and glazed.
