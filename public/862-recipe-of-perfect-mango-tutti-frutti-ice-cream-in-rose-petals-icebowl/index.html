The ingredients needed to make Mango tutti frutti ice cream in rose petals icebowl:
  1. Prepare 2 cup fresh cream
  2. Prepare 1 cup condensed milk
  3. Take 1 cup mango puree
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup tutti frutti
  5. Prepare 2 drops mango essence (optional)
  6. Get bowl For ice
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup rose petals
  8. Prepare 2 bowl different sizes
  9. Make ready 1 cup water

Instructions to make Mango tutti frutti ice cream in rose petals icebowl:
  1. In a bowl add fresh cream blend it with electric beater now add condensed milk again blend
  2. Add mango puree mix it and mango essence and tutti frutti
  3. Mix it and transfer it any airtight container and freeze about 6 to 8 hours into deep freezer
  4. For ice bowl take big size bowl add 1/2cup water and freeze it for bottom now add 1/2cup water and rose petals and place small bowls with water into center and freeze it for chilled
  5. Remove small bowl from center with apply some water now add mango ice cream into ice rose bowl and garnish with tutti frutti over it and enjoy
