The ingredients needed to make White Chocolate and. Cherries Trifle:
  1. Make ready 500 grams sliced cherries
  2. Take 1 1/2 can condensed milk
  3. Get 550 ml milk
  4. Take 2 tsp corn starch
  5. Prepare 270 grams white chocolate
  6. Get 1 packages tea biscuits (corn starch biscuit or butter biscuit)
  7. Make ready 1 small pieces of chocolate to decorate
  8. Get 10 cherries to decorate

Instructions to make White Chocolate and. Cherries Trifle:
  1. Slice the cherries and take out the core. Keep the cherry juices that come out when you slice.
  2. Mix the condensed milk, milk and corn starch togheter in a pan. Cook until it thickens into a cream.
  3. Turn off stove top and add the 270 grams of white chocolate in small pieces. Mix until the chocolate melts.
  4. Use a medium baking dish.
  5. Mix in a bowl the milk and cherry juice that was leftover to soak the biscuits.
  6. Line the bottom of the baking dish with the biscuits immersed in milk. Start the process of the dessert by intercalating layers one after another, one of cream, then the chopped cherries and last the moist biscuit.
  7. The last layer should be cream. Garnish with chopped chocolate and cherries.
