The ingredients needed to make Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal Muffins:
  1. Prepare 1 cup oats
  2. Prepare 1 cup buttermilk
  3. Make ready 1 cup flour
  4. Make ready 1 tsp. baking powder
  5. Take 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp. salt
  7. Take 1 tsp. cinnamon
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp. nutmeg
  9. Take 1 egg
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup brown sugar
  11. Prepare 1/4 cup oil
  12. Get 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  13. Make ready 1 medium apple, peeled, cored and diced

Steps to make Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. In a small bowl combine oats and buttermilk. Allow oats to soak in buttermilk for 15 minutes.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg.
  3. Once oats have sat for 15 minutes, add in the egg, brown sugar, vanilla extract and oil. Mix well to combine.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry and combine well.
  5. Fold in the diced apples.
  6. Spoon equally in a greased muffin tin. Bake for 12 minutes.
  7. Allow to cool on cooling rack. These can be frozen for popping in the microwave when needed. Enjoy!
