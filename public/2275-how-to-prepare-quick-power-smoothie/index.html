The ingredients needed to make Power Smoothie:
  1. Take 1 cup Mixed frozen fruit (berries, pineapple, mango, whatever you have available)
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup Plain greek non-fat yogurt
  3. Get 1/4 cup water (add more water for thinner consistancy)
  4. Get 1 Scoop of whey protein powder

Steps to make Power Smoothie:
  1. Combine fruit, yogurt and water in a blender. (A vitamix blender works best)
  2. Blend on high until smooth (approx. 30 seconds)
  3. Add protein powder and blend on high for 5 seconds.
  4. Serve
