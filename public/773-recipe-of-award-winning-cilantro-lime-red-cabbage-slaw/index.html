The ingredients needed to make Cilantro Lime Red Cabbage Slaw:
  1. Prepare 1/2 red cabbage
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  3. Make ready 3 tbsp sugar
  4. Get 3 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  5. Take 2 cup cilantro leaves, chopped
  6. Get 1/2 cup lime juice

Instructions to make Cilantro Lime Red Cabbage Slaw:

  2. Combine vinegar, sugar, cabbage, cilantro, mayonnaise, and lime juice in a bowl, cover.
  3. Chill until chilly, this will really depend on your fridge
  4. Serve.
