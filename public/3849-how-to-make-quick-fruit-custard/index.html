The ingredients needed to make Fruit Custard:
  1. Take 1/2 Ltr full cream milk
  2. Prepare 3 Tbsp Fresh cream
  3. Prepare Some Chopped fruits of your choice
  4. Get Some Raisins
  5. Make ready 2 Tbsp jaggery powder+ 1 Tsp sugar
  6. Take 2 Tbsp Custard powder
  7. Prepare 1 Tbsp butter

Instructions to make Fruit Custard:
  1. Mix Custard powder with 1/4 cup of milk and keep aside.
  2. Heat milk in a pan at low flame with sugar and add Custard mixture. Cook 3-4 min at low flame and then turn off the flame.
  3. Now add butter and mix & let the cool down it in refrigerator for 20 min. Now add fruits, raisins and mix.
  4. Serve chilled.
