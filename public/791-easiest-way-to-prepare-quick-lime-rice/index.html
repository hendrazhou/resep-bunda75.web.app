The ingredients needed to make Lime Rice:
  1. Get 1 tbsp oil
  2. Get 1 cup bismati rice
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp lime juice
  4. Get 2 cup water
  5. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp cilantro

Instructions to make Lime Rice:
  1. Heat oil or butter until melted
  2. Add rice and lime juice
  3. Stir for 1 minute
  4. Add water and salt bring to a full rolling boil
  5. Cover turn heat to simmer ove low heat until rice is tender and water is absorbed about 25 minutes
  6. Fluff rice with fork and add cilantro
