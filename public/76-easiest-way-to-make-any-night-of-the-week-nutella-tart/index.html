The ingredients needed to make Nutella Tart:
  1. Get pasta frolla dough
  2. Take 200 grams all-purpose flour
  3. Get 140 grams unsalted butter
  4. Get 60 grams sugar
  5. Take 1 egg yolk
  6. Take 1 pinch salt
  7. Take 300 grams nutella

Instructions to make Nutella Tart:
  1. Softly mix all the ingredients for the pasta frolla until you have an homogeneous dough
  2. Put it in the fridge to rest for 20 minutes
  3. Flatten 2/3 of the dough using a rolling pin (i do not have one, so i have to use an empty bottle, but it works :P )
  4. Put it into a round cake pan
  5. Spread nutella on it using a spoon
  6. Make the borders and using the last 1/3 of dough make a net on the top
  7. Bake in the oven at 180°C for ~ 40 minutes
