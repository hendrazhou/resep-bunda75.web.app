Steps to make Crispy nachos chicken:
  1. In a mixing bowl combine maida, eggs, mustard sauce, red chilli powder, lemon juice, salt, pepper powder with 2 tbsp water. make a smooth batter. marinate the chicken strips in this batter for 2-3 hrs.
  2. In another mixing bowl combine bread crumbs, nacho crumbs & coriander leaves.
  3. Carefully place the marinated chicken strip in the bread-nachos crumb mixture & coat evenly from all sides. repeat the same process for remaining marinated chicken strips. refrigerate the bread coated chicken strips for 1/2 hr.
  4. Heat oil & deep fry the chicken strips on medium heat until light golden from all sides. - serve hot with crisp fried french fries & coleslaw salad.
  5. For coleslaw salad : combine all the coleslaw ingredients in a mixing bowl & refrigerate for 1 hr.
  6. Serve crisp hot
