Instructions to make Ruffle Sriracha Nachos:
  1. Place Ruffles in a single layer on plate

  3. Place chopped parsley or cilantro atop meat
  4. Place grated cheese atop meat
  5. Microwave on high thirty Seconds
  6. Take out and place dashes of Sriracha sauce on cheese
