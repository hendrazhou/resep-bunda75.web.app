The ingredients needed to make Easy flan pie:
  1. Take 1 sponge flan 200 g
  2. Get 1 pack of instant vanilla pudding mix
  3. Make ready 1 cup milk
  4. Get 8 strawberries sliced
  5. Take 1/2 banana sliced
  6. Get 1 cup cool whip
