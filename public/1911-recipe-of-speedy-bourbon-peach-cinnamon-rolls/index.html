Steps to make Bourbon peach Cinnamon Rolls:
  1. Slice your peaches fairly thin and put into a bowl.
  2. Add your bourbon and brown sugar, just enough bourbon to coat the peaches and brown sugar
  3. In a greased muffin pan, put a small pat of butter at the bottom
  4. Line the bottom with sliced peaches
  5. Top with your cinnamon roll and press down firmly
  6. Bake 350 for 15/20 mims depending on your oven.
  7. Use a butter knife to run around the edges, flip your pan over and enjoy your cinnamon rolls!
