Instructions to make 20 minute muffins:
  1. Place a sieve on a big mixing bowl, add 1 cup plain flour, 1 tsp baking powder, ½ tsp baking soda, stir everything well.
  2. Now add ½ cup fine sugar,3 tbsp tooti frooti, choco chips mix well.
  3. Now add ¼ cup oil,½ cup curd, ½ tsp vanilla essence, mix everything well then add ¼ cup water, whisk well and make sure there is no lump present.
  4. Now place muffins butter paper into the muffins tray, pour the batter into greased & lined moulds add tooti frooti on the top of it.
  5. Bake at 180°c for about 20 minutes.
  6. Used tooti frooti, choco chips both with the same process
