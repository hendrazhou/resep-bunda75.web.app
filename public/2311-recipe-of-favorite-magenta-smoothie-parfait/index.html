The ingredients needed to make Magenta Smoothie Parfait:
  1. Take 1 mango sliced
  2. Get 1 cup pineapple
  3. Get 1/2 cup beet root
  4. Get 1 teaspoon Ginger minced
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup Greek yogurt plain
  6. Get 1/2 cup blue berries
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon Honey
  8. Prepare 1 tablespoon chia(optional)

Steps to make Magenta Smoothie Parfait:
  1. Blend the mango, beet, pineapple, ginger, and honey. If it's too thick you can add a little water at a time.
  2. Add the Greek yogurt to the cup
  3. Add blueberries
  4. Then add the smoothie mix
  5. Chia seeds optional *
