The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti cake:
  1. Make ready 2 cups flour
  2. Get 1.25 cups sugar
  3. Get 1/2 cup tutti frutti
  4. Get 2 tbsp butter
  5. Prepare 3/4 cup milk
  6. Make ready 2 eggs
  7. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  9. Make ready 3 tsp vanilla essence

Instructions to make Tutti frutti cake:
  1. Preheat oven at 180°c
  2. Sieve the flour, baking powde and baking soda and keep aside
  3. Mix tutti frutti with a little flour(2tbsp) and set aside
  4. Combine butter and sugar
  5. Add the eggs one by one to sugar butter mixture and mix well
  6. Now add vanilla essence, milk and mix well
  7. Now add the flour, baking powder and soda mixture and mix until creamy batter is formed and avoid lumps.
  8. Now fold in tutti frutti
  9. Now grease a cake tray and dust with flour
  10. Pour the batter into tray and knock towards the counter to make it evenly spread and also knock back the air
  11. Bake for 35-40minutes
  12. Check by inserting toothpick
