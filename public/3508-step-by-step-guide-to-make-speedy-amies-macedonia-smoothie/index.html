The ingredients needed to make AMIEs MACEDONIA smoothie:
  1. Get 300 grams watermelon, cut into chunks
  2. Take 300 grams melon, cut into chunks
  3. Get 100 grams grapes
  4. Get 4 prunes
  5. Prepare 3 stick celery
  6. Get 2 peaches
  7. Take 1/2 apple
  8. Get 1 fresh juice of half lemon
  9. Prepare 1 mint leaves (optional)

Steps to make AMIEs MACEDONIA smoothie:
  1. Place all fruits except watermelon and melon in a blender and add lemon juice. Process until all fruits are liquefied and mixture is smooth.
  2. Place watermelon and melon in a big glass and pour the fruit mixture. You can add crushed ice cubes, if you want. Garnish with mint leaves, if desired.
  3. Serve immediately. Share and enjoy.
