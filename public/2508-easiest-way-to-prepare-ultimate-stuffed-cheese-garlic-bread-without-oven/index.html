The ingredients needed to make Stuffed cheese Garlic bread without oven:
  1. Get 250 gms maida
  2. Prepare 1 teaspoon yeast
  3. Take TO taste Salt
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon grated garlic
  6. Take 100 gm cheese
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon crushed pepper
  8. Make ready 50-100 ml water

Steps to make Stuffed cheese Garlic bread without oven:
  1. In 1cup luck warm water put yeast, sugar and salt. Give rest for 10 minutes
  2. Knead the dough with this water. Give rest for10 -30 minutes
  3. Roll the dough in 1inch thik. Make roti.
  4. Melt the butter put pepper and crushed garlic. Spread it in half roti. Put grated cheese in half portions. Fold it. Brush the butter in upper side. Make a mark. put it in the pan.
  5. For baking heat the PAN. Put salt in it. Put stand keep the pan in it. Bake for 30 minute.
