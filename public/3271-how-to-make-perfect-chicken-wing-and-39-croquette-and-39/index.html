The ingredients needed to make Chicken Wing 'Croquette':
  1. Get 4 medium chicken wings
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp pepper
  3. Prepare 2 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 2 1/2 tsp ajinomoto
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  6. Prepare 1 egg
  7. Prepare 5 tbsp kentucky powder
  8. Prepare 1 self raising flour
  9. Get 1 oil to fry

Steps to make Chicken Wing 'Croquette':
  1. cut the chicken wings in 2
  2. In a bowl mix all ingredients with wings except the flour n oil
  3. Let it marinated for 2hours
  4. Put the flour in a plate
  5. take 1piece of marinated wings dip in flour properly then put in oil to fry
  6. Let it cook till brown by turning 2 or 3 times
  7. Put oil in a saucepan on medium heat
  8. Serve with bread or eat like snacks
