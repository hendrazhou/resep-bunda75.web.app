Instructions to make Fruits in custard sauce:
  1. Cut grapes into halves.Peel and cut apples into slices.
  2. Heat milk in a pan.Boil.
  3. Dissolve custard powder into 2 tbsp cold milk.Add to boiling milk.Stir continuously to avoid lumping.
  4. Add sugar,cook for more 2 minutes.Remove from heat.
  5. Take out in a bowl and keep in refrigerator to chill.
  6. When chilled,add vanilla essence and lemon juice.Mix.
  7. Add all sliced fruits.Mix Serve.
