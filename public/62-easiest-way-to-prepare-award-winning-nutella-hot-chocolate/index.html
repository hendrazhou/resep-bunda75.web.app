Steps to make Nutella hot chocolate:
  1. Add 1/3 cup of milk in a small saucepan and 3 tablespoons of Nutella. Put on medium heat.
  2. Whisk them together until blended
  3. And the remaining milk into the saucepan. Increase heat to medium-high. Whisk again until hot and frothy.
  4. Pour into ur favourite mug and enjoy! You may also add spray cream on top.
