Steps to make Fruit Custard:
  1. Firstly milk boiled.
  2. Take some portion from that warm milk and add custard powder 5 table spoon.Mix it well.
  3. Add sugar and custard powder mix into the warm milk.Stir well so that lumps get disappeared.
  4. Add nicely cut fruits into it.Keep it in fridge for 4 to5 hours.
  5. Serve it cold and sweet yummy custard.
