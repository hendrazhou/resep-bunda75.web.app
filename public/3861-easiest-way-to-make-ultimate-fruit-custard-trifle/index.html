The ingredients needed to make Fruit custard trifle:
  1. Get 1 ltr milk
  2. Take 3 tbsp custard powder (vanilla flavor) mix in cold milk
  3. Take 1 cup sugar
  4. Get as required Fruits as per choice cut into cubes
  5. Get as required Jelly (any flavor)
  6. Make ready as required Chocolate for Garnishing
  7. Get as required Sliced cake
  8. Take drops Lemon few

Instructions to make Fruit custard trifle:
  1. Heat pan add milk and sugar let it boil and add custard powder slurry and whisk it to avoid lumps until it get thicker….. Now let it cool on room temperature
  2. Boil water and jelly powder stir it until it get completely dilute…. Now set it in plate and cut into cubes….. Cut fruits into cubes add sugar and few drops of lemon
  3. Take a tray or glass put a cake in bottom layer…. Then add jelly…. Now add custard… Then layer with fruits and again with custard…. Now garnish it chocolate and some fruits
