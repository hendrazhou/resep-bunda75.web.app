Steps to make My Loaded Chicken & Bacon Nacho Cheesey Bake ðŸ˜‰:

  2. Next add the plain Nancho crisp and spread all over
  3. Next the thick cut bacon
  4. Add the Chicken on top of the bacon
  5. Add 5 tbls BBQ Sauce over the chicken
