The ingredients needed to make Lime, beer and chipotle roasted pork:
  1. Prepare 2 limes
  2. Get 1 (12 oz) can of beer
  3. Get 3 chipotle peppers in Adobe sauce, sliced
  4. Prepare 6 garlic cloves, sliced
  5. Prepare 1 Tbsp olive oil
  6. Make ready 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
  7. Make ready 4 thick cut pork chops or a 12oz tenderloin

  3. Put pork in a baking dish. Cover pork with blended marinade and refrigerate for 1 hour.
