Instructions to make Super Simple Nachos:
  1. Go to Costco and get an 8 pack of black beans, that gigantic 5 pound bag of tortilla chips, a 2 pack of shredded Mexican cheese blend, and a rotisserie chicken.
  2. Dump a good amount of chips on a cookie sheet, shred some chicken, and open/drain the beans.
  3. It goes beans, then chicken, then cheese then sprinkle the jalapenos in the cheese. Put on the middle rack of the oven on broil until your cheese changes colors and the chips that didn't get anything almost burn
