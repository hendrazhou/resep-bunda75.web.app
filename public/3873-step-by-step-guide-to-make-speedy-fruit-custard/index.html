The ingredients needed to make Fruit custard:
  1. Get 1 litre milk
  2. Get 2 tbsp vanilla custard powder
  3. Take 1/2 tsp Cardamom powder
  4. Prepare 2-3 tsp trutti frutti
  5. Prepare 1 apple (peel and cut into small pieces)
  6. Take 1 banana (peel and cut into small pieces)
  7. Take 1 mango (peel and cut into small pieces)

Instructions to make Fruit custard:
  1. In a saucepan add milk,boil it. Take a small bowl add custard powder and 2 spoon milk, make a lump free mixture. Gradually Pour this into milk.Stir continuesly. Simmer on low medium flame. When mixture thickens, turn off the flame.
  2. Transfer it in bowl. Let it cool
  3. Add fresh fruits,trutti frutti and cardamom powder. Mix it well.Refrigirate It for 2 hrs.
  4. Transfer it in serving bowls or cups Gsrnish with Truti frutti. Serve chilled.
