The ingredients needed to make Royal sago Falooda.:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup Sago/Sabudana
  2. Prepare 2 tsp Sabja seeds/Basil seeds
  3. Prepare 1 scoop any ice cream
  4. Take 1 tbsp Rose syrup
  5. Make ready 1 handful chopped dry fruits like almonds,raisins
  6. Take 2 tsp Strawberry Jelly
  7. Take 1/2 Glass milk
  8. Take 1 cherry to garnish
  9. Take 1 tsp dry coconut powder
  10. Make ready 4+ 1/2 Cup water
  11. Take 1 tsp Tutti frutti

Steps to make Royal sago Falooda.:
  1. Boil 1/2 cup sago for 10 mins in a high flame in 4 cup water.Stir it continously and then drain all water and pour cool water over it.Soak sabja seeds with water for Half hour
  2. Take a glass.Add Rose syrup tp it then add soaked basil seeds.Add boiled sago to it.
  3. Mix rose syrup with milk in a bowl.Add this to the glass in step 2.
  4. Add some jelly,dry fruits and coconut powder.Now add any ice cream to this glass.
  5. Garnish this falooda with dry fruits and tutty frutti and dry fruits
