The ingredients needed to make Salsa for nachos:
  1. Make ready 1 Diced Tomato
  2. Prepare 1/2 of a chopped onion
  3. Prepare 1 clove of diced Garlic
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil (extra virgin is optional)
  5. Prepare salt and pepper
  6. Prepare 2 tsp tomato paste
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp water

Instructions to make Salsa for nachos:
  1. Spread oil on pan (preferably a small pan) and heat until good for frying.
  2. Fry garlic on pan until light brown.
  3. Fry onions until close to soft.
  4. Add tomatoes and stir fry whole mixture for 2 minutes(or until the tomatoes get soft).
  5. Add tomato paste.
  6. Add water to mixture and stir for a little bit until it thickens.
  7. At this point, you can have a taste of the salsa. Add some more water and/or paste if you wanna change the consistency or taste.
  8. Add salt and pepper
