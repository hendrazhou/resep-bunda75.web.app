The ingredients needed to make Honey Lime Chicken:
  1. Prepare 6 pcs boneless chicken thighs with the skin on
  2. Make ready 6 cloves garlic,minced
  3. Prepare some cilantro leaves, minced
  4. Make ready For the sauce:
  5. Get 3 Tbsp honey
  6. Take 3 Tbsp soy sauce
  7. Get 1,5 Tbsp fresh lime juice
  8. Take 1 tsp paprika powder
  9. Get to taste salt and black pepper

Steps to make Honey Lime Chicken:
  1. In a small bowl, mix well all the sauce ingredients.
  2. Arrange the chicken on a baking pan lined with aluminum foil.
  3. Add the sauce and mix the chicken with the sauce. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  4. Cover the chicken with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in a 400 F preheated oven for 20 minutes.
  6. Open the aluminium foil cover. Stir the chicken with the sauce again. Bake for another 3-5 minutes or until the chicken is golden brown. Remove from the oven.
  7. Garnish with minced cilantro leaves.
  8. Enjoy! 😋
