The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. Get 4-6 pieces French loaf
  2. Make ready 50 grams garlic
  3. Take 50 grams green onions finely chopped
  4. Take 1 capsicum
  5. Get 1 tsp oregano
  6. Take 1 tsp chilli flakes
  7. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp coriander
  9. Prepare 50 gms butter
  10. Prepare to taste Salt
  11. Get as required Grated cheese

Steps to make Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. Take 50 g of butter in a bowl.
  2. Now add chopped capsicum, garlic and green onion.
  3. Now add coriander, chilli flakes and oregano.
  4. Mix it well.
  5. Spread the mixture on the bread and add cheese on it.
  6. Now bake it for 5 to 10 minutes at 180 degrees.
