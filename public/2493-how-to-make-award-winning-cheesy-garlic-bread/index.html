The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. Take 4 Bread Slices
  2. Get 4 Cheese slices
  3. Get 1 Chopped onion
  4. Get 2 Chopped green Chilli
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup Butter
  6. Get 1 tbsp Garlic paste
  7. Prepare to taste Salt

Instructions to make Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. Melt butter lightly in the pan, add garlic paste and cook for few seconds.. Switch off the gas and keep aside.
  2. Take bread Slices, spread the butter mixture.Add green chillies, chopped onion on that, spread the cheese slice. Press it firmly.
