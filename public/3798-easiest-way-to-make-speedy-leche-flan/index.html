  2. Take 1 can evaporated milk
  3. Get 1 can condensed milk
  4. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Prepare 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  6. Take 1 cup granulated sugar

Steps to make Leche Flan:
  1. Mix all the ingredients together.
  2. Melt 2 to 3 tablespoons of sugar in a lianera until it's golden brown.
  3. Pour the mixture into a lianera with melted sugar.
  4. Stream it for 20 to 25 mins. Let it cool, then put it in the fridge overnight before serving.
