The ingredients needed to make GUAVA DIP Or SPREAD:
  1. Prepare 1 kg Guava fruit
  2. Take 2 Cup Water
  3. Take 1 cup Sugar
  4. Prepare pinch orange food colour
  5. Take 1/2 cup Lemon juice
  6. Prepare 2 Spoon Desi Ghee

Instructions to make GUAVA DIP Or SPREAD:
  1. Take 1 and half kg fresh Guava wash it and cut into pieces then put it into pressure cooker with 1 or 2 Cup Water then keep on medium flame for 1 or 2 whistle for boiling the Guavas Afterwards,Switch off the flame.
  2. After that drain the water of Guavas in a Container then put the Guavas in a mixing jar and blend it.
  3. Now after blending all Guavas it's become a smooth paste.Now with the help of Drainer, Drain the Guavas paste in a Container and removing the Guavas Seeds. In 1 and half kg Guavas you will get 1 big bowl ful Guavas paste in equal proportions you need sugar.
  4. Now take a Kadhai or Pan add 1 big bowl Guava Paste equal proportions Sugar 1 big bowl then add half Cup Lemon juice,2 Spoon Desi Ghee then Stir it Continuesly for 5 to 7 min and add food Colour again Stir it for 15 to 20 min then Guavas paste become thick.
  5. Now at last Ghee float over Surface, Switch off the flame put the paste into Container and Cool it down on room temperature and Refrigerate for 2 hours your GUAVA DIP Or SPREAD is Ready to Enjoy.Eat With Roti, Parathas and Bread.
