The ingredients needed to make Nutella cake with Coconut:
  1. Prepare 3 Eggs
  2. Prepare 1 cup Coconut yogurt
  3. Get 200 grams Wheat flour
  4. Take 6 tbsp Coconut flour
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup Vegetable oil
  7. Prepare 200 grams Sugar
  8. Make ready grated coconut
  9. Make ready Nutella
  10. Get 6 tbsp Coconut milk

Steps to make Nutella cake with Coconut:
  1. Beat eggs and sugar until very creamy.
  2. Add flour and coconut flour with baking powder, vegetable oil, coconut milk and yogurt coconut. Mix all ingredients well.
  3. Then the best moment comes: the Nutella! You can mix it in mass or simply spread it over the dough. The result is different, but I guarantee it hard to choose the best.
  4. Grease and flour a baking dish, pour the mixture you prepared and if you have not added the Nutella, now is the time.
  5. Bake this cake in a preheated oven at 180°C (356°F) for about 35-40 minutes.
  6. Bake this cake in a preheated oven at 180 ° C for about 35-40 minutes. Baked the cake, let it cool. At this point you can serve it or decorate it with Nutella after having it melted and the grated coconut.
