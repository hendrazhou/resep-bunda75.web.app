The ingredients needed to make spicy mango salsa:
  1. Make ready 1 mango
  2. Make ready 1 jalapeno (fresh or jar)
  3. Take 1 red pepper
  4. Get 1 green onion
  5. Prepare 1 lemon
  6. Take 1 lime
  7. Make ready 1 avocado
  8. Prepare 2 clove of garlic
  9. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  10. Make ready 1 cilantro

Steps to make spicy mango salsa:
  1. dice the mango put into a bowl
  2. chop or dice the jalapeno, green onions, garlic, red pepper, cilantro and avocado
  3. add chopped ingredients to the mango
  4. add 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  5. 1 table spoon on lemon juice
  6. add 1 teaspoon of salt
  7. mix and serve with chips, fish, chicken or tacos
