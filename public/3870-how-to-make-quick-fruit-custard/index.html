The ingredients needed to make Fruit Custard:
  1. Get 3 cup milk, boiled
  2. Take 2 tbsp custard powder
  3. Get 2 tbsp sugar
  4. Take 1/2 apple, chopped
  5. Prepare 1 small banana, sliced
  6. Get 1 tbsp Pomegranate krenels
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp Almonds, chopped

Steps to make Fruit Custard:
  1. First take a wide pan add milk bring to a boil, add sugar, stir till sugar dissolves.
  2. Take a small bowl add custard powder and 1 tbsp water mix well.
  3. Add custard mixture into the boiled milk stir regularly till thicken, then flame off.
  4. Cool down custard to room temperature. Add fruits and nuts. Refrigerate for 1-2 hour. Serve chilled.
