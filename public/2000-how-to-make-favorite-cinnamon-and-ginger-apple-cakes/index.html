The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon and ginger apple cakes:
  1. Take 50 grams brown sugar
  2. Make ready 75 grams butter
  3. Get 100 grams golden syrup
  4. Get 100 grams black treacle
  5. Prepare 1 tsp ground ginger
  6. Take 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Make ready 100 ml milk
  8. Get 1 egg
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  10. Prepare 150 grams buckwheat flour
  11. Get 1 large bramley apple, peeled and finely chopped
  12. Prepare icing
  13. Take 1 icing sugar
  14. Prepare 2 lemons

Instructions to make Cinnamon and ginger apple cakes:
  1. Preheat the oven to 170 celius. Prepare a fairy cake tray with liners, or you could make one big cake.
  2. Melt the butter, sugar, golden syrup, treacle, ginger and cinnamon in a pan over a low heat
  3. Dissolve the bicarbonate of soda in 1 tbsp of boiling water
  4. Take the saucepan off the heat and let in cool slightly. Mix in the milk, egg and bicarb
  5. Then mix together the apples and flour before stirring in the liquid ingredients
  6. It is a very wet mix so use a ice cream scoop/ladle or tablespoon measurer to fill the cases two thirds full
  7. Bake for 20 minutes or until risen and bouncy
  8. Leave to cool before icing
  9. For the icing juice the lemons and add enough icing sugar to make a thick icing. Spread over the top of the cakes. Enjoy!
