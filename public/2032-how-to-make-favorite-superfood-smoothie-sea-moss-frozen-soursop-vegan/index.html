The ingredients needed to make SUPERFOOD Smoothie (sea moss, frozen soursop) Vegan:
  1. Get 4 oz sea moss gel
  2. Make ready 1 cup (or more) frozen soursop pulp
  3. Take 1.5 cup spring water
  4. Get 2 tsp minced ginger
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  6. Prepare 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
  7. Take 2.5 tbsp (or more) agave or honey
