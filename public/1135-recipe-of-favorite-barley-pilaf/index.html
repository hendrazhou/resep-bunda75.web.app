The ingredients needed to make Barley Pilaf:
  1. Prepare 1 cup barley
  2. Take 3 cups water
  3. Make ready 1 tablespoon powdered chicken stock
  4. Take 1 small onion
  5. Take 2 teaspoons crushed garlic
  6. Get 60 grams shitake mushrooms
  7. Make ready 4 sage leaves (1 teaspoon if dried)
  8. Get 1/4 lime
  9. Make ready 1 tablespoon butter
  10. Make ready 1 tablespoon soya sauce

Instructions to make Barley Pilaf:
  1. In pot put barley, stock and water bring to boil. Allow to simmer for 25 to 30 minutes until barley tender
  2. Chop onion, mushrooms and sage.
  3. In a pan saute onions and garlic in butter, when browned add mushrooms and sage, saute till brown
  4. Add soya sauce and lime, stir till all brown bits removed from bottom of pan.
  5. Drain barley and add to mushroom mix
  6. Mix together and serve as a side dish
