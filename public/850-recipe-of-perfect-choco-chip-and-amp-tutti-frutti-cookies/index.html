Steps to make Choco chip & Tutti frutti Cookies üç™:
  1. Preheat a pan with salt and stand for 10 min at medium flame.
  2. Take butter and sugar in a bowl & mix/ whisk it nicely. Now add milk, flour, vanilla essence, sugar & mix with a spatula with light hands. Divide the dough into 2 parts and add choco chips and Tutti frutti in both.
  3. Cover the dough and refrigerate for 25-30 mins.
  4. After 25 mins Start making cookies and put some choco chips and Tutti frutti on the top.
  5. Now keep 2-3 cookies in a plate and put it into preheated pan and bake for 18-20 mins.
