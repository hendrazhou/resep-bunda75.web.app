The ingredients needed to make Bananas Batch 8:
  1. Get 4 large ripe bananas
  2. Take 1/4 cup peach brandy
  3. Make ready 1 tablespoon sugar
  4. Get As needed salt
  5. Prepare 1 stick butter

Steps to make Bananas Batch 8:
  1. Cut the bananas lengthwise. Melt the butter in a pan. Add the bananas cut side down.
  2. After 10 minutes turn the best you can. Sprinkle lightly with salt across the top.
  3. Sprinkle with sugar and add the peach brandy. Simmer till the alcohol has evaporated. Cover and let rest, till ready to serve. I hope you enjoy!!!
