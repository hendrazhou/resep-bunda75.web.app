The ingredients needed to make Mango balsamic vinaigrette:
  1. Take 1 mango peeled and cut
  2. Get 1 cup white balsamic vinegar
  3. Take 1 small knob of ginger
  4. Take 1 clove garlic
  5. Make ready 4 TBSP brown sugar
  6. Make ready 3 TBSP mango jam (you can sub extra mango and sugar if needed)
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  8. Take 2 cups olive oil
  9. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper

Steps to make Mango balsamic vinaigrette:
  1. In a blender add all ingredients EXCEPT oil and start to puree.
  2. Slowly add oil until dressing emulsifies
  3. Taste and adjust as you see fit (more sugar if you want sweeter, vinegar for more tart, water if too thick)
