The ingredients needed to make Cheese garlic bread:
  1. Make ready 4 slices white bread
  2. Prepare 50 g butter
  3. Get 50-60 g mozarella cheese cube
  4. Prepare 8-10 garlic cloves
  5. Make ready 1 tsp origano or herbs
  6. Get 1 tsp chilli flakes

Instructions to make Cheese garlic bread:
  1. In a pan, add 2 tsp butter and lighly fry the bread slices (2 slices at a time). Keep aside
  2. In the same pan, add the remainin butter and crushed or finely chopped garlic.Saute for few seconds till get the nice aroma. Keep aside for colling
  3. Now take a bread slice, apply the butter garlic mixture, sprinkle origano & chilli flales. On top add grated cheese, again origano & chilli flakes. Heat the pan, add the bread slice and cover it till cheese is melted. Alternatively the bread slice can be kept in microwave for few seconds toelt the cheese.
  5. Cut each slices into two pieces. Transfer to serving plate. Sprinkle origano & chilli flakes if required.
