The ingredients needed to make Whole wheat flour Tutti Frutti cake:
  1. Get 1 cup wheat flour
  2. Get 1 cup curd
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp baking powder
  5. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  6. Get 2 drop yellow food colour
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup Tutti Frutti
  8. Get 1/4 cup condensed milk
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup ghee/ oil

Steps to make Whole wheat flour Tutti Frutti cake:
  1. Take curd and beat it,then take ghee and sugar,beat well.
  2. Now add condensed milk and mix it well.
  3. Now mixing it again by adding baking powder and soda.
  4. Seive the flour,dust the tooth fruity.
  5. Now gradually add flour in batter and mix with spatula.
  6. Add milk and mix with wire mixer just for smooth batter,no lumps.
  7. Add colour and tooty fruity,mix nycly.
  8. Take mold,transfer the batter, tap it twice.
  9. Add tooty fruity and out in oven.
  10. Bake it in pre heated oven for 30 to 35 minutes in 180° c.take out and check.
  11. After cooling demold,see your perfect tooty fruity cake is ready.
  12. Cut in the pieces and serve or put in the container.
