Steps to make Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. Firstly activate yeast with 1/4 cup Lukewarm milk, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1teaapoon active yeast For 5 minutes.
  2. Add 1 cup maida, pinch of salt, chopped garlic powder, mixed herbs, 1 tablespoon butter. Knead the dough for 10 mins. coat oil and rest for 2 hours.
  3. Punch the dough and flatten it to round shape.
  4. Mix 1/4 cup butter, mixed herbs, chilli flakes, chopped garlic, coriander leaves, corns.
  5. Then spread on top generously and top with mozzarella cheese around 1/4 cup on half of the dough.
  6. Fold the dough and seal the edges, brush remaining butter mixture, mark marks over the bread without cutting them fully.
  7. Bake in preheated oven at 200°c for 15/20 mins or till the bread turns brown on both the sides.
  8. Serve with mayo / ketchup
