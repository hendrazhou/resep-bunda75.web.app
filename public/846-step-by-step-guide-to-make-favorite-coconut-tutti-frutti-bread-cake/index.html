The ingredients needed to make Coconut tutti frutti bread cake:
  1. Take 5 slices bread(I used tuti fruti bread)
  2. Make ready as required Some loose tuti fruti for Garnishing
  3. Make ready 1 bowl freshly grated coconut
  4. Prepare 1tbp of freshly prepared tutti frutti syrup
  5. Get 1 bowl freshly prepared thick cream
  6. Prepare 4tbp grinded sugar
  7. Prepare For tuti fruti syrup
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  9. Prepare as required Some loose tuti fruti
  10. Prepare 1tbp grinded sugar
  11. Get For freshly prepared cream(or whipped cream)
  12. Take 1 cup fresh cream without water
  13. Take 1.5 cup milk powder
  14. Make ready 1tbp vanilla essence
  15. Prepare 2tbp grinded sugar(or icing sugar)

Traditionally tutti frutti cake is made with eggs just like plain cake in Indian bakeries. Here this eggless tutti frutti cake recipe is made with curd and oil as main ingredients. Instructions to make Coconut tutti frutti bread cake:
  1. In bowl add water, loose tuti fruti and grinded sugar. Boil it for 1 minute at high flame.
  2. In one bowl add one cup fresh cream without water, whisk it for 2 minutes. Then add 2 tbps grinded sugar and vanilla essence and whisk for another one minute
  3. Now take a slice of bread first apply 1 tbps tuti fruti syrup. Then apply freshly prepared cream.
  4. Now repeat these same steps for other 4 slices also
  5. Now garnish all sides with grated coconut. If moisture is there in grated coconut then on 30 sec cook in a pan and remove.let it cool and then garnish with it
  6. Now garnish with some tuti fruti and any topping you like. Instant bread cake is ready
