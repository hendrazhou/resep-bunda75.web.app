The ingredients needed to make Watermelon Punch:
  1. Get 1 medium watermelon
  2. Get Granadine syrup/any syrup available
  3. Make ready 1.5 L Strawberry flavoured drink powder
  4. Make ready 60 g Soda water/ Sprite
  5. Prepare 1 cup lime juice

Instructions to make Watermelon Punch:
  1. Get your ingredients ready
  2. Peel and cut watermelon in to chunks, then blend till smooth
  3. Sieve the watermelon juice set in large bowl add lime juice then powdered strawberry flavoured drink
  4. Finally add soda water in this recipe I use Sprite, stir well
  5. In a glass cup add grenadine syrup or any syrup available you have (I use my home made red syrup in this recipe) then add ice block then your watermelon punch done and enjoy.
