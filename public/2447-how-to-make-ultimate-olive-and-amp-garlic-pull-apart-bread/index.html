The ingredients needed to make Olive & Garlic Pull Apart Bread:
  1. Prepare 2 can pillsbury grands butter tastin biscuits (8pks)
  2. Take 6 tbsp jarred minced garlic
  3. Get 3/4 tbsp italian seasoning
  4. Prepare 4 oz can sliced black olives (well drained)
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup melted butter
  6. Prepare 2 cup motzarella cheese shredded
  7. Get 1 cup pizza sauce or more if u need.

Steps to make Olive & Garlic Pull Apart Bread:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. for this one I used a springform pan.
  3. cut biscuits into 6-8 equal pieces and spread around the pan in layers.
  4. melt butter and stir in garlic and italian seasoning.
  5. spread sliced olives over biscuits
  6. brush garlic butter sauce all over biscuits and olives until gone.
  7. sprinkle entire top with motz cheese
  8. bake for about 30-45 minutes or until cooked through.
  9. remove from pan and serve alongside my pull apart pizza! and a bowl of piza sauce for dipping!
  10. hint: if not garlicy enough for you.. make extra grlic butter sauce and use it for dipping as well!
