The ingredients needed to make Aunt Flo Nutella French Toast:
  1. Get 8 slice thick cut bacon, cut into bits
  2. Get ground black pepper
  3. Get 8 slice cinnamon bread
  4. Get Nutella
  5. Prepare 3 large eggs, beaten
  6. Make ready 1 cup whole milk
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp brown sugar
  8. Take tsp 1/8 ground cinnamon
  9. Make ready splash of vanilla extract
  10. Prepare pinch salt
  11. Get butter
  12. Make ready syrup
  13. Prepare powdered sugar

Instructions to make Aunt Flo Nutella French Toast:
  1. Fry bacon pieces in skillet. Pepper to taste. Drain on paper towel.
  2. Make 4 sandwhiches with cinnamon bread and Nutella, placing the crushed bacon pieces inside.
  3. Combine eggs, milk, brown sugar, cinnamon, vanilla extract, salt in MED bowl. Whisk together evenly.
  4. Heat LG skillet on MED/H with butter.
  5. Take Nutella sandwiches, one at a time, dip into egg mixture, covering both sides equally. Place in heated pan. Cook 3-4 minutes on each side making sure not to burn them.
  6. Serve with maple syrup and powder sugar and any leftover bacon pieces you have!
