Instructions to make Chocolate popcorn:
  1. Add veg oil into the dry pot (not on fire)
  2. Add your corn,cover the pot and place it on fire(in a low heat)and start shaking just above the fire every 10 seconds until the corn stop popping,turn off the fire and open
  3. Turn it into tray or bowl,place the pot on fire again and add butter,sugar, flavor(optional) when the sugar completely melt add in your popcorn and mix until its well combine,turn off the fire and add your powdered milo or bournvita mix well (make sure the popcorn is hot when adding the milo)
