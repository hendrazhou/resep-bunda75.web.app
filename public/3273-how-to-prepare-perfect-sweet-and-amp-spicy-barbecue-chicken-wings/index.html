The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Spicy Barbecue Chicken Wings:
  1. Make ready 24 pieces chicken wings
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup sweet & spicy barbecue sauce
  3. Take 1/4 cup sweet chili sauce
  4. Take 2 tbsp butter
  5. Get 1/3 cup brown sugar
  6. Get 1 tsp smoked paprika
  7. Take 1 tsp chili powder
  8. Take 1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
  9. Take 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp finely chopped chives (optional)
  11. Get 1 tsp onion powder
  12. Prepare 1 cup flour
  13. Get 1 cooking oil for frying
  14. Take 2 tbsp hot sauce (Louisiana or hot sauce of your choice)

Steps to make Sweet & Spicy Barbecue Chicken Wings:
  1. Wash & pat dry chicken. Season with onion powder, paprika, black pepper, chili and red pepper flakes. Cover & refrigerate, if not cooking right away.
  2. Combine barbecue sauce, sweet chili sauce, butter, hot sauce and brown sugar in a small sauce pan. Heat on low until smooth. Set aside.
  3. Meanwhile, heat oil.
  4. Lightly dredge wings in flour. Fry until golden brown.
  5. Drain cooked wings on paper towel. Keep warm and pour sauce all over wings. Mix well to coat each piece evenly.
  6. Sprinkle with chopped chives and serve with buttermilk or pepper corn ranch with a side of fresh veggies. Enjoy!
  7. Note: I purposely left out the salt, since all the sauces & spices are enough to really highlight the chicken. Trust me, you won't need or miss it at all in this recipe.
