The ingredients needed to make Mira's Milo Cookies:
  1. Get 125 grams Butter
  2. Take 3/4 cup Castor Sugar
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cup Self-Raising Flour
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup Milo Powder
  5. Take 1 Egg

Instructions to make Mira's Milo Cookies:
  1. Beat butter and sugar until smooth and creamy.
  2. Beat in egg until it's fluffy.
  3. Add flour and milo powder. Mix until it is well combined.
  4. By using spoon, shape the dough into balls. Don't worry if it's a little sticky.
  5. Bake for about 15-25 minutes, 160°c. Cool completely on a wire rack and enjoy your delicious cookies!
