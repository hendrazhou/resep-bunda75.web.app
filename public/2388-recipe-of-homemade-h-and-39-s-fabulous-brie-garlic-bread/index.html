The ingredients needed to make H's fabulous Brie Garlic Bread:
  1. Get 1 loaf ciabbatta or fresh french loaf
  2. Take 5 large cloves garlic crushed. more if required for a large loaf.
  3. Get 1 packages brie cheese and/or Camembert Cheese
  4. Prepare 1 stick butter, again, more if you have a large loaf

Instructions to make H's fabulous Brie Garlic Bread:
  1. preheat oven to 200
  2. slice the loaf in slits
  3. spread butter evenly into each slit.
  4. spread freshly crushed garlic into each slit
  5. add sliced cheese into each slit.
  6. roll in tin foil (shiny side in).
  7. bake for 15-20 Mins.
  8. enjoy with a barbeque or with oven roasted lamb loins and mash potatoes
