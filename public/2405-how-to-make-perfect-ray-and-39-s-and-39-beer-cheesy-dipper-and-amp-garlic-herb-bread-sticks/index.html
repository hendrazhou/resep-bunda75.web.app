The ingredients needed to make Ray's' Beer Cheesy dipper & garlic herb bread sticks:
  1. Make ready 1/4 cup butter
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup flour
  3. Make ready 3 cup cheddar cheese
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp parsley flakes
  5. Make ready 1 tsp garlic salt
  6. Get 1/2 tsp McCormick worshestire seasonings
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp herb seasoning
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp pepper
  9. Prepare 1/2 box 8 oz.softened cream cheese
  10. Prepare 2 cup dark beer
  11. Get bread sticks
  12. Take 1 pillsbury garlic bread sticks
  13. Prepare 1 parsley flakes
  14. Get 1 course ground pepper
  15. Make ready 1 garlic salt

Instructions to make Ray's' Beer Cheesy dipper & garlic herb bread sticks:
  1. make your cheese sauce adding butter all seasonings till butter melts, add in flour cook about 1minute, whisking well.Now add in beer cook till thickened add in cream cheese & cheese turn off heat when melted.
  2. You can add as much beer as you like depending on thickness. I usually use 2-4 cups
  3. roll out garlic bread sticks, add seasonings as you like, twist n bake as directed
