The ingredients needed to make Paneer Stuffed garlic bread:
  1. Make ready for DOUGH..
  2. Prepare 2 cups all-purpose flour
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp sugar

Steps to make Paneer Stuffed garlic bread:
  1. All-purpose flour dry instant yeast sugar and salt mix properly and make a dough and key pressed for 4 hours
  2. Take open add 1 tablespoon vegetable oil add chopped garlic & all veggies and stare now add grated paneer salt oregano and chilli flakes.
  3. Make a chapati by pizza dough and keep one side stuffing and put a mozzarella slice and fold the bread.
  4. Make a mark of cut and preheated oven 250 degrees both rod for 20 minutes.
  5. After 20 minutes garlic bread is ready and brushing by some butter.
