The ingredients needed to make Cheese Wheat Garlic bread:
  1. Take for garlic bread dough:
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup warm water
  3. Take 1 tsp dry yeast
  4. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  5. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Take 1 tsp oregano
  7. Make ready 1 tsp chilli flakes
  8. Take 1 cup wheat flour
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp water
  10. Make ready FOR GARLIC BUTTER:
  11. Prepare 3 tbsp butter
  12. Take 2 tbsp crushed garlic
  13. Make ready 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  14. Prepare Other ingredients:
  15. Prepare 1/4 cup grated cheese
  16. Make ready 2 tsp chilli flakes
  17. Get 2 tsp oregano

Instructions to make Cheese Wheat Garlic bread:
  1. In a bowl take warm water. Mix sugar in it and then mix dry yeast. Keep it aside for 10 minutes.
  2. Take a large bowl, put wheat flour in it. Then put oregano and chilli flakes in it. Mix well. Then add salt and the yeast mixture to the bowl.
  3. Knead the dough. Coat the dough with oil and cover and keep it aside for one hour.
  4. Take another bowl put butter, garlic and coriander in it. Mix well.
  5. Take the kept aside dough and punch it. Knead it once again for 2 minutes.
  6. Make the dough flat, apply some garlic butter on the base. Then put cheese on one side of the base.
  7. Fold the dough and seal the edges.
  8. Brush garlic butter on top, also put chilli flakes and oregano.
  9. Make cuts in the garlic bread, don’t cut completely.
  10. Bake in a preheated oven at 200 degree Celsius for 15 minutes.
  11. Cut the garlic bread into pieces and serve.
