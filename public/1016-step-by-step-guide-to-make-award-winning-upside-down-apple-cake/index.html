  2. Take 2 large apples and 1tbsp tutti frutti

Instructions to make Upside down apple cake:
  1. Arrange apples and tutti frutti in a greased cake tin lined with butter paper. Sprinkle sugar, cinnamon powder and nutmeg powder over it.
  2. For cake batter sieve the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl add water, oil,brown sugar and whisk for around 3-4 minutes.
  4. Now add all the dry ingredients in it and fold together by cut and fold method. Do not over mix.
  5. Finally add the cake mixture to the tin lined with apples.
  6. Preheat the oven and Bake at 180 degree C for 35 minutes.
  7. After 35 minutes check whether the cake is cooked with a skewer. If it comes out clean it is cooked.
  8. Immediately flip the cake when warm.
  9. Serve hot with vanilla ice cream and caramel sauce.
