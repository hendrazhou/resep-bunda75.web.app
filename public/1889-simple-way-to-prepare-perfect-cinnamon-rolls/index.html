The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon rolls:
  1. Prepare 1 cup warm milk
  2. Take 1 teaspoons salt
  3. Take 2 tablespoons sugar
  4. Get 4 tablespoons butter
  5. Make ready 1 package yeast
  6. Make ready 3 cups sifted flour
  7. Prepare 1/2 cups currants or raisins
  8. Prepare 5 round tablespoons sugar
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoons cinnamon

Steps to make Cinnamon rolls:
  1. Place milk salt sugar in bowl blend
  2. Add yeast and half of flour mix
  3. Add remaining flour and currants knead
  4. Cover let rise 2 to 3 hours till double in size
  5. Knead
  6. Roll to 1/2 inch thick
  7. Brush with melted butter
  8. Top with cinnamon sugar mix
  9. Roll like a jelly roll
  10. Cut in 1 inch slices
  11. Place on baking sheet brush with melted butter
  12. Let rise 1 hour
  13. Bake at 400 for 20 minutes
