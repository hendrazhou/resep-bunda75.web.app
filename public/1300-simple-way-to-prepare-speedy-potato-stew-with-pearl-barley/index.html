The ingredients needed to make Potato stew with pearl barley:
  1. Prepare 500 g potato
  2. Take 1 white onion
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp paprika powder
  4. Prepare 1 tsp smoked paprika
  5. Prepare Chilli flakes (optional)
  6. Prepare 80 g pearl barley
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  8. Take Salt and pepper

Steps to make Potato stew with pearl barley:
  1. Fry the onion with a little bit of oil.
  2. Take it of from the heat and add paprika powder and smoked paprika. Take it off from heat otherwise paprika can burn and become bitter.
  3. Add potato, barley and enough water to cover it. Season it with salt and pepper. Cook it on medium heat.
  4. Cook it until potato and barley are ready and soft. Serve it with salad or gherkins. Enjoy!
