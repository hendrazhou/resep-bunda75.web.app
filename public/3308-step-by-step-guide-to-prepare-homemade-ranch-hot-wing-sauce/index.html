Steps to make Ranch hot wing sauce:
  1. Melt stick if butter over medium heat.
  2. Add entire bottle of dressing ranch or blue cheese. i like either one but hubby doesnt like blue cheese so i usually use ranch.
  3. Turn up heat to medium high. keep stirring for about 5 minutes. be careful it doesnt boil over.
  4. Add parsley and ranch packet. turn heat down to medium and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour over wings or chicken. i once tried it with pasta and cut up chicken breast with cheese. it was like buffalo pasta. today im pouring it over drum sticks and throwing in my crockpot.
