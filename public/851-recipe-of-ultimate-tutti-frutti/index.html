The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti:
  1. Take 250 gm watermelon peel
  2. Get 250 gm sugar
  3. Take 1 ltr water
  4. Make ready As needed food colour, red , orange , green
Steps to make Tutti frutti:
  1. The white part of melon peel of gently cut it into small pieces
  2. Boil those pieces in water for 5 mint after boiling sieve them
  3. Then in a pan add 1bowl of sugar and 2bowl of water make it to boil
  4. Then put the melon pieces in sugar water and boil for 5 to 7 mint
  5. After that divided those pieces into three equal parts in bowl
  6. Then add any food colour of your choice in those bowls
  7. Mix it well with the help of a spoon
  8. Then leave them covered like this for 4 to 5 hours
  9. Then sieve it with help of siever
  10. Put them separately in a tray and leave to dry
  11. If it is not drying then you can dry it for some time even in the sun
