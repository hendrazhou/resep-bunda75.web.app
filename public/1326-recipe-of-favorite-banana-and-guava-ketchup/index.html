The ingredients needed to make Banana and guava ketchup:
  1. Get 1 large banana
  2. Get 3 large guava
  3. Take 2 tbsp honey
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp your favorite vinegar I used coconut vinegar
  5. Prepare 1 water for thinning
  6. Make ready 1 pinch salt

Instructions to make Banana and guava ketchup:
  1. Chop the banana.
  2. Peel the guava. Cut the guava into half remove the seeds. Chop the guava up. Put banana and guava into pot.
  3. Add to a pot with 2 tablespoonfuls of water. Add honey, vinegar and salt.
  4. Stir this together mixing well. Mash then add a little water if needed add teaspoonful at the time. If needed. simmer 10-12 minutes.
  5. Now you can blend it for smooth creamy ketchup or you can have it chunky.
  6. Chill and refrigerate if you not using right away will keep at least 2 weeks in the refrigerator.
  7. Hope you enjoy!
