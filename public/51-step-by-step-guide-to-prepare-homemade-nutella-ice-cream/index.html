The ingredients needed to make Nutella Ice Cream:
  1. Prepare 1 cup milk
  2. Get 2 cup heavy cream
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup additional sugar
  5. Get 4 egg yolks
  6. Take 1/2 cup nutella
  7. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)!!

Steps to make Nutella Ice Cream:
  1. In a sauce pan pour the milk, heavy cream and 1/2 cup of sugar. Cook until sugar dissolves
  2. Meanwhile in a bowl add the egg yolks and 1/4 cup of sugar. Use an electric mixer and mix for 3-4 mins
  3. Pour a little bit, 3/4 cup, of the hot mixture into the bowl. Mix together. Then pour that mixture back into sauce pan & cook on lowest setting for 8 more mins while stirring frequently
  4. In a bowl add the nutella and spread it a little. Pour half of the hot mixture over a strainer and use electric mixer to mix together
  5. add second half of hot mixture over a strainer to the bowl along with cinnamon + vanilla extract and stir with a spatula until blended together. ( looks like chocolate milk)
  6. I dont have an ice cream machine, so i do the following after its ready
  7. When its ready, I pour the mixture into a chilled bowl and put into the freezer. After every 30 mins stir lightly using a whisk until hardened.
  8. When done and ready to serve you can add some bananas or chocolate syrup on top :)
