Instructions to make Green Drink:
  1. Add water to blender.
  2. Cut cucumber into small chunks and add to blender.
  3. Blend.
  4. Cut chunks off of the Apple and add to blender as you cut.
  5. Increase blender speed and add remaining ingredients one at a time.
  6. Blend until liquefied.
