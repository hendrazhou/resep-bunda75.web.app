Instructions to make Cucumber,Spinach and Green tea Smoothie..#wecare:
  1. Boil 1 1/4 th cup of water for and switch off the flame.
  2. Add a green tea bag into it and allow it to brew for about 3 to 4 minutes with a lid on.
  3. Allow it to come down to the room temperature.
  4. Add freshly chopped baby spinach leaves to a blender.
  5. Next, add chopped cucumber to it along with the lemon juice and honey.
  6. Blend all into a fine smoothie full of vitamins and minerals.
  7. Serve immediately. Chill it before use if desired.
