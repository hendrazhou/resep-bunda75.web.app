The ingredients needed to make Fried Buffalo Chicken Wings:
  1. Make ready 1/4 cup Franks hot sauce
  2. Get 2 dash Of ground pepper
  3. Get 1 dash Of garlic powder
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup Flour
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp Paprika
  6. Get 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
  7. Get 1/4 tsp Salt
  8. Make ready 10 Chicken wings
  9. Get 1 1/2 Bottle of vegetable oil in a pan

Steps to make Fried Buffalo Chicken Wings:
  1. Combined flour, cayenne pepper, paprika, salt and pepper into a big ziplock bag and shake.
  2. Add thawed chicken wings into bag and shake till they are fully covered.
  3. Pour vegetable oil in pot and heat to 375°F
  4. Once heated drop 4-5 chicken wings in and cook till there's a golden brown crust.
  5. While waiting for them to fry, combined in a bowl, hot sauce, pepper, garlic powder and mix.
  6. While each chicken wing is fried dip in the sauce mix and serve!
