Steps to make Chrissy's Summer Vodka Mix!:
  1. fill a glass with desired amount of ice
  2. pour about 2 oz. (2 shots) of Titos vodka
  3. add about 2 oz. of pineapple juice, and 2 oz. of cranberry juice. (use more if u like)
  4. add 1 or 2 oz. of the limearita and stir
  5. squeeze a little fresh orange juice in and stir.
  6. stick a couple fresh orange slices in your drink with a sprig of mint
  7. taste and adjust ratios for desired flavor if needed. Enjoy!
