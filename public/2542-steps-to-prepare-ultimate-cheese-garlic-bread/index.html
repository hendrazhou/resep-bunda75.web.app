The ingredients needed to make Cheese garlic bread:
  1. Prepare 4 slice Bread
  2. Make ready 50 gms Butter
  3. Prepare 50 gms Grated processed cheese
  4. Take 4 Finely sliced garlic clove
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Chilli flax

Instructions to make Cheese garlic bread:
  1. Heat butter on a pan.
  2. Add garlic cloves and roast it.
  3. Roast the bread on the pan and spread the butter.
  4. On the top of the bread add roasted garlic.
  5. Spread grated cheese on it.
  6. Sprinkle some black pepper powder and cover the bread.
  7. Cook on a low flame till melt the cheese.
  8. Sprinkle some chilli flax and serve.
