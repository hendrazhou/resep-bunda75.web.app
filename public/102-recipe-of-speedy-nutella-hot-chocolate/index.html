Steps to make Nutella Hot Chocolate:
  1. Pour about 1/4 of the milk into a saucepan on medium-low heat.
  2. Add the Nutella and whisk until blended.
  3. Slowly stir in the remaining milk.
  4. Add a pinch of cinnamon and whisk until hot and frothy.
  5. Carefully pour the mixture into a mug, and add whipped cream, and an extra sprinkle of cinnamon.
  6. Enjoy!
