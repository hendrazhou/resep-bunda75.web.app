The ingredients needed to make Auntie Dee-Dee's Fire Roasted Salsa:
  1. Take 12 roma tomatoes
  2. Take 4 jalapeno peppers
  3. Make ready 2 serano peppers
  4. Prepare 1 medium red onion, chopped
  5. Take 1 cup cilantro, chopped
  6. Make ready 1 tbs salt
  7. Get 1 lime
  8. Take avocado, optional

Instructions to make Auntie Dee-Dee's Fire Roasted Salsa:
  1. On a grill, blacken tomatoes and peppers. Takes about 20-25 minutes
  2. Blend tomatoes, peppers and salt
  3. In a bowl, mix together tomato mixture, cilantro, and the juice of 1 lime
  4. For the perfect guacamole, add prepared salsa to a cubed or mashed up avocado
