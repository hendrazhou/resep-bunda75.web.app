The ingredients needed to make Chunky Strawberry Smoothie (Jamba Juice-Copy Cat):
  1. Make ready 1/4 cup Frozen Strawberries
  2. Make ready 1 each banana (Frozen banana works too)
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup Vanilla Yogurt ( Greek Yogurt works)
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Peanut Butter
  5. Make ready 10 each Goji Berries
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Chia Seeds
  7. Take 1/2 tsp Cacao Powder
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup Quaker Oats (Oats are about 75% Gluten Free they are made in same area as non Gluten Free grains, however I did some research and they are ok to eat in very light moderation.)
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup Almond Milk
  10. Take 1 tbsp Gluten Free Granola (For Topping)

Instructions to make Chunky Strawberry Smoothie (Jamba Juice-Copy Cat):
  1. Combine all remaining ingredients in your blender (Ninja, Bullet, Smoothie maker). Blend until all ingredients have blended together. Please note this is a thick smoothy (spoon eating). However if you'd like a more liquidity texture add more almond milk until you achieve desired consistency.
  2. Pour in your favorite cup :) sprinkle the gluten free granola on top, along with some slices of banana. Enjoy!!!
