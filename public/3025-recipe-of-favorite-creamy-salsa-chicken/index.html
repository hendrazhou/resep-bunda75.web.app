Steps to make Creamy salsa chicken:
  1. Place frozen chicken breasts in slow cooker.
  2. Pour jar of salsa on top of chicken and cook on low eight to 10 hours or until you can shred with a fork.
  3. Add cream cheese and allow to cook for 30 to 60 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
