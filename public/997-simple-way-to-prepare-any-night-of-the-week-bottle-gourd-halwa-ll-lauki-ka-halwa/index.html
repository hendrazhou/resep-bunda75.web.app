The ingredients needed to make 🌟Bottle gourd halwa ll lauki ka halwa💚:
  1. Make ready 500 gms bottle guard
  2. Take 400 gms sugar
  3. Prepare 500 ml milk
  4. Get 4 tbsp ghee
  5. Get 1 Cup assorted nuts
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp multi color tutti frutti
  7. Prepare Pinch green food color (totally optional)

Steps to make 🌟Bottle gourd halwa ll lauki ka halwa💚:
  1. Firstly, peel off the bottle gourd and shredded it finely.
  2. Heat a processed butter in a pressure cooker and add shredded bottle gourd and Cook it for 5 minutes now add sugar, milk mix it and Cook it for 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Now cover it with a lid and pressure cook it for 3 or 4 whistles, switch off the flame and let it cool completely, take it in a serving bowl and garnish with Assorted nuts and tutti frutti.
