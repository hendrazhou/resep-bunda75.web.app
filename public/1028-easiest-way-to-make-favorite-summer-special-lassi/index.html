The ingredients needed to make Summer Special Lassi:
  1. Make ready 750 grams Curd
  2. Take 2 cup Cold water
  3. Prepare 1 cup Sugar
  4. Get 1 cup Ice-Cubes
  5. Take 1 cup Grated coconut
  6. Prepare 5 tbsp Bournvita
  7. Get 1/4 cup Raisins
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup Cashew nuts
  9. Get 1/4 cup Tutti Frutti

Steps to make Summer Special Lassi:
  1. Take Curd in a large bowl. Add water, sugar and ice cubes.
  2. Churn it for 5 minutes.
  3. After Churn, pour it into serving glasses.
  4. Garnish with grated coconut, Bournvita, Raisins, Cashew nuts and tutti frutti.
  6. Then ready to serve.
