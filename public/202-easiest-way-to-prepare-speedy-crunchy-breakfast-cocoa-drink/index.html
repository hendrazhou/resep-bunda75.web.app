The ingredients needed to make Crunchy Breakfast Cocoa Drink:
  1. Get 1/3 cup Crunchy chocolate cereal
  2. Get 1 tsp Cocoa powder
  3. Get 1/2 cup Whipped cream
  4. Take 4 tbsp Milo or Ovaltine ( ready mix choco malt drink )
  5. Prepare 2 cup Cold water
  6. Take 1 Ice cubes

Steps to make Crunchy Breakfast Cocoa Drink:
  1. Open the ready mix choco malt drink and mix with cold water.

  3. Finely chop cereal and add them in the drink.
  4. Decorate with whipped cream and cocoa powder.
