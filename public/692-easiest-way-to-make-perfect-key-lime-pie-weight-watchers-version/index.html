The ingredients needed to make Key Lime Pie (Weight Watchers version).:
  1. Take 1 reduced fat graham cracker crust
  2. Prepare 1 small pack lime sugar free jello
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup boiling water
  4. Prepare 2 small containers yoplait light key lime yogurt
  5. Get 1 tub (8 oz) fat free cool whip

Instructions to make Key Lime Pie (Weight Watchers version).:
  1. Dissolve the jello in the boiling water.
  2. Stir in yogurt with wire whisk.
  3. Fold in cool whip.
  4. Spread in crust and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  5. Enjoy!
