  8. Take 1 garlic salt

Instructions to make BBQ Chicken Wings:
  1. put the water in a pan
  2. add salt and sugar
  3. bring to a boil stirring frequently
  4. let boil until all sugar and salt is dissolved
  5. add ice cubes to brine and allow to cook
  6. pour brine over thawed chicken wings
  7. let set for 1 hour at minimum
  8. drain brine from chicken wings
  9. sprinkle paprika and garlic salt in wings
  10. grill them for 10 minutes or until done.
  11. cover with any bbq sauce you like and enjoy
