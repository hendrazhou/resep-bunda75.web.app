Instructions to make Eggless tutti frutti cake in cooker:
  1. Preheat the cooker using salt place a stand in the cooker after that grease the baking tin spread butter paper in it grease it also
  2. Take a mixing bowl add curd,powder sugar and butter mix it very well
  3. Sieve the dry ingedients
  4. Add the dry ingredients in the wet ingedients add vanilla essence and salt mix it add tutti frutti mix it
  5. Bake it in low medium flame for 55 to 60 minutes enjoy it with tea or coffee
