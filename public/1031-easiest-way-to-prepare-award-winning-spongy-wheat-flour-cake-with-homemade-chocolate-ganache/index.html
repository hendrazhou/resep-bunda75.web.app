The ingredients needed to make Spongy Wheat Flour Cake with homemade Chocolate Ganache:
  1. Prepare For Cake Batter:-
  2. Get 1 cup wheat flour
  3. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  4. Take 3/4 cup oil
  5. Make ready 1 cup milk
  6. Get 1 ripe banana
  7. Get 3 tsp cocoa powder
  8. Get 1 tsp custard powder
  9. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  10. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  11. Prepare 1 tsp vinegar
  12. Prepare 2 tsp tutti-frutti
  13. Prepare For Chocolate Ganache:-
  14. Take 1 cup milk
  15. Prepare 1 tsp refine flour
  16. Get 3 tsp sugar
  17. Get 1 dairy milk chocolate
  18. Take As required Fruit jam and tutti frutti for decoration

Steps to make Spongy Wheat Flour Cake with homemade Chocolate Ganache:
  1. In a mixing bowl take banana,sugar and oil mix well by whisking until sugar dissolves.
  2. Now take all rest ingredients except baking powder,baking soda and vinegar,mix well and make a smooth batter.
  3. Finally add baking soda,baking powder and vinegar,mix all for 1 minute in one direction and batter is ready to bake.
  4. Transfer cake batter in a greased cake tin and place it in a cooker for 35 minutes on low flame and let it bake.
  5. After 35 minutes check the cake by inserting a toothpick,if it's comes clean it means your cake is ready otherwise bake it for more 5 minutes.
  6. Take out the cake tin carefully and allow it to cool completely,meanwhile start the procedure for making homemade chocolate ganache.
  7. In a pan take all ingredients,mix well then switch on the flame and cook it and keep stirring constantly until it becomes a thick paste.
  8. Switch off the flame and allow it to cool completely….apply on cake evenly from all the sides and garnish the cake with fruit jam,powdered sugar and tutti-frutti.
  9. Spongy Wheat Flour Cake with homemade chocolate ganache is ready to serve.
