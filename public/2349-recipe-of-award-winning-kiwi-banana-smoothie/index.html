The ingredients needed to make Kiwi Banana Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 1 handful baby spinach
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup apple juice
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup vanilla greek yogurt
  4. Get 1/2 cup water, skim milk or unsweetened almond milk
  5. Make ready 1/2 small ripe banana(preferably frozen)
  6. Get 1 kiwi peeled and cut in half
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp ground flax seeds
  8. Take 5 ice cubes
  9. Make ready 1 optional packet of splenda or a small spoonful of honey. I prefer my smoothie without the added sweetness in this one
