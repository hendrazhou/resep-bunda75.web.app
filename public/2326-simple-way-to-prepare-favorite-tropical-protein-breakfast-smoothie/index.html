The ingredients needed to make Tropical Protein Breakfast Smoothie:
  1. Prepare 1/2 Banana sliced (70g)
  2. Get 1 cup Baby Spinach (21g)
  3. Take 2 Frozen Strawberries (28g)
  4. Make ready 3 Frozen Pineapple Chunks (38g)
  5. Take 3 Frozen Mango Chunks (43g)
  6. Make ready 3/4 cup Silk Protein Nut Milk
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup Non-Fat Vanilla Greek Yogurt
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup Nutmeal Mixture
