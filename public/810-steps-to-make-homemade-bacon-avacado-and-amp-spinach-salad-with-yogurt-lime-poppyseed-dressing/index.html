The ingredients needed to make Bacon Avacado & Spinach Salad with Yogurt Lime Poppyseed Dressing:
  1. Take Salad
  2. Prepare 3 cup baby spinach
  3. Make ready 4 slice bacon, cooked and chopped
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 cup strawberries, hulled and chopped
  5. Take 1 avacado, chopped
  6. Get 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp goat cheese, crumbled
  8. Prepare Yogurt Lime Poppyseed Dressing
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup greek yogurt
  10. Take 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
  12. Take 1 tbsp honey or agave syrup
  13. Prepare 1 tsp poppyseeds
  14. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper
  15. Take 1 lime, juiced and zested

  2. Mix all ingredients for dressing in a seperate bowl and dress salad. Garnish top of salad with sliced almonds and bacon crumbles.
