The ingredients needed to make Cheese Garlic Bread:
  1. Get 1 cup maida(all purpose flour)
  2. Take to taste salt
  3. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  5. Take 2 tsp curd
  6. Make ready 1 tsp oregano
  7. Take 1 tsp oil
  8. Get 1/4 cup milk
  9. Get 25 gm butter
  10. Take 1 tsp crushed garlic
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup crushed cheese

Steps to make Cheese Garlic Bread:
  1. Firstly, in a large mixing bowl take maida, salt, baking soda, baking powder, curd, milk, oregano, oil and sugar. mix well.
  2. Start to knead the dough. Add water as required.
  3. Further, coat the dough with oil and rest for 1 hours.
  4. Further, punch the dough.
  5. In a small bowl mix in butter, garlic and Oregano.
  6. Spread the prepared garlic butter over the dough generously.
  7. Further top with mozzarella cheese on half of the dough.
  8. Also season with chilli flakes and oregano.
  9. Fold the dough and seal the edges. - furthermore, brush generous amount of prepared garlic butter.
