Steps to make Mango custard jelly:
  1. Prepare strawberry jello according to packaging instruction. Pour 1/3 in a cup or serving glass.. Refrigerate until set.
  2. Prepare the custard: According to packaging instructions. Let cool. Then pour over strawberry jello mixture.. Let set in refrigerator.
  3. Prepare mango jello according to packaging instructions. Let it cool. And pout over custard mixture.
  4. Washed. Peeled and cut mangoes in cubes.. Distribute cubed mangoes over mango jello. Refrigerate and let set.
