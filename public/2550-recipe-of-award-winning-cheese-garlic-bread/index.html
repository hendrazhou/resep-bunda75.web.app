The ingredients needed to make Cheese garlic bread:
  1. Get 5 round slice of bread
  2. Take 2 tbsp cheese spread
  3. Prepare 1 tsp garlic powder
  4. Make ready 1 cube process cheese
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Oregano
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp Chilli flakes
  7. Get 1 tbsp butter
