The ingredients needed to make Tutti Frutti Semolina Cake:
  1. Get 150 grams semolina
  2. Prepare 150 grams sugar
  3. Get 200 grams curd
  4. Get 50 gm oil
  5. Take 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon soda
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon vanilla essence
  9. Take 2 tablespoons Tutti Frutti
  10. Make ready 2 tablespoons chopped dry fruits
  11. Get 4-5 drops yellow color

Instructions to make Tutti Frutti Semolina Cake:
  1. Mix rava, curd and sugar. Cover for 2 hours. After 2 hour, add flour, baking powder and soda and mix.
  2. Now add oil, color, soda and vanilla essence to it. Mix it well Add trutti fruity and chopped dry fruits.
  3. Grease the cake mold. Pour the cake batter in grease mold.
  4. Bake the cake in a pre heat cooker for 45 minutes on low heat.
  5. Put a knife and check. Dimold Cake. Tutti Frutti Semolina cake ready to serve.
