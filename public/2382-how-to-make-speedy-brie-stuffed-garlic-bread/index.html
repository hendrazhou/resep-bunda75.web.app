The ingredients needed to make Brie Stuffed Garlic Bread:
  1. Get 1 baguette (or 2 semi baguettes)
  2. Take 6 Tbs butter, melted
  3. Take 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  6. Get Pinch red pepper flakes
  7. Make ready 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  8. Prepare 1 (7 oz) wheel brie
  9. Get 1/4 cup grated parmesan

Instructions to make Brie Stuffed Garlic Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350° and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Using a serrated knife, cut slits 2" apart in bread, being careful not to cut all the way through. (Hasselback-style!)
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together melted butter, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and red pepper flakes. Brush over and inside bread. Place bread on a baking sheet.
  3. Slice brie into small pieces, then stuff bread with shredded mozzarella and brie slices. Sprinkle with Parmesan. Bake until bread is toasted on top and cheese is melty, about 15 minutes.
  4. Garnish with parsley if desired and serve warm.
