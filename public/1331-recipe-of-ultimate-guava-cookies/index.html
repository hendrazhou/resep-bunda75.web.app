The ingredients needed to make Guava Cookies:
  1. Take 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract, optional
  2. Prepare 1 8oz. pkg. of Cream Cheese, softened
  3. Make ready 1 stick of butter, softened
  4. Take 2 cup of All purpose flour
  5. Take 1 Guava Paste

Instructions to make Guava Cookies:
  1. Cream softened cream cheese and butter together. Add flour in increments and mix with hands. Refrigerator for several hours or best overnight.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°F and lightly spray cookie sheet with butter. On a lightly floured surface roll dough thin - approx. 1/8" thick. Cut into 2 inch squares. Place a slice of guava paste on each square.
  3. Fold dough over guava paste. Bake at 375°F for 20 minutes.
  4. If desired, sprinkle with powdered sugar and enjoy.
