The ingredients needed to make Banana Pudding Trifle Dessert:
  1. Make ready 2 packages Pepperidge Farm Chessman Cookies, crumbled
  2. Take 6 Bananas, or more as needed
  3. Take 2 cup Milk
  4. Get 1 box French Vanilla instant Pudding (5oz size)
  5. Take 8 oz Cream cheese, softened
  6. Take 14 oz Can Sweetened Condensed Milk
  7. Make ready 12 oz Container Whipped Topping, thawed

Steps to make Banana Pudding Trifle Dessert:
  1. Combine milk and pudding mix in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Combine cream cheese and condensed milk in a large bowl and mix until smooth.
  3. Fold the whipped topping into the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Add cream cheese mixture to pudding mixture and blend well.
  5. Place a layer of crumbled cookies into the bottom of the trifle bowl. Slice bananas into 1/2 inch pieces (circles). Place a layer of sliced bananas over the crumbled cookies. Carefully spoon 1/3 of the pudding mixture over the top of the bananas. Repeat layer of cookies, banana and pudding 2 more times and top with remaining cookie crumbles.
  6. Refrigerate for 4 hours or more and it's ready to serve.
