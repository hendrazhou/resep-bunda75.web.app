The ingredients needed to make Buzzfeed Nutella Brownies:
  1. Get 1 1/2 cups Nutella
  2. Prepare 7 tablespoons melted butter
  3. Make ready 2 eggs
  4. Get 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup flour

Steps to make Buzzfeed Nutella Brownies:
  1. In a small bowl mix the Nutella and melted butter until thick
  2. In a medium bowl mix the powdered sugar with the eggs
  3. Pour the Nutella mixture into the egg and sugar mixture and mix until well combined
  4. Add the flour to the batter and mix
  5. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan
  6. Scoop tablespoons of Nutella randomly
  7. Bake the Nutella brownies at 350 degrees for 40 minutes
  8. Let the brownies cool for 10 minutes
  9. Transfer the brownies to a plate, cut them into squares and ✨Enjoy!✨
