The ingredients needed to make Grill chicken wings with rice broth:
  1. Take 1/2 kg chicken wings
  2. Take 1 cup white rice
  3. Prepare 1 cup mix bell peppers
  4. Get 1 tbs black pepper
  5. Prepare 6 tbs chicken seasoning
  6. Take 1 cup water
  7. Prepare 1 cup water for rice
  8. Make ready little bit green onion (optional)
  9. Make ready salt (optional)

Instructions to make Grill chicken wings with rice broth:
  1. Take off the chicken skin if u want, then wash and let it dry, add chicken seasoning and black pepper, mix it well. After that add water and boiling it on medium heat fire, until only little bit water left.
  2. Take all the chicken to another plate, put rice inside the place that still have little bit water after boiling chicken, give 1 cup water more into it. And boiling again the rice with medium heat fire.
  3. When rice starting to boil, add bell peppers and green onion, mix it, and let it cook until dry.
  4. Put chicken to the pan or if u want, u can grill the chicken on oven or barbeque place.
  5. When it's done serve the chicken and rice together in same place. Smakelijk.
