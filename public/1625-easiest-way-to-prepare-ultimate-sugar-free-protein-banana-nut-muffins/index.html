The ingredients needed to make Sugar free protein banana nut muffins:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup (1 stick) soft or melted butter
  2. Take 3 large ripe bananas
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
  6. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Get 3/4 cup Splenda (or 1/2 cup sugar if you don’t want sugar free)
  9. Make ready 1 1/2 scoops unflavored whey protein powder
  10. Make ready 1 cup AP flour
  11. Make ready 3/4 cup chopped walnuts and/or chocolate chips
  12. Take Equal parts cinnamon and sugar sprinkled on top before baking

Instructions to make Sugar free protein banana nut muffins:
  1. Heat oven to 350. Cream butter, bananas, and egg together.
  2. Add dry ingredients and mix, and nuts/chocolate last.
  3. Put in greased muffin tins or muffin sheets. DONT FORGET TO ADD CINNAMON SUGAR MIXTURE ON TOP!! Bake for 20 min
