The ingredients needed to make Vietnamese chicken wings:
  1. Take 1 lb chicken wing pieces
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 cup cilantro
  3. Make ready 1 clove garlic
  4. Prepare 1 medium jalapeno
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp fish sauce
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
  7. Take 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  8. Make ready 1 cup flour
  9. Get 1 canola oil
  10. Take 1 kosher salt
  11. Get 1 black pepper

Instructions to make Vietnamese chicken wings:
  1. Heat canola oil, lightly flour chicken and fry in batches til golden brown.
  2. Puree cilantro,jalapeno,garlic,fish sauce and lime juice.
  3. Heat butter. Make sure not to burn the butter.
  4. Mix butter and puree together. Toss chicken wings in mixture. Pour onto platter and garnish with leftover puree and limes.
  5. ENJOY!
