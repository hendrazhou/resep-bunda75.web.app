The ingredients needed to make Oats Fruit Custard:
  1. Get 2 cup milk
  2. Get 1 cup oats
  3. Take 1/3 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 3 tbsp custard powder
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp cashew powder
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp almond powder
  7. Prepare Some chopped fruits as per your choice
  8. Prepare As required Cherry for garnishing

Instructions to make Oats Fruit Custard:
  1. Take a pan and dry roast the oats.
  2. Take milk in a pan and boil it totally. In another small bowl take custard powder and 1/4 cup milk and mix well then pour it into a milk. Stir well.
  3. Now add oats, cashew powder, almond powder and again mix it well.
  4. Once it's done pour into a serving bowl and keep it in the fridge and allow to completely chilled. Now take it out from the fridge and assemble with chopped fruit and cherry and serve chilled.
