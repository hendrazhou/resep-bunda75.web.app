The ingredients needed to make Barley manchow veg soup:
  1. Get 1/2 cup barley flour
  2. Prepare 2 cup water (add more or less quantity according to your taste)
  3. Get to taste Salt
  4. Get 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  5. Prepare 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  6. Prepare 1 tsp oil or ghee
  7. Take 1 tsp soya sauce
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp red chilli sauce
  9. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  10. Get 1 tbsp fried noodles
  11. Get 1 cup Chopped vegetables (capsicum,onion,green onion,carrot)
  12. Get 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
