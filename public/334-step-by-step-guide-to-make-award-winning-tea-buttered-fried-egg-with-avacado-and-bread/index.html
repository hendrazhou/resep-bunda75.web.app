The ingredients needed to make Tea, Buttered fried egg with Avacado and bread:
  1. Prepare 3 eggs
  2. Get 2 slices bread
  3. Make ready 1 medium size Avacado
  4. Get 2 table spoons of president butter
  5. Prepare Small bulb onion (washed and sliced)
  6. Prepare 1 red bonnet pepper
  7. Prepare to taste Salt
  8. Take 2 table spoon powdered milk
  9. Take 2 table Spoons of milo
  10. Make ready to taste Sugar

Steps to make Tea, Buttered fried egg with Avacado and bread:
  1. Break egg in a bowl add sliced onion, pepper and beat until fluffy
  2. Place frying pan on fire and add butter, allow to melt then pour egg and allow to fry for sometime under low heat then turn the other side and press frying spoon on top to remove moist.
  3. Boil a cup of water for tea. Add Milo, milk and sugar then stir
  4. Cut Avacado, remove seed and slice.
  5. Serve Tea, Buttered fried egg with Avacado and bread 😋
