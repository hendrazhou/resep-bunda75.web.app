The ingredients needed to make Tangerine and Guava fiesta with crunchy chia seeds:
  1. Make ready 1 guava
  2. Prepare 1 tangerine
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp chia seeds
  4. Get 1 tbsp sugar
  5. Prepare Lime to garnish

Steps to make Tangerine and Guava fiesta with crunchy chia seeds:
  1. Wash and peel your tangerine and divide into segments and cut your guava into pieces
  2. Pour into a blender and add 1/2 tbsp of the chia seeds and iced cold water and blend until smooth
  3. Pass through a sieve to remove seeds
  4. Add a dash of vanilla and sugar and stir
  5. Refrigerate before serving or add ice cubes and serve with a sprinkle of the remaining chia seeds and slices of lime.
