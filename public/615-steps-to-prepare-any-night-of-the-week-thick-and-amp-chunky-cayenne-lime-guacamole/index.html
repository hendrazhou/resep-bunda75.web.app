The ingredients needed to make Thick & Chunky Cayenne Lime Guacamole:
  1. Get 2-3 ripe avocados
  2. Get 6 cherry tomatoes finely chopped
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  4. Take 1 cup chopped onion
  5. Get Juice of 1 small lime
  6. Take Salt
  7. Take Pepper
  8. Prepare Garlic Powder
  9. Get Ground Cayenne red pepper
