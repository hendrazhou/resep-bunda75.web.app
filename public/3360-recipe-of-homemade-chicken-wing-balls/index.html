The ingredients needed to make Chicken Wing Balls:
  1. Take 4 Lbs Ground Chicken
  2. Prepare 2 cups grated parmesan cheese
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  4. Prepare 2 Tbs Italian seasoning
  5. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp poultry seasoning

Steps to make Chicken Wing Balls:
  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl or stand mixer and mix thoroughly.
  2. Using a small icecream scoop or hands. Make meatballs into approximately 2 inch in diameter balls
  3. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F for 12 minutes.
  4. Deep fry the meatballs as soon as they are removed from oven in batches at 350°F for 2 minutes to get a slightly crispy exterior. Do not over crowd in the fryer.
  5. Eat plain or cover with your favorite wing sauce or powder.
