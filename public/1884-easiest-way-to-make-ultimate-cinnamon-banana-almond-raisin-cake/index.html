The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Banana Almond Raisin Cake:
  1. Prepare 5 Bananas pureed
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 C. Flour
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp. Baking soda
  4. Take 1/2 tsp. Salt
  5. Take 2 eggs beaten
  6. Take 1/2 cup butter softened
  7. Take 1/2 tsp. Vanilla extract
  8. Take 1 cup sugar
  9. Make ready Dash Cinnamon
  10. Take Raisin
  11. Make ready Walnuts or almonds optional
  12. Prepare Melted Chocolate
  13. Take optional
  14. Prepare Powdered Sugar

Instructions to make Cinnamon Banana Almond Raisin Cake:
  1. Cream together butter and sugar. - Add eggs and crushed bananas. - Combine well.
  2. Sift together flour, soda and salt. Add to creamed mixture. Add vanilla. Add Raisins,nuts or Chocolate.
  3. Pour into greased and floured loaf pan. - Bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes. Sprinkle powdered sugar - Keeps well, refrigerated.
