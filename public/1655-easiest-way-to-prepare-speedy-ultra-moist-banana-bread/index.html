  2. Get 4 over ripe bananas smashed

  5. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Make ready 1/2 C sour cream
  7. Make ready Dry ingredients
  8. Make ready 1 1/2 C flour
  9. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions to make Ultra moist banana bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. In a large bowl beat wet ingredients together with electric mixer.
  3. Sift dry ingredients in with wet and fold in until you have a smooth batter.
  4. Pour batter into a greased 1.5 quart baking dish.
  5. Bake for 1 hour and let cool before serving.
