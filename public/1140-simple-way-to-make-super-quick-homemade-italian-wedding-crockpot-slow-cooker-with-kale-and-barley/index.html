The ingredients needed to make Italian wedding crockpot/slow cooker with kale and barley:
  1. Get 6 cup vegetable or chicken stock
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup barley
  3. Get 2 carrots, diced
  4. Get 1 onion diced
  5. Get 2 stalks celery diced
  6. Get 6 links of chicken, turkey or pork italian sausage (sweet, mild, or hot)
  7. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  8. Make ready 1/2 tbsp italian seasoning
  9. Prepare 3 kale leaves
  10. Prepare 1 salt and pepper to taste
  11. Make ready 1 parmesan cheese to taste
  12. Make ready 2 cup water (as needed)

Instructions to make Italian wedding crockpot/slow cooker with kale and barley:
  1. Add all ingredients except for kale, sausage and parmesan cheese and water
  2. Empty sausage out of casings a piece at a time to resemble tiny meatballs. Brown them in a frying pan if you wish first. I never have time to do this so I just drop them in the crockpot raw
  3. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 4
  4. About 30 mins before serving taste soup and add water if too salty or too thick, and add chopped kale to crockpot
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste and garnish with parmesan cheese
