The ingredients needed to make Watermelon Lime Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup apple
  2. Take 1 cup cucumber
  3. Make ready 2 cups watermelon
  4. Get 1 lime, juiced
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon honey or to taste
  6. Prepare 1-1 1/2 cups water or ice

Instructions to make Watermelon Lime Smoothie:
  1. Toss all the ingredients into a blender and mix until smooth.
  2. If using frozen fruit, add 1 to 1 1/2 cups of water to help blend it up. If fruit is fresh you may want to add about a cup of ice cubes to thicken it.
