Steps to make Guava shake:
  1. Wash and cut guava into pieces.
  2. Blend guava into mixer grinder with little water. Then add milk and honey. (You can add sugar instead of honey). Blend again till smooth texture.
  3. Strain it and remove seeds. Put into fridge for half an hour. Enjoy chilled guava shake.
