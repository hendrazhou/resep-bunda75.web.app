Instructions to make Chocolate Banana bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 for dark loaf pan. For glass loaf pan to 325.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a separate bowl.
  3. Mix all wet ingredients in a separate bowl.
  4. Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients. Do not over mix. You want a lumpy, thick texture.
  5. Use Pam spray for loaf pan and pour mixture into pan. Sprinkle more chocolate chips on top if desired.
  6. Bake for 60-70 minutes in dark pan. 45-50 mins in glass pan. Cooking times vary by oven type.
  7. Check for doness with a toothpick. Toothpick should come out clean.
