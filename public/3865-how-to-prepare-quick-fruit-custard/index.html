The ingredients needed to make Fruit custard:
  1. Make ready Half litre milk
  2. Get 2 tsp custard powder (vanila flavour)
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped mango
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup grapes
  5. Get 4-5 almonds
  6. Get 4 tsp sugar or to taste

Instructions to make Fruit custard:
  1. Take 1/2 cup cold milk, add 2 tsp custard powder.Heat milk in a pan, let it boil, add sugar and mix well.
  2. Add custard mixture. Cook for 2 mins. on low flame. Turn off the flame.Let it cool down. Cut mango and grapes in pieces.
  3. Add mango, grapes, almonds in custard. Mix well.Refrigerate for one hour.Serve chilled fruit custard.
