The ingredients needed to make Milo Banana bread, in a mug!:
  1. Get 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp Milo
  3. Take 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking powder
  5. Get 1/4 tsp baking soda
  6. Get 1 eggs
  7. Take 1 tbsp milk
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  9. Prepare 1 banana

Instructions to make Milo Banana bread, in a mug!:
  1. Mix flour, Milo, sugar, baking powder, baking soda in a microwave-safe mug
  2. Add egg and mix well
  3. Mix in vegetable oil and milk
  4. Mix in mashed banana
  5. Cook in a microwave for 3 minutes.
