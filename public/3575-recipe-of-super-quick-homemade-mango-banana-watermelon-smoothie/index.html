The ingredients needed to make Mango, banana watermelon smoothie:
  1. Get 1 ripe mango
  2. Get 1 banana
  3. Get 1/4 watermelon
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
  5. Take 1/8 cup cream whipped
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup milk
  7. Make ready Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions to make Mango, banana watermelon smoothie:
  1. Peel mango, banana and watermelon and cut into cubes.
  2. Put them in a blender and add, sugar, cream and milk.
  3. Blend everything and add some water according to your liking. You can add ice cubes.
  4. Serve with some snacks.
