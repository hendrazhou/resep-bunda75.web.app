The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Sugar Shortbread Cookies:
  1. Get For the Cinnamon Shortnread
  2. Prepare 1 cup all purpose flour
  3. Take 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  4. Get 1/4 teaspoon salt
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter, at room temperature
