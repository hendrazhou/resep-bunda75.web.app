The ingredients needed to make Mango-Honey Mustard Tilapia:
  1. Get 6 tilapia filets
  2. Get 6 oz mango fruit spread or preserves
  3. Prepare 6 oz honey mustard
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp tarragon; minced
  7. Make ready 2 tsp cracked black pepper
  8. Prepare 2 tsp onion powder
  9. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
  10. Prepare 1 kosher salt
  11. Take 1 sea salt

Instructions to make Mango-Honey Mustard Tilapia:
  1. Season tilapia with onion/garlic powder and black pepper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine mustard, mango, sugar, vinegar, and a pinch of kosher salt.
  3. Cover fish with the sauce and bake at 400° for approximately 4-5 minutes or until flesh is opaque and thermometer reads 145°
  4. Finish with a touch of sea salt and tarragon.
  5. Variations; Spicy mustard, salmon, capers, parsley, garlic, breadcrumbs, chicken, chervil, thyme, rosemary
