The ingredients needed to make Amrud/peru/guava chutney and green coriander chutney:
  1. Take For guava chutney-
  2. Take 2 guavas
  3. Prepare 3 green chillies
  4. Get 1 tsp cumin seeds
  5. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  6. Take 1 tsp sugar
  7. Make ready 1/2 lime juice
  8. Prepare 1 cup coriander leaves
  9. Take For green coriander chutney-
  10. Get 1 cup coriander leaves
  11. Take 1-2 tsp daliya(roasted grams)
  12. Get 1 tsp sesame seeds
  13. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  14. Prepare 1/2 lime juice
  15. Take 3 green chillies
  16. Prepare 1/2 bowl Fresh coconut
  17. Take 1 inch ginger
  18. Get 5 cloves garlic
  19. Make ready 1 stem of curry leaves
