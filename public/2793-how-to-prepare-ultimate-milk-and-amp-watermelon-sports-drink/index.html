The ingredients needed to make Milk & Watermelon sports drink:
  1. Prepare 2 cups watermelon cubes
  2. Prepare 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp fresh milk cream
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon roasted cumin seeds
  5. Make ready 1 cup milk
  6. Take 2-3 mint leaves
  7. Make ready Salt as per taste

Instructions to make Milk & Watermelon sports drink:
  1. Add 1 cup watermelon cubes to a mixer and blend well to form smooth paste
  2. Add watermelon cubes to a glass. Then pour half of the milk and then add half of the cumin seeds, lemon juice & salt as per taste.
  3. Then add half of the blended watermelon juice. Repeat step 2 & 3 again.
  4. Then add the mint leaves.
  5. Add 2 tbsp fresh milk cream
  6. Place a lemon slice & serve chilled.
