The ingredients needed to make Easy Nutella Mini Cheesecakes (no bake):
  1. Get Pudding
  2. Get 4 tbsp cream cheese
  3. Get 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  4. Take 2 tbsp nutella
  5. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Make ready 1/3 tsp salt
  7. Get Crust
  8. Get 2/3 cup chocolate graham cracker crumbs
  9. Get 2 1/3 tbsp melted butter
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp nutella

Instructions to make Easy Nutella Mini Cheesecakes (no bake):
  1. Mix all ingredients for crust together.
  2. Line a cupcake tray with cupcake liners. (I used foil liners)
  3. Press crust mixture into the liners and place into freezer.
  4. In a bowl, mix together all pudding ingredients and pour into cupcake tray.
  5. Freeze overnight. I thought it would freeze and be rock hard but it was perfectly set and just nice and cool.
  6. Remove foil liners and enjoy! Delicious and easy treats :)
