Steps to make Watermelon smoothie:
  1. Peel the watermelon and cut into small sizes. Do not remove the seeds
  2. Add water and blend it till it becomes smooth. Add about one quarter of a cup
  3. Slice the lemons in half and squeeze the juice into the blended watermelon. The lemons gives it a very sharp taste.
  4. Keep blending till it becomes very smooth.
  5. Allow it to chill in the freezer for some hours before serving!
  6. Calm your nerves with the cold watermelon smoothie!
