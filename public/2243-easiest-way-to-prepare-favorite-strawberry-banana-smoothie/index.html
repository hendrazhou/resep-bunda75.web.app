The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Banana Smoothie:
  1. Prepare 5 strawberries
  2. Get 2 1/2 bananas
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup yogurt
  4. Take 1/4 cup sugar
  5. Take 1/4 cup milk- use a little less for a little thicker
  6. Prepare 1 cup ice

Strawberry banana smoothie recipe made with just three ingredients. You can't go wrong with this simple and delicious smoothie! The BEST strawberry banana smoothie recipe ever! It's the perfect snack or breakfast!

Instructions to make Strawberry Banana Smoothie:
  1. Add all ingredients to blender
  2. Blend to wanted consistency
  3. Enjoy!!
