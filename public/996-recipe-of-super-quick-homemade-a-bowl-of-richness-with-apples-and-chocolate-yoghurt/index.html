The ingredients needed to make A Bowl Of Richness with apples and chocolate yoghurt:
  1. Take 1 red apple
  2. Get 100 gram thick Greek yoghurt (curd)
  3. Get 2 tablespoon Cocoa powder
  4. Prepare 3 tablespoon sweetened condensed milk
  5. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon fresh cream or milk to mix with cocoa powder
  7. Take 3 tsp Homemade tutti frutti for garnishing
  8. Make ready as needed dry fruits (Raisins & cashews )chopped
