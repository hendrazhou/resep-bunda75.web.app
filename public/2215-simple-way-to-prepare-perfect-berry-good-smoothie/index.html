The ingredients needed to make Berry Good Smoothie:

  5. Get 1 T fresh lemon juice
  6. Make ready 3 oz fruit nectar (Mango recommended)
  7. Prepare 1 C strawberry or vanilla yogurt
  8. Get 3 T honey
  9. Prepare 1 T chia seeds
  10. Get 1/2 ea frozen roasted beet (chopped, optional)
