The ingredients needed to make Jelly fruit custard:
  1. Make ready 1 litr milk
  2. Make ready 1 packet (banana flavour) jelly
  3. Make ready 4 Tbsp Vanila custard powder
  4. Make ready 8 Tbsp sugar
  5. Take 1 apple. Small pieces
  6. Get 3-4 bananas. Cut in thin circles
  7. Take 1/2 tsp sugar
  8. Prepare 1-1/4 cup water for jelly

Steps to make Jelly fruit custard:
  1. In a small pan put water, add 1/2tsp sugar, heat. When water start boiling, add jelly crystal packet, off the flame stir continuously till jelly dissolve completely.
  2. Pour the mixture into square or rectangular dish, to set the jelly.(packet per 2 cup water Hai lekin main nai Kam pani use Kia Hai.Is sey achi aur jaldi set ho jati Hai jelly).
  3. In another pan add milk,sugar and heat milk. separate half cup milk for custard paste.
  4. In half cup milk, add custard powder make smooth paste.
  5. When milk start boiling. Add custard paste slowly and stir continuously till it thickens.
  6. Cool it and add fruit pieces in it. Do not add fruit in hot custard.(Fruit is optional). As you like.
  7. Now set it into a bowl. Make pieces of jelly and set on custard. Cool it and serve it.
