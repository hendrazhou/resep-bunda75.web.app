The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip,pb & banana sandwiches:
  1. Make ready 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  2. Prepare 2 tablespoons honey
  3. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  4. Get 2 tablespoons miniature chocolate chips
  5. Take 4 slices whole wheat bread
  6. Make ready 1 medium banana
