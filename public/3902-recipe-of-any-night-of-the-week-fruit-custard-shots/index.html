The ingredients needed to make Fruit custard Shots:
  1. Make ready for custard powder
  2. Get 1 spoon rice flour
  3. Prepare 5-6 Cashews
  4. Make ready 2 spoon milk powder
  5. Get 4 spoon sugar
  6. Make ready 1 glass milk
  7. Make ready 6-7 stands saffron
  8. Make ready 1/4 spoon yellow color
  9. Get 1/4 spoon cardamom powder
  10. Get 1 cup chopped mix fruits
  11. Get 2 spoon green jam
  12. Prepare 1/2 spoon silver balls
  13. Take 1 spoon chocolate syrup

  1. Grinded cashews, sugar, rice flour, milk powder
  2. Mix the powder, saffron, color and cardamom powder in the milk and cook till it thickens.
  3. Turn off the gas and cool it
  4. Now put chocolate syrup in a glass and put the fruit layer,then custard then fruit then jelly then custard then fruit layer and garnish with silver balls,cherry and mix dry fruits
  5. Keep in the fridge for a while then serve
