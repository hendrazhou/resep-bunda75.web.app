The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon rolls:
  1. Prepare 2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Take 2 tbsp sugar
  3. Take 4 tsp baking powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup butter
  6. Get 1 cup milk
  7. Take 1/2 cup butter
  8. Take 1 1/4 cup brown sugar
  9. Make ready 4 tsp cinnamon
  10. Get Glaze
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup icing sugar
  12. Prepare Few drops of milk

Steps to make Cinnamon rolls:
  1. Mix first 4 ingredients and make a well and then cut in butter. Then add milk. Knead and roll into rectangle
  2. Cream butter and cinnamon and sugar together.
  3. Spread mixture over rectangle saving enough to spread on top of your rolls when complete. Roll up the rectangle and cut. Place in a greased pan and spread remaining mixture on top
  4. Bake at 375 for 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. Mix icing sugar and milk together and drizzle over rolls and then serve
