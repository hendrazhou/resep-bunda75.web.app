The ingredients needed to make Blended Pico de Gallo Salsa:
  1. Prepare 1 cup finely chopped tomato seeded, pulp removed OR 1 cup canned, crushed tomatoes
  2. Get 15-20 whole stems of cilantro, cut or hand-torn into 1 inch segments
  3. Take 1 small clove garlic, rough chopped
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon dried oregano (optional for me - some days I'm feelin' it, others not so much)
  5. Get 1/2 cup chopped onions
  6. Prepare 1-2 jalapeños or serranos, rough chopped
  7. Make ready 1 Tablespoon fresh lime juice to start
  8. Get 1 +1/2 Tablespoons neutral flavored oil
  9. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon table salt + a pinch OR 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  10. Get 1/4 teaspoon sugar
