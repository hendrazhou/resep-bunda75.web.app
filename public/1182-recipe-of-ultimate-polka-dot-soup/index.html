The ingredients needed to make polka dot soup:
  1. Prepare 1/3 cup pearled barley
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Make ready 1 small onion, chopped
  4. Get 2 carrots, diced
  5. Take 2 clove garlic, minced
  6. Take 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
  7. Take 1/2 cup frozen corn
  8. Prepare 1 packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
