The ingredients needed to make Red Hot Buffalo Chicken Nachos:
  1. Prepare 2 cups cooked shredded chicken
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Frank's Red Hot buffalo wing sauce
  3. Prepare 10 oz bag of tortilla chips
  4. Prepare 2 cups shredded cheddar or jack cheese

Instructions to make Red Hot Buffalo Chicken Nachos:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Toss chicken and sauce to coat
  3. Place tortilla chips on cookie sheet and top with chicken mixture then cheese
  4. Bake 5-10 minutes or until heated and cheese is melted.
  5. Serve with salsa, sour cream or any other toppings.
