The ingredients needed to make Nan's Red Salsa:
  1. Make ready 28 oz. can tomatoes *
  2. Make ready 1 small onion, roughly chopped
  3. Prepare 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Get 2 tsp. diced jalapeño (to taste)**
  5. Make ready 1 lime, juice only
  6. Get 1/2 tsp. salt
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp. sugar
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp. cumin
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp. smoked paprika
  10. Make ready 3-4 green onions, chopped
  11. Prepare Cilantro, to taste ***
