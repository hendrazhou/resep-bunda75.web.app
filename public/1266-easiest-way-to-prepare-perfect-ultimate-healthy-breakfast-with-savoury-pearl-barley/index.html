The ingredients needed to make Ultimate Healthy Breakfast with Savoury Pearl Barley:

  2. Take 1 portobello mushroom chopped
  3. Take 10 pink bodi chopped
  4. Take 10 basil leaves
  5. Get 1/2 inch ginger peeled and grated
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp coconut oil
  7. Make ready 2 cloves garlic of chopped
  8. Make ready to taste Salt

Instructions to make Ultimate Healthy Breakfast with Savoury Pearl Barley:
  1. Rinse and boil pearl barley for 45 mins.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add in garlic. Saute for 3 mins.
  3. Add in mushrooms and salt. Cook for 5 mins.
  4. Add in pink bodi and saute for 10 mins.
  5. Add in ginger and basil and cook for 15 mins or to your liking.
  6. Salt to taste.
  7. Add in pearl barley and toss.
