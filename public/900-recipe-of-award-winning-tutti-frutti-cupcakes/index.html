  1. Make ready 1.5 cup all purpose flour
  2. Take 1/4 cup milk powder
  3. Take 1 cup sugar
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup butter
  5. Prepare 1 cup tutti frutti (all colour)
  6. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps to make Tutti frutti cupcakes:
  1. Seive all dry ingredients, beat butter,sugar and milk till smooth texture.
  2. Now mix dry ingredients 2 -2 spoon in liquid solution. Now you will get nyc and smooth batter.
  3. Take tuty fruity and 1 tsp flour mix them,now transfer into batter and add drop of vanilla extract. Pour the batter into cups and bake in 160 to 180°C for 25 minutes. Cakes are ready to have bites.
