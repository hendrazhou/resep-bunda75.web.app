The ingredients needed to make Paleo whole30 - Cilantro Lime Cauliflower Rice:
  1. Get 1/2 head Cauliflower
  2. Make ready 1/2 tsp coconut oil
  3. Get 1 tsp ghee
  4. Prepare 1 juice and zest from 1 lime
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  6. Get 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions to make Paleo whole30 - Cilantro Lime Cauliflower Rice:
  1. Cut cauliflower into florets and place in the food processor until it is a rice consistency.
  2. Melt oil and ghee in medium heat
  3. Add cauliflower and saute for 5-10 minutes until it is a rice consistency.
  4. After cauliflower is done add lime and cilantro.
