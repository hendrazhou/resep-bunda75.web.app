The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti bun:
  1. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. Get 1 tbsp milk powder
  3. Get 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
  4. Take 1/4 tsp instant yeast
  5. Take 1/8 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup warm milk
  7. Get 1 tbsp butter
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp tutti frutti
  9. Take as needed milk and butter for brushing

Steps to make Tutti frutti bun:
  1. Combine all purpose flour, milk powder, sugar, yeast and salt in a bowl
  2. Add milk and mix well
  3. Add butter and knead the dough for 10-12 minutes
  4. Oil the dough and cover with a kitchen towl and place in any warm place for 1 hour
  5. Once the dough is doubled, punch the dough and add tutti frutti, mix well and knead for few seconds
  6. Divide the dough into 4 equal portions and make round balls
  7. Arrange the balls in a greased baking tray and brush with some warm milk
  8. Let it rest again in warm place for 30 minutes, the roll will double in size
  9. Again brush the roll with milk and sprinkle some tutti frutti on the top
  10. Bake in a preheated oven at 190c for 15-20 minutes
  11. Once done, remove from oven and brush some melted butter
  12. Enjoy with jam or butter
