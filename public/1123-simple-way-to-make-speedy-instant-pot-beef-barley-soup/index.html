The ingredients needed to make Instant pot beef barley soup:
  1. Make ready 2 Tablespoons butter
  2. Prepare 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  3. Get 2 lbs cubed beef stew meat
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon sea salt
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  7. Prepare 1 cup diced white onion
  8. Prepare 1 cup celery sliced
  9. Make ready 2 cups sliced carrots
  10. Get 4 cloves garlic minced
  11. Get 2 (32 oz) cartons beef broth
  12. Make ready 1 (14 oz) can petite diced tomatoes
  13. Take 2 teaspoons red wine vinegar
  14. Take 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  15. Prepare 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  16. Make ready 1 packet beef gravy mix
  17. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder(I use turmeric twist)
  18. Prepare 2 teaspoons fresh rosemary minced
  19. Prepare 2 teaspoons fresh thyme minced
  20. Get 3 Tablespoons fresh parsley minced
  21. Get 1 cup chopped kale (I use frozen)
  22. Make ready 2 cups quick cooking barley

How to Make Instant Pot Beef Barley Soup.

Steps to make Instant pot beef barley soup:
  1. On the Saute setting heat olive oil and butter till hot and add beef, salt, pepper and garlic powder and mix up.
  2. Add in onion, carrots, celery and minced garlic and continue to cook till beef is browned.
  3. Add in broth, tomatoes, red wine vinegar, soy sauce, worcestershire sauce, packet of gravy mix, turmeric, rosemary,thyme and parsley.
  4. Stir up and place lid on move knob to seal.
  5. Press meat / stew for 35 minutes normal high-pressure.
  6. After 35 minutes turn knob to vent to release steam be careful as it will be really hot.
  7. Make sure button is down before opening lid.
  8. Stir in barley and kale and place lid back on and let sit for 10 minutes.
  9. Enjoy with some baguette bread slices.
