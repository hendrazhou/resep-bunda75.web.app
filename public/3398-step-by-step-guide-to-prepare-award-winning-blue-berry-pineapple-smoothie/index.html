The ingredients needed to make Blue berry pineapple smoothie:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup frozen Blueberries
  2. Get 1 cup canned or fresh Pineapple
  3. Take 1 Banana frozen
  4. Take 1/2 cup or 1 pack of instant oatmeal
  5. Get 1 cup water or fruit juice
