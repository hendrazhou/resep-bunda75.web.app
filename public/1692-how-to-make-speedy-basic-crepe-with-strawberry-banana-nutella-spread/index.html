Instructions to make Basic Crepe with strawberry banana Nutella spread:
  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk eggs.
  2. Add milk, water, salt, melted butter. Stir to combine.
  3. Add flour and beat until smooth. (You can use a blender if you want.)
  4. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over medium heat. Pour the batter in the pan. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
  5. Flip and cook the other side. If getting brown, set the heat medium-low.
  6. After cooked both sides, put the crepe on the plate. Start decorating.
  7. Finish. (I sprinkled powdered sugar on the top.)
