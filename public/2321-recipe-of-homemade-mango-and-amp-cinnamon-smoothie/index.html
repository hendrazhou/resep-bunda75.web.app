The ingredients needed to make Mango & Cinnamon Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 cups Mango (cubed)
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Milk or Low-Fat Milk
  3. Take 1 teaspoon Cinnamon
  4. Take 1/2 cup Ice Cubes
  5. Take 1/4 cup Yogurt or Low-Fat Yogurt
  6. Take 1 tablespoon Honey
