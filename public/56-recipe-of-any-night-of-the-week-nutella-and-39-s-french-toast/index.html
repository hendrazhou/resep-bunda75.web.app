Steps to make Nutella's French Toast:
  1. Crack 2 eggs and put them in a bowl. Then whisk them.
  2. Add a bit of milk in the bowl, then mix it with the eggs.
  3. Add a bit of cinnamon in the bowl, then mix it with the milk and the eggs.
  4. Soak a slice of bread on both sides in the mixture.
  5. Add a piece of butter in a pan, just to let not stick the bread.
  6. Add a slice of bread in the pan and cook each side for a couple of minutes. Pay attention: the bread mustn't be burnt, too dry or raw.
  7. Once it's cooked, garnish it with a lot of nutella (yum!) and strawberries. If you don't like nutella or strawberries you can use anything else, such as honey or jam.
