The ingredients needed to make Joe's Easy Salsa:
  1. Make ready 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5oz)
  2. Get 1 can diced green chiles (4oz)
  3. Get 1 can Rotel (10oz)
  4. Get 1-2 limes
  5. Make ready 1-2 serrano peppers
  6. Make ready fresh cilantro
  7. Get 1 Tsp cumin
  8. Make ready 1 Tsp minced onion
  9. Take 1 Tsp minced garlic

Steps to make Joe's Easy Salsa:
  1. Combine canned ingredients in blender.
  2. Add minced onion, minced garlic, and cumin to blender.
  3. Add fresh squeezed lime juice, cilantro, and serrano peppers to taste.
  4. Blend on low-med setting to desired consistency.
  5. Makes 4 cups. Serve chilled for best taste.
