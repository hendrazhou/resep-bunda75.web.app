Instructions to make Milo cupcakes:
  1. Cream butter with sugar until light and fluffy preferably with a hand mixer
  2. Add one egg at a time while whisking
  3. Add the liquid milk and mix
  4. Now lower the speed of the mixer. Add half of the flour while observing how the thickness goes
  5. Add the milo powder to required amount. P.s milo is never too much😂
  6. Add about a teaspoon of coffee powder for a nice flavour and colour
  7. Add in the remaining flour and baking powder to required consistency
  8. Pour into cupcake sheets and bake for about 30 minutes or until a tooth pick inserted comes clean
  9. Top your cupcakes with some nutella, yum!
