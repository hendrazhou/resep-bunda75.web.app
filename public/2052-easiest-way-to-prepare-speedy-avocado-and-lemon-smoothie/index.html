Instructions to make Avocado and Lemon Smoothie:
  1. Chop up the banana and avocado into cubes and drop into the blender
  2. Cut out the fleshy bits of the lemon, doing your best to remove the seeds, and drop it into the blender. (As opposed to squeezing out the juice, we will include everything in order to keep all the nutrients from the lemon)
  3. Pour ice cubes into the blender
  4. Pour yoghurt into blender
  5. Sprinkle cinnamon into mixture
  6. Pour a sprinkle of cinammon and blend until the mixture is completely creamy and blended
  7. Enjoy!
