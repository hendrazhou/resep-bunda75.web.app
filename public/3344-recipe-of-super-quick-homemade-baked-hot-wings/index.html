The ingredients needed to make Baked Hot Wings:
  1. Get 20 Chicken Wings
  2. Take 1 1/2 tbsp Chili Powder
  3. Take 3/4 tsp Cayenne Powder. (If you like more kick, use 1 or 1 1/4 tsp)
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Garlic Salt
  5. Take 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
  6. Make ready 8 oz Hot Sauce (Frank's)
  7. Get 2/3 can Tomato Soup
  8. Take 1 stick Butter

Steps to make Baked Hot Wings:
  1. Pre-heat oven to 375°F.
  2. Cut wings, throw away the tips.
  3. Melt butter in microwave.
  4. Mix cayenne powder, chili powder, garlic salt, onion powder, hot sauce, and tomato soup in with the melted butter.
  5. Marinade the chicken wings in a bag for at least 2 hours.
  6. Place foil onto a baking sheet (makes cleaning much easier) then spray with cooking oil.
  7. Place wings on baking sheet, bake for 45 minutes.
