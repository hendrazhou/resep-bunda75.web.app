Steps to make Kiwi Smoothie:
  1. Cut 2 kiwis in 4 pieces each and make a puree and add water if required (if the kiwi is hard or fresh add water and if it is quite soft then don't add water as it is already pulpy and juicy).
  2. If the taste of the kiwi is sour, you can add Sugar / honey / brown sugar to dilute the taste.
  3. Now add the smoothie into the glass. - Cut a lemon slice for decoration.
  4. Your kiwi Smoothie is ready to serve.
