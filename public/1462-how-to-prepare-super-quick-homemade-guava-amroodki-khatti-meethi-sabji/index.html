The ingredients needed to make Guava (amrood)ki khatti meethi sabji:
  1. Prepare 2 ripe guava
  2. Take 2 tsp sugar(as required)
  3. Take 2 tsp jaggery
  4. Make ready 2 tsp oil
  5. Make ready 1 tsp cumin seeds
  6. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp garam masala
  9. Get 1/2 tsp coriander leaves
  10. Get 1 chopped green chilli
  11. Get to taste Salt
