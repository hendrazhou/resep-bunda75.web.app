The ingredients needed to make Dark Double Chocolate Banana Muffins:
  1. Take 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  2. Make ready 2 eggs
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup sour cream
  4. Prepare Splash vanilla
  5. Get 1/2 cup milk
  6. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  7. Take 1 cup all purpose flour
  8. Take 1 healthy half cup dark cocoa powder
  9. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  10. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  11. Take 1 cup dark chocolate chips

Steps to make Dark Double Chocolate Banana Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Mash bananas in a mixing bowl. Add eggs and mix. Add sour cream and mix. Add milk and vanilla and mix. Add sugar and mix
  3. Next add the flour, cocoa powder, salt and baking soda. Mix well. Stir in 1 cup of chocolate chips.
  4. Spray muffin tin well with non stick spray. I used an ice cream scoop and it measured out to be exactly 12 muffin.
  5. Bake 25-30 mins or until top of muffins bounce back when touched.
