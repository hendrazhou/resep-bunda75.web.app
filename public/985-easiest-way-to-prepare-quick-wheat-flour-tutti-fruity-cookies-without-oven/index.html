The ingredients needed to make Wheat Flour Tutti fruity Cookies- without oven:
  1. Get 120 gm Whole Wheat Flour
  2. Get 2 tsp semolina
  3. Get 70 gm Clarified Butter / Ghee (I used ghee)
  4. Make ready 80 gm Sugar Powdered
  5. Take 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  6. Prepare 2-3 tsp Milk
  7. Make ready 30 gm Tutti Frutti
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt (if salted butter is used don't add salt)

Instructions to make Wheat Flour Tutti fruity Cookies- without oven:
  1. Mix the whole wheat flour, sugar, baking powder in a bowl. Add clarified butter (ghee).
  2. Mix everything to get a breadcrumb like texture. Add tutti fruity and milk to bind the dough.
  3. Keep the dough in the refrigerator covered with a cling wrap for 30 minutes.
  4. After 30 minutes take the dough out of the refrigerator. Roll the dough into a sheet of thickness ¼ inch.
  5. Using a cookie cutter, cut the cookies of it and place these on greased idli moulds. - - Meanwhile, preheat the idli cooker with salt as a base.
  6. Now place the stand, loaded with cookies, in idli cooker and cover with lid. - - Let it bake for 25-30 minutes on a medium to low flame.
  7. When edges turn golden, turn off the flame and keep the cookies on a wired rack to cool down. - - The healthy, delicious Cookies are ready.
