The ingredients needed to make Mango-Peach Salsa:
  1. Get 1 mango, peeled, small dice
  2. Take 2 small peaches, small dice
  3. Take 1/4 c red pepper, small dice
  4. Take 2 tbsp cilantro, chopped
  5. Get 1/4 c red or yellow onion, small dice
  6. Take 1/2 small lime, juiced
  7. Get to taste Salt, pepper, olive oil
