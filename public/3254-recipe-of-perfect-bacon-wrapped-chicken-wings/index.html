Steps to make Bacon Wrapped Chicken Wings:
  1. pre-heat oven to 375°F
  2. mix ingredients in a bowl besides honey
  3. Sprinkle all the ingredients on wet chicken
  4. wrap bacon on chicken breast and secure with toothpick
  5. Brush honey on the wrapped chicken (if you dnt have brush jst roll the chicken on honey an let it drip a little)
  6. Bake for 20-40min depending on how Brown an crispy you want em
  7. Turn once or twice on occasion
