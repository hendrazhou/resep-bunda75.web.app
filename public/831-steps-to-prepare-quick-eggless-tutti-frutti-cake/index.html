The ingredients needed to make Eggless Tutti Frutti Cake:
  1. Prepare Milk-1/2cup
  2. Get Powdered sugar-3/4cup
  3. Get Refined oil-1/2cup
  4. Get All purpose flour-1cup
  5. Get Baking powder-1teaspoon
  6. Take Bakingsoda-1/2teaspoon
  7. Get Salt-1pinch
  8. Get Vanilla essence-1tbsp
  9. Get Tutti frutti-1/4cup

Instructions to make Eggless Tutti Frutti Cake:
  1. In a bowl add oil and sugar mix well.
  2. Add milk 1/4cup and mix.
  3. Now seive and add flour,bakingpwd,bakingsoda,salt into the bowl and mix well.
  4. Now add the balance milk and mix well.
  5. Now take tutti frutti and add 1teaspoon flour and mix.
  6. Now add the tutti frutti into the batter and add vanilla essence and mix well.
  7. Now pour the cake batter into a greased bowl and microwave for 5minutes or cook in a kadai with 20 to 25mins.
