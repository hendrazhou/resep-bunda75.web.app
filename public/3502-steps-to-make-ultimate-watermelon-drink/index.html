The ingredients needed to make Watermelon Drink:
  1. Make ready 1/2 large watermelon ( seedless or de-seeded)
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup simple syrup (more if you like it sweeter)
  3. Make ready 1/2 medium fresh lemon juice
  4. Take 1 ice
  5. Make ready 1 shot of vodka per serving ( optional )

Instructions to make Watermelon Drink:
  1. Remove rind from watermelon, then cut into 2 inch pieces.
  2. Purée de-seeded or seedless watermelon in blender, then strain into pitcher.
  3. Add juice from 1/2 fresh lemon and simple syrup to pitcher
  4. Pour over ice add optional shot of vodka stir. Serve immediately
