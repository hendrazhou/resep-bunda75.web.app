The ingredients needed to make Sharbat_E_Rayhan (Drink Rayhan):
  1. Prepare 1 Tbsp Basil seeds (sock overnight in water)
  2. Get 2 Tbsp Manuka honey (or to taste) or add sugar syrup to taste
  3. Make ready 2 cups cold water
  4. Take 1-2 Tbsp rose water
  5. Prepare 1/2 bunch fresh dill little crushed in mortar and pestle
  6. Prepare Ice cubes (optional)
  7. Prepare slice lemon & lime
  8. Make ready Few dill leaves for garnish
