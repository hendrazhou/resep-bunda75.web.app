The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Roll Cake:
  1. Take For Cake
  2. Make ready 3 cups all purpose flour
  3. Make ready 1 cup granulated sugar
  4. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon salt
  5. Take 4 teaspoons baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon pumpkin spice blend
  7. Get 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  8. Make ready 2 large eggs
  9. Make ready 4 ounces butter (1 stick), melted, cooled to room temperature
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  11. Make ready For Cinnamon Swirl
  12. Make ready 8 ounces butter salted1 (2 sticks), at room temperature
  13. Make ready 1 cup light brown sugar, paked
  14. Get 2 tablespoon all purpose flour
  15. Get 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  16. Take For Glaze
  17. Get 2 cups confectioner's sugar
  18. Prepare 5 tablespoons whole milk
  19. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla exract
  20. Get 1 /4 cup raisins, optional

Steps to make Cinnamon Roll Cake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350. Spray a 9 by 13 inch pan with bakers spray
  2. Make Cake
  3. Combine flour, baking powder, salt, pumpkin pie spice and sugar in a bowl, whisk to mix
  4. In another bowl whisk milk, eggs and vanilla, whisk in melted butter
  6. Combine milk mixture with flour mixture and stir to blend
  7. Spread evenly in prepared pan
  8. Make Cinnamon Swirl
  9. Combine all ingredients for the topping in a bowl until well combined
  10. Drop topping over batter in mounds
  11. With a thin knife swirl thoughout batter
  12. Bake about 26 to30 minutes until golden and the center is set
  13. Make glaze
  14. Beat confectioner's sugar, milk and vanilla until smooth
  15. Glaze cake while warm
  16. Cut cake into sqares and serve warm. This cake is best served warm. It can be reheated in the,icrowave. It freezes well after cutting and cooling. Just wrap in indivdual sqares. Thaw and microwave just a few seconds until warm
