The ingredients needed to make Quick & Easy Cinnamon Sugar Crescents:
  1. Prepare 1 8 count can crescent rolls (such as Pillsbury)
  2. Take 3 tbs unsalted butter - melted and cooled slightly
  3. Take 2 tsp granulated white sugar - divided
  4. Take 1 tsp ground cinnamon - divided

Instructions to make Quick & Easy Cinnamon Sugar Crescents:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line a baking sheet with foil or parchment paper.
  2. Working one at a time lay out crescent roll flat, brush with a little butter, sprinkle with 1/4 tsp sugar and 1/8 tsp cinnamon. Roll up and place on baking sheet.
  3. When all crescents are rolled up place in oven and bake 10 minutes or until golden. Serve warm. Enjoy!
