The ingredients needed to make Sage & Lime Chicken:
  1. Get 2 Pounds Chicken Breast
  2. Get 1 Oz. Salted Butter
  3. Make ready 3 Tablespoons Dried Sage
  4. Make ready 1 Tablespoon Black Pepper
  5. Make ready 1 Lime
  6. Make ready To Taste Salt

Instructions to make Sage & Lime Chicken:
  1. Wash the Chicken Breast with plenty of water. Add Salt and Black Pepper to taste.
  2. Add the Sage and butter. You want the Butter to be as soft as possible. To achieve this, take it out of the fridge at least three hours in advance. Mix everything well.
  3. Cut the Lime and add the juice to the preparation.
  4. Place the Chicken Breast on the grill at medium heat for 7 min at each side. Serve and enjoy!!
