The ingredients needed to make Sweet potato salsa:
  1. Get 2 each Sweet potatoes
  2. Prepare 1/3 chopped red onion
  3. Get 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  4. Take 1/2 bunch Fresh cilantro
  5. Make ready 1 each serrano chili pepper minced
  6. Get 2 tsp Ground cumin
  7. Take 1 salt and pepper to taste
  8. Prepare 1 each Fresh Lime
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup olive oil or whatever you prefer
  10. Get 1 each tomato, diced

  1. Boil whole sweet potato in water for about 10 to 12 mins.
