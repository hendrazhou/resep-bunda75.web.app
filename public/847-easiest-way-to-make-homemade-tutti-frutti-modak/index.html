The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti Modak:
  1. Make ready 500 gms Dessicated coconut
  2. Prepare 100 gms multi color tuti frutti
  3. Take 2 tbsp sugar
  4. Take 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  5. Prepare as required Chopped dry fruits (almond, cashew nuts, pistachios, raisins)
  6. Take 1 tsp cardamon powder

  3. Now in other pan add 2 tbsp sugar and water and make sugar syrup
  4. We just melt sugar without single strands now add syrup in coconut mixture and cardamom powder mix it well now add dry fruits and tutti Frutti and mix well until it looks like soft dough
