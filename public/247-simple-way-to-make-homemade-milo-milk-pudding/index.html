The ingredients needed to make Milo Milk Pudding:
  1. Prepare 700 ml milk
  2. Take 1 sachet agar powder (7 grams)
  3. Prepare 1/2 sachet konyaku jelly powder
  4. Prepare 100 grams Milo powder
  5. Take 50 ml sweeyened condensed milk
  6. Prepare Sugar if needed

Instructions to make Milo Milk Pudding:
  1. Put the milk in a pan. Simmer it.
  2. Then add the jelly powder, agar powder, Milo powder and condensed milk.
  3. Stir until everything is dissolved.
  4. Keep stirring on low heat. Until the mixture boils.
  5. Then turn off the heat.
  6. Put into a mould. Let the steam dissapear then put in the fridge.
  7. Serve.
