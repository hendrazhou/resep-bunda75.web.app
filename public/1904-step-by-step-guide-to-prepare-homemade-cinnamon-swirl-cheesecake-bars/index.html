Steps to make Cinnamon Swirl Cheesecake Bars:
  1. Preheat the oven to 325. Line a 8 by 8 baking pan with foil, spray foil with bakers spray.
  2. Make crust
  3. In a bowl combine graham crumbs, brown sugar and butter,
  4. Press into pan and bake 10 minutes, cool on rack. Leave oven on
  5. Make Cinnamon Swirl
  6. Combine in a saucepan the brown sugar, cinnamon, flour and butter, cook, stirring over medium low heat until smooth. Set aside
  7. Make Cheesecake Fillimg
  8. In a bowl beat cream cheese. Salt. vanilla and sugar until light and fluffy
  9. Add eggs one at a time and beat in
  10. Add sour cream and fold in
  11. Spread 1/2 of cheesecake Filling over Crust abs smooth out. Using 1/2 of the cinnamon sugar mixture in mounds and swirl through batter
  12. Swirl with skewer
  13. Top with remaking cheesecake fillimg
  14. Add remaining cinnamon mixture, dropping in mounds and swirling like before with a skewer. Bake about 35 to 45 minutes until just set with center slightly jiggly. Cool compleatly on rack to room temperature thecover and refrigerate at least 6 hours or overnight
  16. Lift foil to remove entire cheesecake and cut into bars
