The ingredients needed to make Cheesy garlic bread:

Steps to make Cheesy garlic bread:
  1. In a mixing bowl add flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda, sieve properly. Add sugar, pizza oregano and chili flacks to it, now it’s time to add oil.
  2. Add curd to it, if curd is not sour add 1 teaspoon lemon juice.
  3. Knead the semi-soft dough with milk. Take milk at room temperature.
  4. Take a spoon full of water if needed.
  5. Knead for 5 to 10 minutes. Coat with oil and cover. Rest for 2 hour at a warm place.
  6. Knead the dough properly and divide it into two parts. Roll first part, take some dry flour for dusting.
  7. Apply garlic butter on it and then spread grated cheese as per your choice on half portion. - Sprinkle some pizza oregano and chili flacks over it.
  8. Apply water on the ages and fold half and seal properly. Again apply garlic butter over it.
  9. Again sprinkle pizza oregano and chili flacks over it.
  10. Roll another one and again spread garlic butter over it. Spread some finely chopped onion, capsicum, and boiled sweet corn on a half portion and then cover with both side.
  11. Roll another one and again spread garlic butter over it.
  12. Preheat the kadai for 10 minutes, now bake garlic bread for 20 minutes. And your garlic bread is ready to serve.
  13. Serve with cheesy dip or tomato sauce.
