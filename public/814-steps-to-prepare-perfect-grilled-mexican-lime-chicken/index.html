The ingredients needed to make Grilled Mexican Lime Chicken:
  1. Get marinade
  2. Get 2 tsp salt (i use sea salt)
  3. Take 2 tsp chili powder
  4. Take 2 tsp garlic powder
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup oil
  6. Take 1/2 cup lime juice
  7. Take chicken
  8. Prepare 4 lb Boneless Chicken Breast

Instructions to make Grilled Mexican Lime Chicken:
  1. Mix all ingredients, except chicken, for marinade.
  2. Pound chicken to desired thickness.
  3. Add chicken to the marinade, cover and let marinade at least 2 hours. I put mine in refrigerator over night.
  4. Grill on medium heat for 20 minutes or until done.
  5. Enjoy!
