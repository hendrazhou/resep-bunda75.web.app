The ingredients needed to make Sams Guava Lime Goodness Icie:
  1. Make ready 8 oz guava nectar
  2. Get 2 1/2 cup ice
  3. Prepare 2 limes, freshly juiced (about 3 tbsp)
  4. Get 3 oz 7 Up soda
  5. Make ready 4 oz light rum
  6. Get 1 zest of one lime

Instructions to make Sams Guava Lime Goodness Icie:
  1. Add guava nectar and ice into blender and blend until icy smooth.
  2. Add lime juice, lime zest, 7 Up soda and light rum. Pulse on high until blended well. Serve in a cold or frosty glass with a lime wedge for garnish. Enjoy!
