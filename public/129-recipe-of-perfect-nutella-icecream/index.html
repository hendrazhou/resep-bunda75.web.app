The ingredients needed to make Nutella Icecream:
  1. Get 1/2 cup Brown sugar
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Granulated sugar
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cup Whole milk
  4. Take 3 1/2 cup Heavy cream
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp Vanilla extract
  6. Get 400 grams Nutella

Instructions to make Nutella Icecream:
  1. In a large saucepan, gently heat milk, cream and vanilla extract.
  2. Whisk in sugars until dissolved.
  3. Once sugars have been dissolved, whisk in nutella until fully melted into mixture.
  4. Once combined, transfer mixture to heat proof bowl and refrigerate till cold.
  5. Transfer mixture into an ice cream maker and allow to set as per manufacturers instructions.
  6. Freeze additional few hours if required.
