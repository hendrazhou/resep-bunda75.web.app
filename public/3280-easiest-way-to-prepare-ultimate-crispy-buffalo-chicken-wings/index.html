  4. Prepare 1 veggie oil

Instructions to make crispy buffalo chicken wings:
  1. season chicken wingettes to your likeing
  2. preheat oil in fry pot
  3. mix buffalo sauce w jerk sauce cook in pot together until heated
  4. marinate bread crumbs on chicken wings then over coat them with flour
  5. fry each chicken wing 4-5 mins each till golden brown
  6. once finished drizzle buffalo sauce over cooked chicken and let sit, sprinkle parsley on top
  7. enjoy with the family
