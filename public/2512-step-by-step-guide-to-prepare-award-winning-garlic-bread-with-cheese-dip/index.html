The ingredients needed to make Garlic Bread with Cheese Dip:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup Maida
  2. Take 1/2 tsp Dry yeast
  3. Prepare 1 tsp Sugar
  4. Make ready 1 tsp Olive oil
  5. Prepare 4 tsp Melted butter
  6. Take 2 tsp Grated garlic
  7. Make ready Salt as per your taste
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Oregano
  9. Get 1/2 tsp Pizza seasoning
  10. Take 1/2 tsp Chilli flacks
  11. Take 100 gms Mozzarella Cheese
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp Corn flour

Instructions to make Garlic Bread with Cheese Dip:
  1. Take a small bowl and pour lukewarm water upto 3/4 of the bowl and put sugar and dry yeast.give rest for 10 minutes to activate the yeast.
  2. Take maida and mix salt,grated garlic,Olive oil,Melted butter,Oregano,pizza seasoning,Chilli flacks.make soft dough with activated yeast water.brush with melted butter and keep the dough in room temperature for 2hrs.
  3. Dough will become double in size.
  4. Make chapati with dough(chapati should be thicker in size).now in a small ball take 2 tsp.
