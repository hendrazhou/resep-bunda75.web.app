The ingredients needed to make Corn cheese garlic bread:
  1. Get 4 Bread slices
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
  3. Make ready 2 tsp garlic paste
  4. Get 1 tsp green chili paste
  5. Get 1 tsp chili flakes
  6. Get 1 tsp oregano
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp processed cheese grated
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp mozzarella cheese grated
  9. Take 1 tbsp oil
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp boiled sweet corn kernals
