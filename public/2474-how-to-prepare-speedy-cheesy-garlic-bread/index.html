The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. Make ready 1 loaf or a bougette
  2. Take 10-12 garlic pods crushed
  3. Take 2 tbsp butter
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
  5. Take 4 tbsp parsley chopped
  6. Get As needed Mozzarella cheese

Steps to make Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. Combine garlic, butter, and oil in a microwave safe dish or in a small saucepan.
  2. Heat garlic and butter and oil in microwave for 1 minute or in a small pot over low heat for 3 minutes.
  3. Cut bread loaf in half an inch thick slices
  4. Toast split bread slices
  5. Remove bread when it is toasted golden brown in colour.
  6. Brush bread liberally with garlic oil.
  7. Sprinkle with cheese and parsley.
  8. Bake the slices to brown for another 30 seconds. - Take them out and serve hot
