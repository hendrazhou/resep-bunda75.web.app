The ingredients needed to make Barley and potato soup:
  1. Make ready 2 onion, chopped
  2. Make ready 2 cup barley
  3. Make ready 500 grams bacon bits
  4. Prepare 5 potatoes (large)
  5. Get 1 garlic
  6. Prepare 1 salt

Instructions to make Barley and potato soup:
  1. Fry the chopped onions and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add the bacon bits and cook through.
  3. Add the barley and water (about four cups)
  4. Once the barley is softened through add the chopped potatoes and salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cook until everything is soft enough for your enjoyment.
