Steps to make Home made tutti frutti:
  1. Pill and cut pawpaw into shapes.
  2. Boil for 15mints til transperant.
  3. Drain on a colander.
  4. Add 2cups of water in a source pan or pot, add sugar to the warm water till sugar melt.
  5. Add ur boiled pawpaw to d sugar syrup, allow to boil for 15mints. Put of d stove, add 1 teaspoon of flavour and allow to cool for 5mints.
  6. Divide boild pawpaw into 4 diff bowl and add colours of your choice. I used red, green yellow nd didn't add anytin to d last possion.keep over night to absorb the colour.
