The ingredients needed to make Cheese and garlic bread:
  1. Get 5 bread slices
  2. Make ready 5 garlic flakes chopped into fine
  3. Take 5 tablespoons grated cheese
  4. Get 1 tablespoon oregano
  5. Make ready 1 tablespoon basil
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon chilli flakes
  7. Make ready 5 tablespoons melted butter
  8. Make ready Little coriander leaves chopped into fine
