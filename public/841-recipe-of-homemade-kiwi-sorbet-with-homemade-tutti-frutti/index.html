The ingredients needed to make Kiwi sorbet with homemade tutti frutti:
  1. Take 3-4 kiwi fruits ripe
  2. Make ready 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  3. Get As required Sugar syrup cubes (I usually store them it’s optional)
  4. Make ready As required Homemade tutti frutti for garnishing

Steps to make Kiwi sorbet with homemade tutti frutti:
  1. Peel all the kiwis and set it in freezer for 30 minutes pop in the sugar syrup cubes if you are using.
  2. After 30 minutes remove it then blend it using hand mixer or blender.
  3. Then add condensed milk again whisk it then pop it in a collapsible container garnish with tutti frutti generously set it in refrigerator for about 3-4 hours.
  4. Just scoop it & enjoy. The deliciously kiwi sorbet with homemade tutti frutti is ready..!
