The ingredients needed to make Swiss roll tutti frutti cookies:
  1. Make ready 2 cup maida
  2. Make ready 1 cup powder sugar
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup melted butter
  4. Get 1/2 cup tutti-frutti
  5. Take 1/2 tsp Elaichi powder
  6. Get 3 tsp cocoa powder
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp baking powder
  8. Take as needed Milk for making dough

Instructions to make Swiss roll tutti frutti cookies:
  1. Seave the maida and baking powder, keep aside
  2. Take a mixing bowl add butter and sugar mix together till light and fluffy
  3. Then pour seave maida Elaichi powder, milk and make a soft dough
  4. If the dough seems hard then u can add little milk
  5. Now divide the dough into two parts
  6. Mix cocoa powder in one part and left one part white,
  7. In white part mix tutti-frutti
  8. Now roll the dough separately like roti
  9. Set them one by one
  10. Roll it tightly like a log, warp it and keep it in fridge for 1 hour or till set
  11. Grease a baking tray and cut slices of the log and set it in baking tray, bake it in a preheated oven at 180 degree c for 20 to 25 minutes till golden brown,
  12. Now cool it and keep in a air tight container
