The ingredients needed to make Lime chicken soup instant pot ip:
  1. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. Take 1 small yellow onion chopped
  3. Get 2 stalks celery chopped
  4. Prepare 20 sliced jalapeno
  5. Take 2 heaving tbsp minced garlic
  6. Prepare 1 pound chicken breast diced
  7. Prepare 3 cups chicken broth
  8. Prepare 10 oz can of Rotel
  9. Take 1/2 tsp dried orenano
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp cumin
  11. Get 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  12. Get Fresh or dried cilantro
  13. Take Juice of 1 lime
  14. Get Shredded mild cheddar cheese

Steps to make Lime chicken soup instant pot ip:
  1. Put all ingredients EXCEPT the chicken, cilantro, lime, and cheese in a blender. Puree until almost smooth. There should be some little chunks left. Set aside.
  2. Turn on instant pot in saute high and wait for it to say hot.
  3. Add olive oil and chicken and cook the chicken till it turns white.
  4. Add mixture from blender. Stir and scrape the bits of the bottom.
  5. Seal instant pot and cook on manual (high) 20 minutes. When cooking is done quick release the pressure.
  6. Remove lid. Be careful of the steam. Stir in lime juice.
  7. Ladle into bowls and add cilantro and cheese on top.
  8. Enjoy!!
