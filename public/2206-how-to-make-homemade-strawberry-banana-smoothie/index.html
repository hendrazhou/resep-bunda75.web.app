The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Banana Smoothie:
  1. Prepare 1 Vanilla Petite Creme Yogurt (140, 10)
  2. Prepare 1/2 Banana (54, 0.6)
  3. Prepare 3 medium Strawberries (12, 0.2)
  4. Get 1/2 (so 1/8 cup) Bear Naked Original Cinnamon Granola (70, 3)
