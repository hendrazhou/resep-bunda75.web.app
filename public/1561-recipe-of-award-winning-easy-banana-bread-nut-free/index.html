The ingredients needed to make EASY banana bread (nut free):
  1. Take 4 extra ripe bananas
  2. Get 1/3 cup milk
  3. Get 2/3 cup sugar (you can use brown sugar for a richer darker banana bread)
  4. Prepare 3 eggs
  5. Prepare 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  6. Take 2 and 2/3 cup bisquick
  7. Take 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
