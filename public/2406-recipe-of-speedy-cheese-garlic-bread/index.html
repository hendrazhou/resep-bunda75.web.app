The ingredients needed to make Cheese Garlic Bread:
  1. Make ready Soft french bread OR baguette bread
  2. Prepare Olive oil
  3. Get Minced Dried basil [to taste]
  4. Take Powdered thyme [to taste]
  5. Take Pinch salt
  6. Take 2 garlic cloves, minced
  7. Prepare Shredded Mozzarella cheese
  8. Make ready Shredded parmesan cheese (optional)
  9. Take Garlic paste
  10. Make ready Finely chopped fresh parsley

Steps to make Cheese Garlic Bread:
  1. Preheat oven
  2. Slice bread into thick even round slices. Place bread, cut side up, onto a baking sheet and in a baking pan.
  3. IF USING SOFT BREAD, put the bread in the oven for 5 minutes to get a clean crisp.
  4. In a bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, salt, thyme and basil. Even out the flavors to your taste. Spread generously the mixture onto the whole surface of the bread, covering the sides so it won't burn.
  5. Place in oven for 5 minutes so that the flavors get soaked in.
  6. Spread garlic paste then sprinkle rhe cheeses, setting them into the bread so it wont fall off.
  7. Bake in oven for 10 minutes, until the cheese is melted but not turned into a brown color.
  8. To put the fresh parsley, remove from oven when the cheese starts to melt then sprinkle it, so that it won't wither then continue baking.
