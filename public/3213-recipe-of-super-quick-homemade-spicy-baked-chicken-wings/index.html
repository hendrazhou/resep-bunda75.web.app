The ingredients needed to make Spicy baked chicken wings:
  1. Prepare marinating
  2. Get 1 onion
  3. Take 3 garlic cloves (finely cut)
  4. Make ready 1 ginger
  5. Get 2 tsp salt
  6. Take 1 dried thyme
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
  8. Get 1 paprika
  9. Take 1 dried rosemary
  10. Take 1 tbsp brown sugar
  11. Get 2 squeezed lemons
  12. Get 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions to make Spicy baked chicken wings:
  1. Mix everything in a bowl with the chicken wings
  2. Put in baking pan and cover with aluminium foil. Preheat the oven on 200 C
  3. Let it bake for 1 hour and a half
  4. Remove the foil and let it bake for another half an hour
  5. Serve and enjoy
