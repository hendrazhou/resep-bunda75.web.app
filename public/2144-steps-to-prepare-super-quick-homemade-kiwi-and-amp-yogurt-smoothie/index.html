The ingredients needed to make Kiwi & Yogurt Smoothie:
  1. Take Kiwi 1 - 2 Kiwis
  2. Make ready 1 Scoop Vanilla Ice Cream
  3. Take 2 Tbsp Plain Yogurt
  4. Prepare 60 g Milk
  5. Take 20 g Sugar
  6. Take Ice Cubes 80g

The kiwi is a symbol for New Zealand. People from New Zealand are nicknamed "Kiwis". They have a long beak and brown feathers. kiwi (plural kiwi or kiwis). A flightless bird of the genus Apteryx native to New Zealand.

Steps to make Kiwi & Yogurt Smoothie:
  1. Peel and slice the kiwi(s). Set aside 2 - 3 slices as decoration. Add the rest of the sliced kiwis to a blender.
  2. Add the vanilla ice cream, plain yogurt, milk, sugar and ice cubes to the blender and blend well.
  3. Decorate as you like.
