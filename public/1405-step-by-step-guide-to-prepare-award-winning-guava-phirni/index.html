The ingredients needed to make Guava Phirni:
  1. Take 5-6 Guava(medium size)
  2. Take 1 tsp Ghee
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Pistachios (chopped)
  4. Take 1 tsp Cashew nuts (chopped)
  5. Take 1 tsp Almonds(chopped)

  8. Prepare 1/4 cup Condensed Milk

  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp Cardamom Powder
  11. Take Pinch salt
  12. Make ready 1 cup Water

Steps to make Guava Phirni:
  1. Wash and cut guava. Now heat water in a pan. Add guava pieces. Cover it and 2-3 min.
  2. Now cool it and blend it. Now strain it and get puree. Mean while soak saffron thread in milk.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan. Now add all dry fruits and cook for while.
  4. Add guava puree. Mix well. Cook for min.
  5. Now add safforn, cardamom powder, and condensed milk. Add milk too. Mix well.
  6. Cook 2-3 min. Guava phirni is ready. Keep in fridge, and serve it chilled
