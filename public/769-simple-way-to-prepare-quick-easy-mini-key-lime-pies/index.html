The ingredients needed to make Easy Mini Key Lime Pies:
  1. Prepare 4 egg yolks
  2. Make ready 2 14 oz cans of sweetened Condensed milk
  3. Get 1 cup lime juice
  4. Make ready 12 mini graham cracker pie crusts

Steps to make Easy Mini Key Lime Pies:
  1. Preheat oven to 375° F.
  2. Combine egg yolks, condensed milk, and lime juice. Mix well.
  3. Pour into unbaked pie crusts.
  4. Bake in oven for 13 - 15 minutes.
  5. Allow to cool. Then garnish.
