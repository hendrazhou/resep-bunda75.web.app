Steps to make Chicken wings in schezwan sauce😊:
  1. Marinate the chicken wings with the above marinating ingredients…keep for at least 10 mins.now mix up the flour with the coating ingredients.
  2. Coat the wings with flour mixture and deep fry till golden brown…
  3. Now heat oil add the cloves and whole red chilli…saute few seconds add the sauce ingredients…honey then toss up sauce with fried chicken wings
