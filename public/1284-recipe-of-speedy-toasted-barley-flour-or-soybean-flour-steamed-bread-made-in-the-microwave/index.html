The ingredients needed to make Toasted Barley Flour (or Soybean Flour) Steamed Bread, Made in the Microwave:
  1. Prepare 60 grams Cake flour
  2. Get 10 grams Toasted barley flour (or soybean flour)
  3. Get 25 grams Sugar (cane sugar is optimal)
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Baking powder
  5. Take 130 ml Milk
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Vegetable oil (optional)
  7. Get Topping
  8. Take 1 to 1 (as needed) Toasted barley flour (or soybean flour) and powdered sugar

Instructions to make Toasted Barley Flour (or Soybean Flour) Steamed Bread, Made in the Microwave:
  1. Put all the ingredients, except for the milk, into a bowl and mix them together well with a whisk. Add the milk and vegetable oil.
  2. Pour the batter from Step 1 into microwave-safe moulds (something like silicon cups), place them in a larger bowl, then cover with plastic wrap. It's alright to use a special microwave steaming container.
  3. Microwave the bowl at 500W for 2 to 2 1/2 minutes. Once the batter has stopped looking raw, allow the bread to sit for 2 minutes to steam. (See Helpful Hints)
