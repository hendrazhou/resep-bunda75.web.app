The ingredients needed to make Instant Mango Delight:
  1. Make ready 4-6 ripe mango slices
  2. Get 2 tbsp cottage cheese
  3. Take 4 tbsp milk powder
  4. Take 4 tsp sugar
  5. Make ready Few chopped almonds and tutti -frutti for garnishing

Steps to make Instant Mango Delight:
  1. Cut mango slices and peel.
  2. Take cottage cheese and crumble it.
  3. Mix sugar, milk powder in it and mix.
  4. Place this mixture on the mango slices, and press tightly that the mixture become set properly.
  5. Garnish with chopped almonds and tutti - frutti.
  6. Lastly put in fridge for chill about 1 hour, after one hour take out from fridge serve and enjoy with your kids.
