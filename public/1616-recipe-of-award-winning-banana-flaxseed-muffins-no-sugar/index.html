The ingredients needed to make Banana Flaxseed Muffins (NO SUGAR):
  1. Take 6 Medium Ripe Bananas
  2. Take 1/4 cup Organic Raw Honey
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup Organic Butter, melted
  4. Get 2 Eggs
  5. Take 1 1/2 cups Organic All-Purpose Flour
  6. Take 1 1/4 cups Organic Whole Wheat Flour
  7. Take 1/4 cup ground flaxseeds
  8. Prepare 2 tsp baking soda
  9. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder

Steps to make Banana Flaxseed Muffins (NO SUGAR):
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mash bananas in a bowl. Add honey, butter and eggs, mix well
  3. Add all purpose flour, whole wheat flour, flaxseeds, baking soda and baking powder
  4. Scoop batter into the baking molds (I use silicone baking molds, it is easier to clean). If yours is not silicone, spread nonstick spray or butter into the mold
  5. Baked for about 25 minutes
  6. All Done and enjoy!
