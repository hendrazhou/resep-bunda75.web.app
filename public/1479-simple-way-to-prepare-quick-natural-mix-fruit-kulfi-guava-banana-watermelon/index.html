The ingredients needed to make Natural mix fruit kulfi (guava,banana,watermelon):
  1. Prepare 1/2 ltr boiled milk
  2. Prepare 1 cup powder sugar
  3. Prepare 5 piece cardamom powder
  4. Make ready 1/2 Medium size guava
  5. Get 1 banana
  6. Take 1 fourth watermelon small size
  7. Make ready Crushed pista badam for garnish

Steps to make Natural mix fruit kulfi (guava,banana,watermelon):
  1. Boil milk for ten minutes in medium flame
  2. Put sugar and cardamom powder and stir for two minutes
  3. Grind all the fruits and add into the milk and stir for three minutes then switch of the flame
  4. After some time when milk get cool grind it for one minute and pour into the kulfee cone fridge it for 8 to 10 hour
  5. Now kulfi is ready garnish it with crushed pista badam
