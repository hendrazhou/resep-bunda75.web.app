The ingredients needed to make Super Easy Smoked Sausage & Cabbage in Salsa:
  1. Take 1 Tablespoon oil or butter
  2. Take 2 strips bacon, cut into 1/2" pieces
  3. Get 1/2 a large onion, cut into 1/2" strips vertically
  4. Get 1/2 a medium head of green cabbage, cored and cut into 1-inch pieces
  5. Make ready 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  6. Prepare optional 1 large carrot, cut into 1/3-inch thick pieces
  7. Take 1 pound smoked sausage, cut into 1/2-inch thick pieces
  8. Get 1 cup storebought salsa (like Pace, La Victoria, Frontera, etc.)
  9. Make ready 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  10. Make ready 1 Tablespoon hot sauce of choice
  11. Get 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  12. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  13. Get chopped green onions and/or cilantro for garnish

Instructions to make Super Easy Smoked Sausage & Cabbage in Salsa:
  1. In a large pot or deep frying pan, bring the oil or butter up to medium heat and render a bit of the fat from the bacon.
  2. Add the onion, cabbage, and garlic (and carrot if you're using it) to the pan and cook, stirring occasionally, just until the onions start to turn translucent, about 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Add the sausage, salsa, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, salt, and sugar to the pan, and give it a few gentle stirs to incorporate all the ingredients.
  4. Reduce the heat to medium low, cover, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle green onions and/or cilantro over the top and serve with starch of choice.
  6. Enjoy! :)
