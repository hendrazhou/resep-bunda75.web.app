The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti:
  1. Get 200 gm watermelon white part
  2. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 1/2 cup water
  4. Take as needed Food colours(green,red,yellow)
Instructions to make Tutti frutti:
  1. Firstly take out white part from watermelon and cut into small pieces..
  2. Now take a pan add,,sugar and water..and let it boil..now add watermelon rind pieces and cook on low to medium flame for 20-25 mins..
  3. Now when it turns out transparent off the gas..
  4. And take out in 3 different bowls with 2 tsp of sugar syrup..add required colors…and let it rest for 30 min…
  5. After 30 min colour will b coated on pieces well…
  6. Take a plate or tray,,cover with foil paper…and drain out the pieces from sugar syrup..and let it dry for 2 days in dry place…
  7. Your tutti fruti is ready….
