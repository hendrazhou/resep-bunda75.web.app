The ingredients needed to make Healthy Quick Very Berry Smoothie:
  1. Get 1 1/2 Cup almond milk (or any milk of preference)
  2. Make ready 1/2 Cup Greek plain yogurt
  3. Prepare 2 teaspoons flaxseed
  4. Prepare 2 tablespoons vanilla protein powder
  5. Prepare 4-5 medium-sized strawberries
  6. Make ready 1/2 Cup frozen blueberries (I used frozen but fresh would work)
  7. Take Ice (optional; to chill)
  8. Get 4-5 raspberries (optional)

Instructions to make Healthy Quick Very Berry Smoothie:
  1. Liquids first, pour in almond milk and yogurt into blender followed by the rest of ingredients. Blend until smooth. If too thick, simply add more milk and blend.
  2. Serve immediately or store in air tight container in fridge/freezer. Stays fresh about 3 days.
