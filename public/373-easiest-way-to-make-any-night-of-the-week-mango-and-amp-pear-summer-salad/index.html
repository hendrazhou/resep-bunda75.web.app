The ingredients needed to make Mango & Pear summer salad:
  1. Prepare 1 head bibb lettuce
  2. Get 1 small package spring mix
  3. Take 2 pears, 1 small diced, 1 matchstick cut
  4. Prepare 1 mango, julienned
  5. Make ready 1 cup chopped hazelnuts, toasted
  6. Prepare 3 oz goat cheese
  7. Get 1 pkg cherry tomatoes sliced in half
  8. Prepare 2 carrots, shredded

Instructions to make Mango & Pear summer salad:
  1. Wash and cut your pears. 1 small diced and the other matchstick.
  2. Peel and julienne your mango.
  3. Wash, peel and grate your carrots (or just buy pre shredded bag)
  4. In a bowl add your spring mix and bibb lettuce.
  5. Add all other ingredients and serve with your favorite dressing or you can try it with my mango balsamic vinaigrette in my recipe list.
