  3. Get 250 ml plain yoghurt

  5. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  6. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp vanilla essence
  8. Get Pinch salt
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup mixed tutti frutti
