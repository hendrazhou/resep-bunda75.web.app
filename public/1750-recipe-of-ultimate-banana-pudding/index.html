The ingredients needed to make Banana Pudding:
  1. Get 2 & 1/2 cups whole milk
  2. Prepare 2 egg yolks
  3. Get 2/3 white sugar
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp flour
  5. Take 1 & 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Prepare 1 container cool whip
  7. Prepare 2-3 bananas
  8. Get Nilla wafers

Steps to make Banana Pudding:
  1. In a large saucepan, mix together milk & yolks - turn stove onto mark 4.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour & sugar then add to the egg/milk mixture.
  3. Stir it for 15 minutes straight, literally. (This part is tedious but definitely worthwhile, I promise.)
  4. After it's thickened, take off the heat & stir in the vanilla extract. Set aside.
  5. Slice the bananas.
  6. In an 8x8in or a 9x9in dish do alternating layers of the bananas, nilla wafers, cool whip & pudding.
  7. Cover with plastic wrap & refrigerate for a few hours or overnight.
