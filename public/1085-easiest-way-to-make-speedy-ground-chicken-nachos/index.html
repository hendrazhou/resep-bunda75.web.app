The ingredients needed to make Ground Chicken Nachos:
  1. Prepare 1 packages tostitos chips
  2. Take 3 tbsp fajita seasoning
  3. Get 20 oz ground chicken queso
  4. Get 1/2 cup sour cream
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup queso
  6. Prepare 1 envelope taco cheese
  7. Prepare 1/2 head lettuce chopped
  8. Take 1/2 oz real bacon bits
  9. Get 1 tomato chopped
  10. Make ready 3 stick green onion
  11. Get 1/2 avacado
  12. Prepare 1 oz cilantro

Steps to make Ground Chicken Nachos:
  1. Cook chicken and season with fajita seasoning
  2. Pull apart chicken and add to food processer with can of queso and some sour cream until blended
  3. Add chips to glass cooking dish and cover in chicken queso mix
  4. Sprinkle mixed taco cheese over chips
  5. Top with lettuce, chopped tomatoes, chopped green onion, chopped cilantro, chopped bacon bits, and chopped avacado
