The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti cookies:
  1. Get 1 cup All purpose flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  3. Get 1/4 cup milk powder
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup butter
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup tutti frutti
  6. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Get 1/2 tsp rose essence

  4. Add rose essence in butter and sugar mixture
  5. Mix wet and dry ingredients and form a dough,if dough is difficult to blind then add 2 tbsp milk orelse skip it
