The ingredients needed to make Fast cooking Banana Pudding Trifle:
  1. Make ready 4 cups cold whole milk
  2. Prepare 2 (3.4 oz.) packages instant banana pudding mix
  3. Prepare 3 bananas, sliced
  4. Make ready 1 big bottle of whipped cream
  5. Make ready 1 (12 oz.) prepared pound cake, cubed
  6. Make ready 1 (12 oz.) box vanilla wafers, crushed

Instructions to make Fast cooking Banana Pudding Trifle:
  1. In a large bowl, mix milk and pudding together until smooth.
  2. Prepare all ingredients, put them next to you. Slice banana.
  3. In your trifle dish lay all products in the following order: vanilla wafers, pound cake (you need to crush it), pudding, banana, whipped cream, vanilla wafers, pound cake, pudding, banana, whipped cream… Repeat this pattern till the dish will be filled out.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve or eat right away:)
