Steps to make Hot Buffalo/Lemon Pepper/Barbecue CHICKEN WINGS:
  1. Defrost your chicken wings and separate a few for the fryer and the oven
  2. Pour some oil in your deep fryer and set to 400°f. Preheat your oven to 450°f
  3. Using one bowl microwave your butter on high for 30 sec. and mix well with hot sauce. Grab your second bowl and pour in a couple of tbs of lemon pepper
  4. Add some wings to your fryer and fry until wings are lightly brown on the outside. Place a couple of wings on your oven tray and let them cook for 35 min. or until lightly brown.
  5. When they are done frying, choose to mix either with the hot sauce or lemon pepper seasoning. Once done baking in the oven, spread barbecue sauce all around the wings and put back in the oven for 10 min.
  6. Once you have the amount of wings to your liking, you are now ready to serve and enjoy!
