The ingredients needed to make Harvest Apple Cinnamon Tart:
  1. Take 1 pie crust (I used pillsbury)
  2. Prepare 1 large apple, sliced or chopped (skin on or off,it's up to you)
  3. Prepare 1/2 Fresh lemon juice
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  5. Get 1 tbsp brown sugar
  6. Get 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp apricot jam (or any jam you'd like)
  8. Get 1 1/2 tbsp chopped pecans
  9. Get drizzle of caramel sauce
  10. Take dusting of powder sugar
  11. Get 1 tbsp melted unsalted butter

Instructions to make Harvest Apple Cinnamon Tart:
  1. In a bowl, mix together the white and brown sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, pecans and apples.
  2. Roll out pie crust on a pizza pan, or baking sheet.
  3. Spread jam on the pie crust.
  4. Lay your apple mixture on the pie crust, leaving about an inch from the edges.
  5. You can sprinkle more cinnamon on if you like, then fold up the edges of the pie crust. Lightly baste the edges of crust with melted butter.
  6. Bake at 400°F for about 35-40 min or until golden brown.
  7. Let cool for 10 min after removing from oven. Drizzle with caramel sauce and Dusting of powder sugar.
  8. Move to cooling rack to cool completely.
