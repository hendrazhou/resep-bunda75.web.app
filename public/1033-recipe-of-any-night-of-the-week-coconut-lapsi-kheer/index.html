The ingredients needed to make Coconut Lapsi Kheer:
  1. Take 1/2 Ltr milk
  2. Get 50 gms Coconut powder or grated
  3. Take 1/2 cup Lapsi or dalia
  4. Get 1/2 cup chopped lotus seeds/ makhane
  5. Prepare 1 Tbsp almondette / chirongi
  6. Get some Some raisins or tutti frutti
  7. Take 1 Tbsp milk powder or fresh cream
  8. Make ready 3 Tbsp jaggery powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 Tsp Cardamon powder

Steps to make Coconut Lapsi Kheer:
  1. Heat milk along with cream or Milk powder & Lapsi in a pan.Cook it at low flame.
  2. Now add lotus seeds, Coconut powder and chirongi. Mix it nicely
  3. Cook until it becomes little thick.Then turn off the gas and stir for few seconds.Now add raisins, Cardmon powder and jaggery powder and completely cool down.Garnish with some chirongi and tutti frutti.
