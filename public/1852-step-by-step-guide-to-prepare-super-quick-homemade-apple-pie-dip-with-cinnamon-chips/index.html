The ingredients needed to make Apple Pie Dip with Cinnamon Chips:
  1. Take Apple Pie Dip:
  2. Prepare 2 large granny smith apples, peeled and diced
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup brown sugar
  4. Get 1 tsp. lemon juice
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
  6. Make ready 1/8 tsp. ground nutmeg
  7. Prepare Pinch salt
  8. Take 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
  9. Prepare Cinnamon Chips:
  10. Make ready Tortillas of choice (I use whole wheat)
  11. Make ready Cinnamon sugar mixture
  12. Get 1 tbsp. unsalted butter, melted

Steps to make Apple Pie Dip with Cinnamon Chips:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Line a baking tray with a wire rack over it. Cut the tortillas into chip sized pieces (or use cookie cutters to cut out fun shapes) and lay them out on the wire rack. Brush the melted butter over them lightly and sprinkle with the cinnamon sugar. Bake for 8-12 minutes, until they are crisp.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine all the apple pie dip ingredients except for the vanilla and set the heat to medium. Cover with a lid and let it cook for 20 or so minutes, until the apples are fragrant and have softened. Remove the pot from the heat and stir in the vanilla.
  3. That's it! Let the dip cool slightly. Serve the dip with the cinnamon chips and enjoy.
