The ingredients needed to make Easy Tomato Salsa Dip:
  1. Prepare 5 pcs small tomatoes, chopped
  2. Take 1 pc small red onion, chopped
  3. Prepare 1 pc long green or red chili, chopped
  4. Take 3 tsp pickle relish with dressing
  5. Take 1 clove garlic, chopped
  6. Take 1 stalk green onion chive, chopped
  7. Prepare 3 tsp ketchup or tomato sauce
  8. Prepare 1 handful shredded and seasoned boiled chicken fillet
  9. Take to taste Salt

Steps to make Easy Tomato Salsa Dip:
  1. Combine first 7 ingredients. Mix well in a bowl. Taste and add chilies, onions, or ketchup, according to your preference.
  2. Mix in shredded chicken. Season with salt according to taste. Adjust taste according to preference.
  3. Chill at least 30 minutes or more before serving. Serve with Nachos, corn or potato chips.
