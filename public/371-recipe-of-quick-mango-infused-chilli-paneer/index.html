The ingredients needed to make Mango Infused Chilli Paneer:
  1. Make ready 5-6 mangoes
  2. Get 1 cup paneer
  3. Get 1 medium size onion cut into small cubes
  4. Get 1 capsicum
  5. Prepare 1 tsp finely chopped garlic
  6. Make ready 2 tsp finely chopped green chillies
  7. Take 1 tsp oil
  8. Get 1 tsp chilli garlic sauce
  9. Prepare 2 tsp honey
  10. Make ready 1 tsp black salt
  11. Take Bunch green onions
  12. Prepare 2 tsp roasted sesame seeds
  13. Get For marinate the paneer
  14. Make ready 2 tsp corn flour
  15. Get 1 tsp ginger paste
  16. Make ready to taste Salt
  17. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala powder
  18. Prepare 1 tsp dry mango powder
  19. Make ready 1 tsp green chilli paste
  20. Prepare 1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions to make Mango Infused Chilli Paneer:
  1. First roughly chopped the mangoes then in a blender,add mangoes,dry red chilli,garlic don’t add water blend it to a fine paste, now heat a kadai add the prepared mango puree stir it then add chilli garlic paste,honey cook it for 2 minutes
  2. Then turn off the heat once done keep it aside now in a mixing bowl add the paneer cubes and other spices and corn flour mix it keep it rest for 10 minutes then deep fry those paneer cubes,now heat a kadai add oil add chopped garlic sauté it for 1 minutes
  3. Then add onion cubes sauté it for few minutes then add fried paneer cubes add the prepared mango puree toss it put some green onions mix it turn off the heat garnish it with some ripe chopped mangoes roasted sesame seeds…..
  4. Ready to serve.
