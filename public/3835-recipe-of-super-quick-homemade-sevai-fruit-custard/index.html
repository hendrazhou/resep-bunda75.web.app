The ingredients needed to make Sevai fruit custard:
  1. Take 400 gm milk
  2. Make ready 2 tsp virmicelli
  3. Take 2 tsp tutty fruity
  4. Make ready 4 tsp choco sauce
  5. Take 5 tsp apple pieces
  6. Make ready 4 tsp pomegrante
  7. Make ready 4 tsp banana pieces
  8. Take 5 tsp crush biscuit(with butter and sugar)
  9. Prepare 2 red cherry
  10. Make ready 3 tsp custard
  11. Make ready 1 tsp ghee
  12. Make ready 4 tsp condensed milk

Steps to make Sevai fruit custard:
  1. At first,boil the milk with custard and condensed milk
  2. Then add ghee roast sevai in it and stir well and make a thick mixture.
  3. Take glass and spread syrup with spoon and keep the custard sevai in it
  4. Then,add fruits on it and choco sauce and crush biscuit on it
  5. Lastly,add tutty fruity and cerry on it and serve after keep it in freeze.
