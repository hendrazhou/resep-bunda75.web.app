The ingredients needed to make Caramelized Chicken Wings:
  1. Take 3 pound raw chicken wings
  2. Take 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup soy sauce
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp ketchup
  5. Make ready 1 cup honey
  6. Prepare 1 garlic clove, minced
  7. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  8. Take 1 pinch ground black pepper

Instructions to make Caramelized Chicken Wings:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°
  2. Place chicken in a 9x13-inch DEEP baking dish. Preferably a lasagna dish as the sauce will splatter!
  3. Mix the oil, soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, salt and pepper and pour over chicken
  4. Bake for 1 hour 40 minutes or until sauce is caramelized
