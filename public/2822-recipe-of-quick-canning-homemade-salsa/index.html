The ingredients needed to make Canning Homemade Salsa:
  1. Get 20 each tomatoes
  2. Make ready 4 each onion
  3. Make ready 3 each green bell peppers
  4. Make ready 11 each cayenne peppers
  5. Take 3 each jalapeno peppers
  6. Get 2 tbsp salt
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp ground black pepper
  8. Make ready 3 tbsp sugar
  9. Take 1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp chili powder
  11. Take 5 tbsp cornstarch
  12. Take 1/2 cup vinegar
  13. Make ready 1 can tomato sauce (8 oz.)
  14. Get 2 can tomato paste (6 oz. ea)
  15. Get 1 tbsp garlic powder

Instructions to make Canning Homemade Salsa:
  1. Peel and chop tomatoes, onions, peppers, cayenne and jalapeños (I use a food processor and puree everything)
  2. In a cup of water mix tomato paste and sauce
  3. Combine all ingredients in a large cook pot or canner ( I use an aluminum dish pan)
  4. Bring to a boil for 10- 15 minutes
  5. While salsa is cooking wash and scald 11 pint jars, rings and lids.
  6. When cooking is complete, pour salsa in jars placing a lid and ring on each
  7. Let cool, listening for the tell tale pop of the lid, insuring the jars have sealed.
  8. Wipe off jars, remove rings and store in pantry
  9. Makes about 11 pints
