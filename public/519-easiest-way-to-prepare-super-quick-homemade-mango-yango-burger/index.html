The ingredients needed to make Mango Yango Burger:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Mango
  2. Get 1/4 tsp Ginger powder
  3. Take 1/2 tbsp Cane sugar
  4. Take 1/2 cup Poblano peppers
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Cayenne powder
  6. Prepare pineapples mango mayo
  7. Take 2 tbsp Mango (small dice)
  8. Prepare 2 1/2 tbsp Pineapples (small dice)
  9. Prepare 3/4 cup Mayonnaise
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
