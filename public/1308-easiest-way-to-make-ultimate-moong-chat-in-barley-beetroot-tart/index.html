Instructions to make Moong chat in barley beetroot tart:
  1. For tart:- Take barley flour. Add salt and ghee
  2. Rub ghee well in flour.
  3. Now make beetroot water by adding grated beetroot to 1/2 cup of water followed by boiling,cooling and straining.
  4. Knead dough using beetroot water. Dough should not be too tight.
  5. Now keep aside this dough for 30 minutes.
  6. Now take ball from it.
  7. Roll and paste inside the tart mould.
  8. Bake in preheated microwave for 20 minutes at 180 degree.
  9. Cool and demould.
  10. Tart is ready.
  11. Wash and soak moong for 6 hours.
  12. Boil with salt and turmeric powder and 1/2 cup water for 10 minutes.
  13. Strain extra water.
  14. Add all spices, tomato, potato.
  15. Fill this chat in tart.
  16. Serve by adding namkeen, pomegranate seeds.
  17. Enjoy healthy and tasty breakfast.
