The ingredients needed to make Apple cinnamon bacon wrapped pork shoulder:
  1. Prepare Pork shoulder
  2. Make ready 1-2 packs bacon
  3. Make ready 1 apple
  4. Prepare Half a cup of brown sugar
  5. Make ready 1-2 sticks butter
  6. Prepare Half a teaspoon of cinnamon
  7. Take Clove garlic
  8. Get Salt and pepper

Steps to make Apple cinnamon bacon wrapped pork shoulder:
  1. Pre heat oven to 240…Chop garlic into chunks….Season shoulder with all the seasoning and brown sugar (save some sugar to top off with after you wrap in bacon)….cut slits in the pork shoulder and insert garlic pieces
  2. Chop up half the apple and sprinkle it on the top
  3. Wrap bacon around shoulder
  4. Top with the remaining apple slices
  5. Put in a shallow baking pan
  6. Place butter in pan 1/2 stick one each side
  7. Dust with more brown sugar
  8. Bake low and slow about an hour per pound
  9. Enjoy
