The ingredients needed to make Khatti mithi sabji of guava:
  1. Make ready 250 gms ripe guava
  2. Take 2 tomatoes
  3. Get 1 green chilli
  4. Get 1 small piece of ginger
  5. Get 1 tsp salt
  6. Get 1 tsp red chilli
  7. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric
  8. Make ready 2 tsp dry coriander powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp garam masala
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp curd
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp chopped coriander
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp oil
  13. Get 1 tbsp ghee
  14. Prepare 1 tsp jeera
  15. Get Pinch hing
  16. Take 2 tsp sugar
  17. Prepare 1 cup water

Instructions to make Khatti mithi sabji of guava:
  1. Remove Guava seeds than cut into pieces.
  2. Make paste of tomato chilli and ginger prepare all spices.
  3. Take a pan add ghee oil jeera and hing. Then add paste saute it for 2 minutes then add all spices in it cook well.
  4. Now add curd stir Continues than add chopped guava and 1/2 cup water. Mix well cover the pan cook on medium flame till guava soft.
  5. Now add chopped coriander serve hot with chapati or paratha.
