The ingredients needed to make Nutella Chocolate Brownie In Microwave:
  1. Get 1/4 Cup whole wheat
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  3. Prepare 1/4 Tsp baking powder
  4. Get 2 tbsp coconut sugar
  5. Get 3 tbsp Nutella
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps to make Nutella Chocolate Brownie In Microwave:
  1. Sift flour, baking powder, and cocoa powder 2-3 times. This is a very important step in this brownie I experienced during the trial. While adding Nutella dry flour mixture should be airy otherwise lumps will remain as it is.
  2. Now mix all ingredients in a mixing bowl nicely till Nutella mixed with other ingredients. - Pour this batter in a microwavable bowl, a bigger version of a mug.
  3. Bake for 2-3 minutes. Checks after 1 minutes and then bake for 30 seconds. Check again and bake for another 30 seconds. - Eggless whole wheat Nutella chocolate brownie is ready to serve with your vanilla ice cream or you can have it with coffee in evening snack.
