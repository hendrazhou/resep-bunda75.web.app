Steps to make Tutti fruitti  ice cream:
  1. To make this simple icecream take 500 ml amul cream. And add 1tsp vanilla essence.
  2. Now with blender beat this mixture for 5 to10 minutes.
  3. This cream will double and soft.
  4. After this add condensed milk beat it until it's well blended.
  5. Then we will add tutti fruity and mix all of this together.
  6. Now choose the air tight box. And pour the ice-cream in it. Before closing the box cling wrap the box. So that no ice crystals formed. Freeze the box overnight.
  7. Delicious icecream is ready.
