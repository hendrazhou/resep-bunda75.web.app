The ingredients needed to make Barley-Banana porridge:
  1. Get 3 Bananas , ripened , mashed
  2. Take 1/3 Cup barley grains , soaked
  3. Make ready 1/2 Tablespoon brown sugar
  4. Get 1 Pinch cinnamon powder
  5. Make ready 1/3 Cup milk (or nut or soya , if you prefer)
  6. Get 1 Tablespoon cranberries , frozen (or dried)
  7. Get 3 walnuts , chopped

Steps to make Barley-Banana porridge:
  1. Wash and soak the barley in fresh water for about half an hour.
  2. In a blender, add milk, sugar, bananas, cranberries, cinnamon powder, and make a paste (It doesn't have to be smooth, and there should be some cranberry bits that are visible)
  3. Boil the barley (with very little water), and about 3-4 minutes after the water boils, add the banana paste. cover and boil for about 5 minutes, or till barley is cooked. Note that it will be slightly chewy.
  4. Garnish with walnuts and serve.
