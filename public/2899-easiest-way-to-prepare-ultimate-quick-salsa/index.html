The ingredients needed to make Quick Salsa:
  1. Take 2 Jalapenos, seeded
  2. Take 1 medium onion
  3. Take 2 garlic cloves
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  5. Get 1 (14.5 oz) rotel
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup cilantro
  7. Prepare 1 tsp lime juice (optional)

Steps to make Quick Salsa:
  1. Rinse and seed jalapenos. Then mince.
  2. Peel onion and garlic. Dice onion and mince garlic.
  3. Rinse and chop cilantro.
  4. In bowl add pepper, garlic, onion, cilantro, vinegar, juice, and rotel. Stir together then chill until ready to serve.
  5. For more spice use serrano peppers in place of jalapenos.
  6. For chunky style add 2-3 diced roma tomatoes
  7. Lastly if you like more of lime flavor cut the vinegar and use juice from 1/2 lime instead.
