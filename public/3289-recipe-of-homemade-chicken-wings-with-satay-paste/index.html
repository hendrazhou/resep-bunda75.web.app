The ingredients needed to make Chicken wings with satay paste:
  1. Prepare 8 mid-joint chicken wings
  2. Prepare 1 red chilli (sliced)
  3. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  4. Get 1 union (cut into wedges)
  5. Take 1 stalk spring union (sectioned)
  6. Take 1 a little potatostarch mixture for thickening
  7. Get 2 potato (cut into wedges)
  8. Take 2 tbsp satay paste
  9. Get 250 ml swanson clear chicken broth

Instructions to make Chicken wings with satay paste:
