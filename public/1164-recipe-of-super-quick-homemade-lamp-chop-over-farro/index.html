Steps to make Lamp chop over Farro:
  1. Cool pearl barley in water for 30 minutes (put grain in the water right away)
  2. Wait til boil and then lower the heat
  3. When chewy enough, transfer to bowl
  4. Saute the onion, add to farro
  5. Pour olive oil into saute pan, wait til oil is hot, add lamb chops and garlic
  6. Chop herbs, add to chops, flip after 3 minutes
  7. Take chops off, add 1/4 cup water to pan, 2 lemons of lemon juice
  8. Add herbs, tomatoes, cucumbers and italian dressing to farro
  9. Pour lemon juice mix on chops
  10. Add lemon zest of 1 lemon to chops
  11. Put chops ontop of farro

Serve with lemon wedges, if desired.
