The ingredients needed to make Rhubarb strawberry custard pie:
  1. Take 3 cups rhubarb sliced
  2. Get 1 c strawberries sliced
  3. Make ready 1/4 c all-purpose flour
  4. Make ready 1 c sugar
  5. Take 1/4 t salt
  6. Make ready 3 eggs beaten until frothy
  7. Prepare 1/2 t vanilla
  8. Prepare Crust for 2 layer pie:
  9. Take 2 c flour, 1 t salt, 3/4 c Crisco, 5-8 T cold water
  10. Prepare Refrigerate for 1/2 hr. Roll out

Fold into fourths and cut slits so steam can escape. Place pastry over filling and unfold. ALWAYS use fresh rhubarb just cut in backyard. Frozen fruit and custard do not work together.

Steps to make Rhubarb strawberry custard pie:
  1. In bowl combine sugar, flour & salt. Add rhubarb & strawberries, stir to coat. Spread in tin. Beat eggs & vanilla, pour over filling. Top with pastry
  2. Bake at 425° - 15 min, 350° - 30 min until knife is clear 1 in from center.
  3. Let cool on rack.

ALWAYS use fresh rhubarb just cut in backyard. Frozen fruit and custard do not work together. Frozen fruit will prevent the custard from setting up even if the fruit is drained.
