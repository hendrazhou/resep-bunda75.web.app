The ingredients needed to make Watermelon Cucumber Smoothie:
  1. Prepare 1 cup cold chopped watermelon
  2. Get 1/2 cup cold chopped cucumber
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons lime juice
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon milk flavour
  5. Make ready Small ginger
  6. Prepare Dessicated coconut as toppings
