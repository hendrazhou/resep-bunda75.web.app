The ingredients needed to make Fruits custard:
  1. Make ready 600 ml full cream milk
  2. Get 3 tbs vanilla custard powder
  3. Prepare 75 gm Sugar
  4. Take as needed mix dry fruit (chopped cashew and almond)
  5. Get 1/2 cup pomegranate
  6. Take 1/2 cup mango cubes
  7. Get 1/3 cup tutti frutti
  8. Make ready 3 tsp milk cream

Instructions to make Fruits custard:
  1. Take a cup or bowl add about 100ml milk then add 3tbs custard powder and make slury
  2. Then take a pan or any deep vessel then add left milk warm and then add sugar and heat till sugar dissolve when sugar dissolve completely add slurry little by little and continuously stir to avoid any lump
  3. Continuously stir and cook after that add cream
  4. Cook till it becomes runny thick or leaving big bubbles it will take about 8-10mins
  5. Now off the flame and add 2tbsp milk and mix them pour them in air tight container and leave to cool at room temperature for 15mins then put into freezer for 1hr.
  6. Then take out custard and add all fruits and mix it again put into freezer for 3-4hrs
