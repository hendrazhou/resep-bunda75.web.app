The ingredients needed to make Cretan Ntakos:
  1. Take 4 ntakos (barley rusks)
  2. Get 1 large tomato
  3. Take 8 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  4. Prepare 12 kalamata olives
  5. Take 4 pinch dried oregano
  6. Make ready 120 grams feta cheese (crumbled)

Ntákos, otherwise known as" Koukouvágia" (owl), is a traditional Cretan Food based on barley rusk. On the rusk there is rubbed tomato and the Cretan cream cheese "myzíthra" while the dish is. Ntakos (Cretan Tomato & Feta Rusks). Traditional Cretan ntakos: a rusk sandwich toast topped with the freshest ingredients such as tomatoes, feta cheese, capers and olives! yummy!

Steps to make Cretan Ntakos:
  1. Ntakos should be moist so it needs to be immersed in water for a couple of minutes on both sides.
  2. Chop the tomatos into small cubes and divide on all four rusks.
  3. Sprinkle one teaspoon of extra virgin olive oil on the chopped tomatos. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese.
  4. Chop three olives for each rusk and sprinkle them over the chopped tomatos.
  5. And finally, before enjoying the ntakos with a glass of super cool Moschofilero or some tsipouro, you can sprinkle some dried (or fresh) oregano.
