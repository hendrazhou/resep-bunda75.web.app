The ingredients needed to make Nutella Frosting:
  1. Get 1/3 stick butter
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup heavy cream
  3. Make ready 1 cup Nutella

Steps to make Nutella Frosting:
  1. combine Nutella, butter and cream in medium sauce pan
  2. let mixture come to a simmer for about a minute
  3. shouldn't be too runny. add more Nutella if needed
  4. stir thoroughly and pour on cake
  5. after frosting is on cake, let cool and enjoy!
