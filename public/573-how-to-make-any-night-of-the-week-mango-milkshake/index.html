Instructions to make Mango Milkshake:
  1. Peel of the mango skin
  2. Cut the mango in slices
  3. Add sugar (2 tablespoons or more as you like)
  4. Blend the mango and sugar together
  5. Then add milk and blend it
  6. You can add icecubes or vanilla icecream if you'd like
  7. Bon Apetit!
