The ingredients needed to make Cheese Garlic Bread:
  1. Get 4-5 slice readymade bread
  2. Get 1 Capsicum (chopped)
  3. Get 1 tomato (chopped)
  4. Prepare 1 tsp butter
  5. Take 1 tsp corn flour powder
  6. Get 1 cup milk
  7. Prepare 1 tsp red chili flakes
  8. Take 1 tsp origano
  9. Make ready 7-8 pcs garlic (fine chopped)
  10. Take 2 teaspoon mayonnaise sauce
  11. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  12. Prepare 1/3 tsp salt as per taste
  13. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  14. Get 100 gm cheese

Steps to make Cheese Garlic Bread:
  1. For garlic bread assemble all the ingredients together.
  2. Cut the bread slices into 2 pieces.
  3. In a pan, heat butter and saute garlic.
  4. In a mug take cold milk, add corn flour, black pepper, and mix well. Add to the sauteed garlic.
  5. When the batter turns slightly thick add, mayonnaise.
  6. Season with chili flakes and oregano.
  7. Now add capsicum, tomato and salt. If the batter id thick enough you can switch off the flame.
  8. Spread the prepared mixture on the bread pieces, season with chili flakes and oregano and sprinkle some cheese.
  9. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees for 15 minutes.Bake for 10-15 minutes.
  10. After 10-15 minutes, it will the cheese will be completed melted and baked.
  11. Homemade cheese garlic bread is ready.
  12. Serve with tomato sauce.
