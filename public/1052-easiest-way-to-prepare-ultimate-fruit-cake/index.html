The ingredients needed to make Fruit cake:
  1. Prepare 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Make ready 4 tbsp mango puree
  3. Get 4 tbsp milk
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp Baking soda
  5. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  6. Prepare 1 cup powder sugar
  7. Take 2 tbsp oil
  8. Take As needed tutti frutti
  9. Take 3-4 drops vanilla extract

Steps to make Fruit cake:
  1. Take a bowl add all-purpose flour, mango puree, sugar,oil,baking powder,baking soda,milk and mix it and make the batter
  2. Add tuti fruti and mix it
  3. Now take a microwave bowl and grease it
  4. Add batter and tap it and microwave it for 4 minutes
