The ingredients needed to make Vazhaithandu(Stem Banana) Gravy:
  1. Get 1 stem banana
  2. Take 1 tsp tamarind paste
  3. Prepare 1 tsp dhaniya powder,
  4. Take 1 tsp red chilli powder
  5. Take 2 tsp mustard
  6. Take 2 tsp urad dal
  7. Take 1 cup grated coconnut
  8. Take 1 tsp perungayam
  9. Take 1 tsp salt, cooking oil
  10. Take 1 bunch curry leaves
