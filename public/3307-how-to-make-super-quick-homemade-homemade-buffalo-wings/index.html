Instructions to make Homemade Buffalo wings:
  1. Season the wings with salt, black pepper and secret spice
  2. Heat up vegetable oil for frying in a deep pan and deep fry the wings for about 8-10 minutes
  3. When it’s done, take it out of the oil and set aside
  4. Heat up equal amounts of ketchup and chili sauce in olive oil and pour wings back inside
  5. Toss till wings are evenly coated in the sauce
  6. Take out of the pan and sprinkle some sesame seeds on it.
  7. Serve.!
