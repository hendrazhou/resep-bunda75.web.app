The ingredients needed to make Parmesan Garlic Bread Bites:
  1. Prepare 1 Package Biscuits Pillsbury Grands
  2. Get 1/2 Butter Stick
  3. Get 1 Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  4. Prepare 1/2 Cup Parmesan Cheese Shredded
  5. Get 1 Teaspoon Garlic Salt

Steps to make Parmesan Garlic Bread Bites:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Place stick of butter in a bundt cake pan to melt inside the oven while preheating.
  2. Combine italian seasoning, parmesan cheese, and garlic salt in a bowl.
  3. Pull apart small pieces of the biscuit dough and coat with the seasoning mix.
  4. Place the pieces of dough in the bundt cake pan. Sprinkle the top with the remaining seasoning mixture.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes.
  6. Pull apart to eat.
