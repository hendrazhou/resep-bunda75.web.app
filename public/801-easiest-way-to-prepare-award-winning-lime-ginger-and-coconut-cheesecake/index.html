The ingredients needed to make Lime, ginger and coconut cheesecake:
  1. Get 2 Lime, zest and juice
  2. Get 1 tsp Fresh grated ginger
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Grated coconut
  4. Make ready 100 grams Coffee cookies
  5. Prepare 30 grams Butter
  6. Take 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  7. Prepare 50 grams Fine sugar
  8. Get 200 grams Quark (fresh soft curd cheese)
  9. Make ready 1 1/2 Gelatins
  10. Take 50 ml Double cream
