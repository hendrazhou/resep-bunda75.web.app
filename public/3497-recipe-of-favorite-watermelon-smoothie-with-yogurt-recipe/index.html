The ingredients needed to make Watermelon Smoothie with Yogurt Recipe:
  1. Take 2 cups Watermelon, peeled, deseeded and chopped
  2. Take 1/2 cup Low Fat or Full Fat Plain Yogurt (vanilla)
  3. Prepare 1/2 large Banana, peeled and diced
  4. Take 4-5 fresh Mint Leaves, optional

Steps to make Watermelon Smoothie with Yogurt Recipe:
  1. Remove skin and black seeds from watermelon and cut into large pieces.
  2. Add plain yogurt in a blender jar.
  3. Add watermelon, banana and mint leaves in it.
  4. Blend until smooth puree. Pour it into a serving glass and serve.
