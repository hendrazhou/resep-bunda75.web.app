The ingredients needed to make Sweet Yet Spicy Guava Bbq Wings:
  1. Prepare 2 lb chicken wings
  2. Make ready adobo
  3. Make ready 3/4 cup guava jelly
  4. Take 1/4 cup honey bbq sauce
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp garlic powder
  6. Get 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  7. Take 2 dash oil (for searing)

Instructions to make Sweet Yet Spicy Guava Bbq Wings:
  1. Season chicken with the adobo.
  2. In a bowl mix the guava jelly, bbq sauce, garlic and pepper flakes.
  3. Sear the chiken wings to your desire crispness.
  4. Set oven to 350 and place the seared chicken wings on a baking dish and pour over the guava sauce, cover and bake them for an hour.
