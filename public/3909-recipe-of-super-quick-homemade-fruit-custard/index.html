The ingredients needed to make Fruit custard:
  1. Prepare 1/2 litre milk
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp custard powder
  3. Get 2 tbsp sugar
  4. Take 1 bowl mix fruits chopped
  5. Make ready As needed dry fruits

Instructions to make Fruit custard:
  1. Boil milk in a heavy bottomed pan
  2. Mix custard powder in separate mug with 2 tbsp of milk
  3. Mix custard mixture in boiled milk and keep stirring
  4. Keep on low flame for 10 minutes and keep stirring and add some 1 pinch saffron for nice colour and flavour
  5. Remove from flame and let it cool to room temperature.
  6. Add cut fruits and keep in refrigerator for 2-3 hours.serve cold
