The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin seeds and Pineapple Smoothie:
  1. Get 1/2 bowl Soaked Pumpkin seeds
  2. Get 12 slice pineapple
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 Juice of lemon
  5. Take 1/4 tbsp cinnamon powder
  6. Prepare 3-4 pinch black salt
  7. Prepare 24 ice cubes
  8. Prepare Dry pumpkin seeds for garnishing
  9. Prepare 2 Slices lemon for garnishing

Steps to make Pumpkin seeds and Pineapple Smoothie:
  1. Soak pumpkin seeds in water for 8 hours
  2. Remove the middle part of the pineapple and cut it into small pieces.
  3. After this, put chopped pineapple in the jar of the grinder and add 12 ice cubes.
  4. Put lemon juice in it
  5. After this, add soaked pumpkin seeds.
  6. Add 3 to 4 pinch cinnamon powder in it.
  7. Grind all these into the grinder.After this put sugar in it.
  8. After that put black salt in it. Add 12 ice cubes in it.Grind it into grinder again.
  9. Tasty and healthy pumpkin seeds and Pineapple smoothie is ready.Garnish it with cinnamon powder,dry pumpkin seeds and lemon slices.
