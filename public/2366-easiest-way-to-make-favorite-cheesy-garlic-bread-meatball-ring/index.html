The ingredients needed to make Cheesy garlic bread meatball ring:
  1. Make ready 1 batch pizza dough
  2. Make ready Frozen meatballs
  3. Prepare 1 cup marinara sauce
  4. Take 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  5. Take 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  6. Take Extra marinara for dipping
  7. Take Italian seasoning
  8. Prepare Garlic salt or garlic powder

Instructions to make Cheesy garlic bread meatball ring:
  1. Heat up meatballs, as many as you like. Using bag instructions.
  2. Preheat oven to 350°
  3. Pinch off a ball of the mixture, roll between your hands to make ping pong ball-sized meatball.
  4. With your hands or a rolling pin, flatten out the pizza dough rounds and top with a tablespoon of marinara and mozzarella cheese. (doesn’t have to be perfect)
  5. Place one meatball over the dough and wrap the dough over the meatball pinching at the seams to close. - - Place the balls in a circle, seam-side down, in a cast-iron skillet. - - Mix the melted butter with garlic, salt, and Italian seasoning. Brush the balls with the garlic butter mixture.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes, or until golden brown. Let cool for 5-10 minutes. - - Place a bowl of warm marinara sauce in the middle of the skillet. Sprinkle the balls with parsley. - - Enjoy!
