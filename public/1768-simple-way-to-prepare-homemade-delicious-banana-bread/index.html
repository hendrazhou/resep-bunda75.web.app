The ingredients needed to make Delicious banana bread:
  1. Prepare 2-3 overripe bananas
  2. Take 1/3 cup butter
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon salt
  5. Get 3/4 cups brown sugar (or regular sugar)
  6. Take 1 large egg
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. Make ready 1 1/2 cup flour all purpose
  9. Make ready Optional things that make your banana bread extra special:
  10. Make ready 1 cup cooking oats
  11. Prepare 1/3-1/2 cup vanilla Greek yogurt
  12. Prepare 1 bit of honey
  13. Make ready Cinnamon

Instructions to make Delicious banana bread:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients together. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mash bananas in separate bowl
  3. Mix all ingredients together
  4. Pour mixture into a greased loaf pan.
  5. Bake for 45 or more
  6. Let cool
  7. Enjoy!
