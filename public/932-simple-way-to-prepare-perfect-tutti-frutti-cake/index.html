Steps to make Tutti Frutti Cake:
  1. Coat tutti-fruti with 1 teaspoon maida.
  2. Beat butter and sugar
  3. When butter become light in colour, add milk powder, milk, pineapple essence and beat again.
  4. Add maida, baking powder, baking soda. Mix gently.
  5. Add tutti-frutti and mix gently.
  6. Pour batter in a greased cake tin
  7. Bake in preheated oven 180 degree C for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Insert a toothpick at the center of the cake, if toothpick comes out clean then your cake is ready, or else bake it for 1 more minute. Allow it to cool down completely
  9. Delicious and fluffy 'Tutti Frutti Cake' is ready.
