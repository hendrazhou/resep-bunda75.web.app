The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Waffles:
  1. Get 2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Take 4 teaspoons Baking powder
  3. Make ready 2 teaspoon cinnamon
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Make ready 5 table spoons sugar
  6. Take 100 g unsalted butter (room temperature, chopped)
  7. Prepare 1 and 1/2 cup warm milk
  8. Take 2 eggs
  9. Make ready 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps to make Cinnamon Waffles:
  1. Sift together flour, baking powder, salt and cinnamon. Whisk in the sugar.
  2. Put the butter into the warm milk, it should melt in effortlessly. If not warm the milk a bit.
  3. Be sure that the butter and milk mixture is cool before adding in the eggs and vanilla extract. Whisk to combine.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients bowl, combine well and leave to stand as you prepare the waffle pan.
  5. Heat the waffle pan and grease it. Pour a quarter cup of the batter into the middle section of the pan, allowing it to spread evenly.
  6. Close the waffle pan, this will help to spread batter out evenly.
  7. Cook each side for about a minute if using a cooker top waffle pan.
  8. Serve warm with your favourite toppings. For us it's always butter and honey
