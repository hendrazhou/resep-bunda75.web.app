Steps to make Nacho cheese Dorito casserole:
  1. Heat oil in small skillet. Add shallot and garlic. Cook about 2 minutes, until fragrant.
  2. Combine soup and milk in LG bowl.
  3. Add chicken, shallot garlic mix, seasoning, corn, beans, queso, rotel, sour cream, 1/2 shredded cheese to soup mix. Mix evenly.
  4. Combine both flavors of doritos in LG ziplock bag and crush, using your hands or rolling pin.
  5. Preheat oven to 350.
  6. Spray LG casserole dish with cooking spray.
  7. Spread 1/2 crushed doritos on bottom of greased dish. Add bean, corn, cheese mixture ontop. Top with other half of crushed doritos and the other half of cheese.
  8. Bake for 30 minutes.
  9. Top with whatever toppings you'd like and enjoy!
  10. Remove from oven. Let cool for 5-10 minutes.
