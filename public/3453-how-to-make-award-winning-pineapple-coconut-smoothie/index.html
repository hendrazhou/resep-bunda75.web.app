The ingredients needed to make Pineapple Coconut Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  2. Get 1/2 cup coconut milk
  3. Take 1 tbls Greek yogurt
  4. Prepare 1 tsp honey
  5. Get Pineapple chunk for garnish
  6. Prepare Ice as required

Instructions to make Pineapple Coconut Smoothie:
  1. Blend pineapple juice with coconut milk and honey till Combine.
  2. Add Greek yogurt and ice in last and pulse the blender just two times.
  3. Pour this tasty and healthy Pineapple coconut smoothie in Serving glass and garnish with pineapple chunk.
