The ingredients needed to make Healthy Strawberry Banana Avocado Smoothie:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Strawberries
  2. Make ready 1 Banana (I prefer a frozen one)
  3. Prepare 1/2 Avocado
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup Vanilla Yogurt
  5. Make ready 1/3 cup Skim or Soy milk
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Vanilla extract (optional)
