The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian buffalo wings spinach wrap:
  1. Make ready 1 box Morningstar buffalo wings
  2. Prepare 1 packages spinach tortillas
  3. Get 2 each red bell peppers - sliced thick and diagonal
  4. Prepare 2 each green bell peppers - sliced thick and diagonal
  5. Make ready 6 each mushrooms - diced
  6. Prepare 3 each Roma tomato - diced
  7. Take 1 packages sour cream (squeezable package)
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp White sesame
  9. Take 1 tbsp black sesame
  10. Prepare 1/2 each lime
  11. Get 1 tbsp sugar
  12. Prepare to taste ground black pepper and salt
  13. Prepare 1 cup chopped cilantro

Steps to make Vegetarian buffalo wings spinach wrap:
  1. Toast buffalo wings
  2. In pan, add 2 tbls grapeseed oil or any oil. Add bell peppers, tomatoes, mushrooms, sauteed, add a little salt and ground pepper to taste.
  3. In small bowl, squeeze lime juice, add sugar, ground pepper, white and black sesame, mix well for the sauce.
  4. By now, buffalo wings should be toasted. Place wings in a line on the tortilla, add sauteed vegetables, add sour cream, sesame dressing or sauce on top, add cilantro. Roll by folding both sudes, then roll forward like a burrito.
