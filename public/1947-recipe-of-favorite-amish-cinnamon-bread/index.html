The ingredients needed to make Amish Cinnamon Bread:
  1. Get For the batter :
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup butter - softened
  3. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 1 egg
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  6. Get 1 cup buttermilk or 1 cup milk plus 1 Tablespoon vinegar
  7. Prepare 2 cup flour
  8. Get 1 teaspoon baking soda
  9. Prepare Cinnamon sugar:
  10. Get 1/3 cup sugar
  11. Prepare 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps to make Amish Cinnamon Bread:
  1. Cream butter , sugar, vanilla extract, egg. Add milk , flour, baking soda mix well.
  2. Pour 1/2 of the batter into a greased loaf pan. In a separate bowl. Mix 1/3 cup sugar and 1 teaspoon cinnamon. To create the cinnamon sugar mixture.
  3. Sprinkle 3/4 of cinnamon sugar mixture onto the 1/2 full pan. Add remaining batter to pan. Sprinkle with rest of cinnamon sugar on top.
  4. Swirl batter using a knife. Bake at 350º for 50-60 minutes or until toothpick inserted comes out clean. Let cool in pan for about 20 minutes before removing from pan.
