The ingredients needed to make Barley Honey Lemon Drink:
  1. Take 2 cup barley
  2. Make ready 2 lemon rind
  3. Take 1 1/2 liter water
  4. Prepare SERVING
  5. Get 2 tbsp lemon juice
  6. Prepare 2 ice cube
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp honey
