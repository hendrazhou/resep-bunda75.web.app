The ingredients needed to make Mike's EZ Strawberry Jalapeño Salsa:
  1. Make ready Strawberry Jalapeno Salsa
  2. Take 2 cup Firm Fresh Strawberries [chopped]
  3. Make ready 1 large Fresh Jalapeño Pepper [de-seeded - small chop - i actually use 2]
  4. Get 3/4 cup Firm Diced White Onions
  5. Take 1/2 cup Loosely Packed Fresh Cilantro [chopped]
  6. Make ready 1 medium Lime [juiced - about 1 tablespoon or less + zest]
  7. Prepare Fresh Cracked Black Pepper [to taste]
  8. Take Cracked Sea Salt [to taste]
  9. Get Bagels
  10. Prepare Onion Bagels [lightly toasted]
  11. Take Philadelphia Cream Cheese
  12. Get Options
  13. Make ready Quality Strawberry Jam
  14. Get Or, A Heavy Bread Of Your Choosing

Steps to make Mike's EZ Strawberry Jalapeño Salsa:
  1. Chop and gently mix all ingredients in the Salsa Section. Taste test lime juice at 1/2 tablespoon - then add more as you desire. If your vegetables aren't already chilled - allow salsa to sit for 1/2 hour in the fridge.
  2. Lightly toast bagels and smear cream cheese to taste. Add a quality Strawberry Jam if desired. Or, alow your guests to decide and serve salsa to the side.
  3. Top warmed, cream cheese bagels with Strawberry Salsa.
  4. Feel free to garnish your plates as desired. Featured is a simple plate garnished with dried parsley and various fruits, fresh cilantro and jalapeño. Enjoy!
