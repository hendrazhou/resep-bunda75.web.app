The ingredients needed to make Pecan Cinnamon Shortbread Bars:
  1. Prepare bars
  2. Prepare 2 stick salted butter, 8 ounces total
  3. Get 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  4. Make ready 3 tbsp brown sugar
  5. Get 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Make ready 2 cup all-purpose flour
  8. Prepare 1 1/4 cup finely chopped pecans
  9. Take 2 tbsp brown sugar, for final topping

Instructions to make Pecan Cinnamon Shortbread Bars:
  1. Cream butter and sugars.
  2. Mix in vanilla and cinnamon.
  3. Mix in flour until just incorporated then gently fold in pecans.
  4. Press dough into a well buttered 8"x8" baking pan.
  5. Cook in a preheated 350°F fahrenheit oven for 50 minutes or until lightly golden brown.
  6. Remove from oven, score top with a sharp knife into 9 sections.
  7. Sprinkle with the 2 tablespoons of brown sugar.
  8. Let come to room temperature, then cut through on scored lines. Chill for 30 minutes before serving.
