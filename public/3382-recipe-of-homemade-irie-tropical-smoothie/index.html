The ingredients needed to make Irie Tropical Smoothie:
  1. Take 1 large Sweet Orange, peeled and chopped
  2. Make ready 1 large Ripe Mango (the more ripe the sweeter), chopped
  3. Prepare 3/4 can Crushed Pineapples with juice (can sub fresh pineapple)
  4. Get 1/2 Apple (I used red), chopped
  5. Prepare 2 Carrots, chopped
  6. Get 2 tbsp Cranberry (No Sugar Added) Juice
  7. Take 1 tsp Organic Coconut Oil
  8. Prepare 1 Ice (the more the fresher, the less the sweeter)
  9. Make ready 1 Lime, 1 squirt (Optional)
