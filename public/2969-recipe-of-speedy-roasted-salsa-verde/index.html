The ingredients needed to make Roasted salsa verde:
  1. Prepare 3 lb tamatillo
  2. Make ready 1 large white onion
  3. Prepare 2 green bell peppers seeded
  4. Prepare 3 jalapeños seeded
  5. Take 5 clove garlic
  6. Get cilantro
  7. Make ready salt pepper
  8. Make ready 1 oil spray

Instructions to make Roasted salsa verde:
  1. Husk and clean tamatillos. Chop onion , slice jalapeño, half bell pepper , leave garlic whole. Place all ingredients on a baking sheet lined with tin foil. Easy on clean up.
  2. Spray oil over the top of vegetables and cook in a 500° oven . They should start to char around 20 to 25 minutes.
  3. Put all ingredients from the pan , including the juices, in a blender of some sort. Add the cilantro and salt pepper
  4. Serve over chicken enchiladas or just have it by itself with chips.
