The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon bacon and eggs:
  1. Make ready 3 oz bacon
  2. Prepare 3 large eggs
  3. Get 1 salt
  4. Make ready 1 ground black pepper
  5. Get 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp water
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp butter

Instructions to make Cinnamon bacon and eggs:
  1. In a cold pan add bacon. Then heat. sprinkle top side of bacon pieces with half the cinnamon. When a bit crisp turn and do the same on the other side. Fry till crispy
  2. Add butter to a heated pan let it melt. Add the eggs, don't break the yokes. Now add salt and pepper. Cover and add water to steam cook the top of egg whites, just a few seconds. The yokes should still be runny.
  3. Add to a plate, add bacon serve I hope you enjoy!
