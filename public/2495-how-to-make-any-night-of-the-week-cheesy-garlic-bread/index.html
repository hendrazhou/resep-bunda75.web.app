The ingredients needed to make Cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Take 8 to 10 Baguette – sliced
  2. Make ready 75 gms Butter
  3. Make ready – 8 cloves Garlic crushed
  4. Get ¾ cup Mozzarella cheese – (grated)
  5. Take Oregano – for sprinkle

Instructions to make Cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Pre heat the oven at 180 degree centigrade.
  2. Place the baguette slices on a tray in a row.
  3. Broil it for 2 minutes.
  4. In a pan melt the butter, add crushed garlic and simmer for 2 minutes.
  5. Apply generously the garlic butter on baguette slices, top the slices with grated mozzarella cheese and sprinkle oregano on it.
  6. Broil it for another 5 to 7 minutes at 180 degree centigrade.
  7. Crispy, yummy cheesy garlic bread is ready to serve…..
