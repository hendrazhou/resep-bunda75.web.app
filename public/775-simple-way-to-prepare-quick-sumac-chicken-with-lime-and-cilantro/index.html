The ingredients needed to make Sumac Chicken with Lime and Cilantro:
  1. Make ready 8 large boneless skinless chicken thighs
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup olive oil, extra virgin
  3. Make ready 3/4 tsp salt
  4. Get 1 tbsp ground sumac
  5. Take 1/4 tsp ceylon cinnamon
  6. Get 1 tsp crumbled dry mint
  7. Make ready 1 pinch saffron
  8. Make ready 1 tsp fennel seeds
  9. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground allspice
  10. Get 1/4 tsp ground cayenne
  11. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  12. Prepare 1 zest of lime
  13. Take 1/2 lime, juice of
  14. Prepare 1 bunch cilantro, chopped

Steps to make Sumac Chicken with Lime and Cilantro:
  1. In a medium-sized bowl, mix together the sumac, cinnamon, mint, salt, saffron, fennel, allspice, cumin, and cayenne.
  2. Wash and pat dry the chicken thighs, then toss them into the mix, and then mix thoroughly.
  3. Heat on medium the oil in a 3qt sauce pan.
  4. Place the chicken, about 3 at a time, smooth side down into the oil.
  5. Cook about 6 minutes, and, when easily loosened with a spatula, flip.
  6. Sprinkle a little extra sumac on the tops of the chicken while it cooks for another six minutes.
  7. Flip them again, and sear the new sumac in for about a minute before placing on a dish to rest.
  8. Repeat until all chicken is cooked.
  9. Shred zest of lime on the chicken, then toss on some cilantro, then sprinkle with the lime juice, and then serve and enjoy.
