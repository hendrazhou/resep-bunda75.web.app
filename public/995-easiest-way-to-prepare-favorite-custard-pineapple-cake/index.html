The ingredients needed to make Custard pineapple cake:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup flour
  2. Take 1 cup powered sugar
  3. Take 1/2 tsp custard powder
  4. Take 200 gms pineapple cubes (I used delmonte pineapple)
  5. Take 1/2 butter
  6. Prepare 1 cup milk
  7. Take 1/2 tbsp baking soda
  8. Take 1 tbsp baking powder
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp tutti frutti
  10. Make ready 40 gms almond flakes

Steps to make Custard pineapple cake:
  1. Take a container.
  2. Add flour, sugar, custard powder, butter, and add milk gradually.
  3. Now add baking soda, pineapple cubes, tutti fruitti. Mix well.
  4. The cake batter is ready.
  5. Grease the mould with butter.
  6. Pour the batter.
  7. Add almond flakes.
  8. Tap it. Place a stand inside the pan. Preheat the pan for 5 minutes on low flame.
  9. Place the mould.
  10. Bake for 35/40.
  11. Insert tooth pick. It comes clean.
  12. This means cake is baked completely
  13. Demould the cake into the plate.
