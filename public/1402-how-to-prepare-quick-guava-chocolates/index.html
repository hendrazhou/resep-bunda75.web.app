The ingredients needed to make Guava chocolates:
  1. Get 1 cup dark chocolate
  2. Get 2 tsp Oil
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup guava crush

Instructions to make Guava chocolates:
  1. Melt the dark chocolate with double boiler method.
  2. Add oil and mix.
  3. Take mould fill the chocolate half then put guava crush and fill remaining with chocolate.Tap it and keep in fridge for hour and guava chocolates ready to enjoy.
