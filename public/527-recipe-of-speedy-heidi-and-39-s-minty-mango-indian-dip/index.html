Steps to make Heidi's minty mango Indian dip:
  1. In a breakfast bowl add the sour cream, chutney and mint
  2. Mix thoroughly and place into a nice bowl, sprinkling the top with more dried mint
  3. Serve with puppadums and enjoy with an Indian feast
  4. For the puppadums, place four on a plate, spray with olive oil and microwave for 1 min and transfer to a large bowl or platter. Continue until you have desired amount
