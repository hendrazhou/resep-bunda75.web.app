The ingredients needed to make Guava chutney(amrood ki chutney):
  1. Get 250 gm Guava
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup Coriander chopped
  3. Prepare 2 Green chilli
  4. Get 1 tablespoon Lemon juice
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Rock salt
  6. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Plain salt
  7. Get 1 teaspoon Roasted cumin powder
  8. Prepare 1" Ginger
  9. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder

Steps to make Guava chutney(amrood ki chutney):
  1. Take guava and cut it lengthwise. Check for bugs also
  2. Now remove seeds from them and cut into small pieces.
  3. Cut Ginger also.
  4. Now take a blender jar and put all the ingredients in it.
  5. Blend it in smooth paste. Take it In a vessel.
  6. Your yummy guava chutney is ready to serve.
