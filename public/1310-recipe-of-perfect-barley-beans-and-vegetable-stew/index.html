The ingredients needed to make Barley, Beans and Vegetable Stew:
  1. Prepare 1/4 cup Barley, whole
  2. Get 1/4 cup green gram, whole (mung beans)
  3. Prepare 2 tsp garlic julienne
  4. Take 2 tsp ginger julienne
  5. Prepare 1 cup carrot, chopped
  6. Get 2 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped
  7. Take 1 onion, chopped
  8. Take 1 tomato, chopped
  9. Get 2 green chillis, chopped
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp pepper powder
  11. Take 1/4 tsp cumin powder
  12. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  13. Prepare as needed Salt
  14. Take 2 tbsp ghee
  15. Prepare 2 tsp lemon juice

Instructions to make Barley, Beans and Vegetable Stew:
  1. Wash and soak barley and green gram in sufficient water for an hour.
  2. Heat a pressure cooker. Add a tablespoon of ghee. Add ginger and garlic, sautee for a minute.
  3. Add chopped green chilli and onion, cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add chopped green chilli and onion, cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Now add carrots, tomato, all ground spices and salt. mix up well.
  6. Add about 4 cups of water and pressure cook for 4-5 whistles.
  7. Once the pressure is released add coriander leaves and lemon juice. Serve hot by adding another tablespoon of ghee over it.
