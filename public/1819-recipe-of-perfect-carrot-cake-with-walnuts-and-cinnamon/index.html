The ingredients needed to make Carrot Cake with Walnuts and Cinnamon:
  1. Get 2 eggs
  2. Take 150 g sugar
  3. Take 200 g flour
  4. Make ready 2 carrots
  5. Get 50 g butter
  6. Make ready 50 g walnuts
  7. Make ready 1 coffee spoon of olive oil
  8. Make ready 1 coffee spoon of baking powder
  9. Make ready Cinnamon
  10. Get Vanilla
  11. Prepare (You can also add raisins)

Steps to make Carrot Cake with Walnuts and Cinnamon:
  1. Mix eggs with sugar. Add melted butter and olive oil.
  2. Grate carrots. Crush walnuts in small pieces. Add everything to the butter and mix well.
  3. Add cinnamon, vanilla add flour with baking powder. Should be a consistency of thick sour cream.
  4. Cover baking pan with parchment paper, if you don’t have parchment paper you can cover pan with butter. Purr the butter in. Bake 30min in the 200°C oven.
  5. Decorate with sugar powder
