The ingredients needed to make Aam Panna (Raw Mango Drink):
  1. Take 1 raw mango
  2. Get to taste black salt
  3. Take 1/2 tsp. roasted cumin powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp. ground pepper
  5. Make ready to taste sugar
  6. Take pinch chaat masala
  7. Take 8-10 mint leaves

Steps to make Aam Panna (Raw Mango Drink):
  1. Pressure cook the mango for 2-3 whistles. When cool, remove the pulp and keep aside. Mix together with 3 glasses of water and rest of the ingredients.
  2. Blend it in a mixer. Strain and serve chilled, garnished with mint leaves.
