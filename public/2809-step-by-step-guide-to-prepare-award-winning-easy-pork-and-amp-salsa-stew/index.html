The ingredients needed to make Easy Pork & Salsa Stew:
  1. Get 1.5 pounds pork shoulder, cut into 1.5" cubes & tossed with 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  2. Prepare 1 Tablespoon oil
  3. Get half a red bell pepper, cut into roughly 1/3" wide slices (see pics)
  4. Take 1 small onion, cut into roughly 1/3" wide slices (about half a regular sized onion)
  5. Prepare 1 tomato, cut into eighths
  6. Prepare 3 garlic cloves, peeled and smashed (keeping it easy… ;) )
  7. Prepare 1/4-1/3 cup whatever homemade or storebought salsa you've got on hand
  8. Make ready 1 cup unsalted stock (can be chicken, pork, veg, or even water if you don't have stock)
  9. Take another 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt to add to the stew, depending on how salty the salsa you use. Start with 1/4 teaspoon

Instructions to make Easy Pork & Salsa Stew:
  1. Sear the pork in 1 Tablespoon of oil in a preheated medium high heat pot for about 2 minutes per side.
  2. Add in your onions, garlic, bell pepper, and tomato, and saute for 2 or 3 minutes until the onions begin to turn translucent.
  3. Add in the 1 cup stock, 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt, and salsa and stir thoroughly to distribute ingredients and seasonings evenly.
  4. Still on medium high heat, bring the stew to a boil, and once up to a boil, reduce the heat to medium low and simmer, covered (but with about a 1/2" inch opening on the side), for 20 minutes, stirring 2 or 3 times during the process to redistribute everything.
  5. Uncover the pot and simmer another 15 to 20 minutes, stirring 2 or 3 times, and adjusting the salt in the seasoning when you have 10 minutes left to go.
  6. Enjoy! :)
