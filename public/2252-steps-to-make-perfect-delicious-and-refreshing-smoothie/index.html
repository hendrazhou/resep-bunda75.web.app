The ingredients needed to make Delicious and Refreshing smoothie:
  1. Make ready 2 bananas
  2. Get 1 apple
  3. Take 1 mango
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp Greek Yogurt
  6. Prepare 1 cup ice

Steps to make Delicious and Refreshing smoothie:
  1. Cut all the fruits into medium size pieces. Place them in the blender and mix them for about 1 minute.
  2. Then add greek yogurt and milk. (I used a little bit less than 1/2 cup of milk.) If you want your smoothie liquidy add more milk. Mix well.
  3. Add 1 cup full of ice. Mix well.
  4. Enjoy your delicious smoothie!!!
