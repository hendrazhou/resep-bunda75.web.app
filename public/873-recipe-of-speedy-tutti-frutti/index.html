The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti:
  1. Make ready 500 gm watermelon peel(cut into small size)
  2. Take 200 gm sugar
  3. Get 1 pinch yellow colour
  4. Take 1 pinch red colour
  5. Prepare 1 pinch green colour
  6. Prepare 1 pinch orange colour
  7. Take 1 spoon vanilla essence
Instructions to make Tutti frutti:
  1. Take a pan and add 5 cup water and boil high flame and add raw peel,boil for 3 minutes- medium flame, cover the pen 15 minutes.
  2. Take another pan high flame,4 cup water and add sugar 200gm cook the sugar till it melts.add strained peel,boil till we get one string sugar consistency- medium flame after 30 minutes it's done.
  3. Strain the peel & Divide sugar syrup In 4 parts,(divide your choice how many colors you can need) add colour and vanilla essence.dip the Pell in sugar syrup. Divide the Pell into 4 coloured sugar syrup.keep it like this for 2-3 hours. after 3 hours tutti frutti is coloured nicely.
  4. Take a bowl and a big sieve.and dry the peel one by one all colour Pell spread them evenly.let them dry now.
  5. Leave them in shade for 12 hours to dry.
