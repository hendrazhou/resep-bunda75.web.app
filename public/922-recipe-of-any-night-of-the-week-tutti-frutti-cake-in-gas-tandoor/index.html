The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti cake in gas tandoor:
  1. Prepare 1.5 cup maida four
  2. Make ready 1 cup curd
  3. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  4. Take 1 tsp vanilla essence
  5. Get 1/2 cup tutti frutti
  6. Take 1/2 cup oil
  7. Get 1 cup milk
  8. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  9. Make ready 1/4 baking. Soda
  10. Get pinch of Salt
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp raisins
