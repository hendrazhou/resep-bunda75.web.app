The ingredients needed to make Tutti Frutti Cookies (Eggless & Butterless):
  1. Get 3/4 cup whole wheat flour
  2. Get 3/4 cup Maida (refined flour)
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Vanilla Custard powder
  4. Get 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup Ghee
  6. Get 3/4 cup mixed tutti frutti & few chopped cashew nuts
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp rose essence
  8. Take 1-2 tbsp milk
  9. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  10. Get 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions to make Tutti Frutti Cookies (Eggless & Butterless):
  1. Sift whole wheat & refined flour, custard powder, baking powder, salt and keep aside.
  2. Blend sugar, ghee and rose essence till smooth (I used food processor).
  3. Now mix wet ingredients to dry. Add trutti frutti & nuts also. Now form a smooth dough using little milk.
  4. Form a log & tightly cover with foil. Keep it in freezer for 1 hr.
  5. Preheat the oven at 180°C. Grease & dust the baking tray.
  6. Now cut it into cookies with a sharp knife. Arrange all the cookies on the tray with 1/2-1 inch gap in between.
  7. Bake it at 180°C for 22 mins. Let it cool & store in a airtight container.
