The ingredients needed to make Chicken Provolone:
  1. Take 1 pkg chicken tenders
  2. Get 1 can diced tomatoes
  3. Get slices Provolone cheese
  4. Prepare 1 small can sliced black olives, drained
  5. Get 3 sliced mini multi color sweet peppers
  6. Get 1 tbsp sugar
  7. Get to taste Italian seasoning
  8. Get to taste Dill weed
  9. Take to taste Salt
  10. Get to taste Onion powder
  11. Get leaves Fresh washed spinach
  12. Prepare Parmesan cheese for garnish

Steps to make Chicken Provolone:
  1. Put tomatoes, olives, peppers, sugar and spices into a saute pan and warm to marry seasonings
  2. Line a Pyrex baking dish with foil and line up chicken tenders inside dish
  3. Spoon some of the sauce over the chicken tenders, then top with provolone cheese slices
  4. Bake in 350° oven for 10 minutes to melt cheese
  5. Remove dish and spoon rest of the sauce over the cheesy chicken tenders, replace in oven and bake for 30 minutes at 350°
  6. Wash spinach leaves and remove stems, make a bed of spinach leaves
  7. After baking, remove tenders from dish and place on spinach bed for service, sprinkle with shredded parmesan cheese if desired
  8. Enjoy!
