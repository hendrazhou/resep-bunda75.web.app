The ingredients needed to make Pineapple-Melon-Smoothie:
  1. Take 2 Cups pineapple
  2. Prepare 2 Cups galia melon
  3. Prepare 2 oranges
  4. Get 1 Cup pineapple juice
  5. Take 1 Cup ice , crushed

Steps to make Pineapple-Melon-Smoothie:
  1. Peel pineapple and melon and cut into pieces.
  2. Squeeze oranges.
  3. Put all ingredients and some pineapple juice in food processor.
  4. Add some crushed ice.
  5. Enjoy!
