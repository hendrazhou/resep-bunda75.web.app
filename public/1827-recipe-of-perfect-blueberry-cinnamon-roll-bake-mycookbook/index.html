  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon cinnamon

Instructions to make Blueberry Cinnamon Roll Bake

#mycookbook:

  1. Cut cinnamon rolls into bite-size pieces and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon until well combined.
  3. Place cut up cinnamon rolls into an 8x8 baking dish. Pour blueberries on top followed by the milk mixture.
  4. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until fully cooked. If the top is looking too brown, cover with foil for the remaining time.
  5. Garnish with icing and serve.
