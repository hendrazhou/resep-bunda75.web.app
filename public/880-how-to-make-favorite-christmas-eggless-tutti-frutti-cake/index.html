The ingredients needed to make Christmas Eggless tutti-frutti Cake:
  1. Take 1 cup maida
  2. Get 1 cup sugar
  3. Take 1/4 cup oil
  4. Get 1/4 cup milk
  5. Get 1 tsp vinegar
  6. Get 1 cup curd
  7. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  8. Get 1/4 tsp baking soda
  9. Take 1 drop pineapple essence
  10. Take 1/2 cup tutti-frutti

Steps to make Christmas Eggless tutti-frutti Cake:
  1. Add milk and vinegar and mix, keep it for 10 minutes to curdle, milk should be room temperature.
  2. Mix curd and sugar to a smooth mixture, then add oil and mix till fluffy.
  3. Sieve maida, baking powder and baking soda 2 to 3 times.
  4. Now add the dry mix into curd mix.
  5. Add milk vinegar mix to form a smooth batter, do not over mix.
  6. Now add essence and mix.
  7. Now add all dry fruits and tutti-frutti and again mix well.
  8. Now grease a cake tin and pour the batter in it and bake in a preheated pressure cooker for 40 to 45 minutes or a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Now let it cool for few minutes and cut into your desire shape.
