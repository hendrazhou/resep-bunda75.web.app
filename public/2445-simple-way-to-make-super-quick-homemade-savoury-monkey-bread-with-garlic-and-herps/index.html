The ingredients needed to make savoury monkey bread with garlic and herps:
  1. Get 2 tbsp warm water
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp fast action yeast
  3. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  4. Take 2 1/4 cup flour
  5. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Take 1/2 cup milk
  7. Make ready 1 egg
  8. Get 1 tbsp melted butter
  9. Prepare 4 tbsp melted butter
  10. Get 3 tbsp parsley Finley minced
  11. Make ready 4 clove garlic finely minced
  12. Get 1/4 tsp dried rosemary
  13. Prepare 1/4 tsp dried thyme
  14. Take 2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions to make savoury monkey bread with garlic and herps:
  1. combine yeast, sugar ,warm water
  2. combine flour ,salt , dried rosemary ,dried thyme .
  3. then add yeast mixture, milk ,egg ,and 1 tpls of the minced parsley, 1tpls of the garlic , 1 tpls of butter, 1 cup of the cheese and knead the dough ,rest for 1 hour .
  4. combine 4 tpls butter with the rest of parsley ,garlic, cheese
  5. cut the dough to a 1 inch pieces roll each in butter mixture
  6. put the balls in greased baking dish of your choice above each other let it rest for half hour
  7. bake in 180°F preheated oven for 30 minutes . serve hot.
