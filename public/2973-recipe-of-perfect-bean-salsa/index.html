The ingredients needed to make Bean Salsa:
  1. Take 1 can Garbanzo beans
  2. Make ready 1 can Black beans
  3. Prepare 1 can Kidney beans
  4. Take 1 cup Red onion
  5. Prepare 1 piece Garlic cloves
  6. Take 2 large Limes
  7. Get 1 bunch Cilantro chopped
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 cup Chopped cherry tomatoes
  9. Prepare 1 bunch Green onions
