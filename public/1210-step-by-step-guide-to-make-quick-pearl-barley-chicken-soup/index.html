The ingredients needed to make Pearl barley chicken soup:
  1. Take 400 grams chicken fillet
  2. Take 1 onion, finely chopped
  3. Make ready Half cup chopped leek(white part)
  4. Prepare 1 cup pearl barley
  5. Make ready 2 table spoons thick tomato paste
  6. Take 3 table spoons dried parsley
  7. Get 1 tea spoon dried basil
  8. Prepare 3 carrots, grated
  9. Make ready 1 cup celery stalk, finley chopped
  10. Take 2 table spoons butter
  11. Make ready 2 Chicken bullions(optional)
  12. Prepare 1 tea spoon black pepper
  13. Make ready Half teaspoon turmeric powder
  14. Make ready 2 bay leaves
  15. Make ready Salt
  16. Get 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

Instructions to make Pearl barley chicken soup:
  1. Soak pearl barley overnight in water. It helps barley soup be ready and thick in shorter time.
  2. Melt butter in deep non stick pot. Saute onion until it change colour. Add chicken fillet, bay leaves and turmeric and saute until no more pink.Add tomato paste and fry for few minutes for better colour of soup.
  3. Now add celery, carrot, leek along with barley and its water. I put carrot, celery and leek in chopper forst. Solve chicken stock bullions in hot water. Add to soup. Cover the pot. Bring to boil. After 30 mintes add dtied herbs. Wait another 10 minutes until soup become tick. Add black pepper and salt (if needed) and lemon juice.
  4. Serve soup with crusty bread or plain naan.
