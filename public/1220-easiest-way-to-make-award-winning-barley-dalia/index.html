The ingredients needed to make Barley Dalia:
  1. Prepare 1 cup barley
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup mix vegetables (peas, carrot,capsicum, onion)
  3. Get 1 spoon oil
  4. Make ready 1/2 spoon turmeric powder
  5. Take 1 spoon green chilli paste
  6. Make ready 1 spoon cumin seeds
  7. Take 1 spoon ginger garlic paste
  8. Prepare to taste Rock salt

Steps to make Barley Dalia:
  1. Wash barley Dalia with water 2-3 times. Cut the all veggies.
  2. Now take a pressure cooker, keep it on flame. Add oil. Add cumin seeds, chilli paste, ginger garlic paste. Saute for few seconds. Now add all dalia and all veggies. Add turmeric and rock salt. Add water. Close the lid of the cooker. Keep on flame up to 3 whistles. Serve hot.
