The ingredients needed to make Energy Drink (Immunity Booster):
  1. Take 1/2 cup Beetroot
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup carrot
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup cucumber
  4. Make ready 1 tbls lemon juice
  5. Take 1 inch ginger piece
  6. Take 4-5 mint leaves
  7. Make ready 1 tbls honey
  8. Get Pinch black salt
