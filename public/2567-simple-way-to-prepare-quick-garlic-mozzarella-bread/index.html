The ingredients needed to make Garlic Mozzarella Bread:
  1. Take Dough
  2. Take 250 grm maida
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp yeast
  4. Get 1 tbsp sugar
  5. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Take 3 tbsp unsalted butter melted
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup warm milk
  8. Get 1 egg
  9. Make ready Topping
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp Garlic
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  13. Take 1 tbsp green onion chopped
  14. Make ready Filling
  15. Make ready Mozzarella cheese as required
  16. Make ready Only baking time 25 minutes and preheated oven 5 minutes
