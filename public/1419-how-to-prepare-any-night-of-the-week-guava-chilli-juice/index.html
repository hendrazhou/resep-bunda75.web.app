The ingredients needed to make Guava chilli Juice:
  1. Make ready 3 medium sized guavas
  2. Make ready 2 green chillies (spicy)
  3. Take 4 tbsp sugar syrup
  4. Prepare 6-8 ice cubes
  5. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli powder

Steps to make Guava chilli Juice:
  1. Wash and clean the guavas and chillies
  2. Chop them into pieces and transfer to the juicer. Crush them.
  3. Now with the help of the strainer, strain the juice to get rid of the seeds.
  4. Now add the sugar syrup according to the sweetness desired.
  5. While serving add water in 1:1 ratio with the guava chilli juice.
  6. Coat the rim of the serving glass with red chilli powder. Pour the juice. Put the ice cubes and serve immediately
