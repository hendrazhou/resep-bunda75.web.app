The ingredients needed to make Beef and Barley Stew with Dumplings (Slow Cooker):
  1. Make ready Stew
  2. Prepare 2 large onion (thickly chopped)
  3. Get 450 grams stewing beef (diced)
  4. Prepare 4 tbsp frozen peas
  5. Prepare 3 carrot (thickly chopped)
  6. Take 2 medium maris piper potatoes (peeled and cut into chunks)
  7. Take 40 grams dried pearl barley
  8. Prepare 3 tbsp beef or onion gravy granules
  9. Make ready 800 ml cold water
  10. Make ready 2 beef stock cubes
  11. Make ready 1 tsp dried thyme
  12. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  13. Prepare 1 pinch pepper
  14. Take Dumplings
  15. Take 50 grams vegetable suet
  16. Take 100 grams self-raising flour
  17. Take 4 tbsp water (cold)
  18. Make ready 1 tsp dried thyme
  19. Take 1 tsp dried sage
  20. Take 1 pinch salt
  21. Prepare 1 pinch pepper

Steps to make Beef and Barley Stew with Dumplings (Slow Cooker):
  1. Place all the stew ingredients into a slow cooker and leave to cook on high heat for 4 hours.
  2. Mix the suet, flour, herbs and seasoning in a medium sized bowl. Gradually add cold water and mix until the mixture resembles a thick dough. Divide into 8 balls and place on top of the stew. Cover and cook for 3 more hours.
