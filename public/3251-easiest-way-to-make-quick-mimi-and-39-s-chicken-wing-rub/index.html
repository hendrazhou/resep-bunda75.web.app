The ingredients needed to make Mimi's Chicken Wing Rub:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup Brown sugar
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup paprika
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
  4. Get 1 tbsp pepper
  5. Get 2 tbsp garlic powder
  6. Take 1 tbsp onion salt
  7. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  8. Take 1 tbsp oregano
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp thyme

Steps to make Mimi's Chicken Wing Rub:
  1. Mix all ingredients
  2. Coat all sides, nooks and crannies of the wings with the mixture.
  3. Place on grill (usually about 30 mins)
  4. Enjoy!
