The ingredients needed to make Spicy Chicken Wing with Jamaican Jerk Rub:
  1. Prepare 3 lb Chicken wings
  2. Take 1 Onion
  3. Prepare 3 stalk scallion
  4. Prepare 2 clove Garlic
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Fresh ginger chopped
  6. Take 1 tsp Ground pimento
  7. Get 3 Spring fresh thyme
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp All purpose seasoning
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp Light soy sauce
  11. Prepare 1 tsp Vegetable oil
  12. Prepare 2 Habanero pepper .. Preferably scotch bonnet pepper.. You can use less pepper depending on how much heat you like

Instructions to make Spicy Chicken Wing with Jamaican Jerk Rub:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F
  2. Joint your wings or leave them as a whole.. Wash them off with cold water and vinegar solution
  3. Blend all ingredient together and pure over chicken I'm a baking tray, you can ,air at your wings over night or few hrs
  4. Place in the oven uncover and baked for 45 minutes or until chicken are crispy when touch and golden brown
