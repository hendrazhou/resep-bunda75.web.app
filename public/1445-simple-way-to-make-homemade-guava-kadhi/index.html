The ingredients needed to make Guava kadhi:
  1. Prepare 1 kg guava
  2. Take 200 gram gram flour
  3. Get 500 gram curd
  4. Take as required Oil to fry potatoes
  5. Take 2 tablespoons oil for kadhi
  6. Take 2 potatoes
  7. Get 2 tablespoons green peas
  8. Take 1 inch ginger
  9. Make ready 2 green chillies
  10. Prepare to taste Salt
  11. Get 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  12. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  13. Take 1 pinch asafoetida
  14. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

Steps to make Guava kadhi:
  1. Wash chopped and boil guavas adding 2 glasses of water
  2. After cooling grind well
  3. Sieve it to separate pulp
  4. Meanwhile chopped potatoes,ginger and green chillies
  5. Deep fry potatoes and keep them aside
  6. Mix gram flour and curd in guava pulp
  7. Heat oil a vok and add hing,cumin seeds,chilli powder and turmeric powder
  8. Add guava curd mixture and salt
  9. Add chopped ginger and green chillies and fried potatoes
  10. Add boiled peas in ot and boil for 15 minutes
  11. Then turn off the flame
  12. Serve hot with bajre ki roti
