The ingredients needed to make Mango with Coconut Milk Sauce (LAKSAMANA MENGAMUK):
  1. Make ready 600 gr Kweni mango (still yellow, but not too ripe), cut to taste
  2. Make ready 1/2 young coconut, shaved lengthwise (can be replaced with 100 gr Nata de coco)
  3. Make ready 1 tsp basil seeds, soaked in warm water
  4. Get 75 ml sweetened condensed milk
  5. Prepare Ice cubes as needed
  6. Prepare SYRUP
  7. Prepare 350 gr sugar (to taste)
  8. Prepare 200 ml water
  9. Take SAUCE
  10. Get 800 ml thin coconut milk
  11. Get 3 pandan leaves, tied in a knot
  12. Make ready to taste Salt

Steps to make Mango with Coconut Milk Sauce (LAKSAMANA MENGAMUK):
  1. SYRUP : cook sugar and water until the sugar dissolved, remove from heat and set aside.
  2. Sauce: Boil the thin coconut milk, pandan leaf and a little salt. Stir until boiling (continuously so it doesn't form a separate layer of water and coconut milk, or not break up). Chill.
  3. Put the pieces of mango and coconut or nata de coco in serving glasses.
  4. Top with coconut milk sauce, add the basil seeds and sweetened condensed milk. Add ice and ready to enjoy as you wish.
