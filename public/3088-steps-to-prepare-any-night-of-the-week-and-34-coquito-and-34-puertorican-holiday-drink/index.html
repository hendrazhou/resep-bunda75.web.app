The ingredients needed to make "COQUITO" (Puertorican Holiday Drink):
  1. Make ready 2 can Cream of Coconut
  2. Make ready 2 can Condensed Milk
  3. Make ready 5 can Evaporated milk
  4. Take 2 cup Bacardi Rum (light)
  5. Take pinch Cinnamon
  6. Prepare pinch Nutmeg

Coquito is a traditional coconut punch served at Christmas and New Year's celebrations in Puerto Rico. The creamy, sweet drink is similar to eggnog with rum but with the tropical element of coconut.
