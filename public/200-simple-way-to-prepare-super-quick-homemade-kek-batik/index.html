The ingredients needed to make Kek batik…. ‚ù§‚ù§‚ù§:
  1. Get 200 g milo
  2. Make ready 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup condensed milk
  4. Take 250 g butter (I used less)
  5. Prepare 350 g plain biscuits (anykind of biscuit)
  6. Prepare almond flakes (optional)

Steps to make Kek batik…. ‚ù§‚ù§‚ù§:
  1. Break each biscuit into half.
  2. Melt the butter on the stove at the lowest heat.
  3. Add 3/4 cup of condensed milk and stir it untill mix well.
  4. Add milo and cocoa powder. Stir them untill mix well.
  5. Add the broken biscuits in the milo mixture and mix them together using a spatula.
  6. Turn off the stove.
  7. Put the cake in the container and freeze it for an hour.
  8. You also can add almond flakes on the top before you freeze it.
