The ingredients needed to make spicy tomato rice & crispy chicken wings:
  1. Prepare 1 can peeled tomatoes
  2. Take 1 tsp chicken stock powder
  3. Get 1 large onion (sliced and fry keep oil too)
  4. Get 4 clove garlic (sliced & fry)
  5. Prepare 1 toasted chilli flakes
  6. Prepare 1 toasted chick pea flour
  7. Get 1 dry prawns (grounded)
  8. Make ready 1 fish source
  9. Get 1 chicken wings
  10. Prepare 1 salt

Steps to make spicy tomato rice & crispy chicken wings:
  1. fry onions & garlic keep the oil set aside
  2. put peeled tomatoes in a pot add 1 / 4 cup of water add chicken stock powder, pinch of salt simmer until thickened set aside
  3. season chicken wings and fry
  4. RICE MIXTURE (per serving)
  5. 1 teaspoon of chilli flakes
  6. 1 teaspoon of chick pea flour
  7. 1 tablespoon of onion
  8. 1/2 a teaspoon of garlic
  9. 1 tablespoon of dry prawns
  10. a dash of fish sauce
  11. 2 tablespoons of tomatoes mix
  12. mix well
  13. plate up
  14. enjoy
