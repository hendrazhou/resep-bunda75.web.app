The ingredients needed to make Spicy guava beverage:
  1. Take 1/2 big Guava ripe
  2. Take 1/2 cup Strawberry crush
  3. Get 1/2 Lemon to rim the glass
  4. Get 1/2 tsp Pink salt/rock salt
  5. Take 1/4 tsp Red pepper powder
  6. Take 1/2 tsp Chat masala
  7. Make ready 1tbsp Tamarind sauce(pichkoo)
  8. Make ready For garnishing
  9. Take as needed Mint leaves
  10. Take 2 Grapes
  11. Get 1 Cherry
  12. Make ready 1 Sliced guava triangular piece
  13. Prepare 1 Skewer

Steps to make Spicy guava beverage:
  1. Firstly wash and cut the guava in small cubes then blend in a mixture blender by adding salt, tamarind sauce red pepper powder, chat masala and little by little water
  2. Strain the guava mixture through a strainer, add strawberry crush and give a good mix to combine the strawberry crush with guava mixture well incorporate(don't blend after mixing strawberry crush to maintain a pulpy feel)
  3. Adjust the consistency of beverage by adding little bit of water as needed..check the flavour of the beverage and add salt or spices if required
  4. Cut grapes and cherry into halves..take a skewer and insert grape halves and cherry pieces as shown and keep aside
  5. Rim the glass _take a half of lemon and rub at the edge of yo'rs serving glass..take pink salt in a few plate and spread thinly..hold the glass upside down and rotate on the thin layer of salt to stick the salt evenly
  6. Time to serve _take a tall glass pour guava beverage, garnish with a mint leaf, grape &cherry skewer and a guava slice at the edge
  7. Serve immediately
  8. You have not to give a straw in this beverage as salted rim glass gives a really fantastic mouth feel
  9. Tip_thing to remember :most of the ingredients added in this beverage are those which we add while making guava chat or other fruit chat.
