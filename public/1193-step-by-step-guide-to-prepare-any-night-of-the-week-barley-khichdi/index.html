The ingredients needed to make Barley khichdi:
  1. Prepare 1 cup whole barley
  2. Take as required Water
  3. Prepare As needed Salt
  4. Take as needed Oil
  5. Get 1/2 tbs cumin seeds
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup Chopped carrot
  7. Get 1/4 cup Chopped beans
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup Chopped onion
  9. Take 1/4 tbs grated ginger
  10. Make ready 1/4 tbs grated garlic
  11. Take 2 chopped green chillies
  12. Prepare 1 tbs Schezwan sauce (optional)

Instructions to make Barley khichdi:
  1. Cook the barley by adding water in a pressure cooker upto 4 whistle.
  2. Heat up a pan. Add some oil, cumin seeds, grated ginger and garlic, fry them for a while. Then add onion, carrot, green chillis and beans. Fry them for another 2min.
  3. Add the cook barley after strain all the water and Schezwan sauce and give it a quick mix and cook for another 2min.
