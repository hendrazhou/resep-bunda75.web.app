The ingredients needed to make Rocky Road Nutella Banana Bread:
  1. Take 6 tbsp Nutella
  2. Take 3 Ripe Bananas
  3. Prepare 1/4 tsp Salt
  4. Take 1 tsp Baking Soda
  5. Take 1 tsp Baking Powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup Butter
  7. Make ready 2 Eggs
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp Vanilla Extract
  9. Get 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
  10. Get 1/2 cup White Sugar
  11. Take 1 cup All Purpose Flour
  12. Get 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour
  13. Make ready 1 cup Mini Marshmallows
  14. Get 1/2 cup Chopped Pecans

Instructions to make Rocky Road Nutella Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F.
  2. Spray loaf pan with nonstick cooking spray and dust with flour.
  3. In a large bowl, beat together butter, eggs, sugars, vanilla, bananas, salt and baking soda and powder.
  4. Once completely combined, gradually add in the flour.
  5. Pour your batter into your loaf pan until it?s about 1/3 of the way full
  6. Then add chopped pecans, mini marshmallows and drop the Nutella evenly over the surface.
  7. Pour more batter over the top until your loaf pan is almost full to the top.
  8. Top with more Nuttela, marshmallows and pecans
  9. Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out clean.
