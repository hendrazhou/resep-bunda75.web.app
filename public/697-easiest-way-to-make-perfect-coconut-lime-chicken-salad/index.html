The ingredients needed to make Coconut-Lime Chicken Salad:
  1. Make ready 2 C shredded chicken
  2. Get 1 C toasted coconut
  3. Get 4 limes; zested & juiced
  4. Take 1/4 red or yellow bell pepper; julienne
  5. Get 1/2 bundle cilantro; chiffonade
  6. Get 15 oz mayonnaise
  7. Get 2 T Madras curry powder
  8. Take 1 large pinch kosher salt and black pepper
