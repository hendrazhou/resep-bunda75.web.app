The ingredients needed to make Homemade Salsa Roja:

  5. Get 1 Jalapeño
  6. Make ready 1 Fresno pepper
  7. Make ready 2 clove Garlic
  8. Take 1/2 tsp Sugar
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp Lime Juice

Steps to make Homemade Salsa Roja:
  1. Roast the garlic, shallot, peppers, and tomatillos. Remove seeds from peppers and set aside.
  2. Drain half the juice from the can of tomatoes and add remaining ingredients.
  3. Rough chop the roasted peppers, shallot, garlic, and tomatillos and add to rest of ingredients.
  4. Pulse using hand blender or similar device until desired consistency is reached.
  5. Chill and enjoy.
