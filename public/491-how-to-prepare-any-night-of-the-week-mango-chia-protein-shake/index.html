The ingredients needed to make Mango Chia Protein Shake:
  1. Take 1/2 Mango
  2. Get 1/4 cup Blueberries
  3. Get 1 Chia Seeds
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp Vanilla Protein Powder
  5. Take 1 tbsp Chobani Greek Yogurt
  6. Get 1/4 cup Almond Milk

Mango Chia Shake is loaded with nutrition. This has only three main ingredient- mango, chia and coconut milk. Mango, we Indians love it a lot. It also has protein, fiber and calcium.

Steps to make Mango Chia Protein Shake:
  1. Add Mango to blender
  2. Add Blueberries to blender
  3. Add Chia Seeds and Chobani Greek Yogurt to blender
  4. Add Vanilla Protein Powder to blender
  5. Add Almond Milk to blender
  6. Blend and Enjoy!
