The ingredients needed to make Stuffed cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Take FOR GARLIC BREAD DOUGH:
  2. Take ¼ cup warm milk
  3. Make ready 1 tsp dry yeast
  4. Take 4-5 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  5. Get 1 tsp sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tsp butter
  7. Get 1 cup plain flour
  8. Get 2 tsp oil
  9. Make ready FOR GARLIC BUTTER:
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup butter, melted
  11. Get 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  12. Get 2 tbsp coriander leaves, finely chopped
  13. Take OTHER INGREDIENTS:
  14. Take 3 tsp maida, to dust
  15. Make ready 1/4 cup cheese
  16. Get 3 tbsp mixed herbs
  17. Prepare 1 no boiled sweet corn
  18. Prepare 1 no finely chopped capcicum
  19. Prepare Chilly Dip
  20. Take 3 no fresh red chilly
  21. Prepare 1/2 cup plain mayo
  22. Prepare 1 spoon mix herbs
  23. Prepare Aam panna & Tomato souse for serving

Instructions to make Stuffed cheesy garlic bread:
  1. First, in a large mixing bowl take warm milk, dry yeast and sugar.
  2. Mix well. After 5 minutes…. furthermore, add pinch of salt, garlic and oregano. Additionally add butter and maida. start to knead the dough. Further, coat the dough with oil and rest for 2 hours.
  3. Further, punch the dough.
  4. In a small bowl mix in butter, garlic and coriander leaves.
  5. Spread the prepared garlic butter over the dough generously. further top with corn, capcicum, cheese on half of the dough. also season with chili flakes and oregano. fold the dough and seal the edges. furthermore, brush generous amount of prepared garlic butter.also top with chili flakes and mixed herbs. make marks over bread without cutting them fully.bake in preheated oven at 180 degree celsius for 15 minutes.
  6. Apply ghee on fresh red chilli and roast it on gas. After roasting remove the outer skin and cut it. Mix roasted red chilli, mayo and mix herbs in a mixer. Spicy smoky chilli dip is ready.
  7. Cut the garlic bread into pieces and serve with souse, chilli dip & Aam panna.. Healthy… Tasty… Yummy…
