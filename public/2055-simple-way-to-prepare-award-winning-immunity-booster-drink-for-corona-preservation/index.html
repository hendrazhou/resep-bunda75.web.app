Steps to make Immunity booster drink for Corona preservation:
  1. First of all crush mint, tulsi, ginger, black pepper, cloves and cinnamon stick.
  2. Boil 2 cups of water for 2-3 minutes high flame then add all crushed ingredients.
  3. Then add carom seeds, cumin seeds, fennel seeds, turmeric, ginger powder
  4. Add black salt, jaggery or honey as per taste
  5. Boil it for 3-4 minutes on low flame. Then add lemon juice then strain it.
  6. Ready to serve.
