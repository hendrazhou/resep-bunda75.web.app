The ingredients needed to make Berry Good Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 1/2 C frozen blueberries
  2. Get 1 1/2 C frozen strawberries
  3. Get 1/2 C frozen raspberries
  4. Make ready 1/2 C frozen blackberries
  5. Make ready 1 T fresh lemon juice
  6. Make ready 3 oz fruit nectar (Mango recommended)
  7. Get 1 C strawberry or vanilla yogurt
  8. Get 3 T honey
  9. Take 1 T chia seeds
  10. Get 1/2 ea frozen roasted beet (chopped, optional)
