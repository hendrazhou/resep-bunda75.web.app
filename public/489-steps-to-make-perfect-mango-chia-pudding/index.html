The ingredients needed to make Mango Chia Pudding:
  1. Prepare 1 banana
  2. Take 1/2 mango
  3. Get 1/4 cup coconut milk
  4. Make ready 1 slice fresh ginger
  5. Get 1 tbsp sunflower seeds
  6. Get dash vanilla extract
  7. Get 1 tbsp chia seeds
  8. Get pinch bee pollen (optional)

Instructions to make Mango Chia Pudding:
  1. Blend together banana, mango, coconut milk, ginger, sunflower seeds and vanilla until smooth and creamy.
  2. Stir in Chia seeds and let rest for 10 mins for seeds to swell and thicken mixture.
  3. Serve in a glass and sprinkle with bee pollen and/or more sunflower seeds.
