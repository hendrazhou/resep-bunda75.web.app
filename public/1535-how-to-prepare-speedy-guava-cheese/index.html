The ingredients needed to make Guava Cheese:

  2. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 3-4 tbsp butter
  4. Get as required Pistachio flakes for garnish

Steps to make Guava Cheese:
  1. Add 3 ripened guavas (pressure cooked for 2 whistles) into a mixer jar & blend it in a fine paste without adding any water.
  2. Add the blended guava to a strainer & strain to get smooth pulp.
  3. Heat up a pan. Add the strained guava pulp, 1 cup sugar and let the sugar melt on a low flame.
  4. Add 3-4 tbsp butter to it & let it melt. Keep mixing continuously till the mixture cooks well.
  5. Take a mould greased with butter. Pour in the cooked mixture when it is still hot & spread evenly.
  6. Let is cool & set completely. Remove from mould & cut in desired shape. Garnish with pistachio flakes & serve.
