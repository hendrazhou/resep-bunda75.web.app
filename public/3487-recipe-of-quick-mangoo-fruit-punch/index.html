The ingredients needed to make Mangoo fruit punch:
  1. Make ready 2 ltr mangoo juice or made with mangoo (4 mangoes + 1 ltr water + 7 to 8 tbsp sugar blend to make juice
  2. Get 1/2 cup bananas sliced
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup mangoes cut in cube
  4. Prepare 1 cup Mixed fruit(pineapple berry peach)
  5. Take 1 cup prepared mango jelly
  6. Prepare 1 ltr any soda drink(7up or sprite)
  7. Take Lots of ice

Steps to make Mangoo fruit punch:
  1. Take 2 ltr of mangoo juice (like chausa) tetra pack or you can make in home also by cut mangoes add sugar and water make mango juice,it’s little thick
  2. Now make jelly of one cup of mango flavour set and cut in cube
  3. Now in big bowl or dispenser add mango juice soft soda drink and all fruits and jelly and mix and serve add ice and serve super chilled
