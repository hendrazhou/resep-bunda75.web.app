The ingredients needed to make Fruit Trifle:
  1. Take 1 packages Angel food cake mix
  2. Make ready 15 1/4 oz crushed pineapple, drained
  3. Take 11 oz Mandarin orange segments, drained
  4. Prepare 1 packages vanilla instant pudding mix
  5. Take 8 oz Cool whip
  6. Make ready 12 maraschino cherries

Instructions to make Fruit Trifle:
  1. Bake & cool cake according to package directions. Cut cake vertically into halves. Freeze one half for future use. Tear other half into small pieces.
  2. Mix pineapple & oranges in small bowl.
  3. Prepare pudding according to package directions.
  4. Layer half of each of cake, fruit mixture, & pudding in 2 qt serving bowl; repeat. Spread cool whip over pudding. Garnish with cherries.
  5. Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
