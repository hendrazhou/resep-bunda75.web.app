The ingredients needed to make Guava veg:
  1. Take 4 guavas
  2. Prepare as required coriander leaves
  3. Make ready 2 green chillies
  4. Make ready 2 teaspoons Oil
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon turmeric
  6. Prepare as per taste Salt
  7. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon red chilli
  8. Take 2 teaspoons Sugar
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon Coriander powder
  10. Get 2 Cloves
  11. Take 1 teaspoon Carom seeds(ajmain)
  12. Make ready pinch Hing (If u want)
  13. Prepare 2 tablespoons Water

Instructions to make Guava veg:
  1. After Wash & Cut these guavas in small pieces.
  2. Take pan and prepare tadka 2 teaspoons oil put into a pan and put carom, clove and hing. (hing depends your choice. If u want & not)
  3. After tadka put guava in pan and add turmeric, salt and chilli and green chilli and water and allow to cook by covering the kadai(pan)with a lid on low flame.
  4. In between check and stir.cover again and cook till the guava are cooked nicely.after add sugar and coriander powder and cook 2 mint & garnish coriander leaves.The guava will cook in approx 10 to 15 minute.
  5. Serve with Paratha and roti.
  6. Note- (chillies are being used a little bit more to increase Ayurvedic Property)
