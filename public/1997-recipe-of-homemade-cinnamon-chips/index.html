The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon chips:
  1. Prepare 3 flour tortilla
  2. Get 1 cup cinnamon
  3. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 3 cups cooking oil
  5. Take 1 can of any pie filling you like

Instructions to make Cinnamon chips:
  1. Heat 3 cups cooking oil to 375
  2. Cut tortillas into chip sharps
  3. Mix cinnamon and sugar together
  4. Cook flour tortillas to your liking then add cinnamon sugar mix
  5. Then add the pie filling of your choose
