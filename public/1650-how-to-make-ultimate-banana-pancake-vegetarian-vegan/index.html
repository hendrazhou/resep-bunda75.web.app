The ingredients needed to make Banana Pancake *Vegetarian, Vegan:
  1. Take 2 ripe bananas
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 cup plain flour
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Take 1 tbsp sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp vanilla
  6. Take 1/4 cup milk (use any plants milk for vegan diet)

Instructions to make Banana Pancake *Vegetarian, Vegan:
  1. Mash bananas with a fork.
  2. Mix plain flourx sugar, vanilla,and baking powder in a mixing bowl
  3. Add mashed bananas and milk, mix well.
  4. Pre-heat non stick pan, pour banana mixture on the pan, turn to the other side when one side cooks.
  5. Enjoy with honey or mapple syrup.
