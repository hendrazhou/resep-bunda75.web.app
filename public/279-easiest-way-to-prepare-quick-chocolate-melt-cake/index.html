The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Melt Cake:
  1. Take 1 1/4 cup sugar
  2. Get 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  3. Get 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  4. Get 1 teaspoon baking powder
  5. Make ready 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  6. Take 1 cup Chocolate horlicks Or Ovaltine Or Milo
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. Get 1 cup milk
  9. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  10. Take 1 cup vegetable oil
  11. Prepare 3 large eggs
  12. Prepare 1 cup sour cream (if you don't find then use yogurt)
  13. Prepare For Frosting:
  14. Get Chocolate crunch
  15. Prepare Chocolate chips

Steps to make Chocolate Melt Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 180c. Grease and flour 2 (9-inch) cake pans.
  2. In the bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a small bowl, combine milk and malted milk powder, stirring to dissolve.
  4. Add milk mixture, oil, and eggs to flour mixture, beating at medium speed until smooth.
  5. Add sour cream and vanilla, beating just until combined.
  6. Pour batter evenly into prepared pans, and bake for 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  7. Let cool in pans for 10 minutes.
  8. Remove cakes from pans and let cool completely on wire racks.
  9. For Frosting - Chocolate Crunch & Chocolate Chip
