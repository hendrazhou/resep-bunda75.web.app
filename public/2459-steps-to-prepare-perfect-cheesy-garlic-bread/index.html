The ingredients needed to make Cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Take 1 cup flour
  2. Take 1/4 thcup milk powder
  3. Make ready 1 tsp instant yeast
  4. Get 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  5. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 3 tsp coriander
  7. Take 7-8 cloves garlic
  8. Get 4 tsp butter
  9. Take 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  10. Prepare 1 tsp mixed herbs and chilli flakes
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup Lukewarm milk
  12. Make ready 1 tsp cheese spread

Steps to make Cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Make a bread dough by mixing together, flour, milk powder, sugar, yeast,salt and Lukewarm milk. Knead it for 10minutes to get a smooth ball.
  2. Keep it covered in a warm place for 1hour till it doubles in size. Remove it and roll a big chapati from it. Apply butter, and 1tsp cheese spread on the half side of chapati. Sprinkle chopped coriander and garlic
  3. Sprinkle salt, chilli flakes, mixed herbs and grated cheese
  4. Fold the chapati over the stuffing. Place it on a pie tart mould. Spread some more butter and spices on top and keep it in a dark place for 20minutes
  5. Preheat a Kadhai by adding 1cup salt. Place the tart mould in the Kadhai after giving it a milk and sugar wash. Cover the Kadhai and bake it for 15minutes.
  6. After 15minutes open the Kadhai and check if bread is ready. Remove it from Kadhai. Apply 1tsp butter on top and serve.
