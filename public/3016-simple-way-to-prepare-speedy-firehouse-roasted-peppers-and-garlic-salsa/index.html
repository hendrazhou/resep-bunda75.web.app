Steps to make Firehouse Roasted Peppers and Garlic Salsa:
  1. Grill; red pepper, jalapeños and entire garlic head for approx. 20 - 25 min.
  2. While grilling, rough chop and put in blender; 4 roma tomatoes, habanero peppers, 1/2 of the onion and 1/2 bunch cilantro
  3. Add to blender; oregano, salt, vinegar and lime juice
  4. When done grilling; remove stem, seeds and skin from red pepper.
  5. Remove stems from jalapeños (that's all - keep the whole pepper).
  6. Cut bottom from garlic head (careful- it is hot). Smash out garlic with side of knife. Add @ 6-8 cloves and save the rest to add to taste.
  7. Add peppers and garlic to blender and blend contents.
  8. Pour blended contents into container and dice the 2 remaining tomatoes, onion and desired amount ot cilantro; add to container
  9. Mix with spoon and add salt, water, garlic or habanero s to taste.
  10. Chill and serve
