The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Barley Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 Cup Barley rinsed well
  2. Make ready 1 ,cup of chicken stock from a fresh cooked chicken
  3. Take 1/4 cup cooked chicken
  4. Prepare 1 litre Water
  5. Get 1 Small Carrot chopped really Small
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp Sea salt
  7. Take 1/4 tsp Black pepper
