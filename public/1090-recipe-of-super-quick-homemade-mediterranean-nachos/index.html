The ingredients needed to make Mediterranean Nachos:
  1. Prepare Bag tortilla chips on the border
  2. Get Meat of your choice steak or chicken cooked and diced I use chicken
  3. Prepare 1 cup diced tomatoes
  4. Make ready 1 cup Montreal cheese
  5. Get 1 cup Mozzarella cheese
  6. Get 1/2 cup fate cheese for the top only
  7. Prepare 1 cup chopped lettuce
  8. Make ready 1/2 diced red onion
  9. Take 1 teaspoon sumac
  10. Prepare Cup pitted olives black and green
  11. Prepare 1 avocado diced
  12. Take 2 diced 🌶 chili green
  13. Make ready 2 tablespoon creamy Greek dressing
  14. Make ready Sour cream for dip
  15. Get Salsa for dip

Instructions to make Mediterranean Nachos:
  1. Rub sumac with diced red onion add to the lettuce and tomatoes and olives with avocado and fate cheese and dressing and refrigerate
  2. Over on broiler lay the1/2 bag of chips then the chicken and do not skip on the cheese I put 2 cups of it you could change to your favorite cheese keep watching you do not want to burn it
  3. 10 minutes later put the salad on top and serve while it’s hot with sour cream and salsa
