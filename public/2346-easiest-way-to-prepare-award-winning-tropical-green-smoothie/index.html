The ingredients needed to make Tropical Green Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 1 large handful of fresh baby spinach
  2. Make ready 1 cup water
  3. Get 1/2 cup skim milk(or unsweetened coconut or almond milk)
  4. Make ready 1 cup fresh pineapple
  5. Take 1/2 banana(preferably frozen)
  6. Make ready 1 peeled kiwi fruit
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup plain or vanilla greek yogurt(or pineapple or coconut)
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp ground flax seeds
  9. Take 1 tbsp coconut flakes(mine were sweetened)
  10. Make ready 1 packet of stevia(or artificial sweetener of choice) or a bit of honey
  11. Get 5 or 6 ice cubes

  2. Add all remaining ingredients and blend another 30-45 seconds. Enjoy!!
