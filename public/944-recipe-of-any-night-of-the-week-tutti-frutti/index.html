The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti:
  1. Take Peel of one waltermelon
  2. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 2 cups water
  4. Take Colors of your choice
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
Instructions to make Tutti frutti:
  1. Cut white part of watermelon peel in small cubes.
  2. Boil in water till cooked
  3. Strain and keep aside.
  4. Take water and sugar in a pan and add cooked peel.
  5. Boil for 10 minutes.
  6. Add vanilla essence
  7. Divide in four parts.
  8. Add different colour in each part and mix and let it rest for 15-20 min.
  9. Strain and put in a plate and let it dry for one day and use as required.
