The ingredients needed to make Kesar Flavor Fruit Custard:
  1. Make ready 2 tablespoon Kesar Flavour Custard
  2. Prepare Sugar as per your taste
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup Dry fruits
  4. Prepare 2 Bananas chopped
  5. Prepare 1/2 Mangoes chopped
  6. Get 1/2 cup Pomegranate
  7. Take 1/2 cup Grapes
  8. Take Almonds for garnishing
  9. Prepare 1 pinch Elaichi powder
  10. Get 3 cups Milk

Instructions to make Kesar Flavor Fruit Custard:
  1. First of all..Take a bowl, Add custard powder and milk and mix all well so that there may have no lumps
  2. Now Take another pan…Add milk to boil…Now add custard powder mixture and mix well..
  3. Now add sugar and elaichi powder and mix well
  4. Add chopped nuts and mix well and boil
  5. When the mixture gets thicker and boiled..Turn off the flame and Transfer it into a bowl and Keep the mixture into refrigerator for 10-12 hrs
  6. After refrigerating…Take the bowl…Now add the chopped fruits and mix well
  7. Garnish with almonds over them And Serve Chilled😋🥣
