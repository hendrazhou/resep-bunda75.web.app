The ingredients needed to make Banana cinnamon cake:
  1. Make ready 1/2 banana
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp milk
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  5. Take 1 1/2 tbsp brown sugar
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Get 5 tbsp flour
  8. Prepare 3/4 tsp cinnamon
  9. Get 1 tbsp vegetable oil
