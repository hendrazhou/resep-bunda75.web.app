The ingredients needed to make Garlic cheese Bread:
  1. Take 12 Bread slices
  2. Take 100 gm Butter salted
  3. Take 1 tbsp Garlic chopped
  4. Make ready 2 tsp Oregano
  5. Take 1/2 cup Tomato chilli sauce
  6. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Chilli flakes
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Coriander leaves chopped
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup Cheese grated
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp Ghee

Instructions to make Garlic cheese Bread:
  1. Mix, butter, chopped garlic, chilli flakes, coriander leaves.then beat it in a mixer.Apply or spread on one side of the bread slices.(4 slices).
  2. Spread tomato chillie sauce,cheese,and oregano on each slice of the bread(the other 4 bread slices).
  3. Sandwich each slices and apply butter mix on one side and toast it in a hot pan with 1 tsp ghee.
  4. When it becomes brown,then apply butter mix on other side and toast it on low heat.
  5. Remove from heat.Cut it into diagonal shapes. Serve hot.
