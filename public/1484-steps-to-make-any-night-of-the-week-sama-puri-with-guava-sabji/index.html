Steps to make Sama puri with guava sabji:
  1. Method..for puri..
  2. Taka a wok
  3. Add clarified butter,rice flour,and water in it and mix it well
  4. Cover it and cook it on slow flame for 1min
  5. When it become cold..mix it for 5to 7 mins and make fine dough
  6. Make small puri with the help of hands..
  7. And deep fry all puri on medium flame
  8. When it become golden brown it's done..
  9. For sabji..
  10. Take a pan
  11. Add clarified butter in it
  12. Add guava pieces,salt,chilli,coriander in it and mix it well
  13. Cover it and cook it for 2 mins on slow flame
  14. Serve it with hot sama puri
