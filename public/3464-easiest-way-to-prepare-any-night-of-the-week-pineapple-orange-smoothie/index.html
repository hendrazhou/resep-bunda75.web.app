The ingredients needed to make Pineapple Orange Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 1 cup pineapple
  2. Prepare 1 cup orange
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup apple
  4. Prepare 1 tsp ginger juice
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions to make Pineapple Orange Smoothie:
  1. Cut pineapple, orange and apple into small pieces. Make sure you removed all seeds
  2. Put these pieces in juicer, add ginger and lemon juice in it. And blend it till it become smooth.
  3. Add tablespoon of honey and mix it. Serve it with chilled ice cubes
