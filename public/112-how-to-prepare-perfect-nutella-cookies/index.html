The ingredients needed to make nutella cookies:
  1. Make ready nutella cookie
  2. Take 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  3. Get 1/2 cup white sugar
  4. Get 1/2 cup Brown sugar
  5. Take 2 cup flour
  6. Make ready 1 cup nutella
  7. Make ready 2 eggs
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp vanilla essence
  9. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  11. Take 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  12. Get 1 cup chopped nuts (any kind, I used pecans)
  13. Get chocolate drizzle
  14. Prepare 3/4 bar of cooking chocolate
  15. Take 1/4 cup milk
  16. Prepare 2 tbsp cocoa powder

Steps to make nutella cookies:
  1. mix butter, sugar and eggs until combined
  2. pour in flour, cocoa powder, vanilla essence, nutella, salt and baking soda into first mixture and mix well
  3. lastly, throw in nuts and mix evenly
  4. bake in 180 celcius until brown
  5. for the drizzle, I melted the chocolate, milk and cocoa powder using the water bath method
  6. once the cookies cooled down, I drizzled over the chocolate with a spoon.
