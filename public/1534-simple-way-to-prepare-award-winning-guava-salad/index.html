The ingredients needed to make Guava salad:
  1. Take 5-6 ripe guava
  2. Take 3 tbsps sugar
  3. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Take 1 tsp black pepper powder

Instructions to make Guava salad:
  1. First wash the guava nicely then cut it into small pieces.
  2. In a bowl take all the guava add sugar, salt and black pepper powder and give it a good mix.
  3. Serve it in a plate or bowl and enjoy.
