The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Watermelon chia pudding smoothie:
  1. Prepare 1 cup frozen watermelon
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup frozen strawberry
  3. Get 1 tsp honey
  4. Take 1/2 lemon
  5. Prepare Chia pudding
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp chia seeds
  7. Prepare 1 cup warm almond milk (or any prefers milk)
