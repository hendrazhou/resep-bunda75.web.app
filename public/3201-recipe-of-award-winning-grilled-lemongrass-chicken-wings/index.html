The ingredients needed to make Grilled Lemongrass Chicken Wings:
  1. Get 6 lb organic chicken wings, separate drummies from wing and remove tip
  2. Make ready 3 stalk Lemon grass, green part removed and chopped
  3. Make ready 1 piece ginger 2 inch in size peeled and chopped
  4. Get 1 head garlic, peeled and smashed
  5. Take 5 stalk scallions, chopped
  6. Make ready 1 cup red boat fish sauce
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp garlic powder
  8. Get 1 tbsp ground cumin
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp turmeric
  10. Get 1 tsp black pepper
  11. Get 1 salt to taste
  12. Get 1 cup olive oil, extra virgin
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp crushed red pepper
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp cayenne
  15. Get 1 tbsp chili powder
  16. Make ready 1 yellow onion, chopped
  17. Get 1 tsp cardamom
  18. Prepare 1 cup Shaoxing rice wine

Steps to make Grilled Lemongrass Chicken Wings:
  1. Place all ingredients (except chicken wings) in a blender and blend until smooth. Taste the marinade to see if more salt needed. If the marinade is too thick, add additional rice wine.
  2. Pour marinade onto chicken wings, toss thoroughly and refrigerate over night. The next day remove wings from marinade and remove excess oil.
  3. Grill chicken wings until cooked on the inside and skin is rendered on the outside. Dependent on the grill. Usually takes 25 mins on lower heat with frequent flipping.
