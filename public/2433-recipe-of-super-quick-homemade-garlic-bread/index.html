The ingredients needed to make Garlic Bread:
  1. Take 1 loaf Walmart French Bread
  2. Make ready 1 stick salted butter (room temp)
  3. Take 1 garlic powder
  4. Get 1 oregano
  5. Get 1 parmesan cheese
  6. Make ready 1 shredded mozzarella (optional)

Steps to make Garlic Bread:
  1. preheat oven to 450
  2. slice bread into 1 to 1.5 in slices
  3. butter both sides of bread and lay flat on cookie sheet (covered in foil for easy clean up)
  4. sprinkle even coat of garlic powder on buttered to side. add dash of oregano to each. season as desired with parmesan. (can add shredded mozzarella also)
  5. bake 5-10 min until lightly golden brown
