The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Garlic Beer Bread:
  1. Get 4 1/2 cups baking mix
  2. Prepare 1 3/4 cup shredded Colby and Monterey Jack cheese
  3. Get 1 tbsp Garlic powder
  4. Take 4 tsp butter
  5. Take 4 tsp sugar
  6. Get 1 tsp parsley
  7. Prepare 2 cups (16 oz) beer

Steps to make Cheesy Garlic Beer Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°. Put butter in a large cast iron skillet, (I use a cast iron Dutch oven) put skillet in oven
  2. Whisk together baking mix, parsley, garlic, sugar, and 1 1/2 cups of cheese
  3. Stir in beer until combined, pour over melted butter in skillet, sprinkle with remaining cheese, and bake for 30 min, or until golden brown on top.
