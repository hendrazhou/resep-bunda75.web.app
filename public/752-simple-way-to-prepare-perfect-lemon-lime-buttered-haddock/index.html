The ingredients needed to make Lemon Lime Buttered Haddock:
  1. Get 2 Pieces of Fresh Haddock
  2. Make ready 1 Paprika
  3. Take 1 Onion powder
  4. Prepare 1 Garlic powder
  5. Take 1 Pepper
  6. Take 1 Sea salt
  7. Take Sauce
  8. Make ready 1/4 Of a Lemon Squeezed
  9. Get 1/3 Of a Lime Squeezed
  10. Take 1 tsp Unsalted butter

Instructions to make Lemon Lime Buttered Haddock:
  1. Mix all spices and season both sides of the fish. Sorry I don't really measure my seasoning when cooking seafood I kind of just guesstimate how much flavor I need or want.
  2. Next squeeze your lemon and lime together in a small dish and stir. Set your butter to the side to be used later( you will add the butter to the pan as the haddock is cooking).
  3. Preheat a pan over Medium heat with a cap full of evoo. Once the pan & oil are hot enough add seasoned haddock and cook until one side is completely done & the other side is 3/4 done. As that side is continuing to cook add your butter to an the side of the pan, then pour lemon lime mix to the butter. Do not cover the fish in sauce until fish is completely cooked and out of pan as it gives it a nice kick in flavor.
