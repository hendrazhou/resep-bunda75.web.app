The ingredients needed to make Guava chutany (पेरू चटणी):
  1. Prepare 1 big guava(peru)
  2. Get 2-3 green chillies
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Jaggery(gud) grated
  4. Make ready as needed Salt
  5. Take as needed coriander leaves chopped
  6. Make ready 2-3 garlic cloves
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp Lemon juice
