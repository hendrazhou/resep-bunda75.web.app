The ingredients needed to make Extremely Easy Tomato Salsa:
  1. Make ready 1 big can or 1 quart of home canned whole tomatoes
  2. Take 1/2 white or yellow onion
  3. Make ready 1 handful cilantro
  4. Get 1 lime (juiced)
  5. Get 1/2 tablespoon Tapatio hot sauce
  6. Make ready Pinch salt and pepper

In addition to the ingredients, you'll need a large stock pot or canning pot, a flat steamer rack to go in the pot for water. This homemade salsa recipe is healthy, fresh, and so easy to make!
