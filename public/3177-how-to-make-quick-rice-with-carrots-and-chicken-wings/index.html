The ingredients needed to make Rice with carrots and chicken wings:
  1. Make ready 1 tablespoon sugar
  2. Prepare 1 tablespoon Mirin
  3. Take Soy sauce
  4. Prepare Salt
  5. Take Garlic
  6. Prepare Ginger
  7. Get Pepper
  8. Make ready 4 Chicken wing
  9. Get 1 Carrots
  10. Prepare Rice (3/4 cup)

Instructions to make Rice with carrots and chicken wings:
  1. Chicken wings with salt, pepper, sugar, soy sauces and mirin. Leave it for 20 minutes
  2. Stir fried the minced garlic and ginger. Soy sauce and mirin added into the pan, with carrots and chicken wings for 3 minutes.
  3. Put the chicken wings aside. Pour the sauce with water and rice (the portion of the sauce with water should be the same as the rice)
  4. Put all the ingredients (except the chicken wings) into the rice cooker and start.
  5. After 20 minutes, put the chicken wings into the rice cooker. Leave for 10 more minutes after it was finished.
