The ingredients needed to make Guava kulfi:
  1. Get 200 gm ripe guava
  2. Make ready 200 ml condensed milk
  3. Take 4 tbsp milk powder
  4. Take 1 cup full cream milk
  5. Get 1 cup fresh cream
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp sugar
  7. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  8. Get Handful chopped dry fruits (almond,cashews)

Steps to make Guava kulfi:
  1. Heat milk remove seeds and add diced guava.Cook on low flame for 5 minutes.switch off the gas.keep it cool.
  2. Now take a grinder jar add all the ingredients except dry fruits.grind it to make smooth paste.
  3. Pour in to small paper cups and top with chopped nuts.cover it with foil paper.chill till set around 6-7hour.
  4. Remove from cups carefully.cut and serve.
