The ingredients needed to make Avocado Smoothie:
  1. Take 1/2 avocado
  2. Take 1 banana
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup packed spinach
  4. Take 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  5. Get 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  7. Get 1 teaspoon flax seed
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon bee pollen
