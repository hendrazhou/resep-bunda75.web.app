The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Watermelon chia pudding smoothie:
  1. Get 1 cup frozen watermelon
  2. Get 1/2 cup frozen strawberry
  3. Prepare 1 tsp honey
  4. Take 1/2 lemon
  5. Make ready Chia pudding
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp chia seeds
  7. Make ready 1 cup warm almond milk (or any prefers milk)

Steps to make Strawberry Watermelon chia pudding smoothie:
  1. Make chia pudding, put chia seeds in a cup of warm almond milk and leave it for 10-15 min. You can leave this overnight
  2. Put the smoothie ingredients into a blender and blitz them until smooth.
  3. Pour some chai pudding into and nice glass, then add smoothie and then add chai. Repeat that to get a lovely layer effect.
  4. Enjoy the combination of hot and cold. It’s sooo yummy 😋
