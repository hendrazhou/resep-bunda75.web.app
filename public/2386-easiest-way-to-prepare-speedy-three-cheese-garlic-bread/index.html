The ingredients needed to make Three cheese garlic bread:
  1. Take 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp blue cheese dressing
  3. Prepare 3 clove garlic
  4. Make ready 1/3 cup cheddar cheese
  5. Get 1 loaf french bread
  6. Take 1 1/2 tsp dried basil or italian seasoning

Steps to make Three cheese garlic bread:
  1. mix all ingredients together except the bread.
  2. slice bread
  3. spread cream cheese mixture on bread. you can also add more cheddar to the top.
  4. broil until golden and bubbly
  5. **alternate use: cream cheese mixture can be used as a hot dip and serve with your choice of crackers or pretzels
