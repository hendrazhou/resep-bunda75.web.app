The ingredients needed to make Garlic Bread:
  1. Prepare 10 clove Garlic minced
  2. Prepare 5 tbsp Unsalted butter at room temp
  3. Take 2 tbsp EVOO
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup Grated Parmesan
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp Chopped parsley
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp Oregano
  7. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
  8. Prepare 1 loaf Ciabatta bread
