Steps to make Fruit custard:
  1. Take a pan.Add milk in it and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add custard powder and stir continuesly so that no lumps formed.
  3. Add sugar and mix well. Keep aside for cooling.
  4. Add your favorite fruits. And serve it.
