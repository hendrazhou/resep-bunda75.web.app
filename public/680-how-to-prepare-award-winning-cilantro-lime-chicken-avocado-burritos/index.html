The ingredients needed to make Cilantro lime chicken avocado burritos:
  1. Take 2 Boneless chicken breast
  2. Make ready 1 Avocado - sliced
  3. Prepare 1 Vine ripe tomato - diced
  4. Make ready 2 Limes - halved
  5. Get 1/2 Cup Shredded monterey jack cheese
  6. Make ready 2 TBSP Fresh cilantro - chopped
  7. Make ready 1/2 Cup Sour cream
  8. Take 1/4 Cup Salsa verde
  9. Make ready 2 TSP Tajin brand clasico seasoning
  10. Make ready 1 TSP Chipotle chile powder
  11. Get 1 TBSP Sea salt

Steps to make Cilantro lime chicken avocado burritos:
  1. Juice one lime into a small bowl and add Tajin seasoning and chile powder.
  2. Grill chicken over medium heat while brushing occasionally with lime juice/seasoning mix.
  3. Spread sour cream into center of tortillas and add tomato and avocado.
  4. Once cooked slice chicken and place into tortillas, juice remaining lime over sliced chicken and add in cilantro, salsa verde and cheese. Sprinkle with chopped cilantro and sea salt.
  5. Fold ends of tortillas and role burritos so that they are flat on the sides. Place burritos on griddle for 2-3 min per side, or until tortilla becomes crispy.
  6. Enjoy with a side of sour cream.
