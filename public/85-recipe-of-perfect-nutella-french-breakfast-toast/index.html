The ingredients needed to make Nutella French Breakfast Toast:
  1. Take 4 slice Bread
  2. Take 2 Eggs
  3. Get 1 tsp Vanilla essence
  4. Get 1/4 tsp Cinnamon powder (to taste)
  5. Get 1 as needed Nutella
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Butter
  7. Make ready 1 as needed Fruit of choice (I used strawberries)
  8. Get 1 Desiccated coconut/icing sugar
  9. Get 50 ml Milk (I used almond)

Instructions to make Nutella French Breakfast Toast:
  1. Whisk eggs, milk, vanilla and cinnamon (if using) together
  2. Prepare the bread. Coat two slices of bread with lashings of Nutella and sandwich together
  3. Dip both sides into egg mixture, leaving to soak for 10 seconds each.
  4. On a medium heat, melt the butter in a frying pan. Fry the slices until browned and starting to crisp on each side.
  5. Slice strawberries (or bananas/what you're using) and later on top of the fried french toast.
  6. Scatter with the desiccated coconut or icing sugar and enjoy!
