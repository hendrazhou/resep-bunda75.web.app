Steps to make Cheesy Garlic Bread Roll Ups:
  1. Roll out the crust-less slice of bread, rolling it out completely until it is as flat as a roti.
  2. Spread your filling in the middle of the slice of bread, leaving place around all four edges of the slice of bread. I spread my garlic butter and placed my mozzarella stick on top.
  3. Thinly apply the flour paste around three sides of the bread.
  4. Roll the bread from the side which doesn't have the flour, and stick down the other sides by pressing firmly.
  5. Your bread roll up will now look like a spring roll.
  6. Place a small amount of butter in a frying pan, and allow to melt. Place your bread roll ups and allow them to fry until golden brown. Serve immediately. - - Serving suggestions: instead of cheese, you can use chicken and mayo, mashed potato or even mince.
