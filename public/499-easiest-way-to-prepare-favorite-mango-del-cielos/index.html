  3. Take 2 tbsp sugar

Instructions to make Mango dél cielos:
  1. Take out the 2/3 pulp mango and put it in the mixer with milk and sugar and mix it for 20-30 seconds
  2. Keep this mixture in a vessel
  3. Now take 1/3 part of the mango pulp in the mixer and mix it for 10 seconds
  4. Now pour half of the mix mango of step 1 in a glass. Then pour the mix mango of step 3 and then again pour the rest half of the mix mango step 1.
  5. Use cream or vanilla ice cream to garnish.
