The ingredients needed to make Vanilla tutti fruity cake:
  1. Make ready 11/2 cup maida
  2. Take 2-3 tbsp Powdered sugar
  3. Get 1 Cup condensed milk
  4. Make ready As needed milk
  5. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla essence
  6. Prepare Pinch yellow food colour
  7. Make ready 4 tbsp tutti frutti
  8. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking soda
  10. Make ready Pinch salt
  11. Take 1/2 cup butter

Instructions to make Vanilla tutti fruity cake:
  1. Add butter and powdered sugar in a bowl and mix it well.
  2. Add maida, baking powder, baking soda, salt, in a mixing bowl.
  3. Now add this dry ingredients in butter and powder sugar mixture and mix it well.
  4. Add vanilla essence and tutti frutti in this batter.
  5. Grease butter to cake mould pour this batter and bake it for 25-30 mins.
  6. Now de mould the cake let it cool and then enjoy.
