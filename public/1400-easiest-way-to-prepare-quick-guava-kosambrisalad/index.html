The ingredients needed to make Guava kosambri(salad):
  1. Prepare 1 guava
  2. Prepare 4 tsp moong dal
  3. Get 2 green chillies
  4. Get 2 tsp coriander leaves
  5. Prepare to taste Salt
  6. Take 1/2 tsp pepper powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp chat masala
  8. Take 1/2 tsp jeera powder roasted
  9. Prepare Pinch chilli powder
  10. Get Pinch black salt
  11. Get 1/2 tsp lemon juice

Kosambari -A very simple ,healthy and easy to make soaked lentil and fresh vegetable salad. Can be served as a side dish, salad or for a healthy breakfast. Kosambari or koshambari is a typical south Indian salad made from pulses (split legumes) and seasoned with mustard seeds. The pulses generally used are split bengal gram (kadale bele in Kannada) and split Green gram (Hesaru bele in Kannada).

Instructions to make Guava kosambri(salad):
  1. Wash guava and chop it.
  2. Soak moong dal for 20 mins in hot water.
  3. Chop green chillies, coriander leaves finely.
  4. In a mixing bowl add chopped guava, soaked moong dal, chillies, coriander leaves, salt to taste, black salt and other masalas, lemon juice and mix it well and serve.
