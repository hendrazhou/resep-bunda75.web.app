The ingredients needed to make Italian nachos:
  1. Make ready parmesan cheese
  2. Prepare garlic toast
  3. Prepare 1 loaf bread of choice
  4. Make ready 1 tsp minced garlic
  5. Take 1/2 stick unsalted butter
  6. Take topping part
  7. Get 2 whole chicken breasts, diced
  8. Take 1 sweet onion, diced
  9. Get 2 bell peppers, diced
  10. Take 1 tbsp minced garlic
  11. Get 1 can diced tomatos
  12. Make ready salt, pepper, oregano, basil, white wine vinegar

Instructions to make Italian nachos:
  1. Saute onion, garlic, and pepper with salt.
  2. Add chicken, pepper, oregano, and basil. Cook till the chicken is done.
  3. Add can of tomatos and simmer.
  4. Melt butter with garlic in microwave.
  5. Slice bread and brush with melted garlic butter.
  6. Toast bread in a skillet till golden brown.
  7. Place garlic toast on plate and spoon topping over it to taste. Top with parmesian cheese and serve.
