Steps to make Banana boats in the ninja foodie grill:
  1. Heat grill on grill setting hi using the grill grate
  2. Cut banana in half with peal still on, don’t cut the peal on the bottom of the banana
  3. Place whatever toppings you like in between the slip banana
  4. Wrap banana in aluminum foil and place on grill for about 5-8 minutes or until is melted.
  5. Notes- when camping place on the outside of fire (not in it) remove with tongs let cool before opening very hot.
