The ingredients needed to make Easy Gingerbread Brownies w/ Chocolate-Banana Frosting:
  1. Get 17.5 oz Gingerbread cookie mix
  2. Make ready 1 stick melted butter
  3. Prepare 1 C coconut oil
  4. Get 1 C milk chocolate chips
  5. Get 1/4 C cocoa powder
  6. Take 2 eggs
  7. Prepare 8 oz cream cheese; softened
  8. Prepare 1/4 C cocoa powder
  9. Take 2 C powdered sugar
  10. Get 1 T banana extract
  11. Prepare 2 T melted butter

Steps to make Easy Gingerbread Brownies w/ Chocolate-Banana Frosting:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Whisk together gingerbread cookie mix, 1/4 C cocoa powder, coconut oil, eggs, and melted butter until blended.
  3. Fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Spray an 8 x 8 casserole dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  5. Bake for approximately 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Whisk together cream cheese, 1/4 C cocoa powder, powdered sugar, and butter until smooth. Fold in banana extract. Add a tiny bit of milk if necessary to adjust consistency.
