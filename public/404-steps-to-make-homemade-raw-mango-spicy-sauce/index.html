The ingredients needed to make Raw mango spicy sauce:
  1. Prepare 1 raw mango peel off and grated
  2. Take 1-2 green chilli
  3. Take 1 apple
  4. Make ready 2 cloves garlic
  5. Make ready 1 tablespoon ginger grated
  6. Make ready 2 tablespoon sugar
  7. Get Half tablespoon rock salt
  8. Get Pinch red chilli powder
  9. Take Pinch black pepper powder
  10. Prepare Pinch chaat masala
