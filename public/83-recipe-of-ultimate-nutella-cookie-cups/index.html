The ingredients needed to make Nutella Cookie Cups:
  1. Make ready 1 cup butter
  2. Prepare 1 cup brown sugar
  3. Get 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  4. Take 2 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  5. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  6. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 2 eggs
  8. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla
  9. Prepare 1 cup nutella

Instructions to make Nutella Cookie Cups:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Combine dry ingredients.
  3. Cream butter and sugars.
  4. Add eggs one at a time until fully combined. Beat in vanilla.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes in cupcake/muffin pans.
  7. When done, remove from oven and let sit for 1 minute. Make wells for nutella using a shot glass.
  8. Place nutella on the wells. Nutella will soften upon contact with the hot cookie making it easy to swirl the nutella.
