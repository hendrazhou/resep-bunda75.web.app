Steps to make Cinnamon Uglies:
  1. Mix 1 cup milk 1/4 cup sugar 1/4 cup butter in a saucepan heat to 110°F take off heat add yeast
  2. Mix in big bowl 1 cup sugar 3 1/2 cup flour 2 tablespoons syrup 1 tablespoon cinnamon 1/2 cup soft butter 1 egg beaten all together
  3. Mix #2 with #1 let set 1 hour
  4. While your dough sets mix all glaze ingredients together and set in refrigerator till uglies are done
  5. Pre heat oven to 375
  6. After dough has set roll it out and spread the filling all over it and roll it up and pinch off a little and place on pan do that till dough is gone they are not suppose to be perfect hints the name cinnamon uglies
  7. When done pour glaze over them
