The ingredients needed to make Banana Pudding:
  1. Prepare 35-45 Nilla Wafers
  2. Get 5-6 medium size ripe bananas sliced
  3. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar divided
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  5. Make ready Dash salt
  6. Prepare 4 eggs, separated, at room temperature
  7. Take 2 cups milk
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  9. Take Optional : top with additional nilla wafers or try my never fail meringue

Instructions to make Banana Pudding:
  1. Combine 1/2 cup sugar,flour and salt in top of double boiler. Stir in 4 egg yolks and milk; blend well.
  2. Cook, uncovered, over boiling water, stirring constantly; 5 minutes or until thickened. Remove from heat; stir in vanilla.
  3. Spread small amount on bottom of 1 and 1/2 quart casserole dish; cover with layer of nilla wafers. Top with layer of sliced bananas. Continue to layer wafers, bananas and custard to make 3 layers of each, ending with custard.
