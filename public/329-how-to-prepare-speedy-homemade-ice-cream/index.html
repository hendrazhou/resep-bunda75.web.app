Steps to make Homemade ice cream:
  1. For the condensed milk, add the milk & sugar in a pot, stir continuously over low heat until the sugar dissolve.
  2. Continue stirring to avoid lumps until a semi thick liquid is formed. Tk off the heat & leave to cool
  3. For the ice cream. Pour the milk into a bowl, add the whip cream powder & mix using a hand mixer
  4. Add the vanilla flavor, icing sugar, condensed milk & mix
  5. To make chocolate ice cream, I used Milo
  6. Pour into containers & freeze for 2hrs, churn & freeze for 6-8hrs.
