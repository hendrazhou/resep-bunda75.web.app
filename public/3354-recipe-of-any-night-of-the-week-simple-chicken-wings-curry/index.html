Instructions to make Simple Chicken Wings Curry:
  1. Marinate chicken wings with salt and pepper fo 10-15 minutes.
  2. Saute ginger,garlic,onion in oil add chicken,salt and pepper to taste.cooked for 5 mins
  3. Add carrots,potato and squash cooked for 5 minutes
  4. Get a bowl add coconut powder, milk and curry powder depends on you if you put all the curry powder and add 1 cup of water mix well
  5. Add to the pan and simmer for 5-10 minutes after 10 minutes add milk simmer for 1 minute
  6. Serve and enjoy
