Steps to make Green guava soup:
  1. Wash and chop the green guava and pressure cook for five mins.
  2. Once cooled,strain and churn in the blender.
  3. Heat butter in a pan.
  4. 'Add guava puree,black pepper powder,salt and sugar and lastly we add milk.
  5. Once boiled,switch off the gas.
  6. Add cardamom powder and lemon juice.
  7. Serve hot
