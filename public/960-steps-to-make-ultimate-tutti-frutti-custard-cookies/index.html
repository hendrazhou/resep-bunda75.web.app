The ingredients needed to make Tutti Frutti Custard Cookies:
  1. Get 1-1/2 cup all purpose flour
  2. Make ready 4 tbsp vanilla custard powder
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp fine granulated sugar
  4. Get 12 tbsp unsalted butter, room temperature
  5. Get Pinch salt
  6. Take 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp lemon essence
  8. Make ready 3/4 cup tutti frutti

Instructions to make Tutti Frutti Custard Cookies:
  1. Cream butter and sugar in a large bowl till pale in colour with hand held mixer. Add lemon essence. Give it a quick whisk.
  2. Combine flour, custard powder, cardamom powder, tutti frutti, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Add flour mixture to the creamed butter in 3 parts, whisking after each addition.
  4. Bring the dough together and roll it in a log shape.
  5. Wrap it in a cling film tying the two ends thoroughly and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  6. Preheat oven to 350 deg F. Line a baking tray with oven proof parchment paper.
  7. Unwrap the dough and cut it in 24 equal sized squares.
  8. Place them on the tray leaving a small gap between them.
  9. Bake for 20 minutes flipping the cookies midway through baking time.
  10. Remove from oven and cool them thoroughly. Once cooled, they will harden.
