The ingredients needed to make Mojito Lime Grilled Chicken:
  1. Make ready 4 piece Chicken
  2. Get 5 garlic cloves, minced
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  4. Get 1/4 cup Orange juice
  5. Get 1/4 cup Lime juice
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup canola oil
  7. Take 3 tsp Salt & Pepper
  8. Get 1/2 tsp ground cumin

  10. Get 1 1/2 tbsp cilantro, chopped
