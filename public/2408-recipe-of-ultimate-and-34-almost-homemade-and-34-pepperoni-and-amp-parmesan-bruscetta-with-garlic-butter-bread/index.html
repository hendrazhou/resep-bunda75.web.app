The ingredients needed to make "Almost Homemade" Pepperoni & Parmesan Bruscetta with Garlic Butter Bread:
  1. Get 14 oz store bought Bruschetta (I prefer Sabra brand)
  2. Take 1/3 cup shredded parmesan
  3. Prepare 16 slices pepperoni, chopped very small
  4. Make ready 1 loaf of french bread sliced in 1/4 inch slices
  5. Get 1/2 cup butter melted
  6. Prepare 1 tsp each of garlic salt and Italian seasoning

Instructions to make "Almost Homemade" Pepperoni & Parmesan Bruscetta with Garlic Butter Bread:
  1. Mix brushetta, pepperoni and Parmesan cheese. I do this a few hrs ahead of time so the flavors combine.
  2. Preheat oven to 325. Place French bread slices on a cookie sheet. Melt butter, garlic salt and Ital seasoning.
  3. Brush bread slices with melted butter and bake for 5-7 min or until slightly golden.
  4. When bread slices are cooled down arrange on a serving platter with "homemade" brushetta in the center.
