The ingredients needed to make AMIEs Melon Smoothie:
  1. Get 1 melon, mashed
  2. Make ready 3 cup milk
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup plain or vanilla yogurt (optional)
  4. Prepare 3 tsp honey, or to taste
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup ice cubes
