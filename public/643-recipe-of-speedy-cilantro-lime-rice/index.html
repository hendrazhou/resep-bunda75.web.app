The ingredients needed to make Cilantro Lime Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 cups rice
  2. Get 2 tsp salt
  3. Prepare 1 tsp olive oil
  4. Get 2 bay leaves
  5. Get 2 tbsp finely chopped cilantro
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp lime juice
  7. Take 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps to make Cilantro Lime Rice:
  1. Add 1 tsp salt and the olive oil to your rice mixture. Cook the rice as per the instructions on your rice cooker or the bag (with the bay leaves on top).
  2. Strain out the bay leaves. Mix in the cilantro, salt, lemon, and lime juices. Serve with burrito ingredients for a delicious burrito bowl!
