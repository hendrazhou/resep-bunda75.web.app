The ingredients needed to make Tricolor mousse:
  1. Take 150 gm White chocolate
  2. Make ready 150 gm amul cream
  3. Get 100 gm whipped cream
  4. Prepare 2 drop magic powder colour green
  5. Get 5 gm chopped pistachios
  6. Prepare 2 drop magic powder colour orange
  7. Prepare 5 gm tutti frutti

Steps to make Tricolor mousse:
  1. Firstly whipped the cream till peak consistency
  2. Warm the amul cream and mix the white chocolate then divide into three part.
  3. At first part mix the green color and chopped pista. At second part mix the orange color and tutti frutti. White chocolate with cream is third part
  4. Add whipped cream in all mixture separately then transfer into piping bag
  5. Assembly: Take a glass fill the mouse in layered way
