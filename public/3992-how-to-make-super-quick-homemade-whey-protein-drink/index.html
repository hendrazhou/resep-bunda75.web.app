The ingredients needed to make Whey Protein Drink:
  1. Make ready 1 Handful of blueberries
  2. Prepare 1 Handful of blackberries
  3. Get 1 Handful of baby leaf spinach
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp honey; or to taste
  5. Take 1 cup plain vogurt; or whatever flavored one you'd like
  6. Get 1 Juice of your choice, eye balling if you like it thick or thin
  7. Prepare 1 provided spoonful of whatever type of whey protein you use or prefer

Instructions to make Whey Protein Drink:
  1. In a blender combine all of the listed ingredients and blend until smooth.
  2. At this point you can strain the mix through a strainer if you'd like to take out any chunks that may still be there from the berries.
  3. Add ice cubes in your cup, then pour drink on top.
  4. Or, if you prefer then you can blend ice with your drink mix to make it more of a protein shake.
  5. As Always, Enjoy!!
