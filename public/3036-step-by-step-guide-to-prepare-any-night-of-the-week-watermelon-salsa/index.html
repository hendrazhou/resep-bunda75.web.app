The ingredients needed to make Watermelon salsa:
  1. Get 3 cup chopped watermelon
  2. Take 1/2 red onion
  3. Take 1 cup chopped tomato.
  4. Prepare 1 small jalapeno. seeded n minced
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup fresh cilantro minced
  6. Get 1 tbsp basil
  7. Take 1 tsp lime juice
