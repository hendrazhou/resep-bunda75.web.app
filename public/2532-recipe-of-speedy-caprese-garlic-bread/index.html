The ingredients needed to make Caprese Garlic Bread:
  1. Make ready 1 Loaf Ciabatta bread
  2. Make ready 4 Tbsp Salted butter.
  3. Get 3 Garlic cloves minced
  4. Get 1 packet Fresh mozzarella cheese sliced
  5. Get 1/2 Cup Blasmic vinegar
  6. Make ready 2 Tomatoes in sliced
  7. Take Slat as per taste
  8. Make ready Freshly ground pepper/Pepper powder As per taste
  9. Make ready 1/4 Cup Fresh basil chopped

Steps to make Caprese Garlic Bread:
  1. Step for preparing Blasmic reduction:-
  2. Place the blasmic vinegar in a small sauce pan.
  3. Bring the vinegar to a boil|, decrease the heat to low,and simmer.
  4. Stirring occasionally, until the mixture is reduced by about half.
  5. This should take about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Set aside to cool down.
  7. Steps for preparing garlic bread:-
  8. Preheat oven at 220 *C for 10 minutes.
  9. Horizontally cut the bread loaf.
  10. Place both sides of the baguette on a large baking sheet with the cut side up.
  11. In a small bowl, combine butter and garlic.
  12. Spread garlic butter evenly on bread halves.
  13. Place the mozzarella cheese slices on top of the bread.
  14. Bake the bread for 12-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
  15. Remove the bread from oven.
  16. Top the bread with tomato slices.
  17. Season with salt and pepper.
  18. Add the fresh basil and drizzle with blasmic reduction.
  19. Cut into slices and serve.
