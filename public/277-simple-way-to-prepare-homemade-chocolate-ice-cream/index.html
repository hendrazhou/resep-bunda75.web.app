Steps to make Chocolate ice cream:
  1. Whip the cream till soft peaks form
  2. Add condensed milk and fold in the whipping cream
  3. At this point you can separate if you want two different flavours ie vanilla and chocolate
  4. Add cocoa powder to the mixture + chocolate essence
  5. Put in an airtight container and freeze for 6 hrs or more if not ready
  6. Enjoy as dessert
