The ingredients needed to make Dairy Free Watermelon, Pawpaw and Banana Smoothie with Kale:
  1. Take 1/2 Cup Oatmeal/Oats
  2. Get 2 Cups Watermelon, peeled and chopped
  3. Make ready 1/2 Cup Pawpaw, seeded, peeled and chopped
  4. Prepare 1/2 Cup Kale, chopped with the vein removed
  5. Make ready 1 Cup Ice
  6. Get 1 banana, peeled and chopped

Instructions to make Dairy Free Watermelon, Pawpaw and Banana Smoothie with Kale:
  1. Prepare all your ingredients by peeling and chopping them.
  2. Add all the fruits (watermelon, paw-paw and banana), kale and oats to the blender and blend until almost smooth.
  3. Add the ice and blend until smooth. Enjoy!
