The ingredients needed to make Cold coffee-with soaked sabja:
  1. Get 1 1/2 cup milk
  2. Take 2-3 tbsp sugar
  3. Get 11/2 tsp Coffee
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp chocolate syrup
  5. Make ready Few chopped dry fruits
  6. Take Few tutti frutti
  7. Prepare 1 tsp sabja
  8. Take As required ice cream(optional)
  9. Take 4-6 ice cubes

Steps to make Cold coffee-with soaked sabja:
  1. First soak sabja with 2tbsp water and keep aside for 10-15 mins.
  2. In a mixer jar add coffee sugar ice cubes,chocolate syrup and chilled milk. Churn it for a minute.
  3. Add 1-1tsp sabja to two glass.
  4. Pour coffee to the glasses.
  5. Then garnish with dry fruits and tutti fruti. Add ice cream if you wish too.
  6. Serve chilled.
