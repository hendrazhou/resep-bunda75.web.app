The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti mug cake:
  1. Get 3 tbsp All purpose flour (Maida)
  2. Get 1 tbsp Corn flour
  3. Make ready 2 tsp Sugar
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp Baking powder
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp Curd
  6. Prepare 1 pinch Salt
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp Milk
  8. Get 2 tbsp Oil
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp Vanilla
  10. Get 1 tbsp Tutti frutti

Steps to make Tutti frutti mug cake:
  1. METHOD FOR MAKING MUG CAKES: - Mix all the dry ingredients – all purpose flour, corn flour, sugar, baking powder, salt in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, oil, curd and vanilla. Do not over mix.
  3. Add the mixed wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and make a batter.
  4. Add tutti frutti and fold gently. I added half in the batter and half on the top.
  5. Transfer to a microwave oven safe bowl/ ramekin/ mug. Bake it till it's done.
