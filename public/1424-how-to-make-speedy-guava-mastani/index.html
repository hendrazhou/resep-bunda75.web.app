Steps to make Guava Mastani:
  1. GUAVA MILK SHAKE RECIPE: - in a large blender add 1 cup ripe Guava along with ½ cup thick cold milk. - also add 2 scoops of vanilla ice creams and 1 scoop of Vanilla ice cream.
  2. Finally add 2 tsp sugar and blend. - blend smooth to form a thick milk shake. keep aside
  3. GUAVA MASTANI RECIPE: - in a large glass, firstly add some strawberry syrup and a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  4. Next, pour prepared Guava milk shake, leave space at the top for ice cream scoop and nuts.
  5. Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream - now garnish with chopped cashews, almonds and strawberry syrup.
  6. Serve Guava mastani chilled with a spoon and straw.
