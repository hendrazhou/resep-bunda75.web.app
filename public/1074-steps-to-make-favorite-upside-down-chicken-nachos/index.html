The ingredients needed to make Upside down chicken nachos:
  1. Prepare Chicken tenders
  2. Take 1 jar salsa 16oz
  3. Make ready 1 can Refried beans
  4. Prepare 1 pack taco seasoning or 2 tablespoons of homemade taco seasoning
  5. Take Tortilla chips
  6. Get Cheese
  7. Prepare Topping sour cream any thing you like

Instructions to make Upside down chicken nachos:
  1. Put salsa in first. Add the chicken tenders. Cook on hi for 15-20 with a quick release. (for frozen chicken cook a little longer.)
  2. Break up the chicken, (be careful on what you used don’t mess up the inter pot.) it breaks up easy. (Use silicone)
  3. Add refried beans, taco seasoning, salt to taste, mix until well combined.
  4. Layer with chips, cheese another layer of chips, ending with more cheese.
  5. Air crisp for 3-5 mins, check of so you don’t burn the chips and cheese, this does not take long… just want to melt the cheese.
  6. Notes- in the air crisp I burnt my cheese and had to remove some of the chips, and I just added d more cheese. this burns easy. The recipe said 10 min to crisp it wasn’t even 5 mins and it was burnt, that was a big change I made.
