The ingredients needed to make Cheese garlic bread (without yeast):
  1. Make ready 125 gms All purpose flour
  2. Make ready 70 gms curd
  3. Take 1/2 tsp sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Get 3-4 tsp milk
  7. Get 1 medium onion
  8. Prepare 1 medium capsicum
  9. Take 60-70 tsp Cheese
  10. Take 6 tsp butter
  11. Make ready 2-3 tsp garlic paste
  12. Get As required Mixed herbs
  13. Prepare 5-6 coriander leaves

Instructions to make Cheese garlic bread (without yeast):
  1. In all-purpose flour add salt, sugar, baking soda, curd, mixed herbs and garlic paste. Mix well and knead it into a soft dough using milk. Apply some oil on the dough, cover it and keep aside for one to one and a half hour.
  2. In melted butter add garlic paste, coriander leaves and mixed herbs. Add cheese and mixed herbs in finely chopped onion and capsicum. (add salt if you want).
  3. Roll the dough into into a circle. Apply garlic butter mixture. On one side add onion, capsicum, cheese mixture.
  4. Add some water on the edge of the dough and seal it completely. Apply garlic butter mixture and mixed herbs. Little cuts on above.
  5. Preheat oven at 180° for 10 minutes. Brush butter on oven pan. Bake bread for 10-15 minutes. It's ready! Again apply garlic butter on top and serve hot.
