The ingredients needed to make Cucumber Salsa:
  1. Take 2 cucumbers peeled, seeded, and chopped
  2. Prepare 2 tomatoes seeded and finely chopped
  3. Take 1 small onion finely chopped
  4. Get 3 tbsp minced fresh cilantro
  5. Prepare 1 jalapeno, seeded and diced
  6. Prepare 3/4 cup diced bell pepper
  7. Make ready 1 clove garlic, minced
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup reduced fat sour cream
  9. Prepare 1 -2 tablespoons lemon juice
  10. Take 1 1/2 tsp lime juice
  11. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp seasoned salt
  13. Get 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
