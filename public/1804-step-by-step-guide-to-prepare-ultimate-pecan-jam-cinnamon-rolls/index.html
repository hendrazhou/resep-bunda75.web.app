The ingredients needed to make Pecan/Jam Cinnamon Rolls:
  1. Make ready Dough:
  2. Get 3 1/2-4 cups all purposes flour
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup warm water
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 1/3 cup butter (unsalted)
  8. Get 1/4 ounce packet of active dry yeast
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup scalded milk
  10. Prepare Filling:
  11. Get 1/3 cup sugar + extra for pan
  12. Make ready 3/4 cup crushed pecans
  13. Make ready 1/2 cup melted butter + more for pan
  14. Prepare 2 Tbsp ground cinnamon (or more if you like)
  15. Get 1/2 cup spreadable jam (any flavor)
  16. Get Glaze:
  17. Get 2 cups powdered sugar
  18. Prepare 4 Tbsp butter (softened)
  19. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  20. Make ready 3-6 Tbsp hot water

Steps to make Pecan/Jam Cinnamon Rolls:
  1. In this recipe I will be using Pecans and a 10oz jar of spreadable and natural Polanar Seedless Raspberry Jam. Any flavor can be substituted.
  2. Heat oven to 350°
  3. In a bowl dissolve yeast in warm water and set aside. In a large bowl mix milk, sugar, salt, melted butter and egg. Add two cups flour and mix until smooth. Add yeast warm mixture. Add remaining flour to make dough thicker. Knead dough on lightly floured flat surface for 5 minutes. Roll into a ball and place in well greased bowl. Cover and let rise until double in size (1 to 1½ hours)
  4. Remove dough from bowl and roll out on a floured surface into a 15X9 inch rectangle. With a streight edge, cut edges with a pizza cutter to make clean edges all the way around to make uniform. Place removed edges on remaining dough and roll onto dough surface. Brush melted butter over dough surface. Sprinkle mixed cinnamon and sugar on surface and add a thin layer of jam on top. Add a layer of crushed Pecans. At the 15" inch side, roll dough and pinch the ends. Cut dough into 12 or 15 pieces.
  5. Coat the bottom of baking pan with butter and sprinkle with sugar. Place cinnamon roll slices close together in pan and let rise until doubled in size (about 45 minutes)
  6. When done rising, bake at 350° for about 30 minutes until golden brown
  7. While baking, mix powdered sugar, butter and vanilla. Add hot water 1 Tbsp at a time until Glaze appears to be smooth. Let rolls cool and drizzle Glaze over top of rolls. Your Rolls are amazing.
