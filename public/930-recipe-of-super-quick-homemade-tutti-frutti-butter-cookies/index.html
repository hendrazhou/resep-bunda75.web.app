The ingredients needed to make Tutti Frutti Butter Cookies:
  1. Get 1 cup maida
  2. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup milk powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar powdered
  5. Take 80 gm butter
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp custard powder vanilla flavour
  7. Take 1 tsp Pineapple essence
  8. Make ready 3/4 cup Tutti frutti
  9. Get as needed Milk as required to make dough

Instructions to make Tutti Frutti Butter Cookies:
  1. Beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Sieve maida, baking powder, custard powder and milk powder.
  3. Add this maida mix into butter mix.
  4. Add essence.
  5. Add tutti frutti.
  6. Add milk to make dough little by little.
  7. After making the dough make logs and keep in refrezerator.
  8. Meanwhile preheat the oven at 175C for 15 min.
  9. Take out the logs from freeze cut into desired shapes and bake for 20 min or till the bottom of the cookies change to light brown colour.
  10. After they are completely cooled, keep them in air tight container.
