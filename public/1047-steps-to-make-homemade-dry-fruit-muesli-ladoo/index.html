The ingredients needed to make Dry fruit Muesli Ladoo:
  1. Get 1 bowl Muesli
  2. Take 1 cup dates(pitted)
  3. Prepare 2 tbspn tutti frutti
  4. Take 1 tbspn ghee

Steps to make Dry fruit Muesli Ladoo:
  1. Remove seeds from dates and grind to a coarse mixture
  2. Dry roast the Muesli for 5 minutes. I've added extra almonds and ground them separately.
  3. Heat ghee in a nonstick pan add the ground dates and mix the Muesli and tutti frutti.
  4. When slightly warm make into balls and store in airtight container.
