The ingredients needed to make Fire & Ice Bananas:
  1. Make ready 1/4 Cup Butter
  2. Make ready 2 Tsp Tabasco Sauce
  3. Make ready 2 Ripe Bananas
  4. Prepare Cinnamon
  5. Prepare 3/4 Cup Dark Brown Sugar
  6. Prepare 1/4 Cup Whipping Cream
  7. Make ready Vanilla Ice Cream

Instructions to make Fire & Ice Bananas:
  1. Melt Butter in frying pan, stir in sugar and Tabasco Sauce until smooth. Bring to boil and cook until thick.
  2. Whisk in Whipping Cream. Slice Bananas and add to the syrup. Cook for about 1-minute spooning syrup all over and place in serving bowls.
  3. Top with Vanilla ice cream and sprinkle Cinnamon on top.
