The ingredients needed to make Mango Cool:
  1. Get 1 Mango
  2. Make ready 1 small Banana
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Yoghurt 0%
  4. Get 1 tsp Honey
  5. Prepare 1 pinch Cinnamon
  6. Get 1 tbsp Goji berries

Steps to make Mango Cool:
  1. Wash the fruits. Peel of mango and cut for small pieces. Do the same thing with a banana.
  2. Blend it quickly with honey and cinnamon until ingredients have combined.
  3. Take a tall glass, put 3 big tbl spoons of banana-mango mixture on the bottom. Then put 2 tbl spoons of yoghurt on the mixture and again 2 tbl spoons of mixture on the yoghurt. Now You have a fancy 3 coloured dessert! :)
  4. Now pour the Goji berries on the top of Your dessert.
  5. Put for 15 minutes to the fridge and serve cool!
