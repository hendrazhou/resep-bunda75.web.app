The ingredients needed to make Salsa Chicken:
  1. Get 5 piece boneless chicken breast
  2. Get 1 envelope taco seasoning
  3. Prepare 1 jar of salsa ( whatever you like)
  4. Take 1 taco bells spicy ranchero sauce
  5. Prepare 1 shredded chedder cheese
  6. Make ready 1 tortilla strips

Instructions to make Salsa Chicken:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Spray pan or glass dish with non stick spray.
  2. Sprinkle taco seasoning over chicken. (I try to coat it on both sides) place in oven for 20 mins. May take longer depending on thickness of chicken.
  3. After 20 mins add the jar of salsa and sprinkle chedder cheese over chicken. Place back in the oven till the cheese has melted usually another 10 mins.
  4. After chicken is done pour spicey ranchero sauce over chicken and sprinkle tortilla strips on top.
  5. I usually make Spanish Rice with it and its delish!! Hope you enjoy ☺
