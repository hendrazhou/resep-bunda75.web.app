The ingredients needed to make Cheese garlic bread:
  1. Make ready 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  2. Make ready 2 tablespoons butter
  3. Take 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  4. Make ready 1 loaf crusty bread, split
  5. Make ready 3 tablespoons grated cheese, Parmigiano or Romano, optional
  6. Take as required Chopped fresh parsley

Instructions to make Cheese garlic bread:
  1. Combine garlic, butter, and oil in a microwave-safe dish or in a small saucepan. Heat garlic and butter and oil in the microwave for 1 minute or in a small pot over moderate-low heat for 3 minutes.
  2. Toast split bread under the broiler. Remove bread when it is toasted golden brown in colour. Brush bread liberally with garlic oil. Sprinkle with cheese, if using, and parsley. If you added cheese, return to broiler and brown 30 seconds. Cut into chunks and serve.
  3. Your garlic bread is ready
