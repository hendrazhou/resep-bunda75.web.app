The ingredients needed to make Tutti fruitti cake baked in pateela:
  1. Get 2 cups flour
  2. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Get 4 eggs
  4. Make ready 1 cup powdered sugar
  5. Get 1 cup oil
  6. Make ready Few drops yellow food colour
  7. Prepare Tutti frutti as req
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp maida
  9. Get 1 tsp vanilla/lemon essence

Steps to make Tutti fruitti cake baked in pateela:
  1. Subse pehle maida aur baking powder ko 2 bar chan lein.
  2. Ab ek bowl mein 4 eggs ko volume double hone tak beat karen.
  3. Phir sugar add karen aur creamy hone tak beat karen.ab essence aur oil add karen aur 2 min beat karen.
  4. Mold ko grease kar lein aur pateele ko koi b stand rakh ke preheat hone rakh dein for 15 min medium flame pe.
  5. Ab dry ingredients ko eggs mein fold karen ache se aur food colour add kar ke mix karen.
  6. Tutti frutti ko maida me coat kar add karen batter mein aur batter ko mold mein dal ke preheated pateele me 40 min slow medium flame pe bake kar lein.
  7. Thanda hone pe mold se nikal ke cut karen aur serve karen.
  8. Soft and yummy cake enjoy karen.
