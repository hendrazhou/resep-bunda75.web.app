Instructions to make Mango muffins:
  1. For better results work with all liquid ingredients at room temperature (e.g. eggs, butter, etc.)
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together (e.g. brown sugar, granulated sugar, bread flour, quick oats, etc.)
  3. Mix all liquid ingredients together (e.g. oil, mango puree, eggs, etc.)
  4. Incorporate liquid mix into dry ingredients. Add lemon zest and diced mangos
  5. Pour batter into greased muffin pan (3/4 full).
  6. For streusel: Mix all indicated ingredients until crumble (preffered by hand)
  7. Add streusel topping if desired.
  8. Bake on preheated oven at 400°F for 15 minutes
