The ingredients needed to make Creamy salsa dip from scratch:
  1. Prepare 1 roma tomato
  2. Take 1 1/2 tablespoons onion
  3. Prepare 1 clove garlic minced
  4. Make ready Sprinkle of salt and pepper
  5. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon mrs.dash seasoning
  6. Take 1/8 teaspoon basil
  7. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon cream cheese
  9. Get to taste lemon juice
  10. Take 1/2 teaspoon white vinegar
  11. Get 1 tablespoon grated cucumber
  12. Make ready 1 tablespoon grated apple (any kind)

Steps to make Creamy salsa dip from scratch:
  1. Chop tomato and onion finely
  2. Mince garlic and add to tomato and onion
  3. Grate in cucumber and apple
  4. Add lemon juice and vinegar and mix
  5. Add salt, pepper, mrs.dash, crushed red pepper flakes, and basil to mixture
  6. Add cream cheese and stir lightly until cream cheese is mixed in
  7. Chill for best taste and serve with tortilla chips
