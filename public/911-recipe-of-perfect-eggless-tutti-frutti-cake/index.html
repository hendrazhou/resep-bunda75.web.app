The ingredients needed to make Eggless Tutti Frutti Cake:
  1. Take 2 cups all purpose flour/maida
  2. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 1 cup oil/butter
  4. Make ready 1 cup milk
  5. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence
  6. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  8. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  9. Prepare 50 g tutti frutti
  10. Prepare Few drops Yellow food colour

This fruit cake recipe is quick and eggless and results is a super moist vanilla cake studded with tutti frutti. Eggless Tutti Frutti Cake is moist, light and butter free. This recipe is perfect for that delicious slice of cake to go with your cuppa! This Eggless Tutti Frutti Cake is just perfect!

Instructions to make Eggless Tutti Frutti Cake:
  1. In a large bowl cream the butter and sugar with the help of a blender until light and fluffy.
  2. Add milk, vanilla essence, oil/butter, pinch of salt, few drops of yellow food colour to it and mix well.
  3. In another bowl take maida, baking powder, baking soda, three-fourth of the tutti frutti and mix them properly.
  4. Add this maida mixture to the cream and mix it with cut and fold method for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Grease the baking tray with oil/butter. Pour the cake batter into it. Sprinkle rest of the tutti fruttis on top.
  6. Preheat oven to 180° c. Place the baking tray in the oven and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  7. Cool it for 15 minutes and slice then.
  8. Eggless tutti frutti cake is ready to serve.
