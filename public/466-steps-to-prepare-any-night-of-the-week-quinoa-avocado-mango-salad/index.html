The ingredients needed to make Quinoa Avocado Mango Salad:
  1. Take For the salad:
  2. Take 1 cup cooked quinoa
  3. Take 1 large avocado, pitted and diced
  4. Take 1 cup frozen mango, thawed and diced
  5. Take 1 cup frozen kernel corn, thawed
  6. Get 1 tbsp red onion, diced
  7. Take For the dressing:
  8. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp red wine vinegar
  10. Get 1/4 tsp honey dijon mustard
  11. Prepare 1/4 tsp honey
  12. Make ready pinch cumin
  13. Get pinch cayenne
  14. Make ready pinch coriander
  15. Take to taste salt and pepper
  16. Prepare For the toppings:
  17. Prepare 2 tbsp honey roasted sliced almonds
  18. Take 1 tbsp feta cheese
