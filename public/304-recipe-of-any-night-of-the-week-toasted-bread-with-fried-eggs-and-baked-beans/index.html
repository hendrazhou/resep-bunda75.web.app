The ingredients needed to make Toasted Bread with Fried Eggs and Baked Beans:
  1. Make ready 12 Slices bread, Mayonnaise, Butter, 3 Eggs,

Steps to make Toasted Bread with Fried Eggs and Baked Beans:
  1. Break one of the eggs and pour into a bowl. Add the butter and mayonnaise and mix together properly
  2. Pick up a slice and spread the mix on it.
  3. Take another slice and cover the slice with the mix. Repeat same process with the remaining slices.
  4. Place two sets in the toaster. Close and allow to toast. Repeat same process with the remaining sets.
  5. Remove the toasted bread after being well toasted.
  6. Cut the toasted bread into two halves and set in the plate.
  7. Break the two eggs. Chop the fresh pepper, tomatoes, onions and add to the eggs. Add half a cube, salt and mix together.
  8. Add groundnut oil to the frying pan, allow to get hot. Pour the egg mixture and allow to fry.
  9. Prepare your tea by adding milk, honey, Milo into a cup, add hot water and stir.
  10. Pour your baked beans into a sieve. Serve with the toasted bread, fried eggs with tea.
