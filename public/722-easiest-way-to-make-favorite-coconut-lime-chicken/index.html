The ingredients needed to make Coconut lime chicken:
  1. Make ready marinade
  2. Take 14 1/2 oz coconut milk
  3. Make ready 2 medium limes
  4. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp white pepper
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  7. Take 2 tsp curry powder
  8. Make ready 1 tsp minced garlic
  9. Take chicken
  10. Make ready 6 large chicken breasts, boneless and skinless

Instructions to make Coconut lime chicken:
  1. Zest and squeeze the juice of the limes
  2. Mix in a bowl the coconut milk, lime juice, zest, salt, pepper, ginger, curry powder, and garlic.
  3. Marinate the chicken in a ziplock bag. In a refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
  4. Preheat oven 400° Fahrenheit. Put on a baking pan, and bake 30-35 minutes. Let sit 3-5 minutes, serve hope you enjoy.
  5. If you use the left over marinade boil 7 minutes. I found the chicken turned out moist and delicious without the extra from marinade.
