The ingredients needed to make Beef and Barley Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 lb beef (any steak will do)
  2. Get 1 c uncooked barley
  3. Make ready 5 medium potatoes
  4. Prepare 3-4 carrots
  5. Get 1 medium onion
  6. Get 2 tbsp minced garlic
  7. Make ready 7-8 cups beef broth ( can be replaced with bullion and water)
  8. Make ready 1 tsp parsley
  9. Take 1 tsp celery seed
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp pepper
  11. Take to taste salt
  12. Take 2 tbsp butter (optional)

Steps to make Beef and Barley Soup:
  1. Prepare steak by cutting into 1/2 inch pieces.
  2. Prepare vegetables by cutting into 1/2 inch pieces.
  3. Add all ingredients together in a large pot. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to just below medium heat. Allow soup to simmer for 30-45 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  4. Optional cooking method: combine all ingredients in a crock pot and cook on low for 5-6 hours or on high for 3-4 hours. Meat will be more tender using this method.
