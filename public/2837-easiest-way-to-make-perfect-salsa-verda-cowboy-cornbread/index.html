The ingredients needed to make Salsa verda cowboy cornbread:
  1. Get 1 cup salsa verda
  2. Take 1 lb ground meat
  3. Get 1 box cornbread mix
  4. Prepare 1 egg
  5. Take 1/2 cup milk
  6. Make ready 1 can sweet corn drained
  7. Take 1 lb shredded cheese
  8. Get 1 can refried beans

Steps to make Salsa verda cowboy cornbread:
  1. First cook up your ground meat. Drain grease. Return meat to skillet and add salsa verda till well mixed, set aside.
  2. In a bowl mix egg, milk, cornbread mix and drained can corn. Mix well. Set aside.
  3. In a baking dish spread refried beans on the bottom. Then sprinkle on shredded cheese about 1/2 a cup. Then spread out meat mixture. Add the rest of cheese. Then pour on the cornbread mix.
  4. Bake in oven on 350° for 30 minutes. Then remove from oven and cut and serve. To make it extra yummy top with salsa of choice, sour cream, diced tomatoes, and/or avocados
