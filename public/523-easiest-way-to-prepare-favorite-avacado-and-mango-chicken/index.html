The ingredients needed to make Avacado and mango chicken:
  1. Prepare 2 large chicken breast
  2. Make ready 1 lemon pepper
  3. Take 1/4 cup chopped onion
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup green peper chopped
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp lemon juice
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp lime juice
  7. Get 2 mangos chopped
  8. Prepare 2 avacado chopped

Steps to make Avacado and mango chicken:
  1. Slice chicken breast in half to make 4 thin pieces, season with lemon pepper
  2. Add 2 tablespoon of olive oil to skillet and cook chicken on med heat till done
  3. Meanwhile mix together the rest of ingredients
  4. When chicken is cooked thoroughly take out of skillet and top with mango Avacado mixture and serve
