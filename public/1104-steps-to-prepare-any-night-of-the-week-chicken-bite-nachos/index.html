  1. Make ready 1 packet Nachos
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup garlic butter
  3. Prepare 1 cup bite sized chicken
  4. Make ready To taste Red chilli flakes
  5. Prepare to taste Salt
  6. Prepare 1 cup bell peppers julliens
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Mayonnaise
  8. Make ready to taste Orgeno accd
  9. Prepare as needed Coriander for garnishing

  3. Now take a platter arrange Nachos there add chicken filling in it add julliens and coriander leaves for garnishing serve it
