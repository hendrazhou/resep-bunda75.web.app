The ingredients needed to make Matcha smoothie bowl:
  1. Make ready 1 frozen banana
  2. Make ready 1 frozen avocado
  3. Get 2-4 dates
  4. Make ready 1-2 cups soy milk
  5. Get 1 tablespoon matcha (depending on quality of matcha)
  6. Make ready Yogurt
  7. Make ready Seasonal fruits
  8. Get Muesli
  9. Take Dried berries
  10. Take Chia seeds
  11. Prepare Almonds

Instructions to make Matcha smoothie bowl:
  1. Blend the banana, avocado, soy milk, dates, and matcha together (you can add chia seeds here if you want). Added dates depends on your level of sweetness, more dates- the sweeter it is.
  2. Place the smoothie mixture in a bowl.
  3. Top with yogurt, chia, seasonal fruits, dried berries, muesli and almonds (optional).
