The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti Curd Cake No Egg:
  1. Prepare 250 gm curd
  2. Prepare 2 cup Maida (All-purpose flour)
  3. Prepare 1 cup sugar powder
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup oil or butter
  5. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanila essence
  7. Make ready 1 tbs tutti- frutti
  8. Prepare 2 tsp dry fruits chopped like (almonds, pistachios, cashew)

Instructions to make Tutti frutti Curd Cake No Egg:
  1. First we take a bowl. Add curd, maida, sugar and oil. mix them well.
  2. Beat eat for 5 min. Add vanila ess, soda.
  3. Add some tuti- fruty and dry fruits in it.
  4. Grease a cake mould with butter and pour batter in it.spread some tuti- fruty and dry fruits on top.
  5. Bake it at 180 degree for 30- 35 min in microwave.
  6. Check it with toothpic. prick it in cake if becomes clear, it means cake is ready.
  7. Cool it and demould it and serve.
