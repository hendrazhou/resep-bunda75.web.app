The ingredients needed to make Cozy Chamomile Smoothie:
  1. Prepare 10 pitted prunes
  2. Get 3/4 cup Chamomile tea ( I used two tea bags for a stronger flavor)
  3. Take 1 Banana
  4. Take 1 Orange
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup Greek Honey Yogurt
  6. Make ready 3/4 cup Goat milk (healthy & nutritious you can use whatever milk you like)

Steps to make Cozy Chamomile Smoothie:
  1. Put the prunes in a heatproof bowl and pour over the hot tea. Leave to stand for 30 minutes or until the tea is cold.
  2. Peel the banana and cut it into chunks.
  3. Cut the peel and pith away from the orange and divide into segments.
  4. Tip the prunes and tea into a blender, add the banana and orange and blend until smooth.
  5. Add the yogurt and milk and blend again until combined.
  6. Pour into glasses.
