The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Get 2 bananas
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup honey
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp almond milk
  5. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Make ready 2 cup whole wheat flour
  7. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Take 1/4 cup Greek yoghurt

Steps to make Banana Bread:
  1. Put all the ingredients into the bowl
  2. Use a mixer to mix them well for 4 minutes
  3. Preheat the oven to 350F
  4. Spray the loaf pan with organic olive oil
  5. Put the mixture into the loaf pan
  6. Bake for about 45 minutes
