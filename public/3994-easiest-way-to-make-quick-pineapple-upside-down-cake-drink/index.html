Steps to make Pineapple upside down cake drink:
  1. Fill glass with ice
  2. Pour in your vanilla vodka
  3. Fill glass with pineapple juice and stir.
  4. Add the grenadine.. pour slowly so that it gradually settles to the bottom.
  5. Add cherries.. a straw and enjoy
