The ingredients needed to make AMIEs Grilled PINEAPPLE with NUTELLA:
  1. Prepare 1 tsp olive oil
  2. Make ready 2/3 cup mascarpone cheese, room temperature
  3. Take 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Make ready 2/3 cup nutella spread
  5. Take 6 tbsp whipping cream
  6. Take 3 tbsp pistachio nuts, coarsely chopped
  7. Make ready 2 pineapples, peeled, cut crosswise into 1/2-inch-thick slices OR
  8. Make ready 1 can pineapples, cut round

Steps to make AMIEs Grilled PINEAPPLE with NUTELLA:
  1. Lightly oil your outdoor grill or indoor grill pan. Grill the pineapple slices until slightly brown, about 2 minutes per side. Flip pineapples only when grill marks form. Set aside.
  2. In a bowl, whisk the mascarpone and vanilla until mixed well. Set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk nutella and whipping cream together. Set aside.
  4. On a large serving platter, arrange pineapple slices. Pour the nutella sauce, top with mascarpone and sprinkle pistachios all over.
  5. Makes 10 to 12 servings.
