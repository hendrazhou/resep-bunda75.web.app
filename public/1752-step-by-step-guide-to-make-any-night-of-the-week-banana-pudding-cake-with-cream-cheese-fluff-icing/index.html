The ingredients needed to make Banana Pudding Cake with Cream Cheese fluff icing:
  1. Prepare 1 box French vanilla cake mix
  2. Make ready 3 eggs
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  4. Get 1 cup milk
  5. Prepare 1-4 serving box instant banana cream pudding
  6. Take 1 jar Beechnut banana baby food
  7. Take 1 cup crushed mini Nila Wafers
  8. Get 8 oz cream cheese
  9. Make ready 1 jar marshmallow fluff
  10. Take 1 cup powdered sugar
  11. Make ready Extra Nila Wafers for garnish

Instructions to make Banana Pudding Cake with Cream Cheese fluff icing:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and lightly flour 2 - 9"round cake pans, or place liners in regular size cupcake tins. (Recipe yields a dozen cupcakes, and 1 - 9" round cake perfectly)
  2. In a large bowl, add cake mix, eggs, oil, milk, and pudding mix. Beat for 2 minutes on medium speed using an electric mixer. Stir in banana baby food, and crushed wafers until combined.
  3. Evenly distribute the cake batter into prepared cake pans or cupcake tins. Bake in oven for 25-30 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. (If making cupcakes, use approximately 1/3 cup batter per cupcake)
  4. While cakes are cooling, mix cream cheese, marshmallow fluff and powdered sugar in a large bowl until shiny and smooth.
  5. Dip each cooled cupcake into the icing or pour and spread icing over the cooled cakes. Use any remaining Nila Wafers as garnish. Enjoy!
