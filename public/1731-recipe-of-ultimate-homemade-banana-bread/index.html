The ingredients needed to make Homemade Banana Bread:
  1. Prepare 4 VERY ripe Bananas
  2. Get 2 1/2 Cups All-purpose flour
  3. Prepare 1 cup Brown sugar
  4. Take 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  5. Take 1 stick butter melted
  6. Make ready 2 eggs
  7. Take 1 1/2 tsp Vanilla
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  9. Make ready 1 tsp salt

Steps to make Homemade Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350° (for metal loaf pan) or 320° (glass dishes).
  2. Grease loaf pan or glass dish of your choice with non-stick spray.
  3. Mash bananas and mix in all wet ingredients.
  4. In a separate mixing bowl, mix together dry ingredients. Gently add dry ingredients to the wet mixture; using a hand mixer to mix together.
  5. Pour mixture into a greased pan or baking dish.
  6. Cook until a toothpick inserted in center comes out dry. Make adjustments according to altitude and temp of oven.
  7. Enjoy!
