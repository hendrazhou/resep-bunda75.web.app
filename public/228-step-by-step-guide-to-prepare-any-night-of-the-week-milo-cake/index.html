The ingredients needed to make Milo cake:
  1. Prepare 1 cup flour
  2. Make ready 4 packs milo
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Take 1/4 spoon salt

Instructions to make Milo cake:
  1. Add all dry ingredients and seave all together
  2. Mix everything, add 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  3. Add 3/4 cup of milk
  4. Add 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  5. Add 2 eggs, add 1tsp of vanilla flavor
  6. Mix well until smooth
  7. Pour in a baking pan
  8. Tap to remove bubbles
  9. Bake at 180° for 30 to 35min
  10. I can garnish with syrup or chocolate Frosting
