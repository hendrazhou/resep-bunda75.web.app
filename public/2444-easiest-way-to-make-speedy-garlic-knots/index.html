The ingredients needed to make Garlic Knots:
  1. Prepare 1 pizza dough or 12 oz can of biscuits
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup olive oil
  3. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  4. Make ready 3 tsp parmesan cheese
  5. Prepare 1 tsp oregano

Instructions to make Garlic Knots:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. And grease a baking sheet.
  2. (I use pizza dough but you could also use a can of biscuits). Rip off small pieces of dough and roll out then tie in a knot. Then lay about an inch or 2 apart on the baking sheet.
  3. Bake for about 8 to 10 minutes for biscuits (maybe a little longer for pizza dough) or until the dough is golden brown.
  4. Mix all the other ingredients together in a bowl then brush over the baked knots. Serve with marinara sauce and enjoy :)
