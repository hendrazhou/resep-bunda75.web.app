The ingredients needed to make Loaded Potato Nachos:
  1. Prepare Meat—————–
  2. Take 4 slices bacon, 2 ounces
  3. Make ready 6 ounces chicken thigh boneless and skinless
  4. Get 1-1/2 pound ground beef
  5. Prepare 15 ounce can of pinto beans
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  7. Prepare 1-1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

  11. Take 1 teaspoon onion powder

  14. Prepare 1-1/2 pound yellow potatoes
  15. Prepare To taste salt
  16. Take Cheeses and other—————-

  18. Prepare 4 ounces sliced bacon

Steps to make Loaded Potato Nachos:
  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Remove the bone and skin from the chicken thigh. Dice and put in a pan with heat.
  3. Add two ounces of sliced bacon.
  4. Wash and slice the potatoes
  5. Fry in batches in the hot oil.
  6. Move to paper towels to absorb excessive amounts of oil and salt to taste.
  7. Add the beef along with the spices stir well and cook till browned.
  8. Add tomato paste taste and adjust spices. Add the beans and simmer for 15 minutes smash half of them.
  9. Lay the potatoes on a baking pan sprayed with nonstick spray.
  10. Add half the meat mixture.
  11. The cheese. Oh fry the rest of the bacon crisp.
  12. Add half the cheese to the top of first layer of meat.
  13. Crumble the bacon and add on top of the cheese.
  14. Add the rest of the meat mixture and the rest of the cheese.
  15. Put into oven till the cheese has melted.
  16. Serve with sour cream on top. I hope you enjoy!!!
