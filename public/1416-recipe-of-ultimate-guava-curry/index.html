The ingredients needed to make Guava Curry:
  1. Make ready 2 Guava (medium sized)
  2. Make ready 2 Tomatoes
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup Curd
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp Ginger paste
  5. Take 1 tsp Chilli(chopped)
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp Oil
  7. Get 2-3 Cloves
  8. Make ready 1 inch Cinnamon
  9. Take 1 Black Cardamom
  10. Get 1 tsp Fannel Powder
  11. Make ready 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  12. Make ready 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  13. Get 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  14. Get Pinch asafoetida
  15. Get To taste Salt
  16. Get 1 tbsp Jaggery

Instructions to make Guava Curry:
  1. Wash and cut the guava. Now make paste of tomato, curd, ginger and chilli.
  2. Heat oil, add cloves, cinnamon, and black cardamom. Now add cumin seeds. Now add turmeric asafetida, fennel powder and red chilli. Mix well, and cook for while at low flame.
  3. Add tomato mixture, cook it at low flame for 3-4 min. Now add diced guava. Mix well, cover it and cook for 3-4min.
  4. Add jaggery, mix it. Off the flame. Guava curry is ready to serve.
