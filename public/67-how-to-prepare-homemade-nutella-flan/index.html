The ingredients needed to make Nutella Flan:
  1. Prepare Nutella Flan
  2. Take 1 can evaporated milk
  3. Prepare 1 can condensed milk
  4. Prepare 5 eggs
  5. Take 1/2 cup nutella
  6. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  7. Prepare 1 tsp powdered cocoa
  8. Get caramel sauce
  9. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  10. Take 1/4 cup water

Steps to make Nutella Flan:
  1. Mix all ingredients in the blender
  2. Pour into the pan with caramel sauce on bottom
  3. Cook for 55 minutes at 350℉, check with a tooth pick in the center to be dry and slightly firm.
  4. For caramel sauce use a sauce pan and cook sugar and water until melted and change color to light brown ( caramel color)
