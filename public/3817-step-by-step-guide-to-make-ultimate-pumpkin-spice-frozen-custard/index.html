The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Spice Frozen Custard:
  1. Get 2 cup heavy cream
  2. Prepare 1 cup whole milk
  3. Make ready 2/3 cup sugar
  4. Get 5 large egg yolks
  5. Prepare pinch salt
  6. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 1 1/4 tsp pumpkin spice
  8. Make ready orange food coloring

Instructions to make Pumpkin Spice Frozen Custard:
  1. Mix cream, milk, and sugar.
  2. Beat egg yolks.
  3. Temper egg yolks with the hot mixture.
  4. Combine tempered egg yolks with the rest of the mixture.
  5. Heat until mixture reaches 170°.
  6. Add pumpkin spice, vanilla, salt, and food coloring.
  7. 25 minutes in the Cuisinart.
