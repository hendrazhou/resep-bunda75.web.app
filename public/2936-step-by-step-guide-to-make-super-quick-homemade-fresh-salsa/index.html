The ingredients needed to make Fresh salsa:
  1. Get 1 large tomato
  2. Prepare 2 green tomatillos (optional)
  3. Prepare 1-2 Serrano Chiles
  4. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Take Handful cilantro
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup water

Steps to make Fresh salsa:
  1. Simmer tomato, tomatillos and Serrano Chile for about 10 minutes
  2. Then take skin of red tomato and put in blender with the tomatillos, Serrano Chile, cilantro, water and salt. Pulse 2 to 3 times
  3. Then pour into a bowl and add diced onion if desired.
