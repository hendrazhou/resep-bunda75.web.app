The ingredients needed to make Lassi: Refreshing Yogurt Drink:
  1. Prepare 2 cups yogurt, plain
  2. Make ready 6 ice, cubed and crushed
  3. Get 3 teaspoons sugar, white
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions to make Lassi: Refreshing Yogurt Drink:
  1. Use a mixer to beat the yoghurt and crushed ice until smooth.
  2. While blending add in the sugar, salt and 1½ cup of cold water. Keep mixing until a layer of foam forms on top.
  3. Serve cold in long glasses.
