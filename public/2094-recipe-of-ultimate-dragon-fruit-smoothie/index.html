The ingredients needed to make Dragon fruit smoothie:
  1. Get 1 chopped dragon fruit
  2. Make ready 10 mint leaves
  3. Take 2 tbsp honey
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
  5. Take 1 lemon juice
  6. Get as needed Dragon fruit slice and scoops of dragon fruit
  7. Prepare as needed Crushed ice

Steps to make Dragon fruit smoothie:
  1. Take dragon fruit,honey,sugar,mint leaves and lemon juice in a mixer
  2. Add crushed ice
  3. And blend all of them
  4. Take serving glass and pour smoothee in it
  5. Garnish with slice of dragon fruit scoops and mint
  6. And serve cold
