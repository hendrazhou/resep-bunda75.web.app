The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti dry fruit cake:
  1. Get 3 eggs
  2. Take 125 gm Castor Sugar
  3. Get 125 gm Refined Flour
  4. Make ready 100 gm Unsalted Butter
  5. Get 5 gm Baking Powder
  6. Make ready 5 gm vanilla/orange essence
  7. Get 1 Pinch Salt
  8. Prepare 50 gm Mix Tutti Frutti
  9. Prepare 25 gm Raisins
  10. Prepare 25 gm Almonds
  11. Take 25 gm Cranberry

Instructions to make Tutti frutti dry fruit cake:
  1. Add essence and mix well
  2. Add melted butter drip by drip and keep whisking all the time
  3. Fold sieved refined flour and baking powder mixture in egg & sugar mixture
  4. Add all the tutti frutti and dry fruits
  5. Pour the batter in greased cake tin and bake at 160 *C for 35-40 minutes.
  6. Cut in slices and serve with a cup of tea or coffee
