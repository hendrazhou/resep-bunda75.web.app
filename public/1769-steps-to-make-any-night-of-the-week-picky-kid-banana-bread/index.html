The ingredients needed to make Picky kid Banana bread:
  1. Take 2 over ripened bananas
  2. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 egg white
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  5. Get 1/2 cup flour
  6. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Get 1/4 tsp baking soda
  8. Prepare 1/8 tsp salt
  9. Prepare 1/8 tsp cinnamon
  10. Prepare Pinch nutmeg
  11. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  12. Get Optional- chocolate chips
