The ingredients needed to make Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal:
  1. Take 1 apple
  2. Prepare 7 spoons quickcook oatmeal
  3. Get Milk (your choice of type)
  4. Make ready Pinch salt
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp brown sugar
  6. Get 1 knob butter
  7. Prepare 1 tsp cinnamon powder

Instructions to make Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal:
  1. Cut apples into cubes.
  2. Combine butter, apples, brown sugar and a bit of water in a pan and saute till brown.
  3. Heat milk in a pot and add a pinch of salt.
  4. Add oatmeal and a teaspoon of cinnamon.
  5. When oatmeal is a satisfactory consistency, pour into bowl.
  6. Add apples on top and serve!
  7. Happy breakfast!!!
