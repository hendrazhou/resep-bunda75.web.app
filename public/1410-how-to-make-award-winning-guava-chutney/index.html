The ingredients needed to make Guava chutney:
  1. Make ready 1 big /250 grams guava
  2. Take 2 green chillies
  3. Prepare 1/2 inch ginger
  4. Get as per taste Salt
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp zeera powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  7. Take 1/2 cup coriander leaves
  8. Get 1/2 lemon juice
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp rock salt
