The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Pecan Pie:
  1. Prepare 1 9-inch Pie Crust
  2. Take 8 oz Pecan Halves
  3. Get Filling
  4. Make ready 3 Eggs
  5. Take 3/4 cup Light Corn Syrup
  6. Prepare 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
  7. Take 1/4 cup Butter (melted)
  8. Take 1 1/2 tbsp Cinnamon Liquor
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  10. Take 1 pinch Ground Nutmeg
  11. Make ready 1/4 tsp Salt
  12. Take 1 tsp Vanilla Extract

Instructions to make Cinnamon Pecan Pie:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, mix together filling ingredients thoroughly.
  3. Spread all but a handful of the pecan halves into pie shell.
  4. Pour filling mixture over the pecans in the pie shell. Top with remaining handful of pecan halves.
  5. Bake at 350°F 30-45 minutes or until toothpick comes clean out of center.
