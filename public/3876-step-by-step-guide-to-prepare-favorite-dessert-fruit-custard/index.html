The ingredients needed to make Dessert

Fruit custard:

  1. Prepare 1 kg Milk
  2. Take 4-5 spoon Custard powder
  3. Get To taste Sugar
  4. Prepare Freshly chopped seasonal fruits
  5. Make ready 2 cup mix fruit(bananas, apple, chikoo)
  6. Get 1/2 cup Tinned fruits
  7. Prepare handful mix fruits(Chopped walnuts, almonds )

Instructions to make Dessert

Fruit custard:

  1. Boil milk, add sugar and let it dissolve, stir continuously,
  2. Dissolve custard powder with cold milk or water, pour in the boiling milk, stir continuously to avoid lumps. Let it become thicker, remove from heat, keep aside for cooling
  3. Peel and chop fruits, tinned fruits nuts, add to the custard sauce, mix well,
  4. Garnish with slivered almonds and walnuts, chocolate shavings and sprinkles, drizzle fresh cream, chill and serve
