The ingredients needed to make Salsa, Cheddar, Sour Cream & Avocado Pizza:
  1. Make ready Base
  2. Make ready 1 packages Pizza dough (fresh, from bakery)
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp olive oil
  4. Get Topping
  5. Prepare 1 cup salsa (Pico de Gallo)
  6. Make ready 1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  7. Take 1/2 can chick peas
  8. Prepare 5 tbsp sour cream
  9. Get 1/3 onion, chopped
  10. Take 4 small sweet peppers
  11. Get 1/2 avocado, ripe

Steps to make Salsa, Cheddar, Sour Cream & Avocado Pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F Fahrenheit. Spread out pizza dough over oiled pan.
  2. Chop the peppers and onions. Pour on the salsa first as the sauce, and then add the onions and sweet peppers.
  3. Dollop on the sour cream and sprinkle the chick peas. Finish with cheddar cheese.
  4. Cook for 20 minutes or until the crust crisps. While the pizza is out and cooling, add slices of avocado on top. Delicious!
