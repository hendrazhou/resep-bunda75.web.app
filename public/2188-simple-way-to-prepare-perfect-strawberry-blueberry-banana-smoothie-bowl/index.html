The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Blueberry Banana Smoothie Bowl:
  1. Get 150 ml almond milk / soy milk / coconut milk
  2. Get 125 ml plain yoghurt / vanilla yoghurt
  3. Make ready 150 gr strawberry (fresh or frozen)
  4. Make ready 75 gr blueberry (fresh or frozen)
  5. Get 2 bananas frozen
  6. Prepare honey (optional)
