Steps to make Spinach Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. Preheat Oven to 350Â°F
  2. Take Baguette Loaf and Slice straight down the middle that it makes to halves
  3. Chop Garlic Clove into fine pieces
  4. In a sauce pan put on medium heat and add a half cup of butter with chopped Garlic Cloves
  5. Let shimmer for about 3 mins then place to cool for about 2 mins.
  6. Spread butter mixture evenly on both side of the bread halves
  7. In a Sautee pan cook 2 packages on spinach may seem like a lot but spinach shrinks
  8. Evenly distribute spinach on both halves of the Baguette
  9. Spread a Layer of Shredded Cheese
  10. Repeat steps 8 and 9 again
  11. Finally sprinkle Garlic powder on top evenly and place in oven
  12. Let cook for 10 mins
