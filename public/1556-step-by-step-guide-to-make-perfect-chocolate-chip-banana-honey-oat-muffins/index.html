The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip banana honey oat muffins:
  1. Make ready 3 ripe bananas
  2. Take 1/4 cup coconut butter/oil
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup honey
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp ground flaxseed (optional)
  8. Get 1 cup flour
  9. Take 1 cup oats
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup chocolate chips
