The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Sugar Donuts:

  3. Take 1/2 cup White sugar

  5. Get Vegetable Oil

Steps to make Cinnamon Sugar Donuts:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl and set to the side
  2. Turn on oil to heat up 325°F
  3. Open package of biscuits separate and put hold in middle
  4. Fry for 40 seconds each side.
  5. Drain oil
  6. While hot dip in dry mixture
  7. Enjoy!!!
