The ingredients needed to make Refreshing watermelon smoothie:
  1. Take 1 cup Watermelon pieces
  2. Get 2 cups milk
  3. Prepare 2 tsp sugar adjust
  4. Take as required crushed ice cubes
  5. Prepare as required Watermelon slices for garnish
  6. Prepare as required Mint leaves for garnish
  7. Prepare 1 pinch Black Salt
  8. Make ready Half lemon juice
  9. Prepare 1 pinch cumin powder
  10. Get as required Fresh Homemade milk cream or nuts if you want

Steps to make Refreshing watermelon smoothie:
  1. Add all'the ingredients in a blender
  2. Blend all the ingredients until it becomes a smooth mixture.
  3. Pour watermelon smoothie into the glasses.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint leaves.
  5. Enjoy the tropical summer special watermelon smoothie.
