The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon cookies:
  1. Take 2 eggs
  2. Make ready 150 g sugar
  3. Prepare 0.5 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  5. Prepare Pinch salt
  6. Prepare 100 g butter
  7. Get 250 g flour

Instructions to make Cinnamon cookies:
  1. Break 2 eggs, add 150g of sugar and mix them until it will look like mass on photo.
  2. Add soda and cinnamon, then mix.
  3. Grate butter, then add it to mass and mix.
  4. Add flour, then knead the dough until it will stop stick to hands.
  5. Smear pan with oil.
  6. Form spheres from dough. You can use teaspoon for making them if you want them to be totally equal. Make 20 spheres then put them on the pan.
  7. Cook in oven with 180 C° temperature for 15-20 minutes (do not overcook them!) When they're done, put them on plate and let them cool down. That's it. I will appreciate any feedback. Bon appetit!
