The ingredients needed to make puerto Rican Coquito:
  1. Take 2 can coconut milk
  2. Get 2 can condensed milk (14 oz)
  3. Make ready 2 can cream of coconut (15 oz)
  4. Prepare 5 can egg yolks
  5. Get 1/4 cinnamon
  6. Take 1 dash nutmeg
  7. Take 1 tbsp vanilla
  8. Get 1 oz bottle barcardi rum
