The ingredients needed to make Avocado Papaya smoothie:
  1. Prepare 1 cup diced papaya
  2. Get 1 cup diced avocado
  3. Take 2 tablespoons honey
  4. Get 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  5. Make ready 1 cup ice cubes
  6. Take 1/2 cup cold water

Steps to make Avocado Papaya smoothie:
  1. In a blender, place avocado, 1 tablespoon honey, lemon juice, 1/2 cup ice cubes, and 1/4 cup water. - Purée until smooth. - Set aside.
  2. Wash the blender and place the remaining ingredients. - Purée until smooth.
  3. In a glass, pour the half of the avocado smoothie followed by the papaya smoothie.
  4. Serve and drink as a breakfast smoothie!
