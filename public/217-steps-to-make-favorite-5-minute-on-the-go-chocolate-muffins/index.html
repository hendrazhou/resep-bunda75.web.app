The ingredients needed to make 5-Minute On-The-Go Chocolate Muffins:
  1. Make ready 5 tbsp all-purpose flour
  2. Take 2 tbsp sugar
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp cocoa powder (milo or other chocolate drink does fine too)
  4. Make ready 5 tbsp milk
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp oil
  6. Take 1/3 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1 egg
  8. Take Optional Flavoring
  9. Prepare Nutella
  10. Prepare ground cinnamon
  11. Make ready vanilla extract

Instructions to make 5-Minute On-The-Go Chocolate Muffins:
  1. Mix together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder and cinnamon (if using).
  2. Stir in the milk, oil and egg. Add more milk tablespoon by tablespoon if the mixture is too dry. Take caution though, the end product should resemble a thick, sticky paste.
  3. Add Nutella and/or vanilla ectract if using.
  4. Fill muffin cups halfway.
  5. For a lava filling, fill a small piping bag with Nutella or chocolate cream and pipe it into the middle of the muffins.
  6. Pop the muffins into a microwave for 2 minutes or until well risen and spongy to the touch. Not too long, or it will dry up and harden.
