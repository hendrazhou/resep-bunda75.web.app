The ingredients needed to make M&N's breakfast oat drink:
  1. Make ready 1/4 cup quick oats (uncooked)
  2. Get 1 medium banana
  3. Prepare 1 cup water
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup almond milk
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp agave syrup
  6. Get 1 dash cinnamon
Instructions to make M&N's breakfast oat drink:
  1. Take all ingredients and put in blender, blend till smooth.
  2. Poor in a cup with ice and enjoy
