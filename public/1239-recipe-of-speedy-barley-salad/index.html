The ingredients needed to make Barley salad:
  1. Prepare 1 cup barley soaked for 3 hrs and then boiled till tender
  2. Take 1 cucumber finely chopped
  3. Get 8-10 mushrooms chopped
  4. Make ready 1 onion diced
  5. Take Salr
  6. Take Pepper
  7. Take 1 tsp oregano
  8. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
  9. Take Chopped coriander
  10. Take 2 tsp apple cider vinegar

Instructions to make Barley salad:
  1. Heat olive oil in a pan. Add onions and mushrooms. Saute on high flame for 1 or 2 minutes. Add salt, pepper and oregano. Mix well. Set aside to cool.
  2. In a bowl take the boiled and strained barley. Add cucumber, chopped coriander and apple cider vinegar. Mix well. Now add the mushrooms and onion mix. Toss everything together. Yummy barley salad is ready. Enjoy!
