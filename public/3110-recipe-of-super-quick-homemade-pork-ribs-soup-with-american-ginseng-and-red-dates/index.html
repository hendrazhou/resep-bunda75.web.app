The ingredients needed to make Pork Ribs Soup With American Ginseng And Red Dates:
  1. Take 850 grams pork ribs
  2. Make ready 2 liter water
  3. Take 1 cup American ginseng "beards"
  4. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 10 red dates

Instructions to make Pork Ribs Soup With American Ginseng And Red Dates:
  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil then quickly blanch the ribs then set aside, then quick rinse the mix herbs (optional 2) and drain too
  2. Put all the pot, the blanched ribs with American ginseng, Soloman Seal Herbs, Red Dates and few slices of ginger or mix match herbs (optional 2)
  3. Add water and put into the pressure cooker on high for 35 minutes
  4. Once cooked add salt seasoning and let it simmer for another 5 minutes then serve and enjoy
