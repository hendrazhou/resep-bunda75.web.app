The ingredients needed to make Stuff cheese garlic bread (in cooker):
  1. Take 1 & 1/2 cup maida/all purpose flour
  2. Get 7-8 Garlic cloves
  3. Get 1 bowl Cheese shredded
  4. Take 1/2 bowl Corn
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Oregano
  6. Prepare to taste Salt
  7. Take 1 & 1/2 tbsp Dry yeast
  8. Take 1 cup Warm water
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Sugar
  10. Get as needed Butter

Steps to make Stuff cheese garlic bread (in cooker):
  1. First take warm water, sugar and dry yeast mix well in bowl and set a side for 10min till the yeast it bubbly and activated.
  2. Now add maida, garlic, salt, oregano mix well & add yeast mixture. It will from into sticky dough.
  3. Add oil & knead well. Cover the bowl and rest of 2hours till it is size the double.
  4. Take a butter in bowl & garlic and oregano and mix well.
  5. At this point preheat cooker. spread salt into the cooker (gasket and shitty not used)
  6. Take a dough and knead well. Divided into two parts.
  7. Make it roti and spread the garlic butter mixture and sprinkle corn, cheese over it. Fold it over & seal the edges
  8. Now take a tray and sprinkle butter and oregano.
  9. Now place the garlic bread over it and use the cutter into stick.
  10. Take a stand or one plate in preheat cooker and put this tray & close the cooker.
  11. First high flame for 5 minutes and then medium flame for 15 to 20 minutes. Garlic bread reddy for eat. Serve with mayonnaise or cheese dip.
