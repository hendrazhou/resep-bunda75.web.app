The ingredients needed to make Berry Cheesecake Green Smoothie:
  1. Get 1 handful fresh baby spinach
  2. Get 1/2 cup skim milk
  3. Get 3/4 cup water
  4. Make ready 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  5. Take 1 oz reduced fat cream cheese,softened
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup heaping of blueberry or vanilla low fat greek yogurt(I like dannon light and fit greek)
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp ground flax seed
  9. Make ready 1 splenda,stevia or truvia packet or a bit of honey(2 tsp)
