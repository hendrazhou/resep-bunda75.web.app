The ingredients needed to make Mini Garlic Monkey Bread:
  1. Get 1 small can of Pillsbury Grands buttermilk biscuits
  2. Take 3 tbsp butter - melted
  3. Make ready 2 clove garlic - minced
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp dried parsley
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese

Steps to make Mini Garlic Monkey Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Lightly grease 6 capacity muffin pan.
  3. Flatten bicuits out with palm and cut each into bit size pieces and place in a bowl.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix remaining ingredients.
  5. Pour mixture over biscuit bits and coat.
  6. Evenly distribute pieces into muffin tin. Top with additional cheese.
  7. Bake for 9-12 minutes or until golden brown.
