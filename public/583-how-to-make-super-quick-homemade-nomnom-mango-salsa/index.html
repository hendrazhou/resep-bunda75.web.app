The ingredients needed to make NomNom Mango salsa:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cucumber
  2. Get 1/2 small red onion
  3. Take 1 large red bell pepper
  4. Make ready 1 jalapeño
  5. Take 1 large mango
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp minced garlic
  7. Prepare season to taste
  8. Take 1 crushed pepper
  9. Get 1 salt
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
  11. Get 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  12. Take 1 lime

Instructions to make NomNom Mango salsa:
  1. dice jalapeño
  2. squeeze & pulp lime
  3. marinade jalapeño in lime juices while you dice up all other ingredients
  4. dice all ingredients
  5. mix
  6. season with cilantro, salt, crushed pepper, & lemon juice
