  4. Prepare Veg oil
  5. Make ready Knorr

Instructions to make Toasted bread,fried Eggs and Hot Choco:
  1. Beat ur eggs in a bowl,add a portion of d sardine and little Knorr and whisk properly.
  2. In a frying pan,heat up a little Veg oil and pour in the eggs,fry 2mins per sides until done and set asides.
  3. In a bowl,mix d butter and remaining sardine,mash together and spread on d bread.
  4. Set bread on Toaster and toast for abt 3-4 mins.
  5. In a Tea cup,pour in ur Milo,milk and sugar and add Hot water.
  6. Your Toasted bread,Eggs and Hot Choco is ready.
