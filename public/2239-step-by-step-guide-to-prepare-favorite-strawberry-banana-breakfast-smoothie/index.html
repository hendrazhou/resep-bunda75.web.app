The ingredients needed to make Strawberry banana breakfast smoothie:
  1. Take 1 medium-large banana
  2. Make ready 1 cup strawberries
  3. Get 1 the juice of 1 cara cara orange
  4. Prepare 1 cup Greek yogurt
  5. Take 1 handful or two of ice

Instructions to make Strawberry banana breakfast smoothie:
  1. Put the fruit in a blender and blend well
  2. Add the yogurt and blend well. I use a high setting for this do it comes out smooth
  3. Add the ice. A few cubes at a time if you need. I use the ice crusher setting so there are no chunks left. Blend well until smooth.
