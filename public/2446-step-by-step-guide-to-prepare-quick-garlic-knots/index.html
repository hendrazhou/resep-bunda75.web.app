The ingredients needed to make Garlic knots:
  1. Prepare 1/4 cup Canola oil
  2. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
  3. Get 3 tbsp Parmesan cheese
  4. Take 1 tsp Drird oregano
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Dried parsley flakes
  6. Prepare 1 12 oz. tube buttermilk biscuts

Instructions to make Garlic knots:
  1. cut each biscuit into thirds. roll each piece into a 3 inch rope and tie into a knot.
  2. place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheet
  3. bake at 400°F for 8 to 10 minutes until golden golden brown
  4. in a large bowl combine the remaining ingredients
  5. brush oil mixture over knots
