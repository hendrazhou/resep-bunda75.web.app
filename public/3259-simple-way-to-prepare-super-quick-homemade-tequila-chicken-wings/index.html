  1. Get 6 chicken wings

  3. Prepare 1 tbsp cumin
  4. Prepare 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  5. Get 1 tsp onion powder
  6. Get 1 tsp garlic powder

Steps to make Tequila Chicken Wings:
  1. Marinate wings in tequila for one hour.
  2. Pat wings dry with a paper towel.
  3. Toss in oil. Season. Grill skin side down to get a nice grill mark or toss straight into the oven.
  4. Bake at 400° for approximately 20 minutes or until thermometer reaches 165° and wings are caramelized and crispy.
  5. Variations; Adobe, mole, cinnamon, dried cilantro, lime juice
