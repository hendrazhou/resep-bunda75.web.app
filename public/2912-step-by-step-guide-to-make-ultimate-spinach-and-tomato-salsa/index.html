The ingredients needed to make Spinach and Tomato Salsa:
  1. Get 4 Roma Tomatoes
  2. Take 3 Clove Garlic
  3. Get 1/4 Yellow Onion
  4. Prepare 1 Habanero Pepper (Feel free to substitute for Jalapeno)
  5. Take 2 Cup Spinach
  6. Take 1/2 Lime Squeezed
  7. Take 3 Tsp. Salt
  8. Get 3 Tsp. Pepper
  9. Take 2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
  10. Take 3 Tsp Cumin

Steps to make Spinach and Tomato Salsa:
  1. Roughly chop onion, tomato, garlic, spinach and squeeze the lime in and add to a food processor.
  2. (Wearing gloves) cut the stem off of the habanero and slice it in half. With a finger, pull out as many seeds as you can. Roughly chop and add to food processor.
  3. Add spices and pulse food processor multiple times until well blended.
  4. Serve with anything where salsa is needed, I.e. everything. Enjoy!
