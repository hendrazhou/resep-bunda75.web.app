The ingredients needed to make nutella french toast:
  1. Take 4 slice texas toast
  2. Take 4 eggs
  3. Get tsp cinnamon
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  6. Take 1 nutella spread
  7. Get 2 bananas

Instructions to make nutella french toast:
  1. preheat griddle/fryin pan to med-high heat
  2. slice texas bread in half
  3. spread nutella on both inside halves
  4. thinly slice and place banana slices on only one piece of bread.
  5. close both halves together
  6. whisk together eggs, cinnamon, milk and vanilla
  7. dunk bread in egg mixture then place on grill/pan
  8. cook for 10 minutes or so then flip and do the same to otherside until egg on bread is cooked
  9. spinkle with brown sugar or icing sugar or more bananas or maple syrup or all of the above!
