The ingredients needed to make Organic pearl barley water:
  1. Make ready 1 cup pearl barley or barley
  2. Make ready 2 ltr water
  3. Get 3 tbsp Sugar (depends on your preference)
  4. Make ready 1 tsp lemon juice

It is a source of fibre and bulks food to make tasty, hearty meals. Pearl barley is milled to give it a soft texture and a pearly white appearance. Barley is a versatile grain with a soft texture similar to pasta once cooked. Organic Pearl Barley is one of the healthiest grains you can find.

Steps to make Organic pearl barley water:
  1. Rinse the pearl barley two to three times.
  2. Soak the barley for one to two hours or overnight to ease it while cooking.
  3. Prepare a pot with water and place in the soaked barley. Bring it to a boil.
  4. After boiling, simmer the barley with low medium heat for one hour until it is soft and chewy.
  5. After 1 hour, you may add the sugar into it and stir it properly. Switch off heat.
  6. Serve it while it is hot or let it cool down.
