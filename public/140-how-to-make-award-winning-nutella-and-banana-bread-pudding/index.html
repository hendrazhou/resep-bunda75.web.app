The ingredients needed to make Nutella and Banana Bread Pudding:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  2. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  3. Take 4 pieces bread roughly chopped into small cubes
  4. Get 1 egg beaten
  5. Take 1/4 cup Nutella
  6. Take 1/2 banana cut into small pieces

Instructions to make Nutella and Banana Bread Pudding:
  1. In a large bowl, mix together the milk and vanilla extract. Add the bread cubes into the bowl and let them soak for a few minutes.
  2. In another large bowl, mix the egg with Nutella.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the bowl containing the bread and stir.
