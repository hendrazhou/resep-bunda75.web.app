The ingredients needed to make Air Fryer BBQ Chicken Wings:
  1. Get 2 pounds chicken wings
  2. Take 1 tbsp brown sugar
  3. Make ready 1 tsp sea salt or kosher salt
  4. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
  5. Take 1 tsp onion powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp smoked paprika
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  8. Take 1/4 tsp baking powder
  9. Prepare Olive oil spray
  10. Take BBQ sauce

Steps to make Air Fryer BBQ Chicken Wings:
  1. Mix the spices and baking powder together in a small bowl.
  2. Pat the chicken wings dry with a paper towel.
  3. Rub the mixture over wings until well coated and place wings on air fryer cooking rack or bucket.
  4. Spritz wings with olive oil spray.
  5. Set air fryer to 390 degrees. Add wings to air fryer and cook for 25-30 minutes. Rotating trays about half way through cooking or if a basket shake at least twice so they cook evenly.
  6. Remove from air fryer brush with your favorite BBQ sauce and enjoy!
