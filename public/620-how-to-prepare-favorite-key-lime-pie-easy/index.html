The ingredients needed to make Key Lime Pie EASY:
  1. Take Crust
  2. Prepare 1.5 cups fine crushed graham crackers
  3. Take 1/3 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 6 TBS melted butter
  5. Take Filling
  6. Take 28 oz sweet condensed milk (2 cans)
  7. Take 1/2 cup sour cream
  8. Take 3/4 cup key lime juice
  9. Take Zest of 2 Limes or 4 Key Limes
  10. Get Whipped cream topping
  11. Take 1 cup heavy cream
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup confectioners sugar
  13. Take 1 tsp vanilla

This Key Lime Pie is an American classic. Ours is made with lime juice and sweetened condensed milk in a graham cracker crust and topped with whipped cream. This all American pie is easy to make, too. My easy key lime pie gives the classic recipe a much-needed makeover.

Steps to make Key Lime Pie EASY:
  1. Preheat oven 350/375 Crush graham crackers, add sugar, then melted butter
  2. Press down on sprayed pie or tart pan and bake for 7-9 mins. Let cool 20 mins
  3. While that cools, make filling
  4. Combine all filling ingredients with whisk and pour on top of cooled crust. Bake for 10-12 mins and cool completely. Then chill pie 2 hours
  5. While that’s cooling. Make whipped topping. Combine all ingredients until a stiff peak forms and heavy cream isn’t loose anymore. Must look like whipped cream !
  6. Decorate and enjoy COLD😌. Wasn’t that tooo easy ?
