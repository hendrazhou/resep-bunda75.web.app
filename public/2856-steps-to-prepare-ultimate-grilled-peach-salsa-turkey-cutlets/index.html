The ingredients needed to make Grilled peach salsa turkey cutlets:
  1. Get 1 lb turkey cutlets
  2. Take Seasonings
  3. Get Chili powder
  4. Take Black pepper
  5. Take Marinade
  6. Make ready 1 cup gold peak peach tea
  7. Take 1/4 cup worcestershire
  8. Take 1 tbls minced garlic
  9. Get Peach salsa
  10. Get 1/2 chopped cucumber
  11. Take 2 fresh chopped peaches
  12. Get 1/4 cup red onion
  13. Get Lime juice from 1/2 of a lime
  14. Get leaves Fresh parsley
  15. Take Splash gold peak peach tea

Instructions to make Grilled peach salsa turkey cutlets:
  1. Season with chili powder & black pepper.
  2. Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl. Marinade the cutlets in the fridge for a minimal of 3 hours.
  3. Prepare the peach salsa by chopping up the peaches cucumber,red onion & parsley leaves. Squeeze in lime juice & add a dash of the peach tea. Set aside.
  4. Preheat the grill to 375. Cook each cutlet for 7-10 minutes on each side or until desired doneness. Serve with peach salsa on top & a side of asparagus!
