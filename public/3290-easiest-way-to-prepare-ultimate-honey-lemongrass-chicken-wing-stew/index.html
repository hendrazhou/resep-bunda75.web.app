The ingredients needed to make Honey lemongrass Chicken wing stew:
  1. Take 500 grams Chicken Wings
  2. Make ready 80 ml Chinese cooking wine
  3. Take 2 tbsp Oyster sauce
  4. Get 2 tbsp Honey
  5. Get 100 ml Chicken stock
  6. Take 300 ml Water
  7. Prepare 80 ml Lime juice
  8. Make ready 1 lemon grass
  9. Make ready 1 salt
  10. Prepare 1 pepper
  11. Prepare 1 garlic powder
  12. Prepare 1 chilli powder

Instructions to make Honey lemongrass Chicken wing stew:
  1. First of all you have to rince and cut the chicken wings. I use only the drummetes.
  2. Work all the spices into the meat. ( garlic, chilli, salt, pepper )
  3. You will need a big pan with lid !
  4. Put oil into the hot pan
  5. Fry the chicken until there is no more raw meat visible on the outside.
  6. Pour the rice wine, chicken broth, water, honey, lime juice and oyster sauce over the wings.
  7. Lay the lemongrass on the top of wings.
  8. Give it a stir
  9. Put the lid on the pan.
  10. Bring it to boil and reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 1 - 1 1/2 h.
  11. Serve with rice or whatever side dish you prefer. Bon appetite. The meat should fall from the bones ;-).
