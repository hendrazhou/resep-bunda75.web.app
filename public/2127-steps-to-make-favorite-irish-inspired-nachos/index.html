The ingredients needed to make Irish inspired nachos:
  1. Prepare 1 quart lamb broth see recipe attached
  2. Take 6 large new red potatoes
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  4. Take 1 tbsp marjoram
  5. Prepare 1 tsp granulated garlic powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground black pepper, fresh
  8. Get 1 small onion I used yellow
  9. Make ready 1 vegetable oil to pan fry
  10. Take 1/2 cup flour
  11. Make ready 1 cup minced lamb

Steps to make Irish inspired nachos:
  1. Heat lamb broth - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/358612-shepherds-pie
  2. Take lamb mince it up slice onions a thinly slice potatoes
  3. Fry the potatoes in vegetable oil
  4. When the float and they are crisp set on paper towel to absorb excess oil add spices while they are hot
  5. Add onions and lamb to a bit of oil
  6. After the onions are done add quarter cup oil add flour
  7. Stir till flour is smooth add broth stir till your desired thickness add water if need to thin but you want this thick but still able to run
  8. After lamb gravy is done take a plate add a layer of fried potatoes then gravy then the potatoes
  9. On top of the second layer of potatoes add cheese then strain the gravy add remaining lamb and onions top off with a few potatoes
  10. Hope you enjoy
