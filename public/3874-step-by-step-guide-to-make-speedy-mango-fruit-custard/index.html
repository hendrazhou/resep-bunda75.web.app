The ingredients needed to make Mango Fruit Custard:
  1. Make ready 2 cup milk
  2. Make ready 3 tbsp custard powder
  3. Take 1/4 cup milk
  4. Get 1/4 cup sugar
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup mango pulp
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup pomegranate
  7. Take 1 cup chopped apple
  8. Take 1/2 cup chopped mango
  9. Get Vanilla ice cream

Instructions to make Mango Fruit Custard:
  1. In a large kadai boil the milk and stir it occasionally.
  2. Mix the 1/4 cup chilled milk with custard powder without lumps. Pour the custard milk mix in boiling milk and add sugar. Stir continuously without lumps until milk turns thickens and turn creamy.
  3. Cool the mixture completely and then add the mango puree and mix well. Refrigerate it for 2hours.
  4. While serving add the chopped fruits and mix. Now the mango fruit custard is ready. Can serve in ice cream bowl.
