The ingredients needed to make Fruit Salsa With Cinnamon Tortilla Chips:
  1. Make ready 1 pint strawberries
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup pineapples (I used canned)
  3. Get 1 cup peaches (I used canned)
  4. Take 3 kiwi's
  5. Prepare 2 Tablespoons fruit preserve
  6. Get optional) I hardly ever use this unless I'm using all fresh fruit. 6 flour tortillas (8-inch)
  7. Take 3-4 Tablespoons butter, melted
  8. Prepare 2 Tablespoons sugar
  9. Make ready 1/2 tablespoon cinnamon
  10. Take cooking oil

Steps to make Fruit Salsa With Cinnamon Tortilla Chips:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Dice up the strawberries, pineapples, peaches and kiwi.
  3. Place the fruit into a bowl and mix to combine. If using fruit preserve add it in now. Place into the refrigerator to chill. I place mine in the freezer to get it colder faster.
  4. Spray two baking sheets generously with cooking oil. Set aside.
  5. Stack tortillas on a plate and slice them into 8 slices. - Place the tortilla slices on the baking sheets.
  6. Melt butter in a bowl - In a small bowl, combine cinnamon and sugar.
  7. Brush tortillas with butter and then sprinkle with the cinnamon sugar mixture.
  8. Bake for 5-7 minutes. (be careful not to over bake. Tortillas will crisp up more as they cool.) - Remove from oven and let cool.
