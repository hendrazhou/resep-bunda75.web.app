The ingredients needed to make Pearl Barley Porridge Sugar Free:
  1. Get 1/2 cup barley pearl
  2. Take 1 1/2 cups water
  3. Take 1/2 cup milk
  4. Get 1/2 tbsp cocoa powder
  5. Take 4 dates
  6. Make ready 1 banana sliced
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup granola (craisins, pumpkin seeds, nuts, coconut chips)
  8. Take 1 tsp cinnamon

Instructions to make Pearl Barley Porridge Sugar Free:
  1. Boil barley in water for 45 mins, until soft and water is absorbed.
  2. Drain water if there is excess. Set aside.
  3. In another pan, warm milk and add cocoa powder, stir to dissolve properly.
  4. Empty barley into cereal bowl and pour chocolate milk over it.
  5. Sprinkle cinnamon.
  6. Add on dates, granola and banana slices.
