The ingredients needed to make Sabudana(Sago) pink falooda:
  1. Prepare 4 cup water, for boiling & rinsing
  2. Prepare 1 cup sabudana / sago
  3. Get 4 cup milk
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp strawberry crush
  5. Take 2 tbsp basil seeds
  6. Get 4 tbsp nuts, chopped
  7. Take 4 tbsp tutti frutti
  8. Get 2 tbsp strawberry jelly

Instructions to make Sabudana(Sago) pink falooda:
  1. Firstly, in a large kadai take 4 cup water and add 1 cup sabudana
  2. Stir and boil for 10 minutes stirring in between.
  3. Continue to boil until the sabudana turns translucent.
  4. Drain off the sabudana once it is cooked completely.
  5. Now rinse with cold water. this helps to rinse off excess starch and prevents from sticking.
  6. Sabudana pearls are ready. keep aside.
  7. Now firstly, in a large kadai take 4 cup milk.
  8. Stir and boil until the milk reduces slightly.
  9. Cool the milk completely. recommend chilling the milk as it gives a good flavour
  10. Now add 2 tbsp  strawberry crush and mix well.
  11. Finally, strawberry milk is ready.
  12. Now take a tall glass, and add 1 tbsp strawberry crush and 2 tbsp basil seeds.
  13. Add 2 tbsp prepared sago pearls and 2 tbsp strawberry jelly
  14. Further add 2 tbsp nuts and 2 tbsp tutti frutti.
  15. Pour in 1 cup chilled strawberry milk and stir gently.
  16. Garnish with chopped nuts and tutti frutti.finally, enjoy sabudana falooda or sago falooda with more strawberry crush
