The ingredients needed to make Ponche:
  1. Get 2 gallon water
  2. Get 3 cinnamon sticks
  3. Make ready 3 oranges cut in four pieces
  4. Prepare 3 apples cut in four pieces
  5. Prepare 2 sugar cane sticks peeled and cut in large pieces
  6. Make ready 8 whole guavas
  7. Take 4 piloncillos (brown sugar/malasses cones)
  8. Prepare 1 jar tejocotes
  9. Make ready 1 sugar to taste
  10. Take 1 may add rum or taquila for adult version
