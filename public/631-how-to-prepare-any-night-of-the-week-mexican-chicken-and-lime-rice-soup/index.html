The ingredients needed to make Mexican Chicken and lime rice soup:
  1. Make ready 4 cups Chicken broth
  2. Make ready 1 avocado
  3. Take 3 chicken breasts
  4. Make ready 4 limes, juiced
  5. Make ready 1 Jalepeno, thinly sliced
  6. Make ready 1 onion, chopped
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup Cilantro, chopped
  8. Make ready 3 cloves garlic
  9. Make ready 2 cups rice, cooked
  10. Get 1 Tbsp oregano
  11. Make ready 2 Tbsp cumin
  12. Take 1 Tbsp chili powder
