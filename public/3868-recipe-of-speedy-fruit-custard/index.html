The ingredients needed to make Fruit custard:
  1. Take Half litre milk
  2. Make ready 2 tsp custard powder
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped mango
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped grapes
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped papaya
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup pomegranate
  7. Prepare As needed almonds,raisins
  8. Prepare As needed tutti frutti
  9. Take 4 tsp sugar or to taste

Steps to make Fruit custard:
  1. Take 1/2 cup cold milk, add 2 tsp custard powder. Heat milk in a pan, let it boil, add sugar and mix well.
  2. Add custard mixture. Cook for 2 mins. on low flame. Turn off the flame. Let it cool down. Cut mango, grapes, papaya in pieces.
  3. Add mango, grapes, papaya, pomegranate, almonds, raisin in custard and mix well. Refrigerate for one hour. Garnishing with some tutti fruiti. Fruit custard is ready to serve.
