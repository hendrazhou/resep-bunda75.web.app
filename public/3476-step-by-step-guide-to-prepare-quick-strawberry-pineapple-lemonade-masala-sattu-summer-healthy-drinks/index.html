The ingredients needed to make Strawberry pineapple lemonade,Masala sattu summer healthy drinks:
  1. Get Masala sattu
  2. Prepare 2 tsp sattu
  3. Make ready 1tsp lemon juice
  4. Get 1/2 tsp chopped onion
  5. Take 1/2 tsp green chilli
  6. Take 1/2 tsp coriander leaves
  7. Take 1/2 tsp -blacksalt
  8. Take 1/4 tsp -rock salt
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp roasted cumin
  10. Prepare 1 cup water
  11. Take Strawberry pineapple lemonade
  12. Get 1 cup chopped strawberry
  13. Take 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  14. Make ready 2 tsp -lemon juice
  15. Make ready 2 tsp -simple syrup
  16. Get as needed ice cubes

Steps to make Strawberry pineapple lemonade,Masala sattu summer healthy drinks:
  1. Take a glass add sattu,water mix it together without any lumbar add all ingredients mix it put some ice cubes and serve chilled
  2. Take a blender add chopped strawberries,water to make a purée
  3. Strain purée to remove strawberry seeds add pineapple juice,lemon w,simple syrup,ice cubes mix it and pour into glass serve chilled garnish with chopped strawberries
