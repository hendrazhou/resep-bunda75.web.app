The ingredients needed to make Cheese Garlic Flower Bread:
  1. Get For Dough:
  2. Make ready 2 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Take 3 tbsp sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Take 1 tbsp yeast
  6. Take 3 tbsp curd
  7. Get 1 1/2 cup warm milk
  8. Take 3 tbsp butter or ghee
  9. Get For Filling:
  10. Get 1 cup cream cheese
  11. Get 1 small chopped onion
  12. Make ready 6 cloves chopped garlic
  13. Prepare 2 chopped green chillies
  14. Make ready 1/2tsp crushed black pepper
  15. Prepare 1/2tsp peri peri masala
  16. Get to taste Salt
  17. Get 1/2 tsp oregano
  18. Prepare 1 tsp melted butter or olive oil

Instructions to make Cheese Garlic Flower Bread:
  1. First of all, in a dough maker add all-purpose flour and make holes. In one hole add sugar, in the second hole add yeast and in the third hole add salt and mix the mixture lightly.
  2. After mixing the dry batter, add curd,butter and lukewarm milk and make a smooth and sticky dough. Collect the sticky dough and put it in a oil greased bowl for fermentation and cover the bowl with a cling film for 1 hour.
  3. Now for the filling, add the cream cheese in a bowl and add chopped garlic, chopped chilli,black pepper, piri piri masala,oregano and 1 tsp butter or oil. Keep the mixture in the refrigerator.
  4. After 1 hour the dough would rise and the holes would be clearly visible. Now place the dough on a clear surface and sprinkle some flour. Now, punch the dough and fill the air into the dough.
  5. Now cut the dough into 16 pieces. Flatten the dough pieces with the help of a roller and make a round shape.
  6. Now brush the piri piri mix butter over the flattened dough. Add 1 tsp of cheese mixture and half fold the bread and make a flower petal as shown in the picture.
  7. Make all the petals. Now place all the petals on a baking tray like a flower and cover the flower for 20-25 minutes.
  8. Now pre heat the tandoor or oven for 5 minutes. Now brush the piri piri butter and sprinkle oregano and chilli flakes on the top of the bread.Place the baking tray inside. And bake for 15-17 minutes in tandoor or bake in an oven at 170℃ for 25 -30 minutes.
  9. After baking take the bread out of the tandoor and let it cool for 5-10 minutes. Now, garnish with butter and sprinkle oregano.
  10. Your cheese garlic flower bread is ready to serve.
