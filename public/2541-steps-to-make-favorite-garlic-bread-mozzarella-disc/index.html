The ingredients needed to make Garlic Bread Mozzarella Disc:
  1. Get 1 packet White/brown bread
  2. Prepare 24 pieces Mozzarella pieces
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup All purpose flour
  4. Get 1/4 cup Water
  5. Take 2 cup Grated Parmesan cheese
  6. Take 1 tsp Black pepper
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp Chopped parsley
  8. Get 11 /2 tsp Garlic powder
  9. Take Tomato ketchup for serving
  10. Take Oil for deep-frying

Instructions to make Garlic Bread Mozzarella Disc:
  1. Roll out each bread piece with rolling pin and with help of round cookie cutter cut into round shape.
  2. In a small bowl add flour and water to form paste and keep aside.
  3. Place one piece of mozzarella in the center of one round.
  4. Spread flour paste around the edges and cover with the second piece of bread.
  5. Press edges together to seal.
  6. Repeat same process with other pieces of bread.
  7. Heat oil in deep pan.
  8. Fry each bread disc until golden brown in colour.
  9. Drain fried discs on kitchen paper.
  10. In a big bowl add parmesan cheese, pepper,parsley and garlic powder.
  11. Mix everything well.
  12. Toss each slightly cooled and drained bread disc in the cheese mixture to coat.
  13. Same way coat all the disc with Cheese mixture.
  14. Serve hot with tomato ketchup.
