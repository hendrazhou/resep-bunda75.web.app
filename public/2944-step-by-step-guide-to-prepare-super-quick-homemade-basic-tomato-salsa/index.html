The ingredients needed to make Basic Tomato Salsa:
  1. Make ready 7 Roma tomatoes
  2. Take 3 fresh red chillies. Whatever kind you like, I used red poblano & jalapeño.
  3. Get 4 garlic cloves
  4. Prepare 1 half a white onion
  5. Prepare 1 bunch green onions
  6. Take 1 bunch cilantro
  7. Prepare 3 limes
  8. Get 1 small dash of vinegar (optional)
  9. Make ready 1 salt to taste

Steps to make Basic Tomato Salsa:
  1. In a large, hot, dry pan char garlic cloves, peppers & tomatoes. Cover to steam soften. Let skins get a little blackened.
  2. While those are cooking add to your blender the onions, cilantro, lime juice. Reserve a little bit of the green parts of the scallions.
  3. Add some salt and blend. Add the rest of the veggies and pulse to desired consistency.
  4. Taste to adjust seasoning and add vinegar if desired. I like just a few drops of rice vinegar.
  5. Enjoy!
