The ingredients needed to make Brad's ultimate breakfast nachos:
  1. Take 1 family size bag tortilla chips
  2. Take 1 dozen eggs, beaten and scrambled
  3. Make ready 1 lb bacon. diced and cooked until crispy
  4. Prepare 6 4 oz chicken fried steaks. cooked and chopped
  5. Make ready 1 medium onion diced
  6. Get 1 small can diced green chiles
  7. Prepare 2 cup shredded cheddar mixed with 1 c colby jack
  8. Make ready 1 preferred salsa
  9. Make ready 1 sour cream
  10. Make ready 1 tabasco or other hot sauce
