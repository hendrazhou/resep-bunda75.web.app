The ingredients needed to make Crusty Garlic Bread:
  1. Prepare 4 garlic cloves, minced
  2. Get 4 tsp olive oil
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  4. Prepare 2 tsp thyme
  5. Make ready 3/4 tsp marjoram
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp paprika
  7. Get 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  8. Take 2 loaves (6 oz each) Italian or French bread

Steps to make Crusty Garlic Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a small bowl, combine garlic & oil. Mix well.
  3. In another small bowl, combine parsley, thyme, marjoram, paprika, & cheese. Mix well.
  4. Cut loaf horizontally. Brush cut side with oil mixture. Sprinkle spice mixture over oil.
  5. Fold back together. Wrap in foil. Place on baking sheet.
  6. Bake until heated through, about 10-15 minutes. Unrap loaves. Cut each loaf in half vertically & serve.
