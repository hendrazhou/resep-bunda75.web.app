The ingredients needed to make Mango Salsa Chicken:
  1. Take 1 ripe mango
  2. Get 1/4 cup drained pickled jalapenos
  3. Take 1/4 cup olive oil
  4. Get 1/4 cup fresh cilantro
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup red onion
  6. Make ready 1 1/2 lb chicken breast
