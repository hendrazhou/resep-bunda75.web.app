The ingredients needed to make Garlic bread pizza:
  1. Take 1 loaf frozen garlic bread
  2. Take 1 can tomato sauce (8oz)
  3. Make ready 1 tsp oregeno
  4. Get 1 tsp parsley
  5. Make ready 1 tsp basil
  6. Get 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  7. Get 1 tbsp honey
  8. Take Pepperoni
  9. Prepare Mozzarella cheese
  10. Take Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps to make Garlic bread pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F
  2. Lay parted garlic bread loafs on ungreased baking sheet
  3. In a small bowl, mix tomato sauce, honey, and seasonings
  4. Lightly cover garlic bread with sauce mixture
  5. Sprinkle desired amount of cheese and pepperoni on top; sprinkle with parmesan cheese
  6. Bake in preheated over 10-15 minutes or untill done all the way through. Cut and serve.
