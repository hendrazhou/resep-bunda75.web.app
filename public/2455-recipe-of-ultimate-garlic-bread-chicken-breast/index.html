The ingredients needed to make Garlic Bread Chicken Breast:
  1. Take 4 Boneless chicken breast
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup flour
  3. Get 1/4 cup butter
  4. Get 2 clove garlic (minced)
  5. Get 1 cup finely grated mozzarella cheese
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  7. Take 1/4 tsp parsley
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp oregano
  9. Take 1/4 tsp black pepper
  10. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  11. Take 1/3 cup grated parmesan (packaged)

Instructions to make Garlic Bread Chicken Breast:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Wash chicken breasts and pat dry
  3. Add flour to a Ziploc bag, and add chicken. Shake to coat
  4. In a medium sized saucepan melt butter on low heat
  5. Add minced garlic to butter and allow to cook for about a minute. Remove from heat
  6. Mix panko, parmesan, mozzarella, and spices in a medium mixing bowl
  7. Dip floured chicken breasts in warm garlic butter, saturating completely
  8. Transfer chicken to the bowl containing the breadcrumb mix and press firmly to coat both sides of the chicken breast. Continue for all chicken breasts
  9. Place breasts on a foil lined pan
  10. Once all breasts are coated, pour remaining butter into remaining panko mixture and mix thoroughly. This should be wet, but the breadcrumbs should have absorbed most of the moisture
  11. Pick up a handful of the mixture and pack it on to the top of each chicken breast
  12. Bake for 30 minutes, or until internal temp reaches 170 to 180. Larger chicken breasts may take longer to cook
