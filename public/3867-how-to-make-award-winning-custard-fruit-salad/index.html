The ingredients needed to make Custard fruit salad:
  1. Take 50 gms Custard powder
  2. Make ready As required Mixed fruits chopped
  3. Take Pinch Salt
  4. Make ready 2 spoons Sugar
  5. Prepare As required Dry fruits (optional)
  6. Prepare Pinch Turmeric powder
  7. Get 1 cup Milk

Steps to make Custard fruit salad:
