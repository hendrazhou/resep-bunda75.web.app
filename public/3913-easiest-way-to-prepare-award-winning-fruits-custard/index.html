Instructions to make Fruits custard:
  1. Boil milk in a heavy-bottomed pan.
  2. Mix custard in a separate bowl with 2 tbsp of milk.
  3. Mix custard mixture in boil milk and keep stirring.
  4. Keep in low flame for 10 minutes and keep stirring.
  5. Remove from flame and let it cool to room temperature.
  6. And cut fruits and keep in the refrigerator for 2-3 hours.
  7. Serve cold.
