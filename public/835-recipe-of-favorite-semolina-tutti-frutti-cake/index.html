The ingredients needed to make Semolina Tutti Frutti Cake:
  1. Take 1 cup semolina
  2. Prepare 4 tbsp tutti frutti
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Take 1/2 cup maida
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence
  7. Take 1/4 cup oil
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
  9. Get 1 cup milk
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup curd

Instructions to make Semolina Tutti Frutti Cake:
  1. In a large mixing bowl take semolina, maida, sugar and curd mix them well.
  2. Add milk slowly in a batch and mix well.
  3. Make sure batter should lumps free.
  4. Add oil and mix well.cover it with a lid and let it set for 30 mins.
  5. Add vanilla extract, baking powder, baking soda mix it well.
  6. Add tutti frutti to it mix it.
  7. Pour the mixture into a greased pot. And add a little more tutti frutti over top
  8. Bake it for 50mins or till done. in kadhai aur preheated oven at 180 degrees.
  9. Once done take it out cut it enjoy hot.
