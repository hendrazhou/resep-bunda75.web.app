The ingredients needed to make Nutella Walnut Cookies:
  1. Get 1 cup Nutella
  2. Get 1/2 cup butter
  3. Get 2/3 cup sugar
  4. Get 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  5. Make ready 1 cup chopped walnuts
  6. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  7. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  8. Make ready 1 egg

Instructions to make Nutella Walnut Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Cream butter, sugar, vanilla and egg until smooth.
  2. Add Nutella mix well. Now mix dry ingredients when adding the flour add a little at a time. Add the chopped nuts last.
  3. Drop by heaping teaspoon on a greased cookie sheet.
  4. Bake 15 minutes or until the tops and edges are dry. Cool and store in an airtight container.
