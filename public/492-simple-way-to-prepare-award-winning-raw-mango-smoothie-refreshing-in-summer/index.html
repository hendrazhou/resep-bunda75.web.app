The ingredients needed to make Raw Mango Smoothie Refreshing in summer:
  1. Prepare 1 raw mango (kairi)
  2. Get black salt
  3. Take 2 tbsp sugar or to taste
  4. Prepare 3/4 tsp roasted cumin seeds powder
  5. Take 15 fresh mint leaves
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup cold water
  7. Get 1 ice cubes

Steps to make Raw Mango Smoothie Refreshing in summer:

  2. Now put mango and all ingredients in a mixer mix well make a smooth drink.
