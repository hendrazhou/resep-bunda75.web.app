The ingredients needed to make Spicy garlic salsa:
  1. Take 28 oz diced tomatoes with juice's
  2. Take 1 small yellow onion diced
  3. Prepare 4 tbsp minced garlic
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup fresh copped parsley
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp dry cilantro
  7. Get 1 tbsp chilli powder
  8. Get 1-2 tbsp pickling salt considering how salty you want it
  9. Prepare 2 tsp crushed red pepper

Steps to make Spicy garlic salsa:
  1. Mix all ingredients in a med sized pan.
  2. Heat over med heat. Bring to a summer stirring occasionally.
  3. Simmer for 15 minutes. Stirring occasionally. Remove from heat.
  4. Cool in fridge for at least one hour.
  5. Enjoy!!
  6. Side note* this recipe works well for canning salsa as well.
