The ingredients needed to make Nutella Waffle With Strawberries:
  1. Take 2 Spoonfuls of Nutella
  2. Take 4 Strawberries, cut and washed
  3. Get 2 Frozen waffles; prepared
  4. Prepare 2 Sprinkles of powdered sugar

Steps to make Nutella Waffle With Strawberries:
  1. Take both waffles and add one spoonful of Nutella to each.
  2. Add cut strawberries to the top of each waffle. (Nutella will hold them in place)
  3. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top of each waffle.
  4. Enjoy!
