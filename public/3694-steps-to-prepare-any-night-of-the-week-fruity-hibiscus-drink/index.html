Steps to make Fruity Hibiscus drink:
  1. Remove dirt from the hibiscus and lightly rinse it with water
  2. In a pot,add your rinsed hibiscus and add your water into into it
  3. Add some ginger and some cloves to give that nice aroma
  4. Peel your pineapple (after washing)and add the back of your the pineapple
  5. Blend the flesh of the pineapple and add into your pot containing the other ingredients
  6. Allow to boil for 30-35 minutes in order to absorb all the added ingredients
  7. After boiling,keep aside and let it cool completely for 2-3hrs
  8. After cooling, sieve the water out of it
  9. Add your sieved blended watermelon and pineapple flavor
  10. Add sugar to your desired taste
  11. Put in your cubes
  12. Cut your cucumber into round shape and decorate your drink with it
