Instructions to make Smoothie pops:
  1. Place everything except whip cream in a blender and blend till smooth
  2. Pour a small amount of smoothie mix in your favorite popsicle molds
  3. Add a little whip cream and then pour more smoothie mix on repeat this once more ending with smoothie mix on top
  4. Place tops on popsicle molds and freeze till solid.
