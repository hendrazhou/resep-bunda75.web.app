The ingredients needed to make Lime n' Dill Rice:
  1. Get 1.5 Cups uncooked rice (I use Jasmine)
  2. Prepare 1 Cup water
  3. Get 2 Cups chicken broth or stock
  4. Make ready 1 Large lime or two small limes (juiced)
  5. Get 2 Grated or minced cloves of garlic
  6. Take 1 Tablespoon dried dill (you can use fresh)
  7. Prepare 1/2 Tablespoons Telecherry pepper (black pepper is fine)

Steps to make Lime n' Dill Rice:
  1. Add rice, water, chicken broth/stock, and garlic together in rice cooker. Mix and let cook.
  2. Once cooked, put rice into a serving bowl or mixing bowl.
  3. Gradually add the dill, pepper, and lime juice all while mixing. If you add it all at once it could clump in one section of the rice.
