The ingredients needed to make Buffalo wing pierogies:
  1. Take 1 packages frozen pierogies or mini pierogies
  2. Make ready 1 Non-stick cooking spray
  3. Take 1/2 cup oil
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup melted butter (1 stick)
  5. Get 1/2 cup hot pepper sauce
  6. Take 1 blue cheese or ranch dressing
  7. Prepare 1 carrot and celery sticks
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp chili powder, cajun seasoning, or creole seasoning

Steps to make Buffalo wing pierogies:
  1. Preheat oven to 400° F. Or if you prefer, deep fry the pierogies in 350°F oil for 3 minutes or until golden brown.
  2. Combine melted butter, oil, hot sauce, and chili powder; and toss with the pierogies.
  3. Coat a rimmed baking sheet with the non stick cooking spray and spread the coated pierogies evenly on the baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 16-18 minutes or until pierogies are puffed and browned.
  5. For best results turn over once, halfway through baking time.
  6. Serve with blue cheese or ranch dressing, carrots and celery.
