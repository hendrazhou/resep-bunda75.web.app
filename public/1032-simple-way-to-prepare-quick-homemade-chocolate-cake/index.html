The ingredients needed to make Homemade chocolate cake:
  1. Prepare 250 gm gram flour
  2. Prepare 1 bowl milk
  3. Prepare 1 bowl sugar
  4. Get 1 bowl fresh malai/cream
  5. Get As needed refined oil
  6. Make ready 1 spoon baking powder
  7. Get 1 pinch Baking soda
  8. Take 1 big cadboury chocolate
  9. Prepare 2 spoon coco powder
