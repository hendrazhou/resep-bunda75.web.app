The ingredients needed to make Watermelon Lemonade:
  1. Prepare 3 Big Yellow Lemons
  2. Take 4 Teaspoon Sugar
  3. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  4. Prepare Watermelon
  5. Prepare Leaves Few Mint
  6. Get Fuzzy drink (7-up or Sprite)

Steps to make Watermelon Lemonade:
  1. Take watermelon. Scoop it out and bend it in the blender.
  2. Pour into ice cube mould and put it in the freezer and leave for overnight.
  3. Next day squeeze Lemons in Jar with salt, sugar and water. Lemonade is ready.
  4. Add half Glass of Lemonade and half glass of any fizzy drink.
  5. Take it out the watermelon ice cubes and add into the glass with the mint leaves
  6. Watermelon Lemonade is ready to serve.
