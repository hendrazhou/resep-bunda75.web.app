The ingredients needed to make Cheese garlic bread:
  1. Take 4 slice bread
  2. Take 5 tsp Butter
  3. Get 4 tsp Garlic chopped
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp Kali Mirchi / black pepper
  5. Get 1 tsp Chilli flakes
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp Oregano herbs
  7. Get 5 cubes Cheese
