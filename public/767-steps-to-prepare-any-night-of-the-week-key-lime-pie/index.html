The ingredients needed to make Key lime pie:
  1. Prepare 1 packages Cream cheese
  2. Make ready 1 can Condensed sweetened milk
  3. Prepare 7 limes
  4. Get 1 heavy whipping cream
  5. Prepare 1 large ready made graham cracker crust
  6. Get 1 coconut

Instructions to make Key lime pie:
  1. 1 box of cream cheese add condensed milk blend until smooth
  2. Squeeze 7 limes so about 1/4 of a cup into the cream cheese blend well
  3. Pour into graham cracker crust
  4. Beat heavy whipping cream pour onto cream cheese
  5. Add lime zest for garnish on pie even toast some coconut for garnish
