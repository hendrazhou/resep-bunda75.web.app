The ingredients needed to make Guava & banana smoothie:
  1. Prepare 1/4 cup oats
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup guava puree ,chilled
  3. Take 1 banana, frozen
  4. Prepare 350 ml almond milk
  5. Get 1/2" piece ginger
  6. Get 1 pinch hot paprika powder
  7. Take 1 pinch salt
