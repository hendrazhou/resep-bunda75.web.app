The ingredients needed to make Strawberry key lime pie:
  1. Make ready Lime Cream cheese
  2. Make ready Lime curd
  3. Make ready 3 large eggs
  4. Prepare 1/2 cups sugar
  5. Prepare 1/3 cups key lime juice
  6. Get 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoons key lime zest
  8. Get Creamcheese
  9. Make ready 32 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  10. Prepare Strawberry, quartered
  11. Get 3 cups strawberry
  12. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  13. Prepare Pie crust
  14. Make ready 1 Pie dough

Steps to make Strawberry key lime pie:
  1. In a large bowl, toss the strawberries with sugar. Put in the fridge for 1-3 hours.
  2. (LIME CURD) Beat the eggs with sugar and lime juice in a bowl. Pour water into a pot, then put the bowl above it, boil at 600 celsius. Constantly stir for 10 minutes until smooth. Be careful not to curdle the mixture. Next, add butter and lime zest, stirring until combined.
  3. (CREAM CHEESE) in a separate bowl, combine cream cheese and sugar, stirring until there are no lumps.
  4. Mix lime curd and cream cheese together until smooth, then set a side.
  5. Preheat the oven to 190 celsius (375 fahrenheit)
  6. Line a pie tin with pie dough.
  7. Empty cream cheese into the pie tin, then put strawberry mixture on top, evenly disturbing the mixture.
  8. Top the die with a full sheets of pie dough (or with stripped pie dough and weave a lattice design)
  9. Bake the pie until the filling is bubbling and the crust is golden brown. Cover the edge of the pie with aluminum foil if it starts to darken.
  10. Let cool slightly before serving
  11. Enjoy with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
