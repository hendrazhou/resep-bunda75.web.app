The ingredients needed to make Nutella Buckeyes:
  1. Prepare 1 jar of Nutella
  2. Get 1 stick butter
  3. Get 3 cup powdered sugar
  4. Make ready 1 packages chocolate candy quik

Steps to make Nutella Buckeyes:
  1. melt butter in the microwave
  2. add melted butter to Nutella and powdered sugar until well blended.
  3. roll mixture into truffle size balls
  4. melt candy quik according to package directions
  5. use a toothpick to dip each piece of candy in the chocolate, put candy on wax paper until it the chocolate candy quik sets up.
