The ingredients needed to make Strawberry and Raspberry Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 6 tbsp vanilla fat free yoghurt
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup Orange juice
  3. Make ready 7 strawberries
  4. Get 12 raspberries
  5. Take 4 Ice cubes

Instructions to make Strawberry and Raspberry Smoothie:
  1. Slice strawberries and raspberries into small pieces
  2. Put fruit into blender
  3. Spoon in the yoghurt and add the orange juice ontop
  4. Put the icecubes in last, secure the lid tight and mix for about 1 minute
  5. Serve in glass and add an extra strawberry on the side for decoration :)
