The ingredients needed to make Red grapes sorbet:
  1. Take 1/2 kg Grapes
  2. Take as needed lime juice(splash)
  3. Get 3 tablespoons Corn syrup
  4. Get 2 tsp Homemade tutti frutti for garnishing
  5. Get 2 tsp sweet syrups for garnishing

Instructions to make Red grapes sorbet:
  1. Wash & freeze the red grapes for about 30 minutes then pop it all in a blender jar pulse it 4 times it becomes a smoothie texture drop some lime juice mix some corn syrup
  2. Now pop it in a silicone mold garnish it with some sweet syrups pop it in the freezer
  3. After 3 hours this will look like this when you demold it the deliciously awesome red grapes sorbet is ready
  4. While you are serving scoop it & garnish it with tutti frutti fresh & juicy which will be like cherry on the cake. It’s just fantastic in taste..!
