Instructions to make Barley Soup:
  1. Wash the barley properly and cook it in a cooker for 5-6 whistle. Don't throw the stuck.
  2. Heat up a pan add oil, chopped ginger and garlic.
  3. Add the chopped onion. saute it. Add salt, pav bhaji masala(for a diff in taste). Add the chopped veggies to the pan and saute it for some time.
  4. Add the cooked barley to the pan, add the black pepper powder and 1 glass of water and cook it for another 5-6min. Soup is ready.
