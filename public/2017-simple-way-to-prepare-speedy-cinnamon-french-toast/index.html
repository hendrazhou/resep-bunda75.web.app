The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon French Toast:
  1. Take 4 slices of bread
  2. Prepare 2 eggs
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup milk
  4. Take 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  5. Take 1 dash ground nutmeg
  6. Get 1 butter
  7. Make ready 1 powdered sugar

Steps to make Cinnamon French Toast:
  1. Heat skillet over medium heat
  2. In medium bowl mix together the eggs, milk, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  3. Dip the bread in the egg wash covering both sides then place the bread on the heated skillet.
  4. Flip the bread over when it is light brown in color (after 2-3 minutes)
  5. When both sides are brown, remove from pan and top with butter and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
