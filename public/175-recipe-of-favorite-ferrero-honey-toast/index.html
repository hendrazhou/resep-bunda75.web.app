The ingredients needed to make Ferrero honey toast:
  1. Make ready 1 cubic bread loaf
  2. Take 50 g butter
  3. Take 3 tbsp honey
  4. Get 3 tbsp Nutella
  5. Take 2 scoops vanilla icecream
  6. Take chocolate topping (optional)
  7. Take whipped cream
  8. Make ready Ferrero Rocher chocolate
  9. Prepare Cashew, pecan, almond (optional)

Instructions to make Ferrero honey toast:
  1. Make 2 deep cuts of each side of cube bread (do not reach to the bottom)
  2. Fill honey into each cut throughly. Then apply butter inside the cut and on top of the loaf.
  3. Bake at 170C for 15 min
  4. Right after take the loaf out from oven, apply nutella on top of the loaf. Top with scoop of ice cream, Ferrero chocolate, nuts, chocolate sauce.
  5. Serve with whipped cream and fruits.
