Instructions to make Orange Honey chicken wings:
  1. Preheat oven to 425° . Olive oil on cooking pan
  2. Mix flour with salt pepper and garlic salt. Create a mixture. Enough for all your wings front and back.
  3. Coat front and back of wings with mayonnaise and mustard amf flour mixture. Place on oven for 20 mins each side.
  4. Mix honey, juice from the squeezed orange, honey mustard and a Ponce of cinnamon in a bowl
  5. Coat the front of the chicken and place in the oven for 10 mins at 425° . Then flip over and coat the back and place for another 10 mins
  6. Enjoy
