The ingredients needed to make Chocolate-Cinnamon Holiday Trifle:
  1. Prepare 16 oz. Frozen whipped topping
  2. Make ready 20 European spiced cookies, finely crushed
  3. Take 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  4. Take 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate morsels
  5. Make ready 2 1/2 cup chocolate pudding
  6. Prepare 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  7. Get 2 cups mini marshmallows

Instructions to make Chocolate-Cinnamon Holiday Trifle:
  1. Thaw whipped topping and crush cookies
  2. Heat cream in small saucepan on medium-high heat 4-5 minutes
  3. Remove from heat, add chocolate morsels but do not stir. Let stand 3-4 minutes. Whisk mixture until smooth.
  4. Spread 1 1/4 cup pudding into large serving bowl.
  5. Combine 8 oz whipped topping with remaining 1 1/4 cup pudding and cinnamon. Spread on top of 1st layer of pudding.
  6. Top with 1 cup cookies.
  7. Stir remaining whipped topping with marshmallows.
  8. Pour melted chocolate over cookie layer.
  9. Top with marshmallow mixture.
  10. Top with remaining cookies, cover and chill at least 1 hour.
