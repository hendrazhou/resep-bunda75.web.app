Instructions to make Salsa verde:
  1. Wash Tomatoes, Green Peppers & Tomatillos & add into a pan to boil for 20 minutes.
  2. Dice cilantro & onions. Leave on side for later.
  3. After boiling is done add tomatoes, tomatillos & peppers into blender, add a pinch of salt & blend.
  4. Pour into container & add cilantro & onions & mix it up
  5. Set in frige for few minutes to cool down
  6. Also enjoy with tortilla chips.
