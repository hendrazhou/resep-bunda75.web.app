The ingredients needed to make Watermelon-Mango Smoothie:
  1. Prepare 2 cups watermelon pieces
  2. Make ready 1 cup alphonso mango pieces
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp honey
  4. Prepare 1 cup coconut water/milk

Steps to make Watermelon-Mango Smoothie:
  1. In mixer grinder jar add chopped watermelon, alphonso mango pieces. Pour honey and blend for few minutes.
  2. Add coconut water or coconut milk,blend again. Pour in 2 serving glasses.
  3. Serve Relish Delicious Watermelon-Mango Smoothie immediately.
