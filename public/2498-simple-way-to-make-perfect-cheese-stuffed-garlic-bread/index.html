The ingredients needed to make Cheese STUFFED GARLIC BREAD:
  1. Get 4-5 Bread sliced
  2. Make ready 3 tbsp butter
  3. Take as required Coriander leaves
  4. Make ready 01 tbsp chilli flakes
  5. Get 01 tbsp oregano
  6. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 01 tbsp crushed garlic
  8. Get as required Grated cheese
  9. Take Few Jalapenos chopped

Steps to make Cheese STUFFED GARLIC BREAD:
  1. In a mixing bowl add 3 tbsp butter, coriander leaves and 01tbsp crushed garlic.
  2. Add 01 tbsp chilli flakes, 1 tbsp oregano,,1/4 tsp salt and mix well.
  3. Take a bread slice and apply garlic spread and put some jalapenos.
  4. Add grated cheese as per taste and cheese level.sprinkle some oregano.
  5. Take another bread slice and apply garlic spread and cover it.
  6. In a heated pan grill it from both sides.
  7. Remove on plate. Cut into pieces and serve hot.
