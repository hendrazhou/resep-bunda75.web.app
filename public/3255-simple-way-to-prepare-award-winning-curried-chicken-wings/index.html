The ingredients needed to make Curried Chicken Wings:
  1. Make ready 12 chicken wings
  2. Prepare 1 small jar briyani curry paste
  3. Get 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  4. Take 1 vegetable oil
  5. Get 1 tbsp ground coriander seed
  6. Take 1 tbsp onion powder
  7. Get 2 tsp garlic powder
  8. Prepare 2 tsp ground white pepper

Instructions to make Curried Chicken Wings:
  1. Toss chicken with just enough oil to cover. Season with dried spices. Marinate up to 48 hours.
  2. Whisk the curry paste and vinegar together.
  3. Bake at 400° on the top shelf of the oven for 20 minutes on each side. Drop to 375° and continue cooking for approximately 15-20 minutes or until wings are crispy and thermometer reaches 165° or above.
  4. Briefly crisp the wings under the broiler on each side if necessary.
  5. Toss with curry in a large mixing bowl immediately. The sauce adheres better when the wings are sizzling. Especially fried wings.
