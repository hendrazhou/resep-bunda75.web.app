The ingredients needed to make Banana 🍌 Peanut Butter 🥜 Jelly Snack:
  1. Make ready 1 large burrito size flour tortilla
  2. Prepare 2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter OR crunchy peanut butter
  3. Take 2 tablespoons jelly
  4. Get 1/8 cup chocolate chip morsels
  5. Get 2 bananas, cut into slices
  6. Make ready Butter cooking spray

Steps to make Banana 🍌 Peanut Butter 🥜 Jelly Snack:
  1. In a skillet lightly sprayed with butter cooking spray lightly toast your flour tortilla……
  2. Place toasted tortilla on top of a plate……..let cool for a few minutes…….
  3. Spread your peanut butter on 1 half side of the tortilla, THEN spread your jelly on the other half of your tortilla……
  4. Spread your chocolate chip morsels all over tortilla…….
  5. Place your banana slices along the peanut butter side……
  6. Roll up your tortilla…..
  7. Slice, serve and enjoy 😉!
