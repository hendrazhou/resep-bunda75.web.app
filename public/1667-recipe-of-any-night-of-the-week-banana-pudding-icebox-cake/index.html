Instructions to make Banana Pudding Icebox Cake:
  1. Make pudding according to box
  2. Arrange a layer of wafers on bottom of 8" springform pan, dipping each wafer in milk.
  3. Put 1/3 of pudding on top of wafers.
  4. Arrange a layer of bananas over pudding.
  5. Top with 1/3 of cool whip
  6. Repeat layers 2 more times
  7. Cover with plastic and freeze until hard about 4 hours
  8. Top with cool whip and wafers if desired
