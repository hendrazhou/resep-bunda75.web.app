The ingredients needed to make AMIEs Special Leche Flan:
  1. Get Caramel
  2. Make ready 100 grams brown sugar
  3. Make ready 50 ml warm water
  4. Take Custard
  5. Take 8 egg yolks plus 1 egg white
  6. Get 1 can evaporated milk or fresh milk using the can of evaporated milk for measurement
  7. Make ready 1 can condensed milk
  8. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract or lemon extract
  9. Prepare 50 ml sifted white sugar

Instructions to make AMIEs Special Leche Flan:
  1. Boil the brown sugar and water in a saucepan, stirring continously until the sugar is melted and turns golden brown. Pour the syrup into flan molds, make sure the whole surface is covered. Set aside to allow the caramel to harden.
  2. Gently combine all the custard ingredients in a large bow, first the eggs, condensed milk, fresh milk, sugar and vanilla. Strain gently into the caramel-lined flan molds.
  3. Cover with aluminum foil and place in a bain-marie. Bake in the preheated oven 160°C (325°F) for 60 minutes or until the mixture is firm. Cool and refrigerate for 4 hours before serving. Overnight is the best.
