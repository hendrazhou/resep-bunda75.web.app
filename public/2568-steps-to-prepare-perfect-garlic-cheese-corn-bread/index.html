The ingredients needed to make Garlic Cheese Corn Bread:
  1. Make ready 10 bread slices
  2. Get 1 cup grated cheese
  3. Make ready 5-6 garlic cloves grated or paste
  4. Make ready 1 tsp all purpose seasoning or oregano
  5. Take 1 tsp red chilli flakes
  6. Get 1/2 cup boiled corn kernels
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp butter
