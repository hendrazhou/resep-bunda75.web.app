Steps to make Lemon Chicken Wings:
  1. Wash and pat dry chicken. Marinade with egg, white pepper and flour. Let it sit for 10 mins
  2. Heat pan and add butter, pan fry chicken until done.
  3. Then pour lemon sauce and mix. Simmer until the sauce thicken and absorbe by the chicken. Remive and serve
