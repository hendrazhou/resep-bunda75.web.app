The ingredients needed to make " Angel Lush Trifle ":
  1. Prepare 3 1/2 oz instant vanilla pudding
  2. Get 2 oz can of crushed pineapple - undrained
  3. Get 1 container cool whip - 8 oz.(thawed)
  4. Make ready 1 prepared angle food cake- already baked - 10 oz.
  5. Get 2 cup chopped strawberries
  6. Prepare 2 cup blueberries
Instructions to make " Angel Lush Trifle ":
  1. Mix dry pudding with can of pineapple - do not drain it.
  2. Gently stir in cool whip.
  3. Cut up angle food cake.
  4. Place a layer of cake on bottom of your bowl.Spread pudding in top. Then the fruit.
  5. Repeat layers. Ending with pudding in top. Finish with your fruit.
