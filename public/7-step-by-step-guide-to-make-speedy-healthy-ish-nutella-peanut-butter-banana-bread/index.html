The ingredients needed to make Healthy-ish Nutella Peanut Butter Banana Bread:
  1. Prepare 3 very ripe bananas
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Prepare 2 cups rolled oats
  4. Take 1/3 cup maple syrup
  5. Take 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  6. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanilla
  8. Prepare 2 tablespoons nutella

Instructions to make Healthy-ish Nutella Peanut Butter Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Place all ingredients except Nutella in blender and blend until smooth
  3. Grease 9 inch loaf pan and add batter
  4. Bake for 30 min
  5. Garnish with banana slices, drizzle with Nutella. Enjoy!
