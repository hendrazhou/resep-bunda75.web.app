The ingredients needed to make Suya sandwich and orange banana smoothie:
  1. Make ready 6 sliced bread
  2. Take 3 pieces iceberg lettuce leaf
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup mayonaise
  4. Make ready 2/3 cup ketchup
  5. Get 1 table spoon mustard
  6. Get 1/2 tea spoon chili powder
  7. Get 1/2 tea spoon black powder
  8. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  9. Prepare Sliced onions, tomatoes and cucumber (optional)
  10. Prepare For the fruit smoothie
  11. Take 2 cups orange juice
  12. Get 3 cups yoghurt
  13. Get 4 ripped bana (finely diced)
  14. Make ready Sugar to taste (optional)

Instructions to make Suya sandwich and orange banana smoothie:
  1. Line up the bread slices
  2. Mix the ketchup, mayonnaise, mustard, chili, black powder, and salt to make a dressing
  3. Spread the dressing on one side of the bread, add the “suya/ tsire” meat on top, arrange the cocumber, tomatoes and onion slices
  4. Spread the dressing on another slice of bread on one side, place it on the other dressed bread and serve
  5. For the smoothie
  6. Blend the diced banana with the orange juice, till well blended
  7. Add the yoghurt and sugar to taste
  8. Blend to a smooth texture and serve chilled.
