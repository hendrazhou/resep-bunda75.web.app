The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti:
  1. Get 500 grams raw papaya
  2. Prepare 7 cup water
  3. Take 2 cup sugar
  4. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extra
  5. Take As required food colour - pink, green, orange (or any of your choice)

Instructions to make Tutti frutti:
  1. Firstly, take raw papaya and peel the skin of it. cut the papaya into small cubes boil the papaya cubes in water for 5 minutes.cook until the papaya cubes turn into semi-transparent,drain off the water and keep aside.
  2. Now in a large kadai take sugar and water.further, add semi cooked raw papaya cubes and stir.boil for 20 minutes stirring in between,consistency of sugar syrup should be 1 string.
  3. Turn off the flame and add vanilla extract. mix well.
  4. Divide the cooked papaya cubes into 3 parts along with sugar syrup,add 4 drops of pink, orange and green food colour to each part and mix well.
  5. Allow to soak for 12 hours or a day making sure papaya absorbs all the colour.
  6. Now drain off the sugar syrup and allow them to dry over kitchen towel. once the tutti-frutti dries off completely it will not be sticky. mix the colours together. finally, tutti frutti is ready to consume immediately or refrigerate for later use.
