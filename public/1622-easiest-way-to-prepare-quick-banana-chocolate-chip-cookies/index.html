The ingredients needed to make Banana Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Prepare 2 Bananas
  2. Take 2 cups Flour
  3. Take 2 tsp Baking Soda
  4. Take 1 tsp Salt
  5. Get 1/2 cup Melted Butter
  6. Take 1 1/4 cups Sugar
  7. Get 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  8. Make ready 1 cup Chocolate Chips

Steps to make Banana Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Squish bananas with a fork and add to a bowl. Mix in sugar, melted butter, vanilla extract and salt.
  2. Add flour and baking soda and mix completely. Then mix in the chocolate chips.
  3. Portion cookies onto a cookie sheet.
  4. Preheat oven to 375°F. Bake for 10-15 minutes (until golden).
