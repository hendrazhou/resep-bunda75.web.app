The ingredients needed to make Guava leaves chutney:
  1. Make ready 1 cup Guava leaves chopped
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup coriander leaves
  3. Take 1 small gooseberry sized Jaggery
  4. Prepare 1 cm ginger
  5. Get 1/2 tsp tamarind juice
  6. Prepare Pinch turmeric powder
  7. Make ready 1 Green chilli
  8. Take 1/4 tsp cumin seeds
  9. Get to taste Salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  11. Prepare 1 cup grated coconut
  12. Prepare For seasoning..
  13. Make ready 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
  14. Make ready 1 red chilli broken
  15. Make ready 1 tsp cooking oil
