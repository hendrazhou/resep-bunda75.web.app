Instructions to make Cheeskut:
  1. Blend the cream cheese, condensed milk and fresh milk until all of the ingredients are mixed well.
  2. Spread a thin layer of the cream cheese mixture on a container. Dip the cream crackers in fresh milk and layer them on to the cheese mixture.
  3. Repeat the steps until the mixture is finish. Spread some Milo as a topping.
