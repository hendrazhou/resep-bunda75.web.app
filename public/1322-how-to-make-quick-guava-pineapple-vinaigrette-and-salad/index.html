The ingredients needed to make Guava pineapple vinaigrette and salad:

  2. Make ready 4 tablespoons guava paste
  3. Get 1-1/2 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  4. Get pinch salt
  5. Make ready 1 cup pineapple juice
  6. Get ——– salad ——–
  7. Get 1/2 head iceberg lettuce
  8. Make ready 1 large peach
  9. Prepare 1 cup diced pineapple
  10. Get 1 large tomato
  11. Get 1 medium apple i used granny smith apple
  12. Get 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  13. Take 2/3 cup roasted salted almonds
  14. Take pinch salt
  15. Take 2 large eggs

Instructions to make Guava pineapple vinaigrette and salad:
  1. Heat the pineapple juice to boil melt the guava paste in juice. stir and it will thicken. add salt and vinegar. let sit.
  2. Chop the lettuce
  3. Scramble the eggs and fry set aside
  4. Dice the tomato, peach, and apple. coat with lemon juice.
  5. Add salt and almonds. toss salad and add dressing.
