Steps to make cheesy garlic bread:
  1. preheat oven to 350°F
  2. cut italian bread length wise. spread margarine on loaf
  3. sprinkle on garlic, basil, italian seasoning, salt, pepper. sprinkle on shredded cheese and the sprinkle on grated cheese. and top with paprika.
  4. bake for 10-15 minutes! enjoy!
