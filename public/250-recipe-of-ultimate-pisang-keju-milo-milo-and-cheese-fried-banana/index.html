The ingredients needed to make Pisang Keju Milo / Milo and Cheese Fried Banana:
  1. Make ready 5 bananas (the ones that are suitable for frying or baking)
  2. Get 2 tbsp butter
  3. Get Cheese as desired
  4. Get Choco condensed milk as desired
  5. Take Milo powder (or any choco malt powder) as desired

Instructions to make Pisang Keju Milo / Milo and Cheese Fried Banana:
  1. Put the butter in a pan. Let it melts.
  2. Put the bananas, I put 3 first and then 2 because it didn't fit.
  3. Fry them until brown on one side. Then turn them around. Don't forget to slightly smash them so they become flat.
  4. Do until everything is done.
  5. Then put the bananas on a plate. Pour dome condensed milk and then shredded cheese then the Milo powder.
  6. Serve.
