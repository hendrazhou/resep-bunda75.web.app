The ingredients needed to make Tutti Frutti:
  1. Get 1 cup Watermelon Rinds(white Part)-cuts in small Cubes
  2. Get 1 bowl Sugar-
  3. Make ready as required Food colour-red, green, yellow
  4. Prepare 3 glass Water-
Instructions to make Tutti Frutti:
  1. Take Watermelon White part, peeloff both upper and lower side.cuts in small Cubes.
  2. In a pan add water two glasses and boil.then add Watermelon pieces and boil only 50%.then drain the Watermelon pieces with the Drainer.
  3. Now take a Pan add one glass of water and one large bowl of sugar and boil to make single strain sugar Syrup.
  4. Then add drained Watermelon pieces in the sugar Syrup and boil.then take three separate small bowl and put Watermelon piece into that Bowl along with sugar Syrup.
  5. Now add pinch of Orange, green and red colour into three separate bowl and mix Well.
  6. Keep these bowl for 30 minutes.then place this Watermelon pieces on a tissue paper and soaked sugar Syrup completely.then put in three different plates.
  7. Let's dry for one day.now very tasty and Delicious Tutti Frutti is Ready.you can store in any airtight container and used for long time.
