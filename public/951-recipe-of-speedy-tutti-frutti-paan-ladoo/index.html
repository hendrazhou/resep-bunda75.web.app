The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti paan ladoo:
  1. Take 10-15 paan leaves/ betal leaves
  2. Make ready 400 gms condensed milk
  3. Make ready 50 gms gulkand
  4. Take 5-7 almonds chopped
  5. Get 5-7 raisins
  6. Take 3-5 pistachios
  7. Take 2-3 cashew nut
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp fennel seeds
  9. Get 1 tsp Cardamom (optional)
  10. Get 50 grams desiccated coconut
  11. Prepare 200 grams tutti frutti

Steps to make Tutti frutti paan ladoo:
  1. Wash all the paan and chopped it nicely an add to mixing Jar an add condensed milk and blend it.
  2. Now take a bowl add gulkand add fennel seeds to it and mix it well.
  3. Now add all the dry fruits into the gulkand an add tutti frutti mix it well.
  4. Now take a pan add desiccated coconut and cook it for 2 mins on very low flame.
  5. Now add paan add condensed milk purée into cooked desiccated coconut and cook it till it gets thick.
  6. Now cook it till it come to room temperature.
  7. Make ball add stuffing to it and make ball an coated with desiccated coconut an chill it an serve it
  8. Enjoy!
