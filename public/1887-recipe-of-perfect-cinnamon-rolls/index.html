Steps to make Cinnamon Rolls:
  1. Pre heat oven 350
  2. Warm milk, add yeast. Stir and allow to bloom.(begins to look foamy or blob growing on top of milk….about 25-30 minutes
  3. Add melted 1/3 cup of butter into mixing bowl. Add milk/yeast mixture. Add 1/2 cup of sugar, salt and 2 cups of flour. Mix.
  4. Once mixed, add remainder of flour. Don't be nervous…dough will be sticky.
  5. Dump sticky dough onto floured silicone baking mat. With floured hands, knead and add additional flour until dough forms into soft ball.
  6. Spray bowl with non stick spray, and add dough back to bowl. Cover and allow to rise in warm area. Once dough has risen or doubled in size, add to floured mat. Knead again. Roll out dough. Slather with butter.
  7. Sprinkle with cinnamon mixture. In this batch, I added blueberry preserves instead.
  8. Roll up dough as if rolling a cigar. If dough feels soft… add to freezer just until firm.
  9. Cut the thickness desired and place in greased pan.
