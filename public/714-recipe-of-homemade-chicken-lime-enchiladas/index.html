The ingredients needed to make Chicken Lime Enchiladas:
  1. Take 4 Tortillas
  2. Take 1 1/2 Chicken Breast
  3. Get 1 cup Monterey Jack Cheese
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup Butter
  5. Get 3 tbsp Corn Starch
  6. Take 1 1/2 cup Chicken Broth
  7. Take 6 oz Sour Cream
  8. Prepare 1 Juice of Lime
  9. Prepare 5 Fresh Ground Pepper Cranks
  10. Make ready 2 tsp Cumin
  11. Take 1/4 cup Cilantro and Green Onion
  12. Get 1 Jalapeño Pepper

Steps to make Chicken Lime Enchiladas:
  1. Mix cheese and chicken together in a bowl.
  2. Roll chicken and cheese into tortillas and set on a baking dish.
  3. Melt butter in a pot.
  4. Next add corn starch and whisk until it thickens.
  5. Next add chicken broth to thin it out.
  6. Now add onion, jalapeño, cumin, lime juice, and pepper and sour cream.
  7. Drizzle over enchiladas.
  8. Top with more cheese.. Because why not?
  9. Bake for 25 minutes at 350°F.
  10. Garnish with cilantro and green onion.
