The ingredients needed to make Whole-wheat Tutti-frutti cake:
  1. Prepare 140 g Wholewheat flour/atta
  2. Take 30 g Cornflour
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Baking Soda
  4. Take pinch Salt
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp vinegar
  6. Prepare 120 g Sugar(adjust acc to taste, I like it less sweet)
  7. Prepare 150 ml Milk
  8. Get 80 ml Oil(I used olive oil)
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Any fruit essence
  10. Get 70 g Tutti Fruity

Instructions to make Whole-wheat Tutti-frutti cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 180 deg. Grease a loaf pan with oil and dust with flour. If available line it with parchment paper.
  2. Toss tutti fruity in tbsp of flour and set aside. This stops the tutti fruity from sinking to the bottom of the pan.
  3. Sift wheat flour, cornflour, salt, baking soda, powdered sugar and mix well.
  4. Now add in milk, oil, vanilla and mix well. Now add in tutti fruity and mix well.
  5. Pour into the pan and bake for 45 mins till a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  6. After cooling thoroughly demould it and slice into pieces
