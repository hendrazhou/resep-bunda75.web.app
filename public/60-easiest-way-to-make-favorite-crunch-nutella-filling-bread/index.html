The ingredients needed to make Crunch Nutella Filling Bread:
  1. Get Dough
  2. Get 1 cup Milk
  3. Take 1/4 cup Oil
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
  6. Take 3 cup Flour
  7. Prepare Filling
  8. Take 1 tsp Nutella

Steps to make Crunch Nutella Filling Bread:
  1. Roll all the bread ingredients together well
  2. When your dough came smooth roll it open
  3. After you rolled the dough cut the dough in squares
  4. Take a square each and fill the middle with nutella
  5. Close the dough in a very tightly way that it won't open
  6. After you filled all the squares and closed them, light up the fryer
  7. When the oil starts boiling put the dough in the fryer
