Instructions to make Cheese garlic bread:
  1. Take a bowl add sugar, Yeast, add water, mix it & keep aside 10 minutes for blooming.
  2. Take another bowl, add maida in it, pour bloomed Yeast, mix it.
  3. Now put this mix dough on the kitchen platform & knead it till shiny & light in waite, if water required add it.
  4. Grease a bowl & keep this dough in it for 20 minutes for rising, sprinkle some oil or butter in it. keep it in a warm place.
  5. After 20 minutes take it out, roll the dough slowly.make 2 balls in it. roll it out some thick, add all stuffing things in the butter spread it on the top of rolled dough.spread some cheese in it.
  6. Flip it & make cut marks on it near about 4 marks,1 inches gap.
  7. Keep it again in a warm place for 15 minutes for rising.place it in an oven tray, spread some butter in it.
  8. After 15 miniutes keep it in the preheated oven at 180 degree for 15 miniutes,or till golden.
  9. After 15 minutes bake it. Take it out & serve it hot.
