The ingredients needed to make Karachi Biscuit:
  1. Take 11/2 cup maida/all-purpose flour
  2. Get 1 cup powdered sugar
  3. Take 1/2 cup tutti frutti
  4. Take 1/2 cup custard powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup soft butter
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped cashewnut
  7. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  8. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
  9. Get 6 tbsp milk

Instructions to make Karachi Biscuit:
  1. Seive maida, custard powder and baking powder in a bowl. Keep aside.
  2. In another bowl, mix butter and sugar to make a cream. Add tutti frutti, vanilla essence, cashewnut and mix well.
  3. Add dry seived ingredients. Knead dough using milk.
  4. Divide the dough into two equal portions. Shape it like cylinder. Wrap tightly in cling wrap.
  5. Keep in refrigerator for 1 hour. Cut into pieces. Arrange the pieces in baking tray and bake for 15 minutes at 180° temperature.
  6. Cool completely and serve!
