The ingredients needed to make Spicy deliciousness:
  1. Prepare 2-1/2 chicken breast boneless and skinless
  2. Get 1 cup water
  3. Make ready 15 ounce tomato sauce
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon Pink Himalayan salt
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
  6. Get 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  7. Get 3 tablespoon chili garlic sauce
  8. Make ready 15 ounce chickpea canned and drained
  9. Take 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  10. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  11. Take 1/2 cup Cabernet Sauvignon Blanc
  12. Make ready 1/3 cup finely chopped onion
  13. Take 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  14. Take 1/4 pound guava paste

Steps to make Spicy deliciousness:
  1. Poach the chicken then shred it. Add it back to the broth. Add onion and chickpea
  2. Add tomato sauce, salt, peppers, garlic, chili sauce, Cabernet Sauvignon Blanc, and sugar.
  3. Simmer 15 minutes add tomato paste and guava paste blend well.
  4. Simmer till it's reduced and thick.
