The ingredients needed to make Eggless sweet lime cake with tutti frutti:
  1. Take 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup rava
  3. Get 1 cup milk
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup oil/ghee
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  6. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  8. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  9. Make ready 1.5 tsp lemon juice
  10. Take 10-15 pieces tutti frutti

Steps to make Eggless sweet lime cake with tutti frutti:
  1. Add lemon juice into the milk(room temperature) and keep it aside for 10 minutes.
  2. Add ghee/oil(vegetable), powdered sugar and mix well. Add rava and leave for 10 minutes.
  3. Into it add the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Fold them well into soft batter.
  4. Pour this batter into a greased baking tin. Sprinkle some tuti fruti on top. Bake for 30 minutes in an oven preheated for 180 degree Celsius.
