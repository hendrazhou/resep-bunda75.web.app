The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti:
  1. Get 2 cup Watermelon's peel off
  2. Take 250 grams sugar
  3. Take 250 ml water
  4. Prepare As needed Food colour of your choice
  5. Take 2-4 drop Vanilla essence
Steps to make Tutti frutti:
  1. Peel off watermelon, cut the middle portion (between red and green) into small pieces.
  2. Take a karahi add water and sugar,make a solution, when it becomes half, off the flame and add pieces in it for 1 day with 2 drops of food colour, vanilla essence.
  3. After that strain water and dry it under the fan.
