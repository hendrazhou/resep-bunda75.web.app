The ingredients needed to make Peach Mango Super Smoothie:
  1. Prepare 3/4 cup Fresh or frozen peaches
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Fresh or frozen mango
  3. Make ready 1 cup Nonfat greek yogurt
  4. Get 1/2 cup spinich
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup carrot
  6. Get 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  7. Take 1 tbsp safflower oil
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp flaxseed ground or milled
  9. Prepare 3/4 cup 2% milk

Steps to make Peach Mango Super Smoothie:
  1. Use food processor like the Magic Bullet.
  2. Add mango, peach, juice and half of the yogurt and blend.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients and blend until almost liquid.
  4. Enjoy!
