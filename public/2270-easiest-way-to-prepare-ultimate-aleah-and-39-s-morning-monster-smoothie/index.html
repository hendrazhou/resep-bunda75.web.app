The ingredients needed to make Aleah's Morning Monster Smoothie!:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup plain non fat greek yogurt
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  3. Get 1/2 large cucumber peeled
  4. Take 1 stock celery
  5. Take 1/2 cup carrots
  6. Get 1 medium ripe banana
  7. Prepare 1 bunch (handful) spinach
  8. Make ready 1 bunch (handful) kale
  9. Get 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  10. Get 1/2 cup frozen mango
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup frozen pineapple
  12. Prepare 2 tbsp chia seeds
  13. Make ready 4 tbsp ground flax seed

Instructions to make Aleah's Morning Monster Smoothie!:
  1. add yogurt, coconut milk, cucumber, carrots, celery, spinach, and kale in a blender (I use my bullet) blend till smooth!
  2. Now add banana, blueberries, pineapple, mango, chia seeds, and ground flax! Again blend till smooth!
  3. This will make 2 servings, I put one in the fridge for the next day! Chia seeds gel up slightly so if your planning on drinking the next day just give it a good stir!
