The ingredients needed to make Deep fried Nutella s’mores sandwich:
  1. Prepare 2 pieces white bread
  2. Make ready 1 tablespoon Nutella
  3. Take 1 tablespoon marshmallow cream
  4. Take 1 cup bisquick mix + 1/4-1/2 cup water (batter needs to be thick)
  5. Make ready As needed Chocolate syrup to drizzle

Instructions to make Deep fried Nutella s’mores sandwich:
  1. Heat fryer to 375.
  2. Spread Nutella on one piece of bread and the marshmallow cream on the other and put together.
  3. Dip into the batter and fry 3-5 minutes until golden brown.
  4. Cut diagonal and drizzle with syrup. Serve warm.
