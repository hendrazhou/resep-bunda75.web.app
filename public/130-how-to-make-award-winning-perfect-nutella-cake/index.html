The ingredients needed to make Perfect Nutella Cake:
  1. Prepare 1 1/4 tbsp Baking powder
  2. Make ready 1 1/3 cup All-purpose flour
  3. Get 1/4 tsp Salt
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup Unsalted butter, softened
  5. Get 1 cup Castor sugar
  6. Prepare 2 Eggs
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup Milk
  8. Get 1/2 cup Nutella
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp Vanilla extract

Steps to make Perfect Nutella Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 175°C. Grease and dust with flour a medium-sized loaf tin or a 9" diameter pan.
  2. Combine dry ingredients by sifting together. Set side.
  3. Cream butter on medium speed till smooth then add sugar gradually, beating till pale and fluffy (2-3mins). Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Add the Nutella and vanilla extract and just mix in. Now add half the flour mixture and half the milk and mix. Add the remaining halves and blend till smooth.
  5. Pour into the prepared pan and bake for 45-50mins. or till a skewer inserted in comes out clean.
  6. Option: frost with melted Nutella or just dust with icing sugar.
