The ingredients needed to make Tutti-frutti custard cookies:
  1. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. Take 2 tablespoons vanilla custard powder
  3. Get 1/2 cup butter
  4. Take 1/2 cup sugar powder
  5. Get 1/2 cup tutti_frutti
  6. Get 1 tsp cardamom powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  8. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder

Steps to make Tutti-frutti custard cookies:
  1. Sieve flour and baking powder
  2. Take a bowl add all the ingredients and mix the batter well to form a dough adding milk, lastly mix tutti frutti
  3. The dough should be soft
  4. Take around a small lemon size dough in hand and roll it into flat round shape
  5. In between preheated the microwave and place the cookies ball on the baking tray and place some tutti frutti on the top of cookies
  6. Bake the cookies 20 to 25 minutes at 180 degree centigrade
  7. Bake the cookies till it double in size and changes it's colour
  8. And check by a toothpick, if toothpick comes out clean then your cookies is done
  9. Serve when it cool and keep in a airtight jar
