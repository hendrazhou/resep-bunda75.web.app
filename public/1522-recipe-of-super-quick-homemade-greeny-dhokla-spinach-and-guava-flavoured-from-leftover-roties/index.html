The ingredients needed to make Greeny dhokla (spinach and guava flavoured) from leftover roties:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup Guava chopped
  2. Get 1 cup Spinach
  3. Take 2 pieces Green chillies
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup coriander leaves
  5. Take 1 teaspoon sugar
  6. Prepare As per taste Salt
  7. Take 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  8. Get 5 leftover rotis
  9. Get 1/2 Cup sooji
  10. Prepare 2 cups buttermilk
  11. Take 1/2 tbsp Oil
  12. Get 1 tsp Eno

Instructions to make Greeny dhokla (spinach and guava flavoured) from leftover roties:
  1. Take a mixture jar, add spinach, guava, green chilli, coriander, salt, sugar, lemon juice and grind it.
  2. Take another mixture jar add rotis and buttermilk and make a smooth paste
  3. Then take one bowl mix both the mixture and add sooji and buttermilk if needed. And give a rest for 10 mins.
  4. Then add oil, salt and eno and steam it for 10 to 15 mnts.
