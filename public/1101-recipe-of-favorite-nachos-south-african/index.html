The ingredients needed to make Nachos South African:
  1. Prepare 2 cup Chicken shredded
  2. Make ready 1 packages Doritos
  3. Make ready 1 can Chackalaka
  4. Prepare 1 cup Cheese
  5. Make ready 1 dash parmesan cheese
  6. Take 1 each Tomato diced
  7. Make ready 2 small Spring onions chopped
  8. Get 1 cup Lettuce shredded

Steps to make Nachos South African:
  1. Preheat oven to 180°c
  2. Layer doritos in pan
  3. Layer chicken over doritos
  4. Layer tomatoes on chicken
  5. Pour chackalaka over chicken and tomatoes
  6. Layer the cheeses
  7. Bake until cheese melts
  8. Garnish with Lettuce and Spring onions
