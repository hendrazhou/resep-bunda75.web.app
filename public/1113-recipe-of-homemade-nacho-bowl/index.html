The ingredients needed to make Nacho Bowl:
  1. Prepare 150 g gluten and dairy free tortilla chips
  2. Get 2 chicken breasts diced
  3. Prepare 1 onion diced
  4. Make ready 1 red pepper diced
  5. Take 1 garlic clove minced
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  7. Take 1 teaspoon oregano
  8. Take 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  9. Take 1 lime juiced
  10. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon sugar
  11. Get 1 large ripe avocado
  12. Take 2 teaspoons dairy free mayonnaise

Instructions to make Nacho Bowl:
  1. Make the fajita marinade by combining the garlic, cayenne pepper, oregano, cinnamon, lime juice and sugar in a large bowl - Add the diced chicken and stir until coated - Season with salt and pepper
  2. Heat a drizzle of olive oil on a high heat in a large frying pan - Add the chicken and cook for 5 -10 minutes - Add in the diced onion and peppers and cook for a further five minutes
  3. Arrange the tortilla chips in a large bowl or plate - Place the chicken on top of the chips - Half the cherry tomatoes and season with salt and pepper - Place on top of the cooked chicken
  4. Slice the avocado and place on top of the tomatoes - Drizzle with the dairy free natural yoghurt or mayonnaise
