The ingredients needed to make Chocobiscuit:
  1. Take 10 biscuit (pieces,unsweetened)
  2. Get 1 sachet milo
  3. Prepare 1 sachet milk
  4. Get 2 tsp sugar
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  6. Make ready Coffee
Steps to make Chocobiscuit:
  1. This are the ingredients i used
  2. In a clean pot,pour in the water
  3. Then add sugar and stir it
  4. Add the powdered milk
  5. Then add in the milo
  6. Stir it well. Keep stirring until thick,this is how it will look
  7. My choco is ready,allow it to cool just for few second, then put the biscuit and use spoon or ur finger to wipe it
  8. Serve and enjoy with tea or drink of your choice. But me i enjoyed it with coffee.
