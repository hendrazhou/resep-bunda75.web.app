The ingredients needed to make Cheese stuffed garlic bread:
  1. Get 125 g all purpose flour
  2. Get 2 tsp yeast
  3. Get 1 tbsp sugar
  4. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  5. Get 1 tbsp butter
  6. Take 1 cup lukewarm water
  7. Get 2 tbsp garlic butter
  8. Make ready 2 tsp oregano
  9. Make ready 2 tsp parsley
  10. Get 2 tsp chilli flakes
  11. Get 2 tsp basil
  12. Prepare 2 tsp thyme
  13. Prepare 2 tsp rosemary
  14. Get 2 cubes amul processed cheese(grated)
  15. Take 50 g mozzarella cheese(grated)

Steps to make Cheese stuffed garlic bread:
  1. Take 1/4th cup of lukewarm water and add sugar to it and mix it properly and let it dissolve completely. Add the yeast and mix it properly and allow it to bloom for 5 min.
  2. In a mixing bowl,take flour,butter and salt.
  3. Add the bloomed yeast mixture to the flour and knead it to form a soft dough.
  4. Cover the dough with a clean wrap and let it sit at a warm place for 4 hrs.
  5. After 4 hrs the dough will rise to double of it's size.
  6. Sprinkle some plain flour on the platform and shift the dough to the platform and knead it once. Tug the dough to form a ball.
  7. Roll the ball in 6 inch flat bread and place it over a greased tray.
  8. Apply the garlic butter over the flat bread and sprinkle the herbs and chilli flakes.
  9. Now add the cheese on one half of the bread.
  10. Cover the bread with other half and seal the edges properly.
  11. Apply some garlic butter and herbs in the top and make some slits on the bread and bake it for 15 mins in a preheated oven.
  12. After 15 mins, take the garlic bread out of the oven and let it sit for 2 mins and then cut the garlic bread in slices.
  13. Garlic bread is ready to serve. Serve it with garlic mayonnaise.
