The ingredients needed to make Banana Ginger Energy Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup ice
  2. Prepare 2 cup milk
  3. Take 1 cup yogurt
  4. Get 1/2 tsp fresh ginger, peeled and grated fine
  5. Get 2 tbsp brown sugar
  6. Make ready 2 bananas, ripe
