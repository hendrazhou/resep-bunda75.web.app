Instructions to make Apple Cinnamon Spice Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Spray a 9 inch springform pan with bakers spray
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg and allspice in a bowl
  3. In a large bowl beat cream cheese, butter, vanilla and sugar until light and fluffy
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each egg
  5. Stir in flour just until well mixed, do not overmix
  6. Fold in apples and cinnamon chips
  7. Scrape batter into prepared pan, it will be thick. Smooth with an off set spatula
  8. Bake for 55 to 60 minutes until golden and a toothpick comes out just clean. Cool on wire rack for about 45 minutes before realease ng from pan.
  9. MAKE GLAZE
  10. Heat cream just until hot, pour over cinnamon chips in a bowl. Let sit one minute then stir until smooth. Refrigerate about 15 to 20 minutes, stirring often to thicken some
  11. Pour over cake, decorate with white sprinkles while glaze is wet. Let glaze set before slicing
