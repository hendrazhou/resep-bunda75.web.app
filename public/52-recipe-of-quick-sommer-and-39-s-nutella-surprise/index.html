Steps to make Sommer's Nutella Surprise:
  1. Take sandwich wheat bread pieces and flatten with rolling pin-cut crust off
  2. Spread nutella on one side of bread but only about 1/4 of bread should be covered w/nutella
  3. Slice strawberries thin and lay on nutella spread
  4. Carefully roll up bread peice and use nutella as glue for end of bread to hold together
  5. Mix egg & milk in bowl-quickly dunk bread roll in mixture
  6. Place battered bread roll on hot greased skillet slowly turn consistently until golden brown….
  7. Top with powdered sugar if desired
