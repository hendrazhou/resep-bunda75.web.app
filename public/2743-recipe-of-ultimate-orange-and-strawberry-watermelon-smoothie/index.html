The ingredients needed to make Orange and strawberry, watermelon smoothie:
  1. Take 2 cups fresh diced seedless water melon
  2. Take 1 cup Frozen strawberries
  3. Get 1 cup crushed ice
  4. Get 1 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  5. Prepare 1 tbs lime juice
  6. Take Sweetener (e.g honey) if your fruits isn't perfectly ripe
