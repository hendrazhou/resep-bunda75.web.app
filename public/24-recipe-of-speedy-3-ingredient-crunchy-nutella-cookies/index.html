The ingredients needed to make 3 ingredient Crunchy Nutella cookies:
  1. Take 1 cup Nutella
  2. Prepare 1 cup flour
  3. Take 1 egg

Steps to make 3 ingredient Crunchy Nutella cookies:
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix all the ingredients together with a spoon until a dough batter forms.
  3. Form into 1 inch balls. Place on a cookie sheet. Press the balls down with the palm of your hand until they are round and flat. Space them about 1 inch apart.
  4. Bake for 7-8 minutes for the cookies to set.
  5. Let them cook for 6 minutes and transfer them onto a plate. ✨Enjoy!✨
