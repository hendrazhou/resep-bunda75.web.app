The ingredients needed to make Crazy Sweet Fried Banana:
  1. Prepare 2 bananas
  2. Get 1 cup - flour
  3. Take 2 cup - bread flour
  4. Get 1 box - cheese
  5. Take 1 Chocolate Sprinkels
  6. Prepare 2 tsp - Milo
  7. Take 1 Butter
  8. Get 1 Vegetable Oil
  9. Make ready 1 Mineral Water

Steps to make Crazy Sweet Fried Banana:
  1. Cut Banana, make it half. So you get 4 slice of bananas.
  2. Make flour dough in a bowl, mix the flour with mineral water.
  3. Serve bread flour also in a bowl.
  4. Dip the bananas into flour dough, then dip it again to the bread flour. Do it the same step for the next bananas.
  5. Preaheat the vegetable oil in a fry.
  6. Put the bananas into the fry. Fry it till look goldly.
  7. If starting goldly, it means have been done. Put the bananas to a plate.
  8. Give some butter on the topside of the bananas.
  9. Grate the cheese on the whole side of bananas.
  10. Give Chocolate sprinkles, as much as you Love.
  11. Then pour Milo
  12. Ready to Serve, eat it, feel Fab it. ;)
