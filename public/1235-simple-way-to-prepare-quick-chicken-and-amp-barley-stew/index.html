The ingredients needed to make Chicken & Barley Stew:
  1. Get 8 chicken thighs, however you like them i.e skin on &/or bone in
  2. Make ready 75 g pearl barley, rinsed
  3. Get 2 sticks celery, diced
  4. Prepare 75 g frozen peas
  5. Prepare 1 head little gem lettuce, stalks removed & leaves roughly shredded
  6. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  7. Make ready 500 ml chicken stock
  8. Get 1 tsp each of dried parsley & thyme (or use fresh if you have them, just add towards the end)
  9. Prepare salt & pepper

Instructions to make Chicken & Barley Stew:
  1. Heat about 1 tbsp oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. - Add the chicken, skin side down & cook for about 5 minutes until golden. - Set aside on some kitchen paper. - Add a little more oil to the pan if needed then add the onion & celery. Cook until starting to soften then add the herbs & barley. Cook for another minute stirring all the time.
  2. Pour in the stock, season & simmer for 20 minutes. - Add the lettuce, peas & chicken & simmer for another 30 minutes. Serve with crusty bread if you like but it's good as it is!
