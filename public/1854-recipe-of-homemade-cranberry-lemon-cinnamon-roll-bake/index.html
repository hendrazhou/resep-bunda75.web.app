The ingredients needed to make Cranberry-Lemon Cinnamon Roll Bake:
  1. Prepare 2 packs Pillsbury Cinnamon Rolls
  2. Make ready 1 pack cream cheese
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  4. Prepare 2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
  5. Take 1 cup brown sugar
  6. Get Cinnamon & Sugar

Instructions to make Cranberry-Lemon Cinnamon Roll Bake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°. Coat baking dish lightly with cooking spray. Cut cinnamon rolls into one inch pieces. (about 6-8 each)
  2. Soften cream cheese and mix together with berries and brown sugar.
  3. Pour mixture onto cinnamon rolls and coat them well. Spread evenly over bottom of baking dish.
  4. Top with cinnamon and sugar and your favorite syrup to sweeten up the tang of the cranberries a bit.
  5. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until bubbly around the edges. Let cool for 5 mins.
  6. Combine lemon zest and icing. Cover the bake generously.
  7. Enjoy!
