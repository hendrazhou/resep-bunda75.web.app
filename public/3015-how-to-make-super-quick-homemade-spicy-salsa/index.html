The ingredients needed to make Spicy Salsa:

  2. Get 4 clove Garlic
  3. Take 1 bunch Cilantro
  4. Prepare 1 medium White Onion
  5. Prepare 1 Lime
  6. Make ready 2 Jalepeno
  7. Get 3 Habanero
  8. Take 1 tbsp ground cumin
