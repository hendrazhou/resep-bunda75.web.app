The ingredients needed to make Paan gulkand burfi:
  1. Take 1 cup sugar
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  3. Prepare 300 gm mava

  5. Take 2 tsp paan(betal leaves)crush
  6. Prepare For stuffing

  9. Get 1 tsp tutti-frutti
  10. Prepare 1 tsp poppy seeds
  11. Take 1/2 tsp raising

  2. Now turn off the gas and rest 5mintues then add coconut bura and paan crush and mix well now make stuffing take a bowl and mix all ingredients now gress the tin spread the coconut burfi mixture and spread gulkand stuffing from above
