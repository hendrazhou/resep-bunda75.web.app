Steps to make Tangy Chicken & Nachos Cheese Blast:
  1. Marinate chicken with turmeric powder, red chilli powder, musturd oil, salt, black pepper, ginger garlic paste, salt, lemon for 1 hour. Drain out the water comes from chicken after marination. Add corn flour and fry chicken on low flame until it gets crispy.
  2. In oven proof bowl add this fried chicken with crushed nachos. Sprinkle chat masala, jalepeneo pepper and salt and mix it.
  3. Now add cheese on top with some more salt and pepper and let it cook in microwave for 2-3 mins or till cheese melts.
  4. Serve it with mayo and sauce and with some more nachos
