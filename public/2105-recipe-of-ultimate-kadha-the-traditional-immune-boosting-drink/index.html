Steps to make KADHA - The traditional immune boosting drink:
  1. In a pan add water, 1 bay leaf, grated ginger, cloves, crushed black peppercorns and tulsi leaves and let it boil till water reduces to half.
  2. Now add in palm sugar candy,black salt/pink salt, little tea(optional) and let it boil for 5 more minutes.
  3. Strain the mixture in glass add lemon juice/sliced lemon or drizzle few drops of honey (if not adding palm sugar) before drinking and sip it slowly.
