The ingredients needed to make Curried Mango Pork Tenderloin:
  1. Prepare 1 pork tenderloin; trimmed
  2. Prepare 1 bottle H1 sauce
  3. Get 1 small jar mango chutney or preserves
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp red curry powder
  5. Prepare 2 tsp ground coriander seed
  6. Get 1 tsp onion powder
  7. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  8. Prepare as needed salt and pepper
  9. Prepare as needed olive oil

Steps to make Curried Mango Pork Tenderloin:
  1. Marinate tenderloin with half the H1 sauce for 4-48 hours.
  2. Pat tenderloin dry. Toss with enough oil to cover the tenderloin. Season.
  3. Heat H1 sauce and mango chutney until blended in a saute pan. Add tenderloin to pan.
  4. Roast at 350° for approximately 30 minutes or until pork reaches desired doneness. Turn over pork and baste halfway through cooking time.
