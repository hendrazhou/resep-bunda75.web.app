The ingredients needed to make Cilantro Lime Chicken:
  1. Make ready 6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  2. Take 5 small limes-juiced
  3. Get 1 zest of 2 small limes
  4. Make ready 1 cup chopped cilantro
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp granulated garlic
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions to make Cilantro Lime Chicken:
  1. Add all ingredients to a large zip lock bag and marinate chicken for at least 3 hours or overnight for better flavor.
  2. When ready to cook
  3. Preheat oven to 350°.
  4. Put chicken in a 13x9 baking dish and pour marinade over top chicken.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes to an hour until chicken is no longer pink and cooked through.
  6. Serve with your favorite sides with a little juice from the pan on top of the chicken. Enjoy!
