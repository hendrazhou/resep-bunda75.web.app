Steps to make Milkyway bedtime drink:
  1. In a pot heat heat the soy milk untill the milk starts to foam
  2. Add 1 tsp honey and stir
  3. Add vanilla extract and cinnamon powder
  4. Keep stirring untill the powder is completely dissolved
  5. Once the milk has a foamy texture. Pour into the kettle
  6. Pour the milk in your cup and sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon powder
