The ingredients needed to make mango salsa:
  1. Prepare 1 onion
  2. Make ready 1 mango
  3. Make ready 3 plum tomatoes
  4. Prepare 1 (optional) jalapeno
  5. Take 3/4 cup Cilantro
  6. Take 1 lemon
  7. Take 1 pinch of salt

Mango salsa is a chunky salsa recipe that's a perfect blend of sweet and spicy! This BEST fresh mango salsa recipe! Mango salsa with fresh diced mango, red onion, jalapeno, cilantro and lime. When mangoes are in season and plentiful at the market, it's hard to resist making mango salsa.

Steps to make mango salsa:
  1. Get a middle size bowl and set it aside
  2. Take your onion and make middle dice cuts. then place it the bowl
  3. Cut the mango in half, while removing the seed. make little squares cuts inside the fruit and take the knife and remove the skin. I would just look up a tutorial on YouTube about it. but when you're done place it in the bowl
  4. with the plum tomatoes, quarter them and remove the seeds. middle dice as well and place it in the bowl
  5. for the heat cut the jalapeno in half after you remove the stem. remove the seeds inside and fine dice the pepper.
  6. Fine chopped cilantro or rough chop, its up to you. I like mine roughly chopped
  7. juice the lemon over the ingredients in the bowl and stir. add salt for taste
  8. enjoy :D
