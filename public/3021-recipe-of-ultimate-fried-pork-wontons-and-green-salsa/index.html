The ingredients needed to make Fried Pork Wontons and Green Salsa:
  1. Take 1 packages Wonton Wrappers
  2. Make ready 1 lb ground pork
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 2 scallions, trimmed and chopped, greens and whites
  6. Get 2/3 cup sweetned ginger ( find it at sprouts)
  7. Make ready 2 eggs, seperated
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup plain bread crumbs
  9. Get 2 cup oil for frying
  10. Prepare Green Salsa
  11. Make ready 6 medium tomatillos
  12. Get 2 Serranos
  13. Prepare 1 garlic clove
  14. Get 1/3 cup chopped onion
  15. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  16. Prepare 1 cilantro

Steps to make Fried Pork Wontons and Green Salsa:
  1. Mix all ingredients (except ingredients for salsa and 1 egg) in a large bowl.
  2. In a medium sauce pan, fill halfway with water and add the tomatillos and peppers. Bring to a rolling.boil and cook until peppers and chilies are a dull green. Remove ingredients and add the the blender. Add garlic, salt, onion, and cilantro. Blend until smooth.
  3. Take one wonton wrapper and place one small spoonful of pork mixture in the center. Wet all four sides with egg and place another wonton on top of the pork and press down on all sides.
  4. Fold all four corners toward the center of the wonton.
  5. When all Wontons are filled and made, place in hot oil and fry until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve with the green salsa or your choice of sauce. Enjoy!!
