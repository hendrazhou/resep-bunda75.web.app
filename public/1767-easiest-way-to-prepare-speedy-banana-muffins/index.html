The ingredients needed to make Banana Muffins:
  1. Get 200 g plain flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Prepare 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  4. Prepare 4 bananas
  5. Take 150 g caster sugar
  6. Prepare 1 egg
  7. Make ready 75 g melted unsalted butter
  8. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
  9. Make ready 1 tsp ground cardamom
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup nut and dark chocolate chips mix (optional)
  11. Make ready 12 walnut pieces for topping each muffin

Instructions to make Banana Muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 C.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder and bicarbonate of soda together into a bowl, set aside.
  3. Melt the butter in a pan. Meanwhile, mash the bananas in a bowl. Add cinnamon, ground cardamom, caster sugar, melted butter and the egg to the bananas and mash together
  4. Add in the flour mixture to the mash, and mix until smooth. Then mix in nut and dark chocolate chips mix.
  5. With a spoon, put the mixture into a muffin tin with paper baking cases inside.
  6. Place a walnut on top of each muffin.
  7. Put the muffins into the oven for 25 minutes. Then check if they're done by poking one of the muffins with a toothpick. If it is dry, the muffins are ready. If not, put them back in the oven for another 5 minutes, and you're done!
