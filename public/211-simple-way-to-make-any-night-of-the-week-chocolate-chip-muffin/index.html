The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Muffin:
  1. Take Dry ingredients
  2. Take 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  3. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking soda
  5. Prepare 6 tablespoon skimmed milk powder (replaceable with any kind of milk powder)
  6. Get 3 tablespoon chocolate malt powder (e.g. Milo powder)
  7. Take 4 tablespoon chocolate powder
  8. Get 1 pinch salt
  9. Prepare Wet ingredients
  10. Take 2 medium eggs
  11. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  12. Take 3/4 cup fresh milk
  13. Make ready 3/4 cup brown sugar
  14. Make ready 10 tablespoon melted butter (replaceable with vegetable oil)
  15. Take Add-ons
  16. Prepare 1/2 dark chocolate chips
  17. Get Crushed nuts (peanuts/hazelnut/walnut)

Steps to make Chocolate Chip Muffin:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Sift all dry ingredients together into a clean, dry bowl.
  3. In another bowl, whisk eggs gently, just to incorporate the yolk and the white. Add vanilla essence to the egg mixture.
  4. Add the rest of the dry ingredients into egg mixture, whisk to combine. Careful not to overbeat.
  5. Finally, add the sifted dry ingredients into the liquid mixture in few batches - mix with spatula just to combine the ingredients together. Make sure not to overmix or the muffin might turn into cupcakes :)
  6. Pour the mixture into desired muffin cups, filling up to 3/4 of the cups. Sprinkle the crushed nuts and chocolate chips as many as you want.
  7. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until toothpick come out with some crumbly bits attached to it.
  8. Enjoy the chocolatey goodness!!!
