The ingredients needed to make Spicy Lime Grilled Shrimp:
  1. Prepare 3 T Cajun seasoning
  2. Take 1 lime, juiced
  3. Get 1 T vegetable oil
  4. Prepare 1 lb medium shrimp, peeled and deveined (30-40 per lb)

Steps to make Spicy Lime Grilled Shrimp:
  1. In large zip top bag, combine seasoning, juice and oil. Add in shrimp, seal bag and marinade in refrigerator for 20 min.
  2. Preheat grill and lightly oil grate. Remove shrimp from marinade and shake off excess. Discard remaining marinade.
  3. Cook shrimp on grill until pink, about 2 min. on each side.
