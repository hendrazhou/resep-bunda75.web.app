The ingredients needed to make Milo bread:
  1. Make ready 2 cup flour
  2. Get 2 tbsp yeast
  3. Get 1 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp sugar
  7. Make ready 1 egg
  8. Take Water as require
  9. Prepare Milo
  10. Get 1 sachet Milo
  11. Get 1 sachet powdered milk
  12. Take 2 tbsp butter
  13. Get Topping
  14. Get Seasame

Steps to make Milo bread:
  1. Firstly sieve the flour,then add yeast,sugar and salt
  2. Add oil,butter then mix together
  3. Whisk the egg and put half of it and then mix the flour
  4. Add water and knead the flour to make a dough. Keep kneading the flour for like 10minute,cover and allow it to rise for 1hr in a warm place
  5. After that 1hr,see how the dough double it size
  6. Then divide the dough into 12 balls,set aside
  7. Add butter,milk and Milo in a small bowl mix together till all combined
  8. Take one of the dough roll it flat and put little mixture of Milo at the center,close it and mould it with your hand to make a small cycle ball
  9. Grease the pan with butter and arrange the dough like this. Then cover it for 15mnt to rise again
  10. Egg wash the top with the remaining egg and sprinkle same sesame on it
  11. Put on a reheated oven and bake for 20- 25mnt
  12. See the result. Put melted butter on the bread immediately after baking and over it with kitchen towel for 5mint
  13. My Milo bread is ready to eat
