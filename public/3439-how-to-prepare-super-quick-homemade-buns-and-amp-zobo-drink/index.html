The ingredients needed to make Buns & Zobo drink:
  1. Get buns
  2. Make ready 6 cups Flour;
  3. Prepare 1 cup Milk;
  4. Get cup Sugar;1
  5. Prepare 1 Egg;
  6. Prepare 2 tsp Flavour;
  7. Prepare 2 tsp Baking powder;
  8. Prepare sachet Margarine;1/4
  9. Take 3 tsp Yeast;
  10. Prepare zobo drink
  11. Make ready Sorrel flower(zobo flower); 10cksp
  12. Get Pineapple peels; 1cksp
  13. Get 1 sachet Jolly juice;

Instructions to make Buns & Zobo drink:
  1. Measure ingredients. Sift flour, pour in all dry ingredients and rub in margarine.
  2. When it is crumby, whisk eggs and add to it.
  3. Add water and mix into a soft dough. Set aside to raise.
  4. Cut into round ball and smoothen with palm. Fry in vegetable oil.
  5. Wash and boil zobo flowers together with pineapple peels with a bowl-full of water. Sieve out the liquid and set aside to cool.
  6. Pour in the jolly juice and mix. Cool in refrigerator.
  7. Serve chilled zobo drink and buns. Enjoy!
