Instructions to make Cinnamon Roll Apple Tarts:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F
  2. Flatten dough into muffin tin, leaving no holes in the dough
  3. Scoop desired amount of apple pie filling in each cinnamon roll cup
  4. Add a dash of cinnamon and nutmeg if preferred
  5. Bake for 14-16 mins
  6. Drizzle icing after tarts cool slightly
  7. Enjoy
