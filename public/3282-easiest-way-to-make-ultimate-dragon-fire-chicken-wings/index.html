  1. Get Paste
  2. Get 2 tbsp Olive Oil

  4. Make ready 1 tbsp Onion salt
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp Cayene Pepper
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp Crushed Dry Red Chili Flakes
  7. Get 1/4 tsp Ground Black Pepper
  8. Make ready 2 large Garlic Cloves

Steps to make Dragon Fire Chicken Wings:
  1. Preheat over to 450°F
  2. Combine all ingredients into a blender. Blend until it is a paste
  3. Add paste to thawed 4 lb chicken wings.
  4. Marinade for a minimum of 4 hours.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes. Turning once.
