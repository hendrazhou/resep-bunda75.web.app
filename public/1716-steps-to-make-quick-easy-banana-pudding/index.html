Steps to make Easy Banana Pudding:
  1. Mix pudding as directed on box. Pre-Slice bananas or slice them as you go to desired thickness.
  2. Layer Pudding. Then Wafers. Then Bananas.
  3. Continue to layer in that order until you reach the last layer of each.
  4. Add Cinnamon, Sugar, and Cocoa Banana Topping to the last layer of pudding.
  5. Add last layer of wafers including the crumbles! Top with last layer of banana slices. *(here you can also add whipped cream if you have it!) Enjoy!
