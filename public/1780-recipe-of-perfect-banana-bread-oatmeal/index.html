The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread Oatmeal:
  1. Get 1/2 cup milk
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  3. Take 1/2 cup old fashioned rolled oats
  4. Make ready 1 medium overripe banana, mashed
  5. Make ready Pinch salt
  6. Take 1/4 tsp. vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
  8. Make ready 1/8 tsp. ground nutmeg
  9. Take 1-2 tbsp. sweetener of choice (optional)

  11. Prepare Use your favorite oatmeal toppings, for example:
  12. Take coarsely chopped walnuts
  13. Prepare dark chocolate chips
  14. Prepare additional sliced banana
  15. Prepare nut butter or nutella
  16. Prepare raisins

Instructions to make Banana Bread Oatmeal:
  1. Place the milk and water in a medium saucepan (nonstick recommended) over medium-high heat. Stir in the cinnamon, nutmeg and vanilla and let it come to a low boil.
  2. Next stir in the oats, mashed banana, salt and the ground flaxseed (if using). Decrease the heat to medium-low and allow it to simmer for 5-10 minutes, until it reaches your desired consistency.
  3. Stir in your sweetener of choice to taste. The banana will help to naturally sweeten it, so start out with just a little sweetener. Add whatever toppings sound good to you, then eat immediately.
  4. Pictured topped with a tbsp peanut butter, coarsely chopped walnuts, ground flax seeds and hemp seeds, with about 1 tbsp of maple syrup for sweetener.
