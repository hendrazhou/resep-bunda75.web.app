The ingredients needed to make Guava Parathas:
  1. Make ready 1 cup wheat flour
  2. Take 3/4 cup grated guava fruit
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp finely chopped fresh coriander leaves
  4. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  5. Take 1 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp green chillies ginger paste
  7. Get 2 tsp sugar powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 lemon juice
  9. Get 2 tbsp oil for moin
  10. Make ready to taste Salt
  11. Make ready as required Dry wheat flour for rolling
  12. Prepare as required Oil for shallow fry
  13. Prepare To serve with Parathas
  14. Make ready as required Fresh curd(dahi)

Instructions to make Guava Parathas:
  1. Wash and cut semi ripe guava in half. Crap out the seeded pulp with spoon from it as shown.
  2. Grate in mixing bowl. Add wheat flour, chopped coriander leaves,red chilli powder, green chillies ginger paste, salt,sugar powder, lemon juice, oil. Mix well. Add enough water and knead into semi soft dough.
  3. Make 10 equal portions from prepared dough. Take one portion roll it into round ball and flatten it. With help of dry wheat flour roll into 5" diameter round paratha.
  4. Place it on tawa.. shallow fry with little oil on both sides. Similarly prepare all Guava Parathas
  5. Serve Delicious Hot Guava Parathas with fresh curd.
