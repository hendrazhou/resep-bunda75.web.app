The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Cinnamon Roll:
  1. Prepare Dry ingredients :
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Make ready 1 tablespoons granulated sugar
  4. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  6. Get 1/8 teaspoon salt
  7. Take Wet ingredients :
  8. Take 2 tablespoons milk
  9. Get 1/4 cup pumpkin puree
  10. Get 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  11. Take 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  12. Take Filling :
  13. Get 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  14. Make ready 3 tablespoons light brown sugar
  15. Make ready 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  16. Get 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  17. Make ready glaze :
  18. Make ready 1/3 cups powdered sugar
  19. Take 2 tablespoons cream cheese, softened
  20. Prepare 1 tablespoons milk (as needed)

Steps to make Pumpkin Cinnamon Roll:
  1. Preheat the oven to 375, and spray 6 cups in a muffin pan with cooking spray (or use extra melted butter).
  2. Combine the dry ingredients in a medium bowl. - In a small measuring cup, combine the wet ingredients. - Add the wet ingredients to the dry, and stir until a soft dough forms.
  3. Sprinkle a work surface with flour, and roll the dough out into a rectangle about 6"x 6". Spread the melted butter on top of the dough. - Combine the filling ingredients. Press the mixture lightly into the butter on top of the dough. Roll the dough up starting with the long side. Roll away from you. Cut the dough into 6 equal pieces. Put them into greased muffin cups, and bake for 14-15 min.
  4. Stir together the powdered sugar, cream cheese and milk. Add enough milk to achieve a pourable consistency.
  5. When the cinnamon rolls come out of the oven, let them for 5 minutes then drizzle with the glaze.
