The ingredients needed to make Homemade Salsa Verde:
  1. Prepare 1.5 lb Tomatillo
  2. Make ready 2 Serrano Pepper
  3. Make ready 2 Poblano Pepper
  4. Get 3 tbsp Onion
  5. Prepare 3 tbsp Shallot
  6. Take 4 tbsp Cilantro
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp Lime Juice
  8. Prepare 3 clove Garlic
  9. Get 1/2 tsp Sugar
  10. Take 2 tsp Salt
