The ingredients needed to make Guava Curry:
  1. Prepare 12 Small size Guava Fruit
  2. Make ready 2 Tbs Oil
  3. Take 1 Tsp Mustard Seed
  4. Take to taste Salt
  5. Get 1-2 Cup water
  6. Take 2 Tsp Chili Powder
  7. Get 1 Tsp Turmeric Powder
  8. Take 2 Tsp Coriander Cumin Seed Powder
  9. Prepare 1/4 Cup Jaggery Powder

Instructions to make Guava Curry:
  1. Cut Guava in cubes and remove seeds.
  2. Heat the pan on medium flame, add oil and mustard seed. Let them crackle.
  3. Now, add cubed Guava pieces to the pan. - Add all the spices and Jaggery Powder to it
  4. Mix everything well and add water to it.
  5. Close the lid and cook them for 10-15 min. or until Guava cubes become soft and tender.
  6. Mix everything well again, adjust consistency by adding little bit of water at a time.
  7. Cook again for 5 minutes. and Curry is ready to serve with Roti or Rice.
