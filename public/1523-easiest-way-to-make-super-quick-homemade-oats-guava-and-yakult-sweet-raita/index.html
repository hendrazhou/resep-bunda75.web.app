The ingredients needed to make Oats, Guava and Yakult Sweet Raita:
  1. Take 3 medium Guavas
  2. Make ready 200 gm fresh thick curd
  3. Take 1 tsp Chaat masala
  4. Prepare 2 Yakult
  5. Take 2 tbsp Oats
  6. Take 1/2 tsp Black salt
  7. Take 1/4 tsp Jeera(cumin) powder
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  9. Take to taste Salt

Steps to make Oats, Guava and Yakult Sweet Raita:
  1. Keep all ingredients ready. Chop the Guavas to bite size pieces.
  2. Roast the oats on a low flame for 2 minutes. Grind them to a fine powder.
  3. Beat the curd and add yakult to the curd. Add the powdered oats, all the spices and mix well.
  4. Add the Guavas and mix well. Garnish with fresh coriander and cherries. Sprinkle some more chaat masala on the raita.
