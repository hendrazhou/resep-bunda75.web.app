The ingredients needed to make Baked meatless tacos (with fresh salsa):
  1. Make ready 4 large Flour Tortillas
  2. Take 1 can Black Beans, drained but not rinsed
  3. Prepare 2 large cloves Garlic, chopped
  4. Take 16 oz. Ricotta Cheese
  5. Prepare 1/3 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
  6. Take For the Salsa
  7. Make ready 2 large ripe Tomatoes, diced
  8. Make ready 1 med. Sweet Onion diced
  9. Take 1 large clove Garlic, chopped fine
  10. Get 2 Jalapeño peppers, minced
  11. Prepare 1 tsp. dried Cilantro
  12. Get 1 tsp. dried Oregano
  13. Take 1 pinch Salt
  14. Prepare 1 tbs. Lemon juice

Steps to make Baked meatless tacos (with fresh salsa):
  1. Mix your salsa and refrigerate at least 1 hour.
  2. Mash the beans with the garlic.
  3. Mix the cheeses.
  4. Place 1/4 of beans on half tortilla, top with 1/4 of cheese mix. Spread to edge, fold over. Repeat.
  5. Place filled tortillas on greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350° for approx. 25 minutes or until slightly browned.
  6. Serve with salsa.
