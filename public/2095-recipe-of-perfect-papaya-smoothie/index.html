The ingredients needed to make Papaya smoothie:
  1. Get 100 gms papaya (cut into small pieces)
  2. Take 1 cup coconut milk (chilled)
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp honey/sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 inch ginger (optional)
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  6. Get 5 ice cubes
