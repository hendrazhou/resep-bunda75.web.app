The ingredients needed to make Oreo Nutella Cheesecake:
  1. Get 300 grams Oreo biscuits (crushed)
  2. Get 250 grams Philadelphia cream cheese
  3. Get 1 cup Nutella
  4. Get 4 tbsp Butter (melted)
  5. Make ready 4 tbsp Heavy cream (optional)
  6. Get 1/4 Cadbury chocolate

Instructions to make Oreo Nutella Cheesecake:
  1. In a bowl, mix oreo crumbs and melted butter and press it into a cake pan
  2. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and Nutella until smooth.
  3. Pour it into the cake pan on top of the biscuit base
  4. Refrigerate for an hour or two
  5. Add heavy cream and Cadbury chocolate shaving if desired
