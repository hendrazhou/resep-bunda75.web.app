Instructions to make Konkan Raw Mango Curry:
  1. Collect all ingredients.Grate coconut.Peel and cube raw mango.
  2. Heat oil in a kadhai add jeera, mustard seeds,elaichi,cinnamon and cloves.
  3. Add curry leaves and chopped onion.
  4. Saute it till brown.
  5. Now add ginger garlic paste,chilli pdr and turmeric pdr.
  6. Add cubed mango.Cook it for 5 min in 1 cup of water.It cooks very fast.Keep the mango cubes little crunchy.
  7. Now add coconut paste.
  8. Stir well.Add jaggery and salt.
  9. Sweet and sour curry is ready.
  10. Pour in a dish. Garnish with coriander(optional) and serve.
