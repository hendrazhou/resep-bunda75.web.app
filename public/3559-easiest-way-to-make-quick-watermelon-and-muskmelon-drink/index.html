Steps to make Watermelon and muskmelon drink:
  1. First of all peel both melon and deseed both.
  2. Watermelon and muskmelon Cut in to cubes in different bowl.
  3. Grind the watermelon first smoothly and no lumbs.add sugar and salt mix it. Pour in glass and keep in freezer for 30 minutes.
  4. Second grind the muskmelon and sugar smoothly and lumbs free and it also keep and freezer.
  5. After 30 minutes take out both and muskmelon drink pour in glass with help a spoon slowly slowly.
  6. Now it's cool cool drink are ready to drink.
