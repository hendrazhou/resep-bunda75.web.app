The ingredients needed to make Guava Icecream:
  1. Take 200 grams Fresh cream
  2. Prepare 1 Guava fruit
  3. Take 100 grams Sugar
  4. Take 8-10 Mint leaves
  5. Prepare 4-6 Almond / dry fruits

Steps to make Guava Icecream:
  1. Keep all ingredients ready. Put the fresh cream, sugar, mint leaves and pieces of guava fruit in mixer jar. Whip it around 10 minutes.
  2. Transe to a bowl or box, keep it in freezer for 5 to 6 hours. Now Guava Icecream is ready. Put in serving bowl and garnish with badam or any dryfruits.
