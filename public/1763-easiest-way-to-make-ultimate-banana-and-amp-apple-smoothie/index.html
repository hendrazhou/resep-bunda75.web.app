The ingredients needed to make Banana & Apple Smoothie:

  3. Get 1/2 (1 cup) milk
  4. Get 1/2 cup oats
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup yogurt
  6. Get 1 cup ice

Instructions to make Banana & Apple Smoothie:
  1. Add yogurt and milk to a blender.
  2. Add banana and apple slices.
  3. Add oats and ice.
  4. Set blender to fruit smoothie.
  5. Push down ingredients with a wooden spoon until fully blended.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
