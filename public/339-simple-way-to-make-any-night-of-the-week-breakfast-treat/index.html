Instructions to make Breakfast Treat 😋:
  1. Peel your Irish potatoes
  2. Divide the Irish potatoe into 2 and cut into medium sizes directly into water. Wash and drained in a basket. Add Maggi and pinch of salt. Mix and add in hot oil.
  3. Cover the pot or pan and allow to fry till soft not crispy. It will be like Cook-fry.
  4. Crack the eggs in a bowl, add scotch bonnet and sliced onions. Add pinches of salt and Maggi..
  5. Add 3 tablespoon of water into the egg and beat.
  6. Place your nonstick pan on low heat and pour in the Cook-fry Irish potatoes directly form the oil into the pan. Add your egg
  7. Stir and add curry
  8. Cover and allow to cook for 2-3 minutes. Done
  9. For the homemade Grilled beef (Tsire). In a bowl, add all the dry ingredients and mix. Cut the beef into flat & tiny shapes and mix inside the ingredients.
  10. Arrange on skiwers, drizzle oil and grilled in an oven for 20 minutes. Done 💞
  11. For the tea, In a pot add water, 1 bag of lipton and dried Ginger plus cloves and boil.
  12. When it is done, seive with a seiver and add sugar, Milo and milk to your tatse
  13. Your tea is done
  14. Eat with any bread of your choice or just like that… Your breakfast treat is done…
  15. Enjoy 💞
