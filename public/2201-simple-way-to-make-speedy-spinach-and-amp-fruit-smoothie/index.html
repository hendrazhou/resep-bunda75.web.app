The ingredients needed to make Spinach & Fruit Smoothie:
  1. Take 1/3 cup frozen banana chunks
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup frozen pineapple chuncks
  3. Take 1/2 cup frozen berries mix
  4. Get 1/2 cup 100% Apple juice
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup Spinach (I use the bagged Popeyes kind)
  6. Make ready 6 oz Yogurt (Used peach this time)
  7. Get 1/2 large Cucumber
  8. Take Crushed ice

Instructions to make Spinach & Fruit Smoothie:
  1. Place banana, pineapple and berries on bottom of blender then add apple juice and give a quick blend. (I do this because I'm using frozen fruits.)
  2. Then add spinach, cucumber and yogurt. Blend until smoothied. new word? lol
  3. If it's not cold enough for me I add a small handful of crushed ice and blend again. Then I top with some granola that has dried berries too and enjoy this yummy refreshing smoothie.
