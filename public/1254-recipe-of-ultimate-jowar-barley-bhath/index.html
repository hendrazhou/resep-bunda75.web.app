The ingredients needed to make Jowar, Barley Bhath:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Fresh jowar
  2. Make ready 1 cup Barley cooked
  3. Make ready 1 cup Chopped carrots
  4. Prepare 1 Star anise
  5. Take 1" Cinnamon stick
  6. Get 1 inch Ginger chopped
  7. Make ready 2 Clove
  8. Get to taste Salt
  9. Get 3 tsp Coconut oil
  10. Get Cashews and badam few
  11. Make ready 1 tsp Jeera
  12. Take 1 tsp Pepper powder

Instructions to make Jowar, Barley Bhath:
  1. Take a cup of barley and cook smoothly in pressure cooker, strain and keep ready.
  2. In a pan heat oil, fry cashews and badam.
  3. After golden brown add jeera and spices like star anise, cinnamon, ginger, clove.
  4. Add carrot and jowar, mix well and fry till transulant.
  5. Add salt, pepper powder, mix well.
  6. Add cooked barely, mix well and serve in hot.
