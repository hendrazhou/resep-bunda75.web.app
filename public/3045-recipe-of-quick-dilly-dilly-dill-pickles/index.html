The ingredients needed to make Dilly-Dilly Dill Pickles:
  1. Prepare 2 lbs (6-8 lg) pickling cucumbers
  2. Make ready 2 cups Apple cider vinegar
  3. Prepare 2 cups water
  4. Take 3 tbs sugar
  5. Make ready 2 tbs sea salt
  6. Take 1 tbs whole brown mustard seed
  7. Prepare 1 tbs whole peppercorns
  8. Prepare Fresh Dill
  9. Get Dill weed
  10. Make ready 4 tbs minced garlic
  11. Prepare 2 tbs crushed red pepper (or other pepper if you like it hotter! I used all kinds!)

Steps to make Dilly-Dilly Dill Pickles:
  1. In a medium sauce pan, stir in vinegar, water, sugar, salt, mustard seed and peppercorns. Bring to a slight boil and reduce heat to simmer for about 10 minutes. Remove and let cool for about 20 minutes.
  2. Using maybe 3 large jars, slice or coin your cucumbers however you like. Fill each jar to 1/2. Then add some garlic, dill weed, a couple sprigs of fresh dill, and any pepper spices you are using. Repeat to fill the jars.
  3. Using a funnel, pour the brine into each jar adding an equal amount of the peppercorns and mustard seed to each. Make sure you fill to the very top.
  4. Tighten cap on top of each jar tightly and place into refrigerator. For best results, let them sit for a couple days before eating.
