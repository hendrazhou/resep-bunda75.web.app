The ingredients needed to make Mike's Strawberry Mango Habenero Dip:
  1. Make ready ● For The Pureed Salsa
  2. Prepare 4 Cups Fresh Strawberries
  3. Get 1/2 LG Ripe Mango
  4. Take 1 Medium Tomato
  5. Get 1 tbsp Dried Crushed Mint Leaves

  8. Prepare 3/4 Cup Vidalia Onions
  9. Take 1/2 Cup Fresh Cilantro Leaves

Instructions to make Mike's Strawberry Mango Habenero Dip:
  1. Rough chop all fruits and vegetables and place in blender.
  2. Puree mixture in blender. Work in batches if need be.
  3. Chill well and serve with warm regular or cinnamon fresh tortilla chips. Enjoy!
