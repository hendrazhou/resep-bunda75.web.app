The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. Get 5 pcs loaf bread
  2. Get 1 tbsp butter
  3. Get 1 tbsp cream cheese
  4. Get 1/4 cup grated Processed cheese
  5. Take 8-10 Garlic cloves crushed
  6. Get to taste Salt
  7. Take 1/4 tsp pepper powder
  8. Get 1 tsp paprika
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp parsley finely chopped
  10. Make ready 1 tsp oregano
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  12. Get 1 tbsp Red bell pepper ,finely chopped
  13. Take 1 tbsp Olive oil

Steps to make Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. In a bowl mix all ingredients except grated cheese.
  2. Apply generously on all slices.
  3. Sprinkle grated cheese, paprika, oregano and parsley.
  4. Bake it for 10-12 mins.
  5. Serve hot with mayonnaise/ketchup/Chips/ any Dip.
