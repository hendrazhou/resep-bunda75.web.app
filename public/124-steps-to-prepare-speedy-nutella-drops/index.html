The ingredients needed to make nutella drops:
  1. Get 1/4 cup caster sugar
  2. Take 1 cup butter, room temprature
  3. Take 2 cup flour
  4. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Take 1 nutella for drops

Instructions to make nutella drops:
  1. pre-heat oven to 180C (360F) and line baking try with greased paper and set aside
  2. whip butter and sugar until light and fluffy (3-4mins), then add vanilla extract and combine.
  3. mix in flour, then kneed dough until soft, if too sticky add more flour
  4. roll cookie dough into chosen size balls and then flatten evenly, make a depression with your thumb or teaspoon and fill with nutella. I like a lot of nutella on mine so I'd probably use 1/4 to 1/2 a cup depending on how big or small I've made the cookies
