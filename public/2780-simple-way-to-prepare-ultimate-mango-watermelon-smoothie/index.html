The ingredients needed to make Mango Watermelon Smoothie:
  1. Prepare 2 cups watermelon cubes deseeded
  2. Take 1 mango - peeled and diced into cubes
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp fresh curd
  4. Make ready 2 pinches black salt
  5. Prepare 1 tsp fine sugar (optional)
  6. Make ready 4 mint leaves
  7. Prepare 1 tsp rose water
  8. Prepare 1 cup ice cubes
  9. Take For garnish
  10. Prepare sprig Some mango and Watermelon chunks and mint

Steps to make Mango Watermelon Smoothie:
  1. Blend watermelon, mango, water, and sugar and all ingredients together in a blender until smooth.
  2. Place ice into a chilled glass and pour smoothie over ice.
  3. Garnish with Mango and watermelon chunks and mint sprig.
  4. Serve chilled.
