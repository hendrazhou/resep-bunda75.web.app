The ingredients needed to make Barley Healthy Daliya:
  1. Make ready 1cup barley daliya
  2. Get 2 cup water
  3. Make ready 1 cup milk
  4. Take 4 chopped dates
  5. Prepare 4 Almond chopped
  6. Take 2 tsp raisins
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp honey
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp twin colour choco chips for garnishing

Instructions to make Barley Healthy Daliya:
  1. Boil water and add washed barley daliya in it and cook till thickens, now add milk and stir continuously.
  2. Add chopped dates, almond and raisins & mix well when starts boiling switch off gas and pour honey and mix well. Serve Healthy tasty Barley Daliya in a bowl. Garnish with choco chips.
