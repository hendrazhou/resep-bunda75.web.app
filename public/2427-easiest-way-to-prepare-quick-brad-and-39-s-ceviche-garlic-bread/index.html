The ingredients needed to make Brad's ceviche garlic bread:
  1. Prepare 1/2 lb ceviche
  2. Take 1 french loaf split and spread with garlic butter
  3. Prepare 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  4. Get 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  5. Get 2 oz gargonzola cheese, optiinal
  6. Prepare 1 squeeze of lemon juice per half
