The ingredients needed to make Mango Kiwi Smoothie:

  3. Prepare 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  4. Get 1/4 cup sugar- or to your preference
  5. Get 1 cup ice
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup milk- can use a little less if wanted a little thicker
