The ingredients needed to make Guava Chutney:
  1. Prepare 1 Guava(medium size)
  2. Take 3-4 Garlic pods
  3. Get 2 tbsp Green Coriander
  4. Get 1 Green Chilli
  5. Prepare 1/2 inch Ginger
  6. Make ready to taste Salt
