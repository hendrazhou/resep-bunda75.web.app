The ingredients needed to make Maple cinnamon buns:
  1. Take 3 cups sifted flour
  2. Get 6 teaspoons baking powder
  3. Prepare 4 tablespoons butter
  4. Get 1 teaspoons salt
  5. Get 1 egg
  6. Prepare 2/3 cups raisins
  7. Get 3/4 cold water
  8. Get 1 teaspoons cinnamon
  9. Prepare 5 tablespoons maple sugar
  10. Take 1/2 cups dark corn syrup
  11. Take 4 tablespoons butter

Instructions to make Maple cinnamon buns:
  1. Sift together flour sugar salt and baking powder
  2. With a pastry knife cut in butter
  3. Beat egg with enough water to make a cup
  4. Add to the flour along with the raisins
  5. Turn out on floured surface
  6. Knead to a smooth dough
  7. Roll to 1/2 inch thick
  8. Spread with maple sugar and cinnamon mixed together
  9. Combine the dark corn syrup and butter over low heat mixing just until butter melts.
  10. Cut rolls and dip in Carmel place on greased pan
  11. Bake at 400 for 30 minutes.
