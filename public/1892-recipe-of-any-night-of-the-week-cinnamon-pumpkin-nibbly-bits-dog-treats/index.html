The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Pumpkin Nibbly Bits (Dog Treats):
  1. Take 2 1/2 c Whole Wheat flour
  2. Make ready 1 c Pumpkin puree
  3. Get 1 tbsp Cinnamon
  4. Take 1 raw egg

Steps to make Cinnamon Pumpkin Nibbly Bits (Dog Treats):
  1. In a large bowl, combine pumpkin, cinnamon and egg.
  2. Add flour 1/2 cup at a time.
  3. Roll into a stiff dough then cut into 1/2" strips. (or use a pasta machine to roll into linguine width)
  4. Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
  5. Cool completely then break into manageable pieces. Keeps for up to 2 weeks in airtight container at room temperature.
