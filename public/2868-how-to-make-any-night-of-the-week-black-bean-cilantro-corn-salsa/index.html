The ingredients needed to make Black Bean Cilantro Corn Salsa:
  1. Prepare 1/2 of a dry pint cherry tomatoes – diced
  2. Get 1/4 of red bell pepper – diced
  3. Get 1/2 cup black beans - canned – rinsed and drained
  4. Take 1/2 cup corn – drained (I like fresh corn stripped from the cob during the summer time :)
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup cilantro chopped
  6. Take 2 cloves garlic – finely chopped
  7. Take 1 ounce lime juice- fresh squeezed
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon cumin
  9. Get 1 teaspoon salt
  10. Make ready 2 teaspoons sugar
  11. Prepare 1 ounce olive oil
