  1. Make ready 1/2 cup fresh or canned pineapple, cubed
  2. Prepare 1 large banana, cut into chunks
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup apple banana juice
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup plain yogurt (optional)
  6. Get 1/2 cup ice cubes
