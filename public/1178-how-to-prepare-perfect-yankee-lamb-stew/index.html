The ingredients needed to make Yankee Lamb Stew:
  1. Get 1 Lamb shanks, or shoulder, cuts, or leftover leg
  2. Take 1 flour to coat, mixed with salt, pep, and garlic
  3. Get 6 cups Water
  4. Take 6 Beef bullion cubes
  5. Prepare 1 Onion, large chop
  6. Make ready 3 ribs Celery, sliced
  7. Take 6 Carrots, large chunks
  8. Take 1 tsps. each Rosemary, Thyme, Pepper, Oregano, Margoram, Garlic powder
  9. Take 3/4 cup raw Rice or Barley

Steps to make Yankee Lamb Stew:
  1. In fry pan add 1 tbs. Oil. Heat on high, add floured meat. Brown both sides. This may need to be done in 2 batches.
  2. In Crockpot, add water and beef cubes. Add vegs., and spices. Add meat, with drippings. Stir and cook 3 hrs. covered on High.
  3. Add raw rice. Stir.
  4. Cook additional hr., till rice is soft.
  5. Adjust seasonings and serve with biscuits or crusty bread.
