The ingredients needed to make Strawberry, Bacon, French Toast, Nutella Sauce:
  1. Get 2 Large eggs
  2. Get 1 small baguette or bread that's crunchy
  3. Prepare 1 tsp coconut oil
  4. Prepare pinch salt
  5. Make ready 2 x 1/2 cup Chocolate Almond Milk
  6. Prepare 1 handful bacon
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp nutella
  8. Get Mascarpone for spread
  9. Take 1 handful fresh strawberries

Instructions to make Strawberry, Bacon, French Toast, Nutella Sauce:
  1. In a bowl: - Whisk together eggs, coconut oil, salt and almond milk.
  2. Slice the small baguette or bread thats crunchy into squares soak into egg mix.
  3. In a pan: fry bacon. When finish cooking, add butter and add the bread to fry until brown crispy each side.
  4. Sauce: - Add a small pot over gas stove add 1/2 cup almond milk and 2 tbsp of nutella spread until nutella fully melted.
  5. Final finish: - Grab the bread 1 piece of bread, add mascarpone, put other bread on top, add fresh sliced strawberries on top, add some bacon and pour nutella sauce on top.
