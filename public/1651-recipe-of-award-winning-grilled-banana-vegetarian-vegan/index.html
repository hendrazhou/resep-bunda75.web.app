The ingredients needed to make Grilled Banana *Vegetarian/Vegan:
  1. Prepare 2 cavendish banana
  2. Take 1 tbsp margarine
  3. Get 1/2 cup hundreds and thousand chocolate or colours
  4. Get 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese (can be replaced with jam)

Instructions to make Grilled Banana *Vegetarian/Vegan:
  1. Cut up bananas and slice them 1-2 cm thick.
  2. Heat up margarine in a sauce pan, put banana slices in, cook them for 3 mins, turn to the other side so they are cooked both sides.
  3. Place them into platter, and sprinkle hundreds and thousands and cheese or jam. Enjoy them with tea or coffee.
