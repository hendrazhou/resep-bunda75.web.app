The ingredients needed to make grilled pork chops with guava glaze:
  1. Prepare chops and rub
  2. Prepare 6 pork chops three quarter inch
  3. Take 2 tbsp Hungarian paprika
  4. Get 1/2 tsp cornstarch
  5. Get 2 tbsp granulated garlic powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp ground black pepper
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp onion powder
  8. Get 1 tbsp salt
  9. Prepare 1 melted butter enough to brush on chops I used about quarter cup
