The ingredients needed to make Apple and cinnamon cake:
  1. Get 2 eggs
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 1/3 oil
  4. Take 2 tbsp butter
  5. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
  6. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1 cup shredded apple
  8. Take 1 cup flour
  9. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  10. Make ready 3 tbsp apricot jam
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp coconut

Instructions to make Apple and cinnamon cake:
  1. Put sugar in mixing bowl, add eggs and butter then mix it well.
  2. Add oil, cinnamon, baking powder and mix it well
  3. Add shredded apple, flour, vanilla. Mix it together until fluffy.
  4. Preheat the oven for 180°C and cook for 20 min. Use toothpick to check if its fully cook.
