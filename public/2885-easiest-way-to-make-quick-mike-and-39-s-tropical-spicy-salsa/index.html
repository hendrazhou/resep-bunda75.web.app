The ingredients needed to make Mike's Tropical Spicy Salsa:
  1. Make ready ● For The Fruits & Vegetables [all extra firm & fresh]
  2. Take 2 LG Chopped Mangos [peeled - deseeded]
  3. Get 2 Cups Chopped Strawberries
  4. Prepare 1 Cup Chopped Pineapple
  5. Make ready 1/2 Cup Chopped Nectarines
  6. Make ready 2 LG Jalapeno Peppers [deseeded]
  7. Take 2 Habenero Peppers [deseeded - or 1 tsp dried]
  8. Prepare 1/3 Cup Fine Chopped Green Onions
  9. Make ready 1/3 Cup Fine Chopped Vidalia Onions
  10. Get 1/3 Cup Fine Chopped Red Onions
  11. Make ready 1 Cup Cilantro Leaves [loose packed]
  12. Make ready 1/4 Cup Fine Chopped Celery
  13. Make ready 2 LG Chopped Avocados [added to salsa only before serving]
  14. Make ready 1 LG Lime Juiced [deseeded]
  15. Take 1 LG Lemon Juiced [deseeded]
  16. Take ● For The Seasonings
  17. Prepare 1/2 tsp Fresh Ground Black Pepper
  18. Take 3 Dashes Red Pepper Flakes
  19. Get 1 tbsp Honey [optional]

  21. Get ● For The Sides/Options [fresh - as needed]
  22. Make ready Cinnamon Tortilla Chips
