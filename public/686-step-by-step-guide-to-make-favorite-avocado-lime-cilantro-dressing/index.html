The ingredients needed to make Avocado Lime Cilantro Dressing:
  1. Get 2 cups cilantro stalks
  2. Take 2/3 cup Greek yogurt
  3. Get 1 clove garlic, minced
  4. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  6. Take 2 tsp lime juice
  7. Take 1 tsp white vinegar
  8. Prepare 1/2 Avocado, large dice
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup olive oil

Instructions to make Avocado Lime Cilantro Dressing:
  1. Add all the ingredients except the olive oil to a blender. Blend until mixed uniformly.
  2. Add olive oil and mix one last time.
  3. Voila! Yummy dressing
