The ingredients needed to make Sweet Coconut Sandwich:
  1. Get 1 cup Coconut
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup White sugar / country sugar
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon Ghee
  4. Take 1 teaspoon Cashews and raisins
  5. Take 2 teaspoons Tutti frutti
  6. Prepare 2 Bread slices

Steps to make Sweet Coconut Sandwich:
  1. Heat a pan, add a tablespoon spoon, roast the nuts
  2. Once the nuts are roasted, add the coconut and roast for 5 minutes
  3. Now add the country sugar / white sugar to the coconut and cook for 5 to 10 minutes
  4. Make sure the coconut mix doesn’t stick in the walls of the tawa, finally add tutti fruitti
  5. Toast the bread with ghee or butter, apply the coconut stuffing and serve hot
