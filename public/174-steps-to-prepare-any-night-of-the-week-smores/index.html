Instructions to make Smores:

  2. Place 9 graham squares in single layer on bottom of 8 inch square pan. Top with 36 marshmallows halves.
  3. Drizzle with Nutella then cover with remaining graham squares.
  4. Bake 9 - 11 min or until marshmallows are puffed and golden brown.
