The ingredients needed to make Goan Perad/ Guava Cheese/ Guava Jelly:
  1. Make ready 500 gms Ripe guava
  2. Make ready 1 cup Guava pulp
  3. Take 1 cup Sugar
  4. Get 1 Lemon juice
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Butter
  6. Prepare Pinch Pink food colour (optional)

Steps to make Goan Perad/ Guava Cheese/ Guava Jelly:
  1. Pressure cook ripe guava to one whistle.
  2. Grind in mixer and the sieve the guava paste.
  3. Take equal quantities of guava pulp and sugar.
  4. In a non stick pan take guava pulp, sugar, colour, lemon juice and butter.
  5. Mix well.
  6. Cook on medium flame till the mixer starts leaving the sides of the pan.
  7. Add remaining butter to have extra shine.
  8. Pour in a greased pan, level with a spoon and let it set overnight.
  9. Cut into cubes or any shape u want.
