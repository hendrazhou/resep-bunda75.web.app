The ingredients needed to make Amrud ki Chutney/ Guava Chutney:
  1. Take 2-3 guava / amrud
  2. Get 1 tablespoon jaggery
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup washed and chopped coriander
  4. Prepare 2-3 green chillies
  5. Make ready 1 tsp black salt or table salt or as per taste
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon cumin seeds or jeera

Steps to make Amrud ki Chutney/ Guava Chutney:
  1. First of all, wash guava, coriander and green chillies
  2. Chopped everything roughly
  3. In a mixer jar take chopped coriander, green chillies, guava, cumin seeds, black salt and jaggery
  4. Grind it into a smooth paste. Don't add water, it tastes best as it is.
