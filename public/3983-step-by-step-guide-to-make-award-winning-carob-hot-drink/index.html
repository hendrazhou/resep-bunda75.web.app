The ingredients needed to make Carob hot drink:
  1. Make ready 1 cup Raw Cashews
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp Carob powder
  3. Take 4 cup Hot water
  4. Get 1 pinch salt
  5. Get 1 Creamy honey to sweeten your drink

Steps to make Carob hot drink:
  1. These are all the ingredients you will need.
  2. Soak 1 cup of cashews for 1 1/2 hours.
  3. Boil the 4 cups of water. when done, take out the Cashews from soaking water and place them in to a blender with the 4 cups of hot water and blend.
  4. After blending the Cashews add the Carob powder and the pinch of salt. Then add the Honey until you reach your desired sweetness.
