The ingredients needed to make Raw Guava chutney:
  1. Prepare 2 raw guava
  2. Make ready 4 Green chillies
  3. Get 1 bowl coriander leaves
  4. Take 1/4 tsp black salt
  5. Prepare pinch asafoetida
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp cumin seeds
  7. Get 1/2 tsp tamarind pulp

Steps to make Raw Guava chutney:
  1. Cut guava into small pieces.
  2. Chop coriander leaves.
  3. Put guava, chillies and coriander leaves into a blender and blend it.
  4. Add salt, cumin seeds, asafoetida, tamarind and blend again.
  5. Take it out and adjust the consistency by adding water or curd.
  6. Serve with paratha or any snacks and enjoy.
