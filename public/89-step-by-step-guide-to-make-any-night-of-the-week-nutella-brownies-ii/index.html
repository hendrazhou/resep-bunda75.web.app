The ingredients needed to make Nutella Brownies II:
  1. Take 1 cup nutella
  2. Prepare 1 cup flour
  3. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Make ready 2 large eggs
  6. Take 1/2 cup brown sugar
  7. Get 1 tsp vanilla exract
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup melted butter
  9. Get 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup shredded coconut
