Instructions to make Domino's Style Cheesy Garlic Bread:
  1. In a bowl take 1 cup maida, add sugar, curd, baking soda, baking powder, 2 tsp oil, salt, oregano & chilli flakes and give it a mix now kneed a soft dough by adding milk (at a room temperature) Cover the dough and keep it aside for 30 mins.
  2. Now for layering, take a bowl, add butter, shredded garlic, corriender leaves, chilli flakes, oregano. Mix it well and keep it aside.
  3. Arrange all the filling items, chopped properly, as shown in the image.
  4. After half an hour, again kneed the dough for about 2 to 3 mins & roll the dough into a fat paratha. Take 1 tsp of butter and garlic paste and brush it as shown in the image 2 & 3.
  5. Now put the filling inside it with cheese and chopped veggies, oregeno & chilli flakes as shown in pic 1 and fold it, as shown in pic 2. Again apply the butter & garlic mixture over it. Now greese a plate with some oil and place it over the plate and give it a cut as shown in pic 3. Be careful, donot separate it completely.
  6. Now you can even bake it in kadhai or even steam it. I opted for steaming method. For baking in kadai, u need to pre-heat the kadhai by placing some salt in the base, put a stand, cover it wid a lid and heat the kadhai in medium flame for about 10 mins. Then place the prepared garlic bread plate over it, cover it and cook it in a low flame from 12 to15 mins. And it's done. Do the rest in the same way.
  7. But if you are steaming it, place the bread platter in a steamer put a cloth over it and then cover it and steam it for about 10 to 15 mins. and it's ready to serve.
