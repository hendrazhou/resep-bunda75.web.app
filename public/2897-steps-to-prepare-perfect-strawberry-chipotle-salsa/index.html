The ingredients needed to make Strawberry-Chipotle Salsa:
  1. Take 8 oz fresh strawberries; trimmed & rough chop
  2. Get 7 oz Chipotle salsa
  3. Prepare 14.5 oz fire roasted diced tomatoes
  4. Make ready 1 small red onion; rough chop
  5. Make ready 3 cloves garlic; minced
  6. Make ready 2 limes; zested & juiced
  7. Make ready 1 t cumin
  8. Prepare 1 bundle cilantro; rough chop
  9. Take 1 pinch sugar
  10. Prepare 1 large pinch kosher salt & black pepper
