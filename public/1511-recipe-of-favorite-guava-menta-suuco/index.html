Steps to make Guava menta suuco:
  1. Cut off the both ends of guava completely…i would personally recommend not to peel off the skin and also to not to deseed it… because if you  did so you may loose the originality of this succo..!!
  2. Cut it into medium /small pieces.. add it in the mixer jar along with 4/5 mint leaves.. for 2 medium sized guava.Be careful on the mint side don't add it too much.. coz it may spoil the whole flavor
  3. Add some water (not too much coz it tastes better when it is quite thick)
  4. Finally add required ice cubes and sugar..!! blend it to the maximum coz the seeds have to be well grind-ed..
