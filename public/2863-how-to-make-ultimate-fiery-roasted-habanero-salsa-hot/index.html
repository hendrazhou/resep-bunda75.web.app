The ingredients needed to make Fiery Roasted Habanero Salsa (HOT! 🌶):
  1. Get 1 can Fire Roasted Tomatoes (Salsa Style) in Purée
  2. Prepare 1/4 White Onion, sliced
  3. Take 3 cloves Garlic, halved
  4. Get 1 lg Habanero Pepper, sliced in 1/2 or 1/3
  5. Prepare 1 md Jalapeño Pepper, sliced in 1/2
  6. Take 1 md Serrano Pepper, sliced in 1/2
  7. Take 1/4 cup Cilantro, chopped
  8. Get Salt (to taste)

Instructions to make Fiery Roasted Habanero Salsa (HOT! 🌶):
  1. Set oven to low broil and raise rack to upper position.
  2. Line a cookie sheet with aluminum foil. Place onion, garlic and peppers on cookie sheet. Roast until all items are nicely charred on top about 6 minutes. (You might need to remove the garlic aftter 3-4 minutes as it will char first.)
  3. Pour can of diced tomatoes and all other ingredients into a food processor and pulse until you reach your desired consistency.
  4. Place in an air-tight container and refrigerate for at least an hour before serving to let flavors meld. Will keep up to 7-10 days.
