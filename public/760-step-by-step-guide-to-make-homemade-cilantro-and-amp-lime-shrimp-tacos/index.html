The ingredients needed to make Cilantro & Lime Shrimp Tacos:
  1. Take 1 Shrimp
  2. Take 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  3. Get 1 Lime
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp cilantro
  5. Take 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
  6. Get 1 clove garlic, chopped
  7. Get 1/2 spicy pepper
  8. Take 1/4 onion, chopped

Cilantro (leaf coriander) is one of the traditional Mediterranean herbs which commonly recognized as leaf-coriander in Asia. It is widely employed in savory dishes in almost all parts of the world. Cilantro and coriander come of one and the same plant, a pretty annual herb with feathery leaves and large white umbrella flower heads. Always grow cilantro from seed, directly where you want it.

Instructions to make Cilantro & Lime Shrimp Tacos:
  1. Mix ingredients (besides the pepper)
  2. Marinate shrimp 20 minutes
  3. fry shrimp + vegetables in olive oil
  4. Put in Taco, sprinkle cheese etc. on top
