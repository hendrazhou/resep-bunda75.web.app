The ingredients needed to make Whole Wheat Jaggery Cake:
  1. Take 2 cups whole wheat flour
  2. Get 1 cup jaggery
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup curd
  4. Take 1/2 cup oil
  5. Take 1 tsp vanilla essence
  6. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  8. Prepare 1 tsp vinegar
  9. Take 1/2 cup water
  10. Prepare handful tutti frutti or nuts

Steps to make Whole Wheat Jaggery Cake:
  1. Firstly preheat the kadhai with wire stand in it at medium flame for 10 minutes with covered lid.
  2. Take a jaggery and crushed it properly.Now take a bowl add jaggery,add curd and mix it well to make a smooth mixture.Now add vanilla essence and oil in it and again mix it well.
  3. Now sieve flour,baking powder and baking soda in a jaggery mixture and fold it gently.If it looks little tight,then add water gradually to make a smooth consistency.Now add vinegar in it and mix it again gently.
  4. Now grease the cake tin and pour the batter in a tin and tap it 3 times and add some tutti frutti or sliced nuts on it.
  5. Now keep this in a pre heated kadhai and bake it for 35 to 45 minutes at low flame.And check by inserting toothpick,if it comes clean,then cake is ready.
