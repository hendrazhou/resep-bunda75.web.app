The ingredients needed to make Spicy Tequila Lime Shrimp Tacos:
  1. Get Marinate
  2. Make ready 12 garlic cloves crushed
  3. Make ready 2 1/2 Limes Juice
  4. Get 1/2 cup Tequila
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup olive oil
  6. Take 2 tsp Garlic Salt
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp Ground Cayenne
  8. Make ready dash Ground Oregano
  9. Take dash Sea Salt
  10. Prepare 2 lb Fresh Medium Shrimp peeled
  11. Get Tacos
  12. Take 12 Taco size tortillas
  13. Prepare 2 cup finely shredded cabbage
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup Finely diced red onion
  15. Take 1 cup Sharp Cheddar Cheese grated
  16. Take 1 large Avocado, thinly sliced
  17. Make ready Cilantro Lime Jalepeno Drizzle
  18. Take 1 cup mayonnaise
  19. Take 1/2 cup Chopped Cilantro
  20. Take 5 clove Crushed Garlic
  21. Prepare 1 large Jalepeno, sliced

Steps to make Spicy Tequila Lime Shrimp Tacos:
  1. Add all ingredients together except Shrimp.
  2. Microwave for one minute
  3. Set aside to cool while you peel and blot dry your Shrimp
  4. Put Shrimp in gallon size zip lock bag and add marinate
  5. Refrigerate for about 1 hour
  6. Put Shrimp on skewers or use barbecue veggie roasting pan.
  7. Cook on grill until shrimps are pink
  8. Place Cilantro, Jalapeños, and garlic in blender
  9. Pulse 4-5 times to mix
  10. Add Mayo to blender and blend well, about 1 minute
  11. Add small amount of cabbage in each tortilla,
  12. Sprinkle onion and cheese
  13. Lay 2 or 3 avocado slices on top
  14. Spread 4 shrimp on top
  15. Drizzle with Cilantro Lime Sauce
  16. Enjoy
