  1. Make ready 5 medium Tomatoes
  2. Prepare 1 clove Garlic
  3. Take 1 Green chilli
  4. Take 2 tbsp Extra virgin olive oil
  5. Prepare 1 Red onion
  6. Make ready 1 bunch Basil
  7. Take 2 Limes (juice of)
  8. Get Salt and pepper
