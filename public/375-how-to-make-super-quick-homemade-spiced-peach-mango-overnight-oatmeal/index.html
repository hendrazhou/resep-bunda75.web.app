The ingredients needed to make Spiced Peach/Mango overnight oatmeal:
  1. Make ready 1/4 C rolled oats
  2. Make ready 1/4 C milk of choice, I use almond
  3. Get 2 large Tbsp vanilla yogurt
  4. Take 1/4-1/2 cups peaches and/or mangos I typically use frozen
  5. Prepare About 3/4 tsp cinnamon
  6. Get 1/8 tsp Big punch of nutmeg or about
  7. Make ready Honey

Steps to make Spiced Peach/Mango overnight oatmeal:
  1. Mix together oats, milk, yogurt and fruit and place in the fridge for the night
  2. In the morning take out add spices and drizzle with honey enjoy your easy tasty breakfast!
