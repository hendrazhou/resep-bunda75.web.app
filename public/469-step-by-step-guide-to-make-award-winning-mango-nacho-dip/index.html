The ingredients needed to make Mango Nacho Dip:
  1. Get 1 mango; small dice
  2. Prepare 1 avocado; small dice
  3. Get 2 jalapeños; minced
  4. Make ready 1/2 small red onion; minced
  5. Take 1/2 bundle cilantro; chiffonade
  6. Make ready 1/2 orange
  7. Prepare 4 oz diced green chiles
  8. Make ready 1 C shreddded chihuahua cheese
  9. Take 8 oz cream cheese
  10. Get 1 C peach-mango salsa
  11. Prepare 1 pinch kosher salt

Instructions to make Mango Nacho Dip:
  1. Spread cream cheese across the bottom of a small casserole dish. Sprinkle jalapeño, mango, red onion, avocado, and cilantro atop the cheese.(Save about 1/4 of each to garnish the dip after cooking.) Pour half the salsa across. Spread the diced green chiles across. Squeeze the orange juice across the top. Top with chihuahua cheese.
  2. Bake uncovered at 350° for approximately 30 minutes to melt the cheese. Garnish with remaining avocado, red onion, cilantro, jalapeño, mango, and salsa.
