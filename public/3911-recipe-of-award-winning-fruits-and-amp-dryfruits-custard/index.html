The ingredients needed to make Fruits & Dryfruits Custard:
  1. Prepare 2 cups milk
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp vanilla custard powder
  3. Make ready 2-3 tbsp sugar
  4. Get 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  5. Get 1 apple small sized
  6. Make ready 1 banana
  7. Take 2 tbsp pomegranate
  8. Prepare 2-3 tbsp grapes
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp mango
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp almond, cashews
  11. Take 2 tbsp raisins

Steps to make Fruits & Dryfruits Custard:
  1. Wash fruits and chopped chilled them in the fridge.
  2. Chopped Dryfruits keep aside.
  3. Heat milk in a wide pan add sugar, cardamom power and stir till it dissolves.
  4. Take a small bowl add custard powder and 2 tbsp milk stir well. When milk turns hot, pour custard mixture in to the hot milk stir well to prevent lumps. Keep stirring and cook on low flame until custard turns thick.
  5. Switch off the flame cool completely and chill it in the fridge.
  6. Before serving whisk custard add chopped fruits and dry fruits.
  7. Serve fruits& Dryfruits custard immediately.
