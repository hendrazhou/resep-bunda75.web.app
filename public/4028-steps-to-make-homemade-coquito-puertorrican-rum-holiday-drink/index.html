The ingredients needed to make coquito (puertorrican rum holiday drink):
  1. Take 2 can condensed milk
  2. Take 1 can evaporated milk
  3. Prepare 1 can coconut milk or cream
  4. Get 2 tbsp cinnamon
  5. Get 1 1/4 tbsp lime zest
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 2 cup clear rum (preferably Bacardi)
