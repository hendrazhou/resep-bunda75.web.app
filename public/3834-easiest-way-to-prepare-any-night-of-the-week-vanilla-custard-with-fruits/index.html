The ingredients needed to make Vanilla custard with fruits:
  1. Take 1 litre milk
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp vanilla custard powder
  3. Take 1/4 cup sugar
  4. Take 1 tsp vanilla essence
  5. Prepare 2 cups fruit cubes of your choice

Steps to make Vanilla custard with fruits:
  1. Heat milk in a pan,mix custard powder with a little water or cold milk to form a slurry.
  2. Add this to milk and stir continously to avoid lumping.Add sugar also.
  3. When thick,remove from heat Add vanilla essence.Let it cool.
  4. Cut fruits into cubes.Take in a bowl.Pour chilled custard over fruits.Serve.
