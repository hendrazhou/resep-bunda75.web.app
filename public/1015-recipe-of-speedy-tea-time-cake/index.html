Instructions to make Tea time cake🍰:
  1. Take a bowl add 1 cup semolina and 2 tbsp amul butter.
  2. Add 1 packet milk powder, 1 tsp baking powder, and 1/2 tsp baking soda.
  3. Now add 2 drops mawa essence, 2 cups powder sugar,2 tbsp tuttifrutti
  4. Add chopped nuts cashews, almond, pistachio and 2 cups milk
  5. Mix all ingredients well and Now mix using spectula mix throughly until it's well combined.
  6. Now preheat the oven at 180° for 10 minutes
  7. After preheating the oven bake cake for 35 minutes….
  8. After baking let it cool and take out it in serving plate..
  9. Tea cake ready to serve…garnish with fruit jem
