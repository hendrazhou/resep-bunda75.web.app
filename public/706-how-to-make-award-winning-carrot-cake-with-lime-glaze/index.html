The ingredients needed to make Carrot Cake with Lime Glaze:
  1. Make ready 225 grams Flower
  2. Make ready 1 tsp Baking powder
  3. Take 1 pinch Salt
  4. Prepare Koek en speculaas kruiden
  5. Prepare 500 grams Carrot
  6. Prepare 200 grams Brown sugar
  7. Take 150 grams Sunflower oil
  8. Make ready 95 grams Unsalted pistache nuts
  9. Prepare 185 grams Cream cheese
  10. Prepare 150 grams Powdered sugar
  11. Get 1 Lime
  12. Make ready 3 Eggs

Steps to make Carrot Cake with Lime Glaze:
  1. Heat oven to 180C.
  2. Put baking papier in spring cake tin
  3. Mix flower and baking powder in bowl and add Salt and kruiden
  4. Rasp carrot
  5. Mix sugar, Eggs,l and oil in second bowl. When mixed add carrot and nuts. Finally put together with flower mixture and mix.
  6. Put mixture in cake tin and place in oven for 45 minutes.
  7. Let the cake cool down untill room temperature.
  8. Mix cream cheese, powdered sugar together and wash lime. Rasp lime skin and squeeze it. Put lime rasp on the side. Mix juice in cream mixture.
  9. Put Cream mixture on top of cake and sprinkle lime rasp on top.
  10. Bon appetit !
