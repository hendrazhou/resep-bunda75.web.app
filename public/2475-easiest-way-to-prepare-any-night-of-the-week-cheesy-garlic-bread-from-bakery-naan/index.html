The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Garlic Bread From Bakery Naan:
  1. Prepare 2 Bakery Naan
  2. Prepare 4 tsp Salted yellow Butter (Homemade)
  3. Prepare 4-5 garlic pods
  4. Get 2 green chillies
  5. Make ready 2 tsp chopped coriander
  6. Make ready 3 cubes cheese
  7. Get 1 tsp Oragano
  8. Get 1 tsp Chilli flakes
  9. Prepare 2 tsp oil/ butter (for toasting)

Instructions to make Cheesy Garlic Bread From Bakery Naan:
  1. Grate the cheese. Garlic pods and green chillies put in Chilli cutter and crush it. Homemade salted yellow butter.
  2. Take a mixing bowl add butter, crushed green chilies-garlic, chopped coriander. Mix it all well.
  3. Garlic Butter ready.
  4. Sliced the Naan. Apply Garlic Butter. Sprinkle grated cheese
  5. Sprinkle Oragano and Chilli flakes mix.
  6. Now take griddle/ tava on low flame. Apply Butter on hot tava. Put all pieces on tava. Close the lid for 5 mins, So the cheese gets melts.
  7. Second naan in a different way. Just cut on naan. Apply Garlic Butter in slits and put cheese. Toast on tava.
  8. Serve hot with Ketchup.
