The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti fresh & juicy (homemade):
  1. Take As required Watermelon rind
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 cup water
  4. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla essence
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon Strawberry essence
  6. Make ready As needed Red & green food colour

Steps to make Tutti frutti fresh & juicy (homemade):
  1. Wash & Peel the watermelon rind then remove the red & green part take only the white once then chop then in slices first next make in tiny cubes add water sugar & cubes cover it & boil for 15 minutes
  2. Next uncovere the lid & keep stirring & boil till it’s one string syrup while it’s slightly sticky off the stove burner then add both essence mix well
  3. Once it is cool divide in two parts or as you want the colours in tutti frutti then pop the colours separately mix it & soak with sugar syrup for about two hours
  4. Then drain all the syrup & set all the tutti frutti on a kitchen towel or sliver sheet foil once it is dry you can store it.
  5. Tip of the day:- we like tutti frutti a bit juicy for ice creams & cakes so I kept it slightly juicy.and make sure your sugar syrup is only one string otherwise it will stick & cannot be said tutti frutti.!
