The ingredients needed to make Quick homemade cinnamon rolls:
  1. Get for the dough:
  2. Take 2 & 3/4 cups of flour
  3. Prepare 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  4. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Prepare 1 package dry instant yeast
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  7. Get 1/4 cup milk
  8. Get 2 tablespoons butter
  9. Prepare 1 egg
  10. Make ready For the filling:
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup butter softened
  12. Get 1/4 cup brown sugar
  13. Take 2 tablespoons cinnamon
  14. Make ready For the icing:
  15. Prepare 1 cup powdered sugar
  16. Take 3 tablespoons milk
  17. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
