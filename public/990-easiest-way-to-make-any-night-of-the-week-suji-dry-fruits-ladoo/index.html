The ingredients needed to make Suji dry fruits ladoo:
  1. Take 1 bowl suji
  2. Prepare 1/2 bowl milk powdered
  3. Prepare 1 bowl sugar powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 bowl dry fruits
  5. Prepare 1/2 bowl tutti frutti
  6. Make ready 5-6 tbsp milk
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp desi ghee
  8. Take 2-3 drops red food color
  9. Take 2-3 tbsp fresh gratted coconut powder
