The ingredients needed to make My Favorite Salsa:
  1. Make ready 8 tomatillo
  2. Get 1 bunch green onions
  3. Make ready 1 bunch cilantro
  4. Prepare 1 white onion
  5. Prepare 2 clove garlic
  6. Take 1 jalapeño
  7. Take 1 hatch chili
  8. Make ready 1 Poblano chili
  9. Get 1 Serrano chili optional, if you like it hot
  10. Prepare 3 limes
  11. Make ready 1 salt to taste
  12. Make ready 1 avocado

Instructions to make My Favorite Salsa:
  1. In a large, dry skilet on high heat, char the tomatillo, chilies & garlic. I like to cover them to steam a little to.
  2. When they are softened, and the skins are blackened in some places, remove from heat.
  3. In a blender add all other ingredients and pulse a few times.
  4. Now add tomatillo, garlic & chilies. Don't forget the lime juice! You can add water if needed.
  5. Blend to desired consistency, and taste to adjust seasonings.
  6. Enjoy! Great with seafood & grilled meats.
