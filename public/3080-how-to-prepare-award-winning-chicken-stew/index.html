  2. Get 3 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch cubes

  5. Get 3 cobs frozen corn (half sized)

Steps to make Chicken Stew:
  1. Combine everything except the cans of soup in the Dutch oven. Turn on medium heat and start to soften the veggies and cook the chicken.
  2. When the contents start sizzling, deglaze the pot with chicken broth, between a half cup and a cup.
  3. Reduce heat to simmer and cook about 20 minutes stirring every 5 minutes to prevent sticking.
  4. Add the four cans of cream of chicken soup and stir. If mixture is too thick, add chicken broth to thin. Cook another 20 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes or stew will stick.
  5. Remove cooked corn on the cob from pot and cut corn off cob with a knife, then add kernels back to stew.
  6. Cook for another 10 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat, stew will be screaming hot. Let cool 10 to 15 minutes before service.
  8. Ladle stew into bowls, serve and enjoy.
