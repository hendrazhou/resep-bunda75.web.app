Steps to make Oreo milky mocha drink:
  1. Put in your favourite instant coffee and milo into a big mug. Add the evaporated milk. You can adjust the sweetness based on your own preference.
  2. Here is the secret to make this super milky drink! Pour in some hot water only 1/4 of your mug; just enough to melt the mixtures. Make sure the water is super hot!
  3. Throw in some ice cubes until they fully fill your mug. Add some water to fill the space left in your mug and stir them well!
  4. Crush some oreo biscuits the way you like and put them on the top of your drink and walllaaa! enjoy!
