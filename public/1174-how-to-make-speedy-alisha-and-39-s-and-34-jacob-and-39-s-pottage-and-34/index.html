The ingredients needed to make Alisha's "Jacob's Pottage":
  1. Make ready 10 cup water
  2. Get 1 lb lintels
  3. Make ready 2 cup barley
  4. Take 1 1/2 cup granulated chicken bullion
  5. Take 6 piece chicken tenderloins
  6. Prepare 1 can diced tomatoes
  7. Make ready 1 can artichoke hearts (chopped if desired)
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp cracked black pepper
Steps to make Alisha's "Jacob's Pottage":
  1. Put 10 cups of water and the chicken bullion in pot. Add pepper, lentils,barley.
  2. While that's boiling cook your chicken how u prefer. I always pre boil mine then freeze for recipes like this. So mine is already cooked And ready to add along with lentils ect.
  3. When chicken tenderloins are done or if u already had some cooked, add them to ur pot with the barley ect. Add ur can of diced tomatoes also.
  4. After that all simmered together if u like artichokes get them out and chopp them or just rip them up in ur soup! All done! So simple yet sooo goood!
