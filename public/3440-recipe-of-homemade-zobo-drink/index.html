The ingredients needed to make Zobo drink:
  1. Get 2 cups hibiscus flower
  2. Get 8 cups water
  3. Take 2 slices pineapple
  4. Get 1 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  5. Take 2 teaspoons cloves
  6. Prepare Sugar (as desired)

Steps to make Zobo drink:
  1. In a pot, put in water and hibiscus flower and bring to a boil.
  2. After 10 minutes of boiling, add in the cloves
  3. After 5 minutes, add chopped pineapples.
  4. After another 5 minutes, add in orange juice
  5. Let boil for another 5 minutes and take off heat.
  6. Let cool down then add sugar as desired.
  7. Stir and serve.
