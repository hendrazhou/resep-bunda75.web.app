The ingredients needed to make Sriracha-Lime Baked Tilapia:
  1. Prepare 2 tilapia filets
  2. Take 4 tbsp Sriracha
  3. Prepare 1 t onion powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 t garlic powder
  5. Prepare 1/4 t white pepper
  6. Prepare 2 limes; zested
  7. Take 1/4 cup cilantro; chiffonade
  8. Take As needed salt

Steps to make Sriracha-Lime Baked Tilapia:
  1. Season tilapia. Cover with sriracha. Squeeze the juice from one lime over the filets.
  2. Bake at 400° for approximately 5-8 minutes or until fish reaches 145°
  3. Garnish with lime zest and cilantro. Serve with lime wedges.
  4. Try serving over coconut jasmine rice
