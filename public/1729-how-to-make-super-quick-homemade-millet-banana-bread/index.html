The ingredients needed to make Millet banana bread:
  1. Get 2 cups millets flour
  2. Prepare 1 cup all purpose flour
  3. Prepare 5 ripped banana (mashed)
  4. Prepare 4 tablespoon honey
  5. Get 2 tablespoon sugar
  6. Take 3 teaspoon baking soda
  7. Make ready 2 tablespoon flaxseed (powder)
  8. Take 1 egg
  9. Get 1 apple (blend into apple sauce)
  10. Get 1 cup chopped almonds
  11. Prepare 100 g butter (melted)
  12. Get 1 teaspoon salt or to your taste
  13. Prepare 1 pinch orange zeste
  14. Make ready 1 teaspoon rose water(optional) or vanilla, I prefer rose water
  15. Prepare 1 pinch nutmeg

Steps to make Millet banana bread:
  1. Pre-heat the over 350 degree Fahrenheit. Grease and put flour on the bottom of the pan.
  2. Mixed the dry ingredients in a bowl, millet, flour, baking soda, flaxseed, nutmeg, orange zeste, salt, chopped almonds, and sugar.
  3. Mix the egg, honey, apple sauce, honey, melted butter, and rose water or vanilla
  4. Mix the dry ingredients with the liquid ones then pour into the pan. Bake for 45 minutes or till the fork or toothpick you put in the middle of the bread came out clean
  5. Let it cools of then cut it into 12 pieces and serve it with tea.
