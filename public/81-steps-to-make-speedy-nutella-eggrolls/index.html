The ingredients needed to make Nutella Eggrolls:
  1. Take 1/2 cup nutella
  2. Get 5 egg roll wrappers
  3. Get 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  4. Get 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  5. Get 1 vegetable oil

Instructions to make Nutella Eggrolls:
  1. Place eggroll wrapper on flat surface. Put a teaspoon or as desire of nutella. Wet the edges of the wrapper.
  2. Fold the eggroll wrapper like an envelope. Meanwhile in a shallow dish mix the sugar and cinnamon and set aside.
  3. Heat oil and deep fry for a minute or two until desire golden brown. As soon as they are done roll them in the cinnamon sugar and enjoy!
  4. Caution!!! Filling will be hot!!! And they are addictive ;)
