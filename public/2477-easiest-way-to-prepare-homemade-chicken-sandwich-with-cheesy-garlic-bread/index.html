The ingredients needed to make Chicken sandwich with cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Prepare For cheesy garlic bread-
  2. Make ready 8 brown bread slice
  3. Prepare 8-9 cloves garlic
  4. Take as required Chesse gratted
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Black pepper powder
  6. Take 1 tsp Dried oregano
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Red Chilli flakes
  8. Get For sandwich-
  9. Get 1 medium sized onion
  10. Make ready 1 medium sized tomato
  11. Get 1 medium sized capsicum
  12. Make ready some cabbage
  13. Take 50 gms boiled chicken
  14. Make ready as required Honey
  15. Make ready to taste Salt and black pepper
  16. Prepare as required Olive oil

Steps to make Chicken sandwich with cheesy garlic bread:
  1. First, grate the cheese add finely grated /very finely chopped garlic black pepper and oregano. Mix them well. Spread the cheese mixture on the bread.
  2. Now in a pan put olive oil then put the bread and let the cheese melt. Add little ketchup and red Chilli flakes. The bread is ready
  3. Now for the sandwich cut the onion, tomatoes, capsicum and cabbage (you can add more vegetables of your choice) also shred the boiled chicken.
  4. Now in a pan add olive oil, add the onion first stir it a little and then add the rest add salt, black pepper honey and just saute it.
  5. Now take the cheese bread put the stuffing of one bread cover it with the other and it is ready to be served.
