Steps to make Guava Jelly no artificial colour:
  1. Wash and cut guavas
  2. On the flame put a wok add water and citrus mix well
  3. Add guava and let it boil until tender
  4. Take out this boiled guava on a muslin cloth under it keep a bowl to collect the water
  5. This collected water keep on the flame and add the sugar and make syrup of one string
  6. Switch off the flame let this syrup come to room temperature pour in into the clean and dry glass bottle jar and close the lid of bottle and store it in cool place you can refrigerator it too
