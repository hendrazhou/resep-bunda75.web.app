The ingredients needed to make Mango Salsa:
  1. Take 3 ripe mangoes - peeled, seeded and chopped
  2. Make ready 1/2 large red pepper chopped
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup red onion chopped
  4. Get 1/4 cup cilantro chopped
  5. Get 1 jalapeno pepper finely chopped
  6. Get Juice of 2 small limes
  7. Get 1/2 tsp salt

Steps to make Mango Salsa:
  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Cover and let sit for 30 to 60 minutes before serving.
