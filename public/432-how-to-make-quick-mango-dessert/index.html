The ingredients needed to make Mango Dessert:
  1. Prepare 1 pack agar-agar
  2. Prepare 250 ml fresh mango juice
  3. Prepare 100 ml water
  4. Take 1 tbsp sugar (adjust to your liking)
  5. Get 2 cups nata de coco
  6. Get 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced
  7. Take 2 cups cream (regular milk works fine as well)
  8. Take 1/4 cup condensed milk
  9. Prepare Ice
