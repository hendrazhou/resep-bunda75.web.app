The ingredients needed to make Guava chaat:
  1. Prepare 4 guava
  2. Make ready as needed Kasundi
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp Chilli powder
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Sugar
  5. Take To taste Salt

Instructions to make Guava chaat:
  1. Cut the guavas into blocks.
