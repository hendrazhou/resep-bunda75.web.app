The ingredients needed to make Strawberry and tutti fruity marvel cake:
  1. Prepare 1 cup refined flour (maida)
  2. Prepare 1 cup Sugar
  3. Take 1/2 Cup curd
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  5. Make ready 1/2 tbs baking powder
  6. Take 1/2 tbs baking soda
  7. Get 2 spoon strawberry crush
  8. Get 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  9. Get 1 tbs vanilla extract
  10. Take 2 tbs tutti frutti

Steps to make Strawberry and tutti fruity marvel cake:
  1. In a strainer take refined flour, baking powder, baking soda then double strain so that no lumps
  2. In other pan take sugar powder, butter, curd beat it to make paste
  3. Add dry ingredients in it.Add vanilla extract.
  4. Divide it into three parts first part remain same in second part add strawberry crush,in third part add cocoa powder
  5. Now take a cake mould grease it with the help of brush with oil put a butter paper
  6. Pour the mixture first white then red,after it coco mix turn by turn till last.Sprinke tutti frutti over it,now place mould in preheated microwave at180 degree for 40 minute
  7. Now check it with toothpick if clean put out let it cool demold the cake tin and cut into small pieces
