The ingredients needed to make Salsa:
  1. Take 1 Habanero
  2. Make ready 3 Poblano Peppers
  3. Make ready 1 Manzano Pepper
  4. Prepare 2 Tomatoes
  5. Prepare 4 cloves garlic
  6. Take 1 Onion
  7. Take 2 mangoes
  8. Prepare 6 oz can tomato paste
  9. Take 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  10. Take 2 tablespoons sugar
Instructions to make Salsa:
  1. Cut peppers and veggies in halves and place on baking sheet
  2. Broil for about 5-10 minutes until soft and browned
  3. Put veggies in food processor/blender, blend with mango, salt, pepper, tomato paste, and sugar to taste. (Blend to what consistency you enjoy, I like mine pureed.)
  4. Refrigerate.
