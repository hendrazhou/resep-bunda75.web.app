The ingredients needed to make Healthy Mixed Fruits Smoothie:
  1. Take 1 cup Vanilla Frozen Yogurt
  2. Prepare 1 cup V8 V-Fusion Black Cherry Berry Juice (or whatever else wanted)
  3. Take 1 1/2 cup Frozen Mixed Fruits
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup Half/Half Milk
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp Sugar (optional)
  6. Get 1 tbsp Vanilla Extract (optional)
  7. Make ready 1 Whip Cream (optional topping)

Steps to make Healthy Mixed Fruits Smoothie:
  1. Mix frozen yogurt, juice, and milk
  2. Make sure consistency of mixed product is somewhat thick
  3. Add desired amount of fruit, save some pieces of fruit for decoration if wanted.
  4. Add in sugar and vanilla extract for added taste
  5. Blend, and enjoy! :)
