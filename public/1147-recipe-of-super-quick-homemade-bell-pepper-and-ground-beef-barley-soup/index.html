The ingredients needed to make Bell Pepper and Ground Beef Barley Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 lb ground beef
  2. Take 3 bell peppers: red, yellow, & orange
  3. Take 1/2 cup barley
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp parsley
  5. Take 1 large onion
  6. Make ready 1 can (8 oz) stewed tomatoes
  7. Prepare 1 tsp rosemary
  8. Prepare 48 oz beef broth
  9. Make ready 1 salt and pepper to taste

Steps to make Bell Pepper and Ground Beef Barley Soup:
  1. Brown the ground beef in a frying pan with olive oil. Meat should be in small pieces.
  2. Cut the bell peppers and onion into 1 inch pieces.
  3. Add the bell peppers, barley, beef broth, ground beef, stewed tomatoes, rosemary, and parsley into a large pot.
