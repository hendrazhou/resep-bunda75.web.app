The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Roll French Toast Bake:
  1. Prepare 2 cans cinnamon roll dough
  2. Make ready 1/2 C heavy cream
  3. Make ready 1 egg
  4. Make ready 2 t allspice
  5. Take 1 t vanilla extract
  6. Get 2 T butter
  7. Take 1/2 C maple syrup

Instructions to make Cinnamon Roll French Toast Bake:
  1. Whisk together egg, cream, vanilla, and allspice.
  2. Butter a casserole dish. Arrange rolls to fit the casserole dish. Pour cream mixture over rolls. Top with syrup. Bake at 350° for approximately 20 minutes or until rolls are golden brown.
  3. Top with icing after baking.
