The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti muffins (eggless):
  1. Prepare 3/4 th Cup All purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1/3 rd Cup sugar
  3. Get 1/4 rd Cup tutti frutti coated in flour
  4. Take 1/4 th cup oil/melted butter
  5. Get 1/4 cup thick curd
  6. Make ready 1/4 th cup water or as required
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking soda
  8. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  9. Make ready Pinch salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 tbsp lemon juice/clear vinegar
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla essence

Steps to make Tutti frutti muffins (eggless):
  1. First, preheat the oven at 180°c for min 10-15 mins. And line the muffin tray.
  2. Sieve the dry ingredients in a bowl i.e. flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt. Mix gently and set aside.
  3. In another bowl add sugar, oil, water and vanilla extract and mix well until emulsified.
  4. Whip the curd up till smooth, add vinegar mix a bit and keep aside.
  5. Gradually add the sugar mixture to dry ingredients with cut and fold method.
  6. At last, add the curd and vinegar mixture.
  7. Once batter is smooth and lump free.. fold in the tutti frutti. fill the muffin cups 3/4 th and tap on the surface to release air bubbles.
  8. Bake for 12-15 mins or until a skewer comes out clean.
  9. Enjoy.
