The ingredients needed to make Green smoothie:
  1. Prepare 1 apple
  2. Make ready 6-7 spinach leaves
  3. Make ready 2 tsp flax seeds
  4. Get 10-12 almonds
  5. Make ready 1 tsp dessicated coconut
  6. Get 1/4 lemon
  7. Get 1 tsp honey
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup almond or soya milk or water
  9. Make ready Pinch salt
