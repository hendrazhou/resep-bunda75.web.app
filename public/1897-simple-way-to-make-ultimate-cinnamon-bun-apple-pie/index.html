  3. Prepare 1 stick butter
  4. Get 1/2 cup brown sugar
  5. Take 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  6. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions to make Cinnamon bun apple pie:
  1. Peal & Slice Apples put in bowl
  2. (You can do steps 1-3 a day in advance. Keep refrigerated until ready to use.) Lay out plastic wrap. Space out the rolls as you lay them out.
  3. Cover top with more plastic wrap seal in rolls. Make sure no openings.
  4. Use rolling pin to flatten rolls..
  5. Melt butter, add brown sugar, cinnamon, vanilla to sliced apples and mix together.
  6. Place rolled out buns in bottom of baking dish until it's full.
  7. Add apple mixture on top of flattened cinnamon roll.
  8. Next roll out second can of cinnamon rolls to cover the apples.
  9. Preheat oven 350 Bake 45 minutes or until apples soft. Poke with fork.
  10. Add icing
