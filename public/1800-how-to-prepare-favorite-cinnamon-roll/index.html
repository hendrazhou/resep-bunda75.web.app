The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon roll ❤️:
  1. Get For dough
  2. Take 1 cup warm milk (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  3. Make ready 2 eggs, room temperature
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup margarine, melted
  5. Get 4 1/2 cups bread flour
  6. Take 1 teaspoon salt

  9. Take Filling
  10. Get 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  11. Get 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  12. Take 1/3 cup butter, softened
  13. Make ready Icing
  14. Make ready 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  15. Get 1/4 cup butter, softened
  16. Get 1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
  17. Get 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions to make Cinnamon roll ❤️:
  1. Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine. Select dough cycle; press Start.
  2. After the dough has doubled in size turn it out onto a lightly floured surface, cover and let rest for 10 minutes. In a small bowl, combine brown sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Roll dough into a 16x21-inch rectangle. Spread dough with 1/3 cup butter and sprinkle evenly with sugar/cinnamon mixture. Roll up dough and cut into 12 rolls. Place rolls in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking pan. Cover and let rise until nearly doubled, about 30 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  4. Bake rolls in preheated oven until golden brown, about 15 minutes. While rolls are baking, beat together cream cheese, 1/4 cup butter, confectioners' sugar, vanilla extract and salt. Spread frosting on warm rolls before serving.
