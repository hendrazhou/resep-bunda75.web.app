The ingredients needed to make Tutti fruiti eggless cake:
  1. Take 1.5 cup maida
  2. Get 1/2 cup Tutti Frutti
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup Sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup oil
  5. Take 1/2 cup curd
  6. Make ready 1/4tsp baking soda
  7. Get 1tsp baking powder
  8. Make ready 1tsp vanila essence

Instructions to make Tutti fruiti eggless cake:
  1. First in a bowl add curd essence sugar oil and whisk well
  2. Sieve baking soda baking powder maida in it.mix it in cut and fold method do not over mix
  3. Add tuty fruity add water if necessary.pour it in a cake mould
  4. Bake at 180 degrees 25mints.
