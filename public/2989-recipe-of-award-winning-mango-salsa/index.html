The ingredients needed to make Mango salsa:
  1. Get 2 Ripe Mango's
  2. Get 1 Small Red Onion
  3. Prepare 1 lemon
  4. Get 1 Lime
  5. Get 1 Jalapeno or more if you like ot spicy

Mango salsa is a chunky salsa recipe that's a perfect blend of sweet and spicy! This BEST fresh mango salsa recipe! Mango salsa with fresh diced mango, red onion, jalapeno, cilantro and lime. When mangoes are in season and plentiful at the market, it's hard to resist making mango salsa.

Steps to make Mango salsa:
  1. Peel and pit the Mango's. Dice them add to the bowl.
  2. Peel and dice Red Onion. mix with mango's.
  3. Slice Jalapeno and cut the core out dice it add it to the Mango onion.
  4. Slice you're Lemon in half and squeeze the juice's out into the bowl
  5. Slice you're Lime and squeeze the juice's into the bowl as well.
  6. Take a spoon and mix it up.put salsa in fridge for 3 hours or serve immediately.
  7. Feel free to make this Recipe your own you may add more or less.I used only one jalapeno because my family likes a mild salsa.
