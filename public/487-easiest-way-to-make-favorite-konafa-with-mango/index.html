The ingredients needed to make Konafa With Mango:
  1. Make ready 250 grams Konafa
  2. Prepare 1 large Mango
  3. Prepare 125 grams Fresh cream
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Sugar
  5. Make ready 1/3 cup Margarine
  6. Get 125 ml Sugary syrup

Instructions to make Konafa With Mango:
  1. Cut Konafa into small pieces
  2. Put sugar and margarine on it and stir them
  3. Put them in a suitable ban on the stove and stir them continuously till getting a golden color
  4. Take it a way from the stove then put the sugary syrup on it and stir them well
  5. Cut mango into slices
  6. In a suitable container put one layer of Konafa then fresh cream then mango slices then Konafa then fresh cream then mango slices
  7. Put it into fridge till getting cold
  8. Serve it in a suitable plate &decorate its upper surface with mango slices
