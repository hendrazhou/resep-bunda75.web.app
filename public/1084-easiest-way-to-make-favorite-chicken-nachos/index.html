  1. Prepare 1 packages doritos cool ranch
  2. Make ready 1 can cream of chicken
  3. Take 1 can chicken breast 13 oz.
  4. Get 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  5. Take 1/2 cup onions

Steps to make chicken nachos:
  1. preheat oven at 350
  2. mix in a pan cream chicken and chicken breast
  3. in a deep dish pan put 1 layer of doritos
  4. in the top of doritos 1 layer of onions
  5. in the top of onions 1 layer of jalapeno pepper
  6. en the top of jalapeno 1 layer of cheese
  7. repeat the same process. in till finish the ingredient
  8. leave in oven. for 15 min.
  9. enjoy
