The ingredients needed to make Pineapple tutti frutti cupcakes:
  1. Take 100 grams butter softened
  2. Take 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  3. Make ready 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 1 tsp pineapple essence
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup flour/ maidha
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Make ready 1 tbspn Milk
  8. Prepare 2 drops yellow food colour (optional)
  9. Make ready as required Tutti frutti for garnish

Instructions to make Pineapple tutti frutti cupcakes:
  1. In a mixing bowl, take butter and sugar. Whisk till pale and creamy.
  2. Add eggs and pineapple essence. Beat with an electric beater till fluffy.
  3. Sift flour and baking powder. Mix with a spatula using cut and fold method. Add milk and mix gently.
  4. Line the muffin tray with cup cake liners and fill ¾ allowing space to rise to get perfect dome-shaped cupcakes. Sprinkle tutti frutti over each cupcake batter.
  5. In a preheated oven, bake at 180°C for 18-20 minutes. Allow to cool and enjoy.
