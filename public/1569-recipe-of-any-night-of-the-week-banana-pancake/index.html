Steps to make Banana Pancake:
  1. Mash the banana.
  2. Mix the mashed banana with egg and cinnamon powder.
  3. Mix it well.
  4. Cook it on a pan. You might want to sprinkle a little amount of oil to make sure the batter won’t stick to the pan. Sprinkle some raw honey….. Ready to serve!
