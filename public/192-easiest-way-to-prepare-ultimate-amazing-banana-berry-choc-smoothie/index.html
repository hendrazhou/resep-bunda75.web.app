The ingredients needed to make Amazing banana berry choc smoothie:
  1. Get 5 Strawberries
  2. Prepare 1 small box of blueberries
  3. Get 1 bannana
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon honey (for sweetness)
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon peanut butter (optional)
  6. Get 2 tablespoons Milo
  7. Take 1 cup milk

Steps to make Amazing banana berry choc smoothie:
  1. Get your blender out and plug it in.
  2. Put all the fruit inside.
  3. Put the Milo and peanut butter inside.
  4. Put in the milk and honey at the top. (So it is not hard to clean).
  5. Blend!
  6. Serve into your glasses
  7. Enjoy!
