Instructions to make Roasted pork with banana guava ketchup:
  1. Preheat oven 375° Fahrenheit
  2. Put roast in an oven safe dish. Add half a cup of water.Season with sage, parsley salt, and pepper.
  3. Coat the roast with ketchup. Put onions all around and on top of roast. - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/354036-banana-and-guava-ketchup
  4. Roast in the oven uncovered for 1 hour. Add cup of water and cover roast 45 minutes. Let rest 5 minutes serve hope you enjoy!
