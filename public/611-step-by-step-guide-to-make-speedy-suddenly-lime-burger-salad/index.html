Instructions to make Suddenly Lime Burger Salad:
  1. Bring sauce pan to a boil. Add pasta and stir until al dente.
  2. In a separate bowl mix salad seasoning with 1/2cp mayo. Set aside
  3. Strain pasta and rinse with cold water. Drain excess water and mix with salad seasoning. Refrigerate.
  4. In a skillet, heat to medium high, add in oil. Add ground beef and seasonings. Break down and stir sparingly until browned. Squeeze lime juice and stir again. Remove from heat.
  5. Plate pasta at the bottom, followed by ground beef. Top with green onion and drizzle Salsa Verde across the top.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
