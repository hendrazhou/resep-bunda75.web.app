Steps to make Cinnamon Chocolate Christmas cookies:
  1. In a bowl, put in the cookie dough and ground cinnamon.
  2. Knead the dough with the ground cinnamon until completely mixed.
  3. Roll the dough into 17 balls. Butter a tray and place the balls there.
  4. Put a big piece of tin foil on the oven.(You'll need a small oven and I forgot the tin foil while making this)
  5. Place 5 or 4 balls of dough in the oven for 10 minutes or until fully cooked(The dough will melt into a cookie)
  6. Take out cookies out of the oven and onto a plate. Replace tinfoil.
  7. Repeat steps 5-6 until all balls are gone. Put the cookies in the freezer until cooled. Enjoy!
