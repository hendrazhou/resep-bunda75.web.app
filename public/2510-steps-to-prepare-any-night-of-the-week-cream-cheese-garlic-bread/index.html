Steps to make Cream Cheese Garlic Bread:
  1. I add d flour,yeast,sugar,salt,grated nutmeg, butter and milk,mix well,knead on a flat surface for 5munites,cover allow to rise for 1hr.
  2. After risen,punch d dough back, remove all d excess air, flatting d bread on flat surface,cut in to 6pcs
  3. Make small balls Wt d cuts,arrange the 6 balls on greese baking tray,cover allow to rise for another 30munites,after risen brush d bread Wt egg,bake for 15-20munites.
  4. Add cream cheese in a bowl add sugar to it mix well add in a piping bag and keep aside
  5. In a separate bowl add egg,melted butter, Mayonnaise,milk, honey,garlic(crush),dry oregano,dry parsely or leaves,mix well and keep side
  6. Bring d Bread on a chopping board,use shape knife to cuts d bread in to 6-8 segments (depending on d size of d bread)
  7. After making d cuts,fill each segments Wt d cream cheese mixture
  8. Deep d bread in d garlic butter mixture,arrange back on d baking tray,baked again for anothe 8-10munites
  9. Allow to cool,remove from d tray and serve.
