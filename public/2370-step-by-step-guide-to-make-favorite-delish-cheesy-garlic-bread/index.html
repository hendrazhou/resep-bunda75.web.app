Instructions to make Delish Cheesy Garlic Bread:

  2. Mix butter, chopped parsley, and minced garlic. Spread onto each side of bread.
  3. Sprinkle cheese on one half then top with the other.
  4. Wrap in foil and bake for 15-20 minutes at 350.
  5. Enjoy!
