The ingredients needed to make Japanese Chicken Wings:
  1. Take 1 lb chicken wings
  2. Prepare 1 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Prepare 1 cup butter
  5. Get Sauce
  6. Get 3 tbsp soya sauce
  7. Get 3 tbsp water
  8. Get dash salt
  9. Take 1/2 cup white vinegar
  10. Get 3/4 cup sugar

Instructions to make Japanese Chicken Wings:
  1. Dip chicken in egg, then flour. Fry in butter till golden brown (about 10 minutes).
  2. Mix sauce in roaster, then add the chicken.
  3. Cover and Bake at 375°F for 1 hour. Uncover the last 10 minutes.
