The ingredients needed to make Crab Lime Bites:
  1. Make ready 1 can crab meat (6 oz)
  2. Take 3 green onions, chopped
  3. Get 1 tbsp capers, chopped
  4. Make ready 1 tsp dill
  5. Make ready 1 tsp fresh thyme leaves
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp lemon pepper
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp lime juice
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp greek yogurt
  9. Get 3 tbsp real mayonnaise
  10. Prepare 1 spinach leaves
  11. Get 1 vegetable ritz crackers
  12. Make ready 1 fresh thyme leaves for garnish
