The ingredients needed to make Watermelon papaya smoothie:
  1. Get 1 cup Wate melon / tarbooj pulp-

  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp Chat masala -

Instructions to make Watermelon papaya smoothie:
  1. Remove the seed of the watermelon / tarbooj.
  2. Put watermelon pulp and papaya pulp black salt,chat masala in the blender and blend it.
  3. Take a glass, add crushed ice and pour the watermelon,papaya smoothie
  4. Squeeze the lemon juice and mix well.
  5. Garnish with mint / pudina leaves and papaya,watermelon chunks
  6. Serve chilled.
