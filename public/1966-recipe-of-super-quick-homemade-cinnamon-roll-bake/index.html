The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Roll Bake:
  1. Prepare 2 tubs Refrigerated Cinnamon Rolls
  2. Take 4 Tablespoons melted butter
  3. Take 6 Eggs
  4. Make ready 1/2 Cup Milk
  5. Prepare 2 Teaspoons Cinnamon
  6. Take 2 Teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  7. Take 1/2 cup Maple or Pancake Syrup

Instructions to make Cinnamon Roll Bake:
  1. Pour the melted butter on the bottom of a 9x13 glass baking dish.
  2. Cut each cinnamon roll into 8 pieces, and spread evenly over the butter.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla, and pour over the cinnamon rolls.
  4. Pour maple syrup over the mixture.
  5. Bake at 375°F/190°C for 25 min.
  6. Top with the icing from the cinnamon rolls.
