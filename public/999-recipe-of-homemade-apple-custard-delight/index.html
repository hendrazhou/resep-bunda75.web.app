The ingredients needed to make Apple custard delight:
  1. Make ready 2 red apples
  2. Take 500 ml Full fat milk
  3. Make ready 3-4 tablespoons Sweetened Condensed milk
  4. Prepare 2 tablespoons Custard powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon each banana essence, pineapple,strawberry essence or any of your choice
  6. Get As needed Homemade tutti frutti for garnishing
  7. Get as required Some red syrup for garnish (optional)

Instructions to make Apple custard delight:
  1. Boil milk we’ll keep stirring besides mix custard powder in cold milk without lumps & pop all essence your using I used three fruits essence it gives nice flavors to custard
  2. Mix condensed milk further Add custard in milk keep stirring continuously in three minutes off the flame burner let it settle down @ room temperature
  3. Besides Peel & dice apples mix in custard add in a serving bowl mix well garnish it with tutti frutti & red syrup then pop in refrigerator for about an hour then enjoy it chilled..!
