The ingredients needed to make Zobo Drink:
  1. Take 1 cup Zobo
  2. Get 4 cups water
  3. Get 1 tbsp cinnamon powder
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp flavor
  5. Take 3 blended guavas
  6. Prepare 1 cup watermelon juice
  7. Get 1 tsp cloves
  8. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  9. Take 1 cup pineapple juice
  10. Get 1 tbsp ginger
  11. Take Chopped cucumbers

Instructions to make Zobo Drink:
  1. Cook zobo in water, add cinnamon, cloves and ginger
  2. Sieve cooked zobo, add guava juice, watermelon juice, pineapple juice, flavor, chopped cucumbers and sugar. Serve chilled
