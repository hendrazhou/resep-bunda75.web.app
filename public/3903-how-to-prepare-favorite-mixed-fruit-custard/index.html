The ingredients needed to make Mixed Fruit Custard:
  1. Prepare 2 cup milk full cream
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp custard powder
  3. Take 3 tbsp cold milk
  4. Get 1/4 cup sugar
  5. Get 1/4 tsp elachi powder
  6. Prepare 2 cup mixed fruit(mango,banana,apple,pomegrante grapes,)
  7. Make ready You are also used more fruits

Steps to make Mixed Fruit Custard:
  1. Take a thick bottom pan add milk, get the milk boiled.
  2. Take a bowl add 2 tbsp custard powder, add 3 -4 tbsp cold milk, mix well.
  3. Your milk has boiled, add custard mixture. Mix well.
  4. Also add sugar according to you taste.
  5. After some time, milk changes colors dark yellow.
  6. Transfer to a small bowl and cool completely.
  7. Then your custard cool down, add all chopped fresh fruits.
  8. Add elachi powder. Mix well.
  9. Chill for 2 hours.
  10. Serve it chilled.
