The ingredients needed to make Tutti Frutti cake:
  1. Take 1 cup flour
  2. Make ready 2/3 cup sugar
  3. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Prepare 1 egg
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup buttermilk
  7. Take 1/2 cup butter
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
  9. Prepare 1/3 cup tutti frutti
  10. Prepare Pinch salt

Instructions to make Tutti Frutti cake:
  1. Set over at 200°c and grease a cake tray
  2. In a large bowl whisk together the flour, sugar, salt, baking powder and baking soda
  3. To this add egg, vanilla essence, buttermilk and butter
  4. Now using a beater beat the mixture for 2 minutes. Now scrap the sides of container and again beat the mixture for 1minute
  5. Now pour thr batter into the prepared greased tray.
  6. Sprinkle rahein tutti frutti on t0p
  7. Now bake it for 25-40minutes and check it by inserting toothpick
  8. Let it cool down for 10 minutes and serve
