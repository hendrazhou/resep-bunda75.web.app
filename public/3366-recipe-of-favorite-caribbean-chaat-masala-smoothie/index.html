The ingredients needed to make Caribbean Chaat Masala Smoothie:
  1. Get 2/3 cup pineapple chunks, canned
  2. Take 1 banana
  3. Take 1/4 cup orange juice, fresh
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup oats
  5. Get 4 tbsp coconut ,freshly grated
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup almond milk
  7. Take 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Make ready 1 pinch salt
