  6. Make ready ground black pepper

  8. Prepare 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
  9. Prepare 4 tilapia filets

Instructions to make Lime Tilapia:
  1. Preheat oven to 435°F
  2. Coat cooking sheet with foil and spray with nonstick cooking spray
  3. Combine topping ingredients and mix in bowl
  4. Place dry tilapia filets on cooking sheet
  5. Spread mixture over the dry tilapia filets to ensure topping adherence
  6. Sprinkle panko bread crumbs over filets
  7. Bake for approximately 20 minutes (or until the tilapia flakes apart when touched)
