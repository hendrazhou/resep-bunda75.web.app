The ingredients needed to make Nutella cakes:
  1. Make ready FOR THE CAKE:
  2. Prepare 397 gram condensed milk
  3. Make ready 3 eggs
  4. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Prepare pinch salt
  6. Get 150 gram self-raising flour
  7. Take 100 grams dark chocolate
  8. Prepare 1 tsp butter
  9. Take FOR THE FROSTING:
  10. Take 200 gram soft butter
  11. Take 200 gram powdered sugar
  12. Make ready 200 gram Nutella
  13. Prepare 3 tbsp cream or milk

Steps to make Nutella cakes:
  1. FOR THE CAKE: - Melt the chocolate together with the butter.
  2. Stir on low heat until you have a good chocolate sauce.
  3. Put all the ingredients for the cake in a bowl.
  4. Mix for 5 minutes.
  5. Preheat your oven to 180° celsius and cover a square bakingform with bakingpaper. Pour the mixture in to the bakingform.
  6. Bake the cake for 40 minutes in the preheated oven or until done.
  7. Slice through half and in squares. Let it cool down completely before making the frosting.
  8. Mix the butter until fluffy and add little by little the powdered sugar. Mix in the rest for the frosting with a pinch of salt.
  9. Decorate them to your liking
  10. I added a hazelnut on top, but this is optional.
