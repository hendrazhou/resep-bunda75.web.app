The ingredients needed to make Treat day Smoothie with Nutella:
  1. Take 1/2 cup Skimmed Milk
  2. Make ready 1 Apple
  3. Get 5 Strawberries
  4. Take 1 Banana
  5. Prepare 3 tbsp Greek Yoghurt
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Syrup or Honey
  7. Make ready 2 tsp Peanut Butter
  8. Prepare 2 tsp Nutella
  9. Take 1 Orange Juice
  10. Take 4 Ice cubes
  11. Take 3 tbsp Porridge Oats

Instructions to make Treat day Smoothie with Nutella:
  1. Pour the milk into the blender.
  2. Peel and chop the Apple and banana . Chop the strawberries and add all fruit to the blender.
  3. Blend the fruit and milk with 4 ice cubes.
  4. Open blender and add in the Greek yoghurt, nutella, honey and peanut butter.
  5. Sprinkle in the porridge oats and blend.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
