The ingredients needed to make Beef, Mushroom & Barley Soup:
  1. Get 1 tbsp of olive oil
  2. Prepare 1 beef shortribs
  3. Prepare 1 lb of mushrooms, sliced
  4. Take 1 salt & pepper to taste
  5. Make ready 2 whole carrots cut in half
  6. Get 1 whole unpeeled large onion
  7. Make ready 2 ribs of celery, cut in half, with leaves on
  8. Get 1 tbsp fresh thyme
  9. Take 2 cup barley

Instructions to make Beef, Mushroom & Barley Soup:
  1. Heat olive oil in a stock pot, add beef brown on all sides. Season with salt & pepper.
  2. Add mushrooms, carrots, celery & onion, add broth, season with salt & pepper, add thyme.
  3. Bring to boil, then turn down heat to medium low. Cook until beef ribs are cooked.
  4. Add barley cook until tender.
  5. Reason with salt & pepper if needed.
  6. Enjoy.
