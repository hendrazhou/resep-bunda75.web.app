The ingredients needed to make Cocoa Sponge Mug Cake:
  1. Take SPOON = soup poon
  2. Make ready 4 FULL spoons of flour
  3. Make ready 4 spoons sugar
  4. Prepare 3 spoons vegetable oil
  5. Get 3 spoons milk
  6. Prepare 2-3 full spoons of cocoa at choice
  7. Take 1 egg
  8. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  9. Make ready optionals
  10. Make ready 1 FULL spoon of Nutella (mine)
  11. Prepare Vanilla,orange,chips…any topping you fancy
