Instructions to make Island Chicken Wings:
  1. Marinate wings in rum extract for one hour.
  2. Pat dry with a paper towel. Toss with oil. Season.
  3. Grill each side to get a nice grill mark and toss into the oven or toss straight into the oven.
  4. Bake at 400° for approximately 20 minutes or until thermometer reaches 165° and wings are caramelized and crispy.
