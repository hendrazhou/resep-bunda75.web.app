The ingredients needed to make Fresh, Easy Blender Salsa:
  1. Prepare 5 medium tomatoes (I prefer home grown or vine ripened)
  2. Get 2 jalapeños (de-seeded)
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup sweet onion
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup loosely packed cilantro
  5. Prepare Juice from 1 lime
  6. Take 2 tsp minced garlic
  7. Make ready 1 tsp salt (I use sea salt)
  8. Take 1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
  9. Make ready Dash cumin
  10. Prepare Dash chili powder

Instructions to make Fresh, Easy Blender Salsa:
  1. Roughly chop the tomatoes, onion, cilantro. Just to make it blend easier. Big chunks are fine.
  2. Put everything in the blender. Pulse repeatedly for desired consistency, but don’t purée. You want some texture.
  3. Refrigerate at least an hour to cool it and let the flavors sink in.
  4. Enjoy with tortilla chips!
