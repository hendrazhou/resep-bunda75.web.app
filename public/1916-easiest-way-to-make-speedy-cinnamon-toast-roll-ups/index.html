  6. Prepare 1/2 cup butter, melted

  8. Take For Serving
  9. Make ready sweetened whipped cream

Steps to make Cinnamon Toast Roll Ups:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350. Line a baking pan with parchment paper
  2. Roll each bread slice out flat woth a rolling pin
  3. Combine granulated sugar and cinnamon on a plate
  4. Beat cream cheese with powdered sugar and vanilla
  5. Spread on each bread slice
  6. Roll up and dip in melted butter
  7. Roll in cinnamon sugar
  8. Place on prepated pan and bake about 20,inutes until golden
  9. Serve hot with whipped cream for dipping and fresh fruit
