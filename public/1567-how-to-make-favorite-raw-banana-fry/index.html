The ingredients needed to make Raw banana fry:
  1. Take 1 raw banana
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp rice flour
  3. Take 1 tbsp red chilli Powder
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp coriander powder
  5. Take 1 tsp turmeric powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp cumin powder
  7. Take 1 tsp Saunf/fennel powder
  8. Prepare As needed Oil to shallow fry
  9. Make ready to taste Salt

Steps to make Raw banana fry:
  1. Peel raw banana skin and slice it
  2. In a separate bowl add all the powder ingredients and mix well
  3. Add water and make a thick batter. Now add the sliced raw banana to the mixture
  4. Now shallow fry it in a pan
  5. Flip the other side once done. Cook in medium flame only
  6. The fry is ready to serve with rice and dal.
