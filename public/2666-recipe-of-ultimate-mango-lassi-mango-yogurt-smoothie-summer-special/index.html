The ingredients needed to make Mango Lassi (Mango Yogurt Smoothie) – Summer Special:
  1. Make ready 1 cup Riped Mango Plup – (2 medium size mango)
  2. Get 1 cup Curd
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup Milk
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Powdered Sugar (optional)
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp Cardamom powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup Ice cubes
  7. Take For Garnishing :
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup Mango Cube
  9. Prepare 3 tbsp Sliced Pistachios
  10. Take 4 Sliced Cherries
