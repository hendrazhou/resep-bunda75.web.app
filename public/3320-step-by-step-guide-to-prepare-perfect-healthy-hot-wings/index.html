Instructions to make Healthy Hot Wings:
  1. Boil chicken wings in large pot until cooked.
  2. Carefully remove skin and cut wings into hands, forearms, and upper arms. Discard hands and skin. Use a sharp knife and be careful when removing skin so that you don't lose any meat.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together vegetable oil and seasonings. Toss chicken in mixture ensuring that all sides of chicken are coated.
  4. Apply cooking spray to a baking pan and align chicken pieces evenly on the pan. Bake chicken for 10 minutes at 350°F.
  5. Yields 4 servings. For spicier wings, add more cayenne pepper.
