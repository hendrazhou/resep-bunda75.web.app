Instructions to make Garlic & Onion Chicken Wings:
  1. Rinse Wings and dry, then coat in Oil and lay on a baking sheet
  2. Sprinkle a coating of garlic and onion powder and black pepper on the wings after coating them with oil and place back on baking sheet
  3. Place wings in the oven on bake at 350° for an hour
  4. Flip wings over at 30 minute mark and coat with oil and garlic and onion powder again and place back in oven for another 30 minutes
  5. After 60 minutes, depending on the number of wings being cooked, coat with oil one more time and broil to brown the wings on low for just a few minutes
  6. Crunchy on the outside and falls right off the bone
