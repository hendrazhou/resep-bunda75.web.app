The ingredients needed to make Protein banana cookies:
  1. Prepare 2 med very ripe bananas
  2. Make ready 1-1/2 cups rolled oats
  3. Make ready 1/3 cup dark chocolate chips
  4. Get 1/8 teaspoons cinnamon
  5. Make ready 2 tablespoons shilved Almonds
  6. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions to make Protein banana cookies:
  1. Pre heat ove at 375°
  2. On a cookie sheet line with foil and spray with cooking spray.
  3. In a bowl add ripe bananas and swash with the back of a fork. Mix in vanilla, protein, and sweetener
  4. Add oats and gently stir till well combined. Should look like a thick cookie batter.
  5. Add mix ins ; chocolate chips almonds and cinnamon
  6. Scoop 1 heaping tablespoon on a greased cookie sheet lighly press down to form a cookie.
  7. Bake for 10-12 min till the edges are golden.
  8. Cool..and enjoy
