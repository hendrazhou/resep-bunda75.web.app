The ingredients needed to make Amrood Kachalu (Guava Dessert):
  1. Get 1 kg ripe guava
  2. Take 1 cup sugar (taste as you need)
  3. Make ready 1/3 teaspoon black pepper powder
  4. Get 1 pinch salt

Steps to make Amrood Kachalu (Guava Dessert):
  1. Take large mixing bowl and sliced guava.
  2. Add sugar in it and mash it till sugar dissolve.
  3. Add salt and black pepper powder mix it and refrigerate for an hour.
  4. Serve chill amrood kachalu.
