The ingredients needed to make Serrano Tomatillo Salsa:
  1. Get salsa
  2. Make ready 4 tomatillos cut into quarters
  3. Take 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  4. Prepare 1 avocado
  5. Make ready 2 clove garlic
  6. Make ready 2 serrano peppers
  7. Take 1/2 cup cilantro
  8. Take 1/4 cup white onion
  9. Make ready 1/2 lime juice, fresh
  10. Take 1/4 tsp salt

Steps to make Serrano Tomatillo Salsa:
  1. In a food processor or blender, pulse the onion, garlic, serrano pepper, salt until coarsely chopped.
  2. Then add in tomatillo, cherry tomatoes, cilantro. Pulse 4 times.
  3. Then just blend in avocado and lime juice for 2-3 more pulses.
  4. Serve this over pork , chicken. With fajitas or tacos. Or just as a dip.
