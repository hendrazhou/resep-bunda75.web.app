The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon roll casserole:
  1. Prepare 3 cans Pillsbury cinnamon rolls
  2. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 tsp cinnamon
  4. Take 1 stick butter

Steps to make Cinnamon roll casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Cut rolls into quarters and toss in cinnamon and sugar
  3. Put in a 13x9 inch dish
  4. Melt butter and pour on top
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes
  6. Take metal tops off frosting and melt in microwave for about 30 seconds. Pour over casserole and serve.
