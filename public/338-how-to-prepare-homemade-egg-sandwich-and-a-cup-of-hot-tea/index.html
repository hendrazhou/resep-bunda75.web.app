Steps to make Egg sandwich and a cup of hot tea:
  1. Break eggs in a bowl, add chopped onions and pepper, add salt to taste, whisk and fry flat in a frying pan greased with little veg oil. Flip the top surface to fry, fry until golden brown. Set aside!
  2. To make tea, pour hot water in a cup, add milk and milo, add sugar as desired. Stir well!
  3. Cut bread into two halves horizontally, place the fried eggs in the middle and sandwich it. Garnish!
  4. Serve the bread, fried eggs and tea together. Enjoy your breakfast!
