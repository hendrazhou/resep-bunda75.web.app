The ingredients needed to make AMIEs Banana Cherry Smoothie:
  1. Get 1 cup fresh or frozen cherries
  2. Prepare 1 banana, cut in chunks
  3. Prepare 1/2 orange, juiced
  4. Get 1/2 cup plain or vanilla yogurt
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup ice cubes
  6. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
