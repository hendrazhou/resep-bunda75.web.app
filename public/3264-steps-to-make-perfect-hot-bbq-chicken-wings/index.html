The ingredients needed to make hot bbq chicken wings:
  1. Make ready 1 lb chicken wings
  2. Make ready 1 cup bbq sauce
  3. Take 1 lime
  4. Prepare 1 envelope chicken bouillon
  5. Make ready 1/3 tsp soy sauce
  6. Get 1/2 tsp olive juice (from olives jar)
  7. Get 1/3 tsp olive oil
  8. Make ready 1/4 tbsp liquid seasoning
  9. Take 1 pinch oregano
  10. Get 1 pinch black pepper
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp hot sauce (optional)

Steps to make hot bbq chicken wings:
  1. preheat oven at 400°F
  2. cut chicken wings into 2 pieces place in bowl squeeze lime on chicken and wash thoroughly and drain
  3. add all ingredients then stir with a spoon until all fully mixed
  4. pour onto baking pan and let bake for 30 mins on each side
  5. stir chicken wings while still in pan then serve and enjoy
