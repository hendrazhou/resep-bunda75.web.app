The ingredients needed to make Jalapeño, mango salad with tofu patties:
  1. Get 1 cup shoots
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup red pepper, cubed
  3. Get 1/2 a mango, cubed
  4. Make ready 1/2 a nectarine, cubed
  5. Make ready 1/2 a peach, cubed
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp fresh ginger, chopped
  7. Take 1/2 a jalapeño pepper, chopped
  8. Make ready Lemon juice
  9. Make ready Fresh cilantro
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  11. Make ready 2 tofu patties

It goes great with meat or fish dishes. Adjust the spice according to your personal taste.
