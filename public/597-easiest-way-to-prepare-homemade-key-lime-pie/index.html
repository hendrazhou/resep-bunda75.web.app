The ingredients needed to make Key Lime Pie:
  1. Get 3 egg yolks
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup Key Lime Juice, no substitute
  3. Make ready 2 (14 oz) cans Eagle brand sweetened evaporated milk, do not substitute
  4. Take 2 tsp lime zest
  5. Get Store-bought graham cracker crust put in a pie tin

Ordinary limes make an exceptional Key lime pie, and they are a much better alternative to bracingly tart bottled Key lime juice. Instructions to make Key Lime Pie:
  1. Set oven to 360f
  2. Beat egg yolks and then stir into the milk.
  3. Add the lime zest and stir in the lime juice.
  4. Pour into the pie tin with crust and bake at 360 f for 25 minutes.
  5. Cool to room temperature and refrigerate. Serve with whipped cream.
