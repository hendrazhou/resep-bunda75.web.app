The ingredients needed to make Feta Avocado Lime Salad:
  1. Get 2 avocados, cubed
  2. Prepare 1 English cucumber, cubed
  3. Prepare 1 pint cherry tomatoes, quartered
  4. Take 1 can sliced black olives, drained
  5. Get 2 tbsp capers
  6. Take 1-2 cups feta, depending on your taste
  7. Prepare dressing
  8. Take 2 tbsp olive oil
  9. Prepare 4 tbsp lime juice (or the juice from 2 whole limes)
  10. Make ready 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  11. Take to taste Salt and pepper
