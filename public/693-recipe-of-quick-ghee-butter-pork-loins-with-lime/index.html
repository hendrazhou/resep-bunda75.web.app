The ingredients needed to make Ghee/Butter pork loins with lime:
  1. Prepare 6 pork loin chops
  2. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoons paprika
  5. Prepare 1 lime
  6. Prepare 1 stick butter/ 1/4 cup ghee
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon whole leaf thyme
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon ground sage
