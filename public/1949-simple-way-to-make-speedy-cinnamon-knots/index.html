The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Knots:
  1. Make ready 210 g Flour
  2. Get 20 g Corn Starch
  3. Get 10 g Baking Powder
  4. Get 45 g Sugar
  5. Get 50 g Butter (A)
  6. Make ready 50 g Egg (about 1 Egg)
  7. Make ready 100 g Milk (or Water)
  8. Take 100 g Butter (B)
  9. Make ready 120 g Brown Sugar
  10. Take 20 g Cinnamon
  11. Prepare Quantities for Exterior
  12. Prepare Flour

Steps to make Cinnamon Knots:
  1. Line standard muffin tins with paper liners.
  2. Cream the butter (B) and set aside until use.
  3. Add the cinnamon and brown sugar to a medium mixing bowl and mix well. Set aside until use.
  4. Add the flour, corn starch, baking powder and sugar to a mixing bowl and mix well.
  5. Add the butter (A), egg and milk (or water) to the dry mixture and knead well.
  6. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface, about 2-3 mm thick.
  7. Spread the creamed butter (B) on the dough.
  8. Spread the brown sugar mixture over the butter.
  9. Roll up the dough and cut into about 3cm wide pieces.
  10. Place the dough in liners and bake for 25 min. Enjoy!
