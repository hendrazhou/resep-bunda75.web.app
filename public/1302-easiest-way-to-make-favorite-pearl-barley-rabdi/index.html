The ingredients needed to make Pearl barley rabdi:
  1. Prepare 1/3 cup Pearl Barley
  2. Get 2 cups Buttermilk
  3. Take 1 cup Water
  4. Prepare 1 Onion medium size
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Cumin powder
  6. Take 1 tsp Mint powder
  7. Take 1/4 tsp Black salt
  8. Make ready as per taste Salt
  9. Prepare for garnishing :
  10. Take 3-4 Mint leaves

Pearl barley is usually used for making porridge or soups, but it also serves as a great raw material. Pearl Barley is quick cooking & can be used in soups, risottos and more! This is barley with all the bran removed, giving the round, shiny grains a pearlescent white colour. It can be used in soups and stews as a thickener.

Steps to make Pearl barley rabdi:
  1. To make this firstly heat water in a pan and add a pinch of salt in it.
  2. When water starts boiling add pearl barley seeds in it after few around 5-6 minutes you will see something white coming up remove that.It's dirt.
  3. At this time add 1 cup buttermilk in it and stir continously till boil come.
  4. Now swtich off the flame and let it cool till then chopped onion.
  5. After it get cool down add another 1 cup of buttermilk in it with this add chopped onion, black salt, cumin powder and mint powder.
  6. At last mix everything take it out in glass or bowl garnish with mint leaves.
