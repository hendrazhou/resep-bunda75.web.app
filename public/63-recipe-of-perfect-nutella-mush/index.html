The ingredients needed to make Nutella mush:
  1. Prepare 5 packages small marie cookies (or any tea bisquits) but not digestive.
  2. Take 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  3. Take 1 can thick cream (this is not a liquid)
  4. Take 1 small jar of nutella
  5. Prepare 1 cup of crushed nuts or toffee bits
Instructions to make Nutella mush:
  1. Finely crush cookies in food processor
  2. add sweetened milk and thick cream to the cookies and mix. This should be kinda gooey looking not dry . You may need to add more sweetened milk. You have to mix it really good.
  3. put in freezer for about and hour or 2 .
  4. now spread the nutella on top and then sprinkle with crushed nuts or fine toffee bits. ENJOY :)
