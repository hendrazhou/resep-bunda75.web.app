The ingredients needed to make Cheesy corn garlic bread:
  1. Prepare 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. Take To taste salt
  3. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  4. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  5. Get 1/2 cup boiled corns
  6. Make ready 1 tsp chilli flakes
  7. Get 2 cube grated cheese
  8. Make ready 1 tsp oregano
  9. Make ready 1 tsp chopped garlic
  10. Get 1 tsp chopped coriander leaves
  11. Make ready 1 tsp yogurt

Instructions to make Cheesy corn garlic bread:
  1. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda and yogurt to make soft dough. Let it rest for 20 minutes
  2. Cut it in two halves and make 2 big rotis
  3. Cut roti in heart shape with help of cookie cutter
  4. Pan fry heart shapes
  5. Take a bowl mix corn, coriander, chilli flakes, oregano, chopped garlic
  6. Add corn mix in one side, cheese on it and keep in pan till cheese melts
  7. Cheesy corn garlic bread is ready to serve
  8. Serve with schezwan chutney
