Instructions to make Delightful dessert-Fruit custard basket:
  1. For fruit custard- Heat milk in a pan,add sugar and stir until it dissolve,Add custard powder to a small bowl than pour 3 to 4 tbsp of milk to it stir well.Make sure it is free from any lumps.
  2. When the milk turns hot pour mix to the hot milk,keep stirting and cook on a low flameuntil the custard turns thick.Switch off the gas and whisk the custard well to make it smooth. Cool this completely and chill it in fridge.Tham peel and chop the fruits in small pieces and mix it in custard.
  3. For vermicelli basket- Take 1 tsp ghee in panand roast vermicelli till change colour.
