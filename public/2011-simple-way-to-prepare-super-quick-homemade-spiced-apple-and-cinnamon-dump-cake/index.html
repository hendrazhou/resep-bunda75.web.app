The ingredients needed to make Spiced Apple and Cinnamon Dump Cake:
  1. Get 2 can Spiced apple pie filling
  2. Take 1 box Yellow cake mix
  3. Prepare 1 stick butter
  4. Take 1 ground cinnamon

Instructions to make Spiced Apple and Cinnamon Dump Cake:
  1. Set oven at 350. Bake for 45 minutes
  2. Dump both cans of apple pie filling in baking dish.
  3. Sprinkle yellow cake mix over pie filling
  4. Cut butter up in slices and put on cake mix.
  5. Sprinkle cinnamon on top. As much as you desire.
  6. Bake and serve warm or chilled
