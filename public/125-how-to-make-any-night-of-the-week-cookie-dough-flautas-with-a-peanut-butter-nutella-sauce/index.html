The ingredients needed to make Cookie dough flautas with a peanut butter nutella sauce:
  1. Take 1 packages cookie dough (of choice)
  2. Get 1 packages 9" flour tortilla
  3. Make ready 2 cup peanut butter
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp nutella spread
  5. Take 1/4 cup whole milk
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar

Instructions to make Cookie dough flautas with a peanut butter nutella sauce:
  1. preheat deep fryer to 350
  2. take 2 squares of cookie dough and strech in a line over the middle of the tortilla
  3. fold the bottom of the tortilla over the cookie dough. pull tight to avoid a blowout and roll it.
  4. put in fryer, cover with weight and let cook for 2 minutes.
  5. in a medium sauce pan put the peanut butter, nutella milk and sugar on medium heat. stir until smooth.
  6. drizzle sauce over flautas or use as a dipping sauce
  7. enjoy your indulgent fat kid food
