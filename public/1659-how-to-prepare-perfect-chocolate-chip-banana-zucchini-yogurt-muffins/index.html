The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Banana Zucchini Yogurt Muffins:
  1. Get 1 box banana bread mix
  2. Make ready 1 cup shredded zucchini
  3. Prepare 1 cup yogurt
  4. Prepare 1 bag chocolate chips
  5. Take 1 large or 2 small mashed bananas
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup Oil
  7. Make ready 1 cup Water
  8. Take 1 tbsp Cinnamon
  9. Take 1/4 cup Brown sugar

Steps to make Chocolate Chip Banana Zucchini Yogurt Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 425°. Combine banana bread mix and all other ingredients (*except chocolate chips) in a large mixing bowl and beat on low for 1-2 mins.
  2. Once mixture is thoroughly combined, slowly fold in chocolate chips. Pour into cupcake baking tins or bread loaf baking pans, or both.
  3. Bake in oven for 30-45 mins or until muffins and/or bread are passing the "toothpick test" and are a nice golden color around the edges and tops.
  4. Top with extra sprinkles of brown sugar, extra chocolate chips, frosting, or whatever you desire! Enjoy!
