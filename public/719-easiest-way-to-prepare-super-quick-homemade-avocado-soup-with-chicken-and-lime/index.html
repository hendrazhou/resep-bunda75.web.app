The ingredients needed to make Avocado Soup with Chicken and Lime:
  1. Make ready 4 (6 inch) corn tortillas, julienned
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Prepare 1 white onion sliced thin
  4. Prepare 8 garlic cloves sliced thin
  5. Make ready 4 fresh jalapeno sliced
  6. Take 8 oz fresh chicken breast thin chunks
  7. Prepare 1 quart chicken broth
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  9. Take 1 tomato seeded diced
  10. Take salt & pepper
  11. Prepare 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and diced
  12. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions to make Avocado Soup with Chicken and Lime:
  1. Preheat oven 400
  2. Arrange tortilla strips on a baking sheet and bake in preheated oven until lightly browned, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook onion, garlic and jalapenos in olive oil until lightly browned, 4 to 5 minutes. Stir in chicken, chicken broth, lime juice, tomato, salt and pepper. Gently simmer until chicken is cooked, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in avocado and cilantro and heat through. Adjust seasonings.
  4. Ladle soup into bowls and sprinkle with tortilla strips to serve
