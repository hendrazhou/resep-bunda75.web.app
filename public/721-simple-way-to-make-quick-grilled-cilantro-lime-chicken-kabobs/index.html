Steps to make Grilled cilantro lime chicken kabobs:
  1. Put chicken in bag and coat with lime juice and diced up fresh cilantro. Let sit 2-4 hours to marinade
  2. Open bag of skewers and get ready to put in order
  3. While chicken is marinating cut up bell peppers about an inch square
  4. Cut up limes to make each piece a quarter of the lime
  5. Take chicken out of fridge and dice it into about one inch cubes
  6. On each skewer layer bell pepper, chicken, lime, bell pepper, chicken, lime…. continue in same order
  7. Fire up grill till ready and place on grill rotating about every 5-10 minutes till chicken is cooked through.
  8. Enjoy
