Steps to make Nutella cookies:
  1. First you need to add you Nutella, egg and flour.
  2. Then mix all of that together for a good 2 minutes.
  3. After you are done mixing form the Nutella into one big ball.
  4. Wrap the Nutella ball in Cling wrap and place it in the fridge for 15 minutes.
  5. Then you take it out and cut it into sections and form them into balls
  6. Lay them onto a tray and get a wooden spoon and poke wholes in them.
  7. Pre-heat your oven to 450 and leave them in there for 10 minutes
  8. After 10 minutes take them out and fill those wholes with more Nutella
  9. Let them cool and enjoy!!
