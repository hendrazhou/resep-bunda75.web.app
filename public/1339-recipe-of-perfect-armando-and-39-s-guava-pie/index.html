The ingredients needed to make Armando's guava pie:
  1. Make ready 2 1/2 cup sugar
  2. Make ready 250 grams raisins
  3. Make ready 2 egg yolks
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp butter
  5. Make ready 3 kg guava
  6. Get 2 tbsp powdered cinnamon
  7. Get 200 grams nuts (optional)
  8. Take 1000 grams puff pastry

Instructions to make Armando's guava pie:
  1. Pre-heat the ovenproof at 350 C°
  2. Cut the guava and remove the seeds. (You are not to use the seeds)
  3. Cover the ovenproof mold with a tiny layer of butter.
  4. Roll pastry into a big enough rectangle to cover the mold.
  5. Assembly the previously cut guava all over the mold with the rolled pastry.
  6. Mix the sugar, cinnamon, raisins and nuts and stir them all over the guava.
  7. Roll the other half of the puff pastry to cover the top of the pie and gloss it with the yolk. Finally with a fork poke holes all over the top pastry.
  8. Bake for about 40 to 50 minutes till the pastry is browning due to the yolk.
  9. Enjoy.
