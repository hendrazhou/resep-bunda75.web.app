The ingredients needed to make Mandys cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Get 8 medium part cooked/ baked bread rolls
  2. Make ready 2 clove crushed garlic
  3. Take 2 tbsp fresh parsley finely chopped
  4. Prepare 1 cup butter softened
  5. Make ready 1 cup grated cheese use ur favourite kind

Steps to make Mandys cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Pre heat oven to 180℃/350℉
  2. Crush garlic, chop parsley and add to softened butter
  3. Take a part cooked / baked roll put three slices into roll don't go the whole way through, add garlic butter to the cut sides of the roll.
  4. Now u can add cheese to the garlic butter or just sprinkle into cut slices I like to put some of the cheese on top of the bread aswell.
  5. Wrap each roll individually in foil, and bake for 15 minutes until u smell the garlic and the cheese has melted.
