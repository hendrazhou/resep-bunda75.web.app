The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Tutti Frutti Cake in cooker:
  1. Take 1 cup Maida
  2. Get 1 tsp Baking powder
  3. Get 3/4 tsp cooking soda
  4. Take 1/2 cup oil
  5. Make ready 1 cup powdered sugar
  6. Get 3/4 cup Fresh Curd
  7. Get 1/4 th cup -1/2 cup of milk(depends on batter requirement)
  8. Take 10-12 drops vanilla essence
  9. Prepare Tutti Frutti
  10. Get 4 to 5 Badam
  11. Get 8 to 10 Cashewnuts
  12. Take as needed raisins

Instructions to make Vanilla Tutti Frutti Cake in cooker:
  1. In a mixing bowl, sift the flour,baking powder & cooking soda.Seive it 3 times and keep aside. - Take a bowl and add powdered sugar, oil,curd and vanilla essence and beat the mixture using a whisker / blender.
  2. Add the sieved maida mixture in the curd batter.Blend it again well adding required amount of milk till the batter looks almost glossy when done. - Add tutti frutti and raisins and mix it.
