The ingredients needed to make Gluten free cinnamon pear pie:
  1. Take 2 pears
  2. Get 3 Tsp rice flour
  3. Make ready 2 Tsp raw almond
  4. Prepare Optional 1Tsp rolled oats
  5. Make ready 1 Tsp coconut flakes
  6. Get 4 Tsp brown sugar
  7. Make ready 2 teaspoon potato starch
  8. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  9. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon Ginger powder

Instructions to make Gluten free cinnamon pear pie:
  1. Cube pears after peel off its skin
  2. Add half cup of water and cook it through on medium high heat for about 10 minutes Add 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon powder and 1/4 teaspoon of Ginger powder and 1 Tsp of brown sugar
  3. Blend almond, oats and coconut into flour, mix it with rice flour and the rest 3 Tsp of sugar
  4. Cut in about 2 Tsp of cold butter. Mix quickly by hand to corporate
