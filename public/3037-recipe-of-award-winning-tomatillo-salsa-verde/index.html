The ingredients needed to make Tomatillo Salsa Verde:
  1. Get 1 1/2 lb fresh tomatillos
  2. Make ready 6 whole garlic cloves, peeled
  3. Make ready 1/2 onion, chopped
  4. Make ready 1 cup fresh cilantro
  5. Get 2 jalapenos, stems removed and deseeded
  6. Get 1 lime, juice and zest
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp cumin powder
  8. Take 2 tsp honey
  9. Make ready 1 tsp sea salt

Instructions to make Tomatillo Salsa Verde:
  1. Preheat broiler to high.
  2. Remove the papery husk and rinse well. Cut in half, put cut side down on a baking sheet, add garlic cloves. Place pan under broiler for 5-7 minutes, to lightly blacken the skin. Allow to cool for a couple minutes.
  3. Put the tomatillos, garlic, onion, cilantro, jalapenos, lime juice and zest, cumin, honey and salt into a mixer on high speed until all ingredients are finely chopped and mixed.
  4. Store in refrigerator. Makes about 3 cups.
