The ingredients needed to make Eggs, the ones with Mustard Powder:
  1. Prepare 6 large eggs I used farm fresh
  2. Take 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  4. Take 1/2 teaspoon dill weed
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  7. Make ready 1-1/2 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil

Instructions to make Eggs, the ones with Mustard Powder:
  1. Heat the pan with oil. Crack the eggs and add to a bowl. Add the spices. Beat the eggs till just frothy.
  2. Pour the egg mixture into the heated pan. Roll the egg as you raise a side of the pan. Letting the raw egg pour past the cooked bottom layer you just rolled up.
