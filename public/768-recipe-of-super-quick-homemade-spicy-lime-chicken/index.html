The ingredients needed to make Spicy Lime Chicken:
  1. Make ready 3 Chicken breasts
  2. Take 1 Bell pepper, diced
  3. Get 2 cup White rice
  4. Make ready 1 can Black beans
  5. Prepare 1/2 Lime
  6. Prepare 1 as needed Salt and pepper
  7. Get 1 tbsp Chili powder
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp Paprika
  9. Take 1 tbsp Ground Cumin
  10. Get 1 as needed Sour cream
  11. Get 1/2 Onion, diced

Instructions to make Spicy Lime Chicken:
  1. Cut chicken into bite size pieces and brown
  2. Add in paprika, chili powder and cumin. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Cook peppers and onions until tender.
  4. Add cooked white rice, beans and peppers & onions to the chicken.
  5. Squeeze in lime
  6. Serve with a small scoop of sour cream
