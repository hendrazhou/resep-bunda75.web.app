Steps to make Lime Tea:
  1. Add the ice to a glass
  2. Brew the tea bring to a gentle boil cover and remove from heat. Let it steep for 10 minutes.
  3. Strain the tea. Mix the honey with the tea. Add more honey if it's not sweet enough for you.
  4. Pour the tea over the iced lime juice and give it a bit of a stir. Serve I hope you enjoy.
