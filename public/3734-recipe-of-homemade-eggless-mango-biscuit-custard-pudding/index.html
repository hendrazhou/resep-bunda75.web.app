The ingredients needed to make Eggless mango biscuit custard pudding:
  1. Get 12 marie biscuits
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp black coffee powder
  3. Make ready 4 tbsp sugar
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp custard powder
  5. Make ready 450 ml milk
  6. Take 1 crushed walnuts

Have you ever wanted to make a mango pudding but didn't a pudding mix at home? Well, you don't really need a premix. This mango pudding needs very few ingredients and a few minutes to make. With this recipe, you will never need to buy a pudding mix again.

Instructions to make Eggless mango biscuit custard pudding:
  1. Mix custard powder with half cup milk..n boil rest of it with sugar..
  2. Mix custard paste into boiling milk while stirring it n cook it for 3 min.. N den let it cool
  3. Crush Marie biscuits n walnuts separately..
  4. Pour a layer of custard in d tray n put all d crushed biscuits on custard
  5. Mix coffee powder in 20ml of water n pour it on biscuit layer..pour rest of custard..
  6. Garnish with mangoes n walnuts n mint sprigs
  7. Freeze n serve chilled
