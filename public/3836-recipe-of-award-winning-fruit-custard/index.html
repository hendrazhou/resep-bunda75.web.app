The ingredients needed to make Fruit custard:
  1. Get 1 litre Milk(full fat)
  2. Take 4 tbsp Custard powder
  3. Make ready 5-6 tbsp Sugar
  4. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla essence
  5. Prepare 1/2 banana chopped
  6. Make ready 1 apple chopped
  7. Get 1 Kiwi chopped
  8. Prepare 1 pomegranate
  9. Make ready pin h Saffron
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp Cardamom powder
  11. Make ready as needed dry fruits

Instructions to make Fruit custard:
  1. Take a pan add milk in it and bring it to a boil
  2. Add custard powder in half cup milk at normal temperature and mix it properly..
  3. Mix the mixture in boiled milk and stir continuously so that no lumps are formed.. cook it for 5 minutes..
  4. Add sugar in custard and give a boil..
  5. Now add Vanila essence ilachi powder and saffron and mix it..
  6. Let it cook now mix dry fruits or mix fruits in it..
  7. Let it cool in freeze.and serve cool
