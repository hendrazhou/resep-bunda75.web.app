The ingredients needed to make Ranch Dressing:
  1. Make ready 1 cup Mayonnaise
  2. Prepare 1 packages 'Stonyfield' Greek Yogurt plain 5.3 OZ
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Promised land whole milk
  4. Take 1/4 cup butter or heavy cream
  5. Get 1/8 cup Buttermilk (or milk with 1 tablespoon of lemon juice and let it sit for 6 mins)
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp vinegar
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp sour cream

  1. Mix the liquid ingredients together (esp. vinegar) and let it sit while gathering and chopping fresh and dry ingredients.
  2. Mix fresh, dry, and liquid ingredients together.
  3. Serve immediately or let sit in fridge for best results.
  4. Fresh parsley, chives, and dill are optional (one or the other), but they have a different taste as opposed to dried, so I prefer to include both.
