The ingredients needed to make Garlic lime chicken and rice:
  1. Take 1 lb chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  3. Prepare 1/4 tsp pepper
  4. Get 1/2 cup white wine
  5. Prepare 1 cup chicken broth
  6. Take 1/4 fresh lime juice (about 2 small limes)
  7. Get 2 clove garlic, minced
  8. Take 2 tbsp butter
  9. Take 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  10. Take 1 cup uncooked instant rice

Instructions to make Garlic lime chicken and rice:
  1. Spray nonstick pan with cooking spray and heat over medium high.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Cook in hot pan just until browned.
  3. Meanwhile, spray crock pot with nonstick cooking spray. Add chicken broth, garlic, thyme, and lime juice.
  4. When chicken is golden, add to crock pot.
  5. Deglaze saute pan with wine, then add to crock pot..
  6. Cover and cook on low 5-6 hours or on high 3-4 hours.
  7. During last 20 minutes of cooking time, add rice. Cover and cook until moisture is absorbed.
