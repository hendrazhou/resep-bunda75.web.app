Instructions to make Watermelon smoothie:
  1. Cut the watermelon in to half & deseeded them. Cut in small pieces.
  2. Keep the pieces in refrigerator for 10 minutes.
  3. Take them out & put them in the blender along with strawberry crush.
  4. Add 1/2 inch chopped ginger piece & few mint leaves.
  5. Now add ice cubes & blend.
  6. Serve chilled.
