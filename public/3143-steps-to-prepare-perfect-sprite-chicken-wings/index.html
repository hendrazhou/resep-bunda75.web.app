The ingredients needed to make Sprite Chicken Wings:
  1. Prepare 200-300 grams chicken wings
  2. Make ready 5 ml garlic powder
  3. Make ready 15 ml soy sauce
  4. Get 15 ml oyster sauce
  5. Take 2.5 ml black pepper
  6. Take 1 cup Sprite

Steps to make Sprite Chicken Wings:
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine chicken wings, garlic powder, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and black pepper. Mix well then marinate for about 2 hours.
  2. In a non-stick pan, put the marinated chicken then add Sprite. Cover then bring to a boil.
  3. When it boils, lower the heat. Mix continuously until all the liquid has been absorbed by the chicken.
  4. Remove from heat. Serve and enjoy!
