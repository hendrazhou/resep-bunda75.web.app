Instructions to make Homemade Tutti Frutti:
  1. Take a papaya peel and washed.
  2. After washed cut in small 2 pieces.
  3. Take a wok add 4 glass of water then water boiled add papaya pcs boil in 5 minutes.
  4. After boiled then water drain.
  5. After drain take a wok add papaya pieces and sugar mix well and cook for approximately 30 minutes.
  6. After 30 minutes then dry for papaya pieces.
  7. Then take a four plates and divide papaya pieces in equally.
  8. Then take a one plate add yellow colour mixed and keep a side.
  9. Then take a second plate add orange colour mixed and keep a aside.
  10. Take a third plate add green colour mixed and keep a side.
  11. Take a fourth plate add red colour mixed and keep aside.
  12. Then second day take a net garnish tutti frutti spread and full day keep a dry for fan.
  13. Then next day tutti frutti garnish in a plate then dry in a fan
  14. After 2 or 3 days tutti frutti dry,then pour in airtight container and keep in fridge.
