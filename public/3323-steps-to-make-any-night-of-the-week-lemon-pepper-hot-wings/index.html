Steps to make Lemon Pepper Hot Wings:
  1. Clean and season chicken with seasoning and garlic salt
  2. Add small amount of seasoning and garlic salt to flour (heat oil while preparing) coat chicken with flour and fry.
  3. While chicken is frying. Melt butter and pour in large bowl. Add buffalo wing sauce, hotter hot sauce and lemon pepper to butter. (Your desired amount)
  4. After you drain chicken from grease add to hot sauce mix and coat. Place chicken on baking pan. Pour remainder of sauce on chicken. Sprinkle lemon pepper on top.
  5. Broil until top of chicken is crispy or sauce has absorbed in chicken. (Not to long you don't want chicken drying out) Serve with favorite dipping sauce
