The ingredients needed to make Festive Tomato Wedges:
  1. Take 6 medium fresh tomatoes, cut into wedges
  2. Prepare 2/3 cup vegetable oil
  3. Get 1/4 cup white white vinegar ( I used red wine )
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  5. Take 1/4 cup sliced green onions
  6. Take 1 garlic cloves, minced
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  8. Get 1 tsp dill weed
  9. Get 1 tsp each dried basil & salt
