The ingredients needed to make Mango sticky rice:
  1. Prepare 1 Mango
  2. Take 500 kg Sticky Rice
  3. Make ready 65 ml coconut milk
  4. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. Get 1 spoon cornstarch
  6. Prepare 2 spoon sugar
  7. Prepare 100 ml water

Instructions to make Mango sticky rice:
  1. Boiled sticky rice and add salt, wait until half cooked
  2. Then steamed sticky rice was about 10 minutes
  3. Then add coconut milk and cook again until cooked, about 15 minutes
  4. Boiled coconut milk, sugar, water, cornmeal into one until boiling
  5. Cut into small mangoes, size according to taste
  6. Last serve sticky rice, mango and pour coconut milk on it
