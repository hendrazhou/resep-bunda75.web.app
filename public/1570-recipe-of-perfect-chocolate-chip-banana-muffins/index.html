The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip banana muffins:
  1. Prepare 4 bananas mashed
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Take 1/3 cup melted butter
  4. Get 1 egg beaten
  5. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  6. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Get 1 1/2 cups flour
  8. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Make ready Dark chocolate chunks

Instructions to make Chocolate chip banana muffins:
  1. Mix bananas, sugar, butter, egg
  2. In new bowl, mix baking Soda, powder, salt and flour
  3. Mix wet and dry. Pour I to greased muffin tin.
  4. Cut up chocolate if required. Stuck chunks into tops of muffins.
  5. Bake at 178 degrees for 20 minutes
