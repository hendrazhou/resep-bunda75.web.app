The ingredients needed to make Coco and choco balls:
  1. Make ready 1/3 cup condensed milk
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup dessicated coconut
  3. Make ready 10 pcs Pure bliss biscuits
  4. Get 1/8 cup icing sugar
  5. Take 1/4 cup milo
  6. Take Pinch pink color (optional)
  7. Get Toothpicks

Instructions to make Coco and choco balls:
  1. Our ingredients
  2. First crush your biscuits to a fine powder,add the Milo and icing sugar and grind to mix well
  3. Transfer to a plate and add the food color and coconut,mix well
  4. Add the milk and mix,rub oil on your palms and make round shapes
  5. Mix another Milo with small condensed milk in a separate bowl,dip each ball after inserting a toothpick
  6. Arrange on a plate and refrigerate for 30 minutes,but I decorated mine with marsh mallows
