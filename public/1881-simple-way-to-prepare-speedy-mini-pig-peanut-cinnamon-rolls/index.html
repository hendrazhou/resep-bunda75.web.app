Steps to make Mini Pig Peanut Cinnamon Rolls:
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.
  2. Mix whole wheat flour, baking powder and cinnamon until well combined and set aside.
  3. In a large bow mix peanut butter and milk then slowly add dry mixture.
  4. Knead dough into ball and roll onto a floured surface 1/4 inch thick and take a straight edge cut the dough into inch wide strips (I used a pizza cutter).
  5. Roll the strips along the long end to form the ‘roll’ shape.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes, cool and refrigerate.
