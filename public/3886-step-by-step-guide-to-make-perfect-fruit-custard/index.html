Instructions to make Fruit Custard:
  1. Dissolve custard powder in chilled Milk in a bowl.
  2. Take milk in a pan on low flame. Get one boil. Add sugar. Keep boiling for 5 min.
  3. Now add dissolve custard. Boil for 2 min. Pour it another bowl.
  4. Let it cool down to room temperature. Chopped all fruits. Add to the custard milk. Put the bowl in refrigerator to get it chilled. Serve chill and enjoyyyyyyyyyyyy the Fruit Custard
