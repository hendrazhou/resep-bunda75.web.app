The ingredients needed to make Kheer:
  1. Take 1/2 cup normal rice
  2. Prepare 1 litre milk
  3. Get 1 cup Sugar
  4. Prepare 1 tsp cardomon powder
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Assorted nuts
  6. Get 1 tbsp multi color tutti frutti

Instructions to make Kheer:
  1. Firstly soak the rice for a hour.
  2. Now in a pressure cooker add half milk and rice give it a two whistles and open.
  3. Now add remaining milk, cardomon powder, sugar, assorted nuts and cook it for 10 minutes.
  4. When you get proper thick consistancy take it in a serving bowl and garnish with nuts and tuti frutti.
