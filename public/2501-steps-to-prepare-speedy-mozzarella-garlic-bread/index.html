The ingredients needed to make Mozzarella garlic bread:
  1. Take 2.5 cups flour
  2. Make ready 70 gms mozzarella cheese
  3. Take 1 tsp instant dry yeast
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
  5. Prepare 4 tbsp salted butter
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  7. Get 3 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  8. Take 1/2 tbsp chopped garlic
  9. Get 1/2 cup warm milk
  10. Get 1 beaten egg

Steps to make Mozzarella garlic bread:
  1. In a big bowl add yeast sugar, milk. Add egg.
  2. Add garlic,3 tbsp butter, coriander leaves. Mix with spatula. Add flour. Mix. Add flour on plain surface amd put dough adding flour to avoid stickiness.
  3. Using finger tips make soft dough. Put on baking tray and cover with big bowl for 1 hour. Flatten with hand and make cutting on it.
  4. Brush little bit beaten egg on edges. Mix 1 tbsp coriander leaves and 1 tbsp butter. Brush it on top.
  5. Cut cheese into pieces. Make cutting place prominent with spoon and put cheese pieces into cutting place. Pre-heat oven into convection mode. In 180°© bake for 15 minutes. Ready to serve.
