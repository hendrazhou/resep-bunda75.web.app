The ingredients needed to make Mini Banana Chocolate chip muffins:
  1. Get 1 1/4 cup unbleached all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 3/4 tsp baking soda
  3. Make ready Pinch salt (1/4 tsp)
  4. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup white sugar
  6. Get 1/4 cup vegetable oil (or any light tasting oil- not olive)
  7. Get 2 room temp eggs
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup full fat sour cream
  9. Make ready 2 very ripe bananas, mashed
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  11. Take 1/2 cup (or to taste) chocolate chips

Instructions to make Mini Banana Chocolate chip muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. In a bowl, mix the first 4 ingredients and set aside
  3. In another bowl, mix sugar, oil, extract and eggs and beat on medium speed for 2 min. Add mashed bananas and mix another 1 min
  4. Pour 1/2 the flour into the mixture and mix for few seconds until combined
  5. Mix in the sour cream until well blended.
  6. Next put in the rest of the flour mix until just Blended, do NOT overmix.
  7. Stir in chocolate chips
  8. Fill 12 muffin tins, or 24 mini muffin tins. I baked these minis for 14 minutes. Regular size muffins I'd say 20-24 min
