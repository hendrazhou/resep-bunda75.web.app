The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Cheesecake Trifle:
  1. Take 250 grams Frozen or fresh strawberries
  2. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Take 1/4 cup water
  4. Take 1 tsp orange zest
  5. Prepare 100 grams cream cheese
  6. Make ready 3 tbsp dark brown packed sugar
  7. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Get 150 ml wipping cream
  9. Prepare 1 loaf pound cake

Instructions to make Strawberry Cheesecake Trifle:
  1. In a saucepan mix the strawberries, sugar, water, and orange zest. Cook for 15 to 20 minutes on medium heat stirring every once in a while.
  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and mash the berries using a potato masher. Set aside to cool down.
  3. In a bowl beat the cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl beat whipping cream until it forms soft peaks. Fold the whipping cream into cream cheese mixture.
  5. Cut the pound cake into 1 cm cubes and set aside.
  6. In small serving bowls (approximetly 10) put a layer of cake, then strawberry mixture, then cake, then cream cheese, and finally strawberry mixture.
  7. Cover and refrigirate for at least 2 hours.
  8. Serve and enjoy :)
  9. Note: You can make this in a big bowl instead of individual serving bowls if you prefer.
