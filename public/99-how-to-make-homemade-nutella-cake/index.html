Instructions to make Nutella Cake:

  2. Put all ingredients in a bowl and mix with wooden spoon.
  3. Put a baking sheet in a 9inch pan and put in the mixture.
  4. Put in oven to bake for 15/20 minutes or use a toothpick to check if its ready (toothpick should come out clean)
  5. Enjoy!
