The ingredients needed to make Barley Risotto:
  1. Take 1 cup pearl barley
  2. Get 3 cups water
  3. Prepare 2 cubes butter
  4. Get 2 cloves chopped garlic
  5. Get 300 g mushrooms
  6. Get 200 g spinach
  7. Make ready 1 small chopped onion
  8. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  9. Take Cheese for shaving on top
  10. Get 20 g thyme

Instructions to make Barley Risotto:
  1. Boil barley in water over high heat, after 1 boil keep it on slow for 20-25 mins or until done.
  2. In a pan, take butter put garlic and cook until it starts to get golden from edges.
  3. Add onions and cook for 2 mins
  4. Put mushrooms and cook for couple of mins, add salt and pepper.
  5. Add barley (once cooked) and stir, add spinach, stir gently & add herbs
  6. If you like creamy you can add some white wine too and some cheese.
  7. Shave parmesan cheese on top before serving
