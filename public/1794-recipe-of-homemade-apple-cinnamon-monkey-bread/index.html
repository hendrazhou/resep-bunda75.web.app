Instructions to make Apple cinnamon monkey bread:
  1. Cut the cinnamon rolls in 4ths, then put in a bowl
  2. Chop apple filling
  3. Add Apple to cinnamon rolls in a bowl and mix
  4. Spray bundt pan
  5. Add mixture to pan
  6. Bake on 350 for 30 to 40 minutes,Go around Bundt pan with a butter knife to the sides and middle to help come out, then flip onto a plate let cool before adding icing
  7. Enjoy😀
