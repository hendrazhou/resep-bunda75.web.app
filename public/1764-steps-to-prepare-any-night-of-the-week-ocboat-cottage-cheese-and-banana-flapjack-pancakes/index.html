The ingredients needed to make OCB..Oat, Cottage Cheese and Banana Flapjack/Pancakes:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Oatmeal
  2. Make ready 2 cups plain flour
  3. Prepare Half a cup sugar
  4. Take 2 teaspoons Baking powder
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking soda
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  7. Get Half teaspoon salt
  8. Make ready Half a cup of Cottage Cheese
  9. Get 2 over ripe Bananas, mashed to a pulp
  10. Prepare 1 cup laban (mala, or plain drinking yoghurt)
  11. Get Half cup canola oil (corn oil or melted butter is great too)
  12. Make ready 2 eggs
  13. Take 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
