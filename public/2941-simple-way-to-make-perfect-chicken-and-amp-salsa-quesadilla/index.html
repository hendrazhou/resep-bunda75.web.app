The ingredients needed to make Chicken & Salsa Quesadilla:
  1. Get 2 tortillas
  2. Take 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  3. Get 1 can shredded white chicken
  4. Get 1/4 cup mild salsa
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp butter

Steps to make Chicken & Salsa Quesadilla:
  1. Drain chicken and combine with salsa in small bowl.
  2. Butter one side of each tortilla.
  3. Lay one tortilla (butter side down) on pan and apply chicken mixture and cheese.
  4. Lastly put the remaining tortilla on top, butter side up and cook each side about a minute or two.
  5. Add your favorite toppings and ENJOY!
