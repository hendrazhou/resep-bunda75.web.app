The ingredients needed to make Moong chaat in barley tart:
  1. Get For tart
  2. Get 1 cup barley flour
  3. Take 1/2 tea spoon salt
  4. Prepare 1 beetroot grated
  5. Get 1/4 cup ghee
  6. Make ready For chaat
  7. Take 1 cup whole green gram dal
  8. Make ready 1 tea spoon salt
  9. Take 1/2 tea spoon turmeric powder
  10. Take 2 tea spoon chana masala
  11. Make ready 1 tea spoon chat masala
  12. Prepare 2 tea spoon lemon juice
  13. Make ready 1 tomato finely chopped
  14. Prepare 1 boiled potato peeled and chopped
  15. Make ready As needed pomegranate seeds
  16. Make ready As needed namkeen

Steps to make Moong chaat in barley tart:
  1. Rub ghee well in flour.
  2. Now make beetroot water by adding grated beetroot to 1/2 cup of water followed by boiling,cooling and straining.
  3. Now keep aside this dough for 30 minutes.
  4. Now take ball from it.
  5. Roll and paste inside the tart mould
  6. Bake in preheated microwave for 20 minutes at 180 degree.
  7. Cool and demould.
  8. Tart is ready.
  9. Wash and soak moong for 6 hours
  10. Boil with salt and turmeric powder and 1/2 cup water for 10 minutes.
  11. Strain extra water.
  12. Add all spices, tomato, potato.
  13. Fill this chat in tart.
  14. Serve by adding namkeen, pomegranate seeds.
