The ingredients needed to make Barley Soup:
  1. Get 1 cup Barley soaked overnight and pressure cook for 2 whistles
  2. Take 2 onions chopped
  3. Prepare 2 tomatoes chopped
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp crushed garlic
  5. Make ready 2 carrots sliced
  6. Take as needed baby corns cut into big
  7. Take 1 celery stalk
  8. Get to taste black pepper powder
  9. Prepare to taste Salt
  10. Take 1 tbsp rice flour mixed with water to make a slurry

Instructions to make Barley Soup:
  1. In a pan add a tbspn of oil and add the garlic and saute for a while..add the onions next and stir and saute till they turn pink in colour.
  2. Add the tomatoes and cook covered for 10-15 minutes till the tomatoes get soft and mushy.
  3. Now add in the veggies and chopped celery stalks, mix well and add salt and pepper. cover and cook for 5 minutes..
  4. Now add the cooked barley alongwith it’s water add little more water…cover and cook for another 10 minutes.
  5. Adjust the seasonings and add the rice flour slurry.
  6. This will thicken the soup, at this point you can adjust the consistency by adding more of slurry or water.
  7. Serve hot with a dash of lemon juice and your hearty meal is ready to devour.
