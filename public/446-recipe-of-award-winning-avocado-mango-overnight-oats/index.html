The ingredients needed to make Avocado mango overnight oats:
  1. Prepare 1/4 cup rolled oats
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup vanilla soy milk
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup vanilla yogurt
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup diced mango (I use frozen)
  5. Make ready half an avocado
  6. Take sliced banana (optional)
  7. Get 2-3 Tbs chia seeds
  8. Take honey

Steps to make Avocado mango overnight oats:
  1. Mix together all ingredients except honey, (I use Tupperware for easy covering)
  2. Store in fridge over night, wake up, stir, drizzle with honey and enjoy!
  3. Note: you may add sliced banana in your first mix or slice it in before serving.
