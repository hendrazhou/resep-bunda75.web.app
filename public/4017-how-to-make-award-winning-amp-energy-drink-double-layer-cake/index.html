Instructions to make Amp Energy Drink Double Layer Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix strawberry cake mix with cherry drink until most of the lumps are gone. do not add other cake ingredients.
  3. Mix white cake mix with berry drink.
  4. Pour each one into a greased 9 × 13 baking dish
  5. bake them for 18-20 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Allow to cool for about 30 minutes.
  7. leave one cake in the baking dish, spread one container of icing over cake, spread strawberry filling over icing.
  8. Next, place other cake on top for second layer. Spread second container of icing on cake.
  9. decorate as you like. I used a couple drops of food coloring to make pink and purple icing swirls. If you sprinkle a little cherry koolaid in the icing it gives it a little kick!!!
