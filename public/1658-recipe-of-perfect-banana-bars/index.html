The ingredients needed to make Banana Bars:
  1. Take 1/2 cup butter (1 stick)
  2. Make ready 3 eggs
  3. Take 1 teaspoon Vanilla
  4. Take 1 teaspoon baking soda
  5. Make ready 2 cups sugar
  6. Make ready 3-4 really ripe bananas
  7. Prepare 2 cups flour
  8. Take to taste Salt

Instructions to make Banana Bars:
  1. Combine butter, sugar, eggs, bananas, and vanilla and cream it.
  2. Mix flour and baking soda and salt; then add to wet ingredients.
  3. Mix well till you have a cake batter consistency and appearance.
  4. Bake in a greased pan at 350 for 30-35 minutes.
