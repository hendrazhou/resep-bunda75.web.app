The ingredients needed to make Jalapeno nacho cheese:
  1. Get nacho cheese
  2. Take 2 tbsp butter
  3. Get 2 tbsp flour
  4. Get 1 cup milk
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Take tsp 1/8 cayenne pepper
  7. Take 8 oz shredded cheddar
  8. Take 1 cup Jalapeños

Instructions to make Jalapeno nacho cheese:
  1. Melt butter in pan, over medium flame.
  2. Add flour whisk until paste.
  3. Add milk, cayenne pepper, and salt. Then whisk it all together for about a minute.
  4. Turn off heat and add 8 oz of shredded chedder.
  5. Whisk until creamy.
  6. Add Jalapeños (optional)
