The ingredients needed to make Tutti-frutti Sweet Bun (No yeast, No Oven):
  1. Take 1 cup maida
  2. Get 1/2 cup curd
  3. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  4. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Make ready Pinch salt
  6. Take 2 tbsp powdered sugar
  7. Take 2 tbsp milk powder
  8. Take 3 tbsp oil
  9. Take 1/2 cup tutti-frutti
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp milk + 1/2 tsp sugar mix for brushing
  11. Take 1 tsp unsalted butter

Steps to make Tutti-frutti Sweet Bun (No yeast, No Oven):
  1. Take a bowl sieve all dry ingredients and mix, then add oil and curd gradually and knead into a soft dough, in between u can add little oil if dough is so sticky
  2. Then grease with little oil to the bowl and place the dough and cover a plate and keep in a warm place for 20 to 30 minutes
  3. After 30 minutes u will see how the dough is fluffy, now grease your palm with oil, punch and knead again for 10 minutes
  4. Now divide the dough into 4 equal portion and add some tutti-frutti and knead well
  5. In between preheat to your pressure cooker for 10 minutes and grease your cake tin with oil
  6. Now turn round the balls between thumb and finger to make smooth balls and place the balls in the tin and keep 1/2 inch of space between the balls, add some tutti-frutti on top
  7. Brush some milk and sugar dilute over the balls,
  8. Place the tin in preheated cooker and bake for 20 to 25 minutes
  9. Remove from heat and brush little unsalted butter or oil on top and cover a wet towel for 30 minutes
  10. Now demould and enjoy your sweet buns
