Steps to make Spicy Cinnamon "RedHot" Homemade Applesauce:
  1. Peel, core and/or cut into 1-2 inch pieces, and boil apples 30-60 mins or until softened.
  2. Remove from liquid, drain, and smash down with fork.
  3. Add in cinnamon and sugar and Red Hot Candies. Stir well.
  4. Stir in 2-4 cinnamon sticks, cover and bake in the oven at 375° or until bubbly around the edges.
  5. Serve warm or cooled and Enjoy!
  6. Or store and/or freeze for later in mason jars or freezer bags. 1 dozen apples should make about 2-3 pint jars each of both sauce and cider.
