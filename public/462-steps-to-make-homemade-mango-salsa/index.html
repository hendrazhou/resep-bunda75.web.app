The ingredients needed to make Mango Salsa:
  1. Get 1 mango peeled seeded and chopped
  2. Make ready 1 medium size red bell pepper finely chopped
  3. Get 1 red onion chopped
  4. Prepare 2 tsp finely chopped cilantro
  5. Prepare 1 fresh jalapeño chili pepper finely chopped
  6. Prepare 1 tsp honey
  7. Prepare 2 tsp lime juice
  8. Take to taste salt

Steps to make Mango Salsa:
  1. In a serving bowl mix the mango, red bell pepper, red onion, cilantro, jalapeño, honey, lime juice and salt. (All the ingredients)
  2. Cover , and allow to rest for at least 30 minutes before serving. - You can serve it with lettuce hearts stuffed with chicken like the way i did mine (on the photo). - Enjoy!
