The ingredients needed to make Crock-Pot Cinnamon Spiced Applesauce:
  1. Prepare 16 apples
  2. Prepare 1 cup water, more as needed
  3. Prepare Spices and Sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 cups brown sugar
  5. Get 3 tsp cinnamon
  6. Get 1 tsp punkin pie spice
  7. Take 1/8 tsp ground clove

Steps to make Crock-Pot Cinnamon Spiced Applesauce:
  1. Peel apples
  2. Thinly slices and core apples
  3. Put apples and water in Crock-Pot
  4. Cook on low 6 to 8 hours
  5. Mash apples with a potato masher
  6. Add rest of the ingredients, stir
  7. Cook 30 min to incorporate the flavors
  8. Serve
