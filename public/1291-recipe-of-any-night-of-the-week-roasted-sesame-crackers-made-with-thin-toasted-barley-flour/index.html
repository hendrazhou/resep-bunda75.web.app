The ingredients needed to make Roasted Sesame Crackers Made With Thin Toasted Barley Flour:
  1. Prepare 1/2 tsp Miso
  2. Make ready 30 grams Sugar (cane sugar, if available)
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp Toasted barley flour
  4. Take 30 grams White and black sesame seeds
  5. Prepare 1/2 egg or 2 tablespoons milk Egg or milk

Steps to make Roasted Sesame Crackers Made With Thin Toasted Barley Flour:
  1. Add the ingredients listed into a bowl in the order shown, mixing well after each addition.
  2. Drop spoonfuls of the dough on a sheet of parchment paper. The batter should yield enough dough for about 16 crackers.
  3. Lay a piece of parchment paper cut into an 8 cm square over the dough and press down with your fingers to flatten to an even thinness.
  4. Bake the dough in an oven preheated to 350°F/180°C for about 8 to 10 minutes, checking from time to time to see whether they are baking evenly. They're done with they turn a light brown.
  5. Allow them to cool to a crisp, then serve.
  6. Be sure to chew them well to fully enjoy the sesame flavor. Chewing them well also helps the body absorb the nutrients better, too.
