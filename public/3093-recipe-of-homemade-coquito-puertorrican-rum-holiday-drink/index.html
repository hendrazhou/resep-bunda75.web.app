The ingredients needed to make coquito (puertorrican rum holiday drink):
  1. Take 2 can condensed milk
  2. Prepare 1 can evaporated milk
  3. Get 1 can coconut milk or cream
  4. Take 2 tbsp cinnamon
  5. Take 1 1/4 tbsp lime zest
  6. Get 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 2 cup clear rum (preferably Bacardi)

BACARDÍ Coquito is made with BACARDÍ Superior Rum and a mix of vanilla, cinnamon and other festive spices. Coquito is a traditional coconut punch served at Christmas and New Year's celebrations in Puerto Rico. The creamy, sweet drink is similar to eggnog with rum but with the tropical element of coconut. Steps to make coquito (puertorrican rum holiday drink):
  1. Add all the ingredients in a large bowl, mix well until all is blended.
  2. Transfer to a sealed bottle(s)
  3. Refrigerate, the colder the better.
