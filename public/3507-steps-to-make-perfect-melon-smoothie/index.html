Steps to make Melon smoothie:
  1. Take out all the watermelon seeds by a teaspoon
  2. Place chopped watermelon, honeydew, and cantaloupe in a blender
  3. Blend all the melons together
  4. Pour it in a glass cup, and filter it if needed
  5. Enjoy a delicious and healthy melon smoothie!
