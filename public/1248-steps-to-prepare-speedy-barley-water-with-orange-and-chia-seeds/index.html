Steps to make Barley Water with Orange and Chia Seeds:
  1. Soak barley for 3-4 hours
  2. Soak chia seed in 1 cup of water for 1 or 2 hours.
  3. Boil soaked barley with 2 cups of water.
  4. Simmer and cook till the grains become soft.
  5. Strain and reserve the water.
  6. Keep in the refrigerator to cool.
  7. At the time of the serving transfer the barley water to a glass.
  8. Add freshly squeezed orange juice.
  9. Add soaked chia seeds and black salt.
  10. Serve immediately.
