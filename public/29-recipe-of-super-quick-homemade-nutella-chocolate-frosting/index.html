The ingredients needed to make Nutella Chocolate Frosting:
  1. Make ready 1/4 cup soften butter
  2. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  3. Take 1 cup powdered sugar
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup nutella or any hazelnut chocolate spread

Instructions to make Nutella Chocolate Frosting:
  1. Using electric mixer, beat butter and vanilla extract.
  2. Add powdered sugar gradually while continously beating until white and fluffy.
  3. Add in nutella. Beat until well combined.
  4. Pipe into your favorite cupcakes, or top some candies, too.
