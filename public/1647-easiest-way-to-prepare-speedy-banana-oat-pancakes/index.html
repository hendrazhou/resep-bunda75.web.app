The ingredients needed to make Banana Oat Pancakes:
  1. Prepare 1 1/4 Cup Oats
  2. Prepare 4 Bananas for the Dough (1/2 Banana for plating)
  3. Make ready 3 Eggs
  4. Make ready 1/4 Teaspoon Cinnamon
  5. Prepare 1 Tablespoon Vanilla
  6. Take 1 1/2 Tablespoon Baking soda
  7. Get Pinch Salt
  8. Get Blueberries (to your preference)
  9. Make ready 1 Teaspoon Peanut Butter (for each pancake layer)
  10. Take 1 Teaspoon Honey

Steps to make Banana Oat Pancakes:
  1. Mash the bananas and add cinnamon
  2. Add eggs to the mashed bananas
  3. Add vanilla flavor
  4. Baking soda and a pinch of salt
  5. Add 1 cup and 1/4 oats
  6. Blend the mix in a blender
  7. Add blue berries before frying the dough with coconut oil
  8. Add peanut butter and banana after frying and sprinkle cinnamon and a teaspoon of honey on top.
