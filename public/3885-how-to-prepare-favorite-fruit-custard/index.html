  2. Prepare 1 kg milk
  3. Prepare 2-4 tbsp sugar
  4. Take as needed Fruits
  5. Get handful Finely chopped dry fruits
  6. Take as needed Chocolate for garnishing

Instructions to make Fruit custard:
  1. First of all on the flame and bake milk around 1 hour.
  2. Then take a bowl add 2 tbsp of custard powder add 1 tbsp of cold milk.
  3. Mix it well.
  4. Then this custard better mix into milk.
  5. And stir continuously.
  6. Stir till then the milk is thick.
  7. After that add sugar mix it continuously.
  8. When sugar dissolved completely off the flame. And place on till then on room temperature
  9. Then refrigerate it to cool. Take fruit like bananas, mango, apple
  10. Cutted into small pieces add finely chopped dry fruits like almonds, cashews etc.mix it
  11. The fruit custard is ready garnish with fruit chocolate and dry fruits.
  12. I use mango fan for garnishing.
