The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Apple Pancakes:
  1. Prepare 1/3 cup Buttermilk pancake mix
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup Water
  3. Prepare 1 tsp Cinnamon powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 small Apple

Instructions to make Cinnamon Apple Pancakes:
  1. Cut an apple in half. Peel off skin if you like. Using only one of the halves, chop into bite size pieces.
  2. Stir pancake mix & water together. Add in cinnamon powder. Let batter sit for a minute to thicken up.
  3. On medium heat, pour batter into the size pancake you want. Sprinkle in apple pieces. Continue cooking & flipping till both sides are golden brown. Top with honey or syrup. Enjoy!
