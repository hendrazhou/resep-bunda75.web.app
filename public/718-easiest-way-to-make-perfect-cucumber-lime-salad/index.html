The ingredients needed to make Cucumber Lime Salad:
  1. Make ready 1/2 Cucumber, thinly sliced
  2. Prepare 1/4 Red Onion, diced small
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp Cilantro, finely chopped
  4. Take 1 Garlic Clove, smashed & minced
  5. Take 1/2 cup Black Beans, cooked
  6. Get pinch Salt & Pepper
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  8. Prepare 1 Lime, juiced
  9. Get 1 tbsp Olive Oil

Steps to make Cucumber Lime Salad:
  1. Whisk together the lemon juice, lime juice, and olive oil.
  2. Add the garlic, salt, pepper, and cilantro. Mix with a wooden spoon.
  3. Lastly, add the onion and cucumber slices and gently fold the ingredients into each other.
  4. Place in the refrigerator for at least 15 minutes to let the flavors enhance.
