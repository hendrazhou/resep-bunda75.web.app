The ingredients needed to make Wheat Flour Custard Coconut Cake Bars:
  1. Make ready 1/1/4 cup wheat flour
  2. Take 3/4 cup oil/butter
  3. Prepare 1 cup milk
  4. Take 1 cup sugar
  5. Get 2 ripe banana
  6. Take 2 tsp vanilla custard powder
  7. Prepare 3 tsp dessicated coconut
  8. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking soda
  10. Take 1 tsp vinegar
  11. Make ready 4-5 drops vanilla essence
  12. Make ready as per need Tutti-frutti
  13. Make ready as required Dessicated coconut and mix fruit jam for decoration

Instructions to make Wheat Flour Custard Coconut Cake Bars:
  1. In a mixing bowl take banana,oil and sugar,mix constantly until it comes in a creamy texture.
  2. Now add,flour,custard powder,milk,vanilla essence and tutti fruti,mix nicely in one direction for 2 minutes and prepare a thick batter of pouring consistency.
  3. Finally add baking powder,baking soda and vinegar,mix up well for 1 minute.
  4. Pour batter in a greased tin and tap it to avoid air bubbles.
  5. Heat cooker with iron stand into it for 5 minutes on high flame and place the cake tin into it and keep the flame low now and bake it for 40 minutes.
  6. After 40 minutes check the cake by inserting a toothpick or a knife in cake,if it comes clean which means cake is ready,otherwise bake it for 5 minutes more and cake will be ready.
  7. Take off cake tin carefully from cooker and allow it to cool down completely.
  8. Now cut cake in pieces of baars and garnish with dessicated coconut and mix fruit jam.
  9. Wheat Flour Custard Coconut Cake Baars is ready to serve.
