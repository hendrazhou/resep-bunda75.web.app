The ingredients needed to make Basic Vanilla cake loaded with tutti frutti:
  1. Take 1 cup Maida
  2. Take 1/2 cup Milk powder
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup Sugar
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup Oil
  5. Prepare 11/2 tsp Baking powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Baking soda
  7. Get 1 tsp Vanilla essence
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup Tutti frutti
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup Milk

Steps to make Basic Vanilla cake loaded with tutti frutti:
  1. Mix all the ingredients.
  2. Add tutti frutti and mix.
  3. Pour in a greased pan and bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for 20 mins.
  4. Serve topped with cream or just dig in.
