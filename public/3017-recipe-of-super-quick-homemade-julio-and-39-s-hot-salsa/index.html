The ingredients needed to make Julio's hot salsa:
  1. Prepare 6 jalapeno
  2. Get 4 oranges habaneros
  3. Take 1 tomato
  4. Take 1 1/2 onion
  5. Get 1 clove garlic
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp salt
