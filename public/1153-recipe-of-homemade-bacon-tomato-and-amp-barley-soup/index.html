The ingredients needed to make Bacon, Tomato & Barley Soup:
  1. Take 3 tbsp olive oil
  2. Make ready 150 grams streaky bacon, finley chopped
  3. Make ready 1 large red onion,peeled and finely chopped
  4. Make ready 2 stalks celery, finely sliced
  5. Make ready 2 carrots,chopped
  6. Take 2 clove garlic,finely chopped
  7. Make ready 2 can tomatoes
  8. Get 3 cup chicken stock
  9. Get 1/2 cup pearl barley
  10. Take 1/4 cabbage, shredded
  11. Get 1/4 cup parsley,finely chopped
  12. Make ready 1 shaved parmesan to garnish ( optional)
  13. Prepare 1 salt and pepper to taste

Steps to make Bacon, Tomato & Barley Soup:
  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan and gently cook bacon, onion, celery, carrots and garlic until tender.
  2. Add the tomatoes and chicken stock to the pan and bring to the boil.
  3. Add pearl barley, turn heat down and simmer for 30-40 minutes until tender.
  4. Season the soup to taste. Add the cabbage and parsley and cook for a further 15 minutes. Serve as is or garnish with the shaved parmesan.
