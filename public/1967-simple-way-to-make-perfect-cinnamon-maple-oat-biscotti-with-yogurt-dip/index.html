The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon-maple oat biscotti with yogurt dip:
  1. Get 1 cup All purpose flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup old fashioned rolled oats
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  4. Take 1 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  5. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  6. Get 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  7. Make ready Pinch sea salt

  9. Take 1/4 cup grade B maple syrup
  10. Make ready 2 Tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  11. Get 1 Tbsp canola oil
  12. Prepare 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  13. Get 1/4 cup finely diced dried apricots
  14. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped dried cranberries
