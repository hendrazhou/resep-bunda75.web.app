The ingredients needed to make Q-tip's cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Prepare 2 foot long heros
  2. Make ready 1 stick buttter
  3. Get 2 tsp minced garlic
  4. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  5. Take 1/4 tsp parsley
  6. Take 1/4 tsp basil
  7. Take 1 dash salt
  8. Make ready 1 parm cheese
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
