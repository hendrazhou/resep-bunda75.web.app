The ingredients needed to make Butter cookies:
  1. Get 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar powder
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup maida
  4. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon tutti frutti
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup cornflour

Instructions to make Butter cookies:
  1. In a mixing bowl add room temperature butter, sugar powder and vanilla essence
  2. Beat it with hand mixure till white and fluffy
  3. Then add cornflour and maida mix it
  4. Then again beat it till fluffy
  5. Then make small size balls and press 1-1 tutifruity in middle
  6. Put all balls by keeping distance in a baking tray and bake it in the preheated microwave on 150 degrees for 7-8 min. Keep eyes 👀 on it after 5 mins.
  7. Then cool it on cooling rack and store it in airtight container.
