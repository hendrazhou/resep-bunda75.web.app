Instructions to make Banana smoothie with Nectarine:
  1. So, everything is very simple. First, wash the banana and nectarine.
  2. In a bowl for mixing all the ingredients you need to pour 150 g of orange juice. Add banana slices and nectarine, boned (you can remove the skin). Carefully work the blender until smooth and our smoothie is ready!
