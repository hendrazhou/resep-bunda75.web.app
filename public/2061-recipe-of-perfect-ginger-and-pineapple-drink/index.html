  3. Get 1/4 cup lemon juice

  5. Take Tiara pineapple/ginger optional
  6. Make ready cube Ice

Instructions to make Ginger and pineapple Drink:
  1. Assemble all the above mentioned ingredients.
  2. Peel the back of the ginger and cut it into small pieces to save your blender.
  3. Peel the pineapple and cut it.
  4. In a blender add in the ginger and the pineapple.
  5. Add some water and blend it till smooth.
  6. Using a Seiver, seive it, add the lemon juice, sugar or honey, tiara flavor pineapple or ginger optional and mix it very well.
  7. In a glass cup add in the ice cube, some lemon sliced, follow by the juice and enjoy.
  8. Serve with any snacks, I served mine with samosa
  9. That's all
