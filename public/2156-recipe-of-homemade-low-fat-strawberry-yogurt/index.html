The ingredients needed to make Low- fat Strawberry yogurt:

  2. Take 1/2 cup artificial sweetener
  3. Take 1 cup non-fat plain yogurt
  4. Make ready 2 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions to make Low- fat Strawberry yogurt:
  1. In a blender, process the strawberries and the sweetener on low speed for 2 minutes.
  2. Add the yogurt and lemon juice. Process on the highest speed until you get a well-blended puree.
  3. Taste the mix and add some more sweetener if needed.
  4. Serve chilled.
