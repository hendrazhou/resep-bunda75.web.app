The ingredients needed to make Tutti frutti cookies:
  1. Prepare 1 bowl cream (malai)
  2. Take 1 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  3. Get 1 1/2 bowl wheat flour
  4. Prepare handful Tutti frutti (optional)
  5. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon Baking soda
  6. Prepare As needed Ghee for greasing tray

Instructions to make Tutti frutti cookies:
  1. Take cream and powdered sugar in a flat vessel.
  2. Mix them well and add baking soda.
  3. Add flour to it and keep kneading a dough.
  4. Make the rolls of half dough as shown below and refrigerate them for 20 minutes.
  5. Add tutti fruti to the other half dough and mix it well.
  6. Roll these dough also as shon below and refrigerate them.
  7. Preheat your oven for about 100 minutes and 180℃. Now cut slices from the roll and arrange them as shown in the below tray after greasing the tray. Bake them at 180℃ for about 10 minutes. Check them and these may take 5 minutes more at the same temperature.
  8. They will seem soft yet but they are well cooked. Let them dry and enjoy the crispy cookies with your tea, coffee or just milk.
