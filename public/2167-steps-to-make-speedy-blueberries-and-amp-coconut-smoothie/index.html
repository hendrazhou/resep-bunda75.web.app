The ingredients needed to make Blueberries & coconut smoothie 🥥💜:
  1. Prepare 2 1/2 cup Blueberries (frozen are good)
  2. Take 1/2 cup Coconut yogurt (you can also use Greek yogurt)
  3. Make ready 1 Banana
  4. Get 2 cups Water
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Flaxseed
  6. Make ready Coconut flakes (to garnish)
