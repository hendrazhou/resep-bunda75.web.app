Steps to make Nachos & French fries with cheese fudge:
  1. Make a dough of makki ka atta & salt with lukewarm water. - Make as many balls as u can from it.Put a plastic sheet on the rolling board.Put one ball on it & cover with other half of the sheet.Roll it with rolling pin.
  2. Take any mould of ur choice,round,square or triangle. - Put the mould on the chapati,the shape comes out.I give triangle shape with the help of knife.Now pinch them with the fork.
  3. Heat oil in a karahi & deep fry all nachos till they become little pink. - Take them out on the tissue paper so that excess oil is absorbed.
  4. French Fries: cut the potatoes in thin slices. - Marinate them with all the ingredients for 20 minutes.
  5. Heat oil in a karahi & deep fry them.
  6. Serve both starters with cheese fudge.
