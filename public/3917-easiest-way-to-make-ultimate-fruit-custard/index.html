The ingredients needed to make Fruit custard:
  1. Get 3 cups full fat milk
  2. Get 5 tbsp sugar
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp custard powder (vanilla)
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 1 banana chopped
  6. Make ready 1 apple chopped
  7. Prepare 1 cup grapes chopped
  8. Get 1/2 cup pomegranate
  9. Take 3 tbsp fresh cream
  10. Get 1 tbsp rose syrup
  11. Get 1 tbsp kiwi crush

Steps to make Fruit custard:
  1. Ingredients reqd to make fruit custard. In one cup of milk add custard powder.
  2. Allow the remaining milk to come to a boil. Then add the custard milk which you prepared. Now add vanilla extract. Mix well. Now switch off the gas and allow the milk to cool completely.
  3. Add the chopped fruits. Add fresh cream to give some richness to your custard. Keep it in the fridge for 2-3 hrs. Finally garnish it with rose syrup and kiwi crush
