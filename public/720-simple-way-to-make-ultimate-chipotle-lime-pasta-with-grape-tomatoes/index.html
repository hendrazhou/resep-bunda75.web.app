The ingredients needed to make Chipotle Lime Pasta with Grape Tomatoes:
  1. Prepare 1 lb grape tomatoes
  2. Get 1 box pasta
  3. Make ready 1 cup heavy cream (milk can also be used)
  4. Take 1 tsp minced garlic
  5. Take 2 tsp chipotle powder (more or less depending on how spicy you like it)
  6. Take 1 tsp smoked paprika
  7. Get 3 tsp lime juice
  8. Take 1/4 cup butter
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp flower
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
  11. Get 1 tbsp chopped fresh basil

Instructions to make Chipotle Lime Pasta with Grape Tomatoes:
  1. Begin boiling water for pasta as directed.
  2. Melt butter in large saucepan
  3. Add minced garlic and cook until translucent
  4. Add cream and reduce heat until gentile boil
  5. Whisk in chipotle powder and paprika. Add lime juice.
  6. Whisk in1 tbsp of flower. Wait 1 minute to gauge thickness. Continue adding flower until you reach the correct consistency. About 5 minutes.
  7. Slice tomatoes in half.
  8. Add tomatoes to frying pan with oil
  9. Saute tomatoes until they soften slightly, only about 1 minute. Don't over cook because they can get really mushy.
  10. After pasta is done cooking, drain and return to the cooking pot.
  11. Mix cream sauce to pasta.
  12. Serve pasta to bowls, top with tomatoes.
  13. Sprinkle chopped basil on top.
