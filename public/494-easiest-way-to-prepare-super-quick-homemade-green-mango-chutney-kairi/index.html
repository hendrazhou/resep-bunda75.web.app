The ingredients needed to make Green Mango chutney (kairi):
  1. Make ready 2 medium size green mango (kairi) peeled,cut
  2. Get 5 garlic cloves peeled
  3. Make ready salt
  4. Take 1/3 cup fresh mint leaves
  5. Take 5 green chilli or to taste
  6. Take 1/2 tsp sugar optional
  7. Prepare water
