The ingredients needed to make Strawberry-Banana Protein Smoothie:
  1. Take 1/2 cup fat free milk
  2. Take 1/4 cup plain greek yogurt
  3. Make ready 6 tbsp vanilla protein powder
  4. Take 1 tsp chia seeds
  5. Get 1 medium banana
  6. Make ready 10 large strawberries
