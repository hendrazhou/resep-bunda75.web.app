Steps to make Chicken wings with spicy garlic sauce:
  1. Prepare ingredient and spices
  2. Marinated chicken,tamarind and salt. Lift for 15 minute
  3. Grinding all spices until paste
  4. Prepare for Garlic sambal.
  5. Saute spices with vegetables oil until fragrant. Add water and chicken, simmer for 30 minute.
  6. Set aside and start to fry until browned
  7. Fry the chicken sauce again until it dries. Add chicken and mix
  8. Mix chicken with Garlic spicy sauce
  9. Serve
