The ingredients needed to make In a Pinch Garlic Cheesy Bread:
  1. Take 5 hot dog buns
  2. Get 1/2 cup softened butter
  3. Take 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  4. Make ready 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  5. Take grated parmesan cheese, for topping

Steps to make In a Pinch Garlic Cheesy Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Split hot dog buns in half and place on a baking sheet.
  2. Mix together butter and garlic powder.
  3. Spread each bun with a thin layer of the butter.
  4. Top each bun with mozzarella and parmesan cheeses.
  5. Bake for about 10 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
