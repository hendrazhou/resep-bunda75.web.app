Instructions to make AMIEs Nutella Brownies:
  1. Preheat oven to 180ºC (350ºF). Brush a 9"x13" baking pan with shortening then sprinkle with flour to coat surface. Tap excess flour.
  2. Beat nutella and eggs together until grainy. Add flour and blend until combined. Do not over mix.
  3. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, until firm and done. Cool then cut into squares.
