The ingredients needed to make Fresh fruit custard:
  1. Make ready 2 cup milk
  2. Get 4 tablespoons sugar
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons custard powder
  4. Make ready pinch cardamom powder optional
  5. Get 2 small banana
  6. Prepare 1 apples
  7. Take 1/2 dry grapes
  8. Take dry grapes for garnish

Instructions to make Fresh fruit custard:
  1. Add cup milk to a heavy bottom pan on medium high heat. - Once it has heated up a little, take out 3 tablespoons milk from the pan into a small bowl. Add custard powder to that milk and mix well. There should be no lumps. Set it aside.
  2. Stir the milk in the pan in between and let it heat up and come to a gentle boil. Then lower the heat, add sugar and mix until combined.
  3. Start adding the custard mix. Add little at a time and mix after each addition. Whisk continuously.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the custard thickens. Remove pan from heat and let it cool down a bit. At this point add cardamom powder.
  5. At the same time, chop all fruits that you are using for the custard and refrigerate.
  6. Now add a layer of fruits on the bottom of bowl and transfer custard to serving bowl.
  7. Cover and refrigerate the custard for 1 to 2 hours for the flavors to develop. Then garnish with dry grapes and serve fruits custard in bowls!
