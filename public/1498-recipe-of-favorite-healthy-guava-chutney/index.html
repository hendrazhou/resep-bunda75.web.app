The ingredients needed to make Healthy guava chutney:
  1. Get 1 cup chopped guava
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  3. Get 1/2 tsp ginger
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tsp green chilli chopped
  6. Get 1 tsp lemon juice
  7. Take Salt
