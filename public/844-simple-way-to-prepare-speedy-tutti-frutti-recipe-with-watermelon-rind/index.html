Instructions to make Tutti Frutti Recipe With Watermelon Rind:
  1. Remove the green and red part of the watermelon rind.cut the watermelon rind with a knife.peel the skin and cut into pieces to the innerpot add Watermelon rind and 3/4 cup of sugar and 1 cup of water.
  2. Pressure cook for 1 minute and do quik release.then turn on the sim mode.stir and boil for 15_20min stirring.cook until getting one string consistency of sugar syrup and watermelon rind to turn soft yet retain it's shaps.
  3. Turn off the instant pot and add vanilla extract and mix all well.divide the cooled watermelon rind into the equal part with sugar syrup.
  4. Add any food color of your choice.Let them soak for an hour.Now drain off the sugar syrup and allow them to dry over the kitchen towel.
  5. Colouful Tutti Frutti is ready to eat,Top themover ice cream,Pudding,Cake,or eat them as it is,Enjoy!!!
