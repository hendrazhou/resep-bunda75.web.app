The ingredients needed to make Lemongrass and Curry Chicken Wings:
  1. Get 5 lb chicken wings
  2. Make ready 1 cup lemongrass (chopped fine)
  3. Take 3 tbsp curry
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup soy sauce
  5. Take 3 tbsp sugar
  6. Get 2 tbsp salt
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp black pepper
  8. Get 3 tbsp hot sauce (use your favourite)
  9. Make ready 1 garlic (optional)

Steps to make Lemongrass and Curry Chicken Wings:
  1. Mix all ingredients together and marinade for at least 2 hours or preferably overnight.
  2. Place wings on a baking sheet with parchment paper and bake in oven at 400°F for about 30 minutes.
