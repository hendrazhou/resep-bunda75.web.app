The ingredients needed to make Cheesy stuffed garlic bread:
  1. Make ready 1 cup flour
  2. Make ready 1 cup water
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon sugar
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon activated yeast
  6. Get 2-3 tablespoons butter
  7. Take Handful Boiled corn
  8. Get 7-8 garlic cloves
  9. Get 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes
  10. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon mix herbs
  11. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  12. Make ready some Some grated cheese

Steps to make Cheesy stuffed garlic bread:
  1. Take 1 cup of water, Add 1tablespoon sugar and dissolve it, Now add 1tablespoon activated yeast and keep it aside for 10 mins.
  2. Now make a dough out of 1cup maida,salt as per taste from the activated yeast water you may also add some dry flour to make a non sticky dough and cover with a damp cloth for 1hour.
  3. Now after 1hour when the dough is doubled add some more flour if your dough is sticky, now add 2 tablespoons of butter and mix nicely with the dough.
  4. Now to make garlic butter add some Finley grated garlic cloves, chilli flakes, mix herbs and some butter and mix until blends completely.
  5. Now make two sections out of the dough and make thick chapati out of it, now to the chapati spread some garlic butter on top, boiled corn, cheese,chilli flakes and oregano on one side.
  6. Now flip the other end and press the ends to close them slightly and make little cuts and bake for 15-20 mins. Now Enjoy!
