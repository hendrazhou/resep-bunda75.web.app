The ingredients needed to make Berry Smoothie Bowl:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Frozen Strawberries
  2. Make ready 1 cup frozen bananas

  4. Get 1 strawberry yogurt
  5. Prepare 1 cup almond milk
  6. Make ready Toppings
  7. Make ready 1/2 kiwi
  8. Make ready Handful sliced almonds
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp flaxseed
