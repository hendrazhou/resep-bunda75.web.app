The ingredients needed to make Chicken, Corn, and Lime Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  2. Take 1 cup white onion, dice
  3. Take 2 clove garlic cloves, minced
  4. Take 3 cup chicken stock
  5. Get 1 1/2 cup tomatoes, peeled, seeded, dice
  6. Take 1 1/2 cup corn kernels
  7. Make ready 2 each jalapeno chiles, seeded, minced
  8. Make ready 1 tsp cumin, ground
  9. Prepare 1 cup chicken thigh meat, fat trimmed, thinly sliced
  10. Take 3 tbsp cilantro, chopped
  11. Get 1 1/2 tbsp fresh lime juice
  12. Get salt
  13. Take pepper

Instructions to make Chicken, Corn, and Lime Soup:
  1. Heat oil over medium heat, add onion and garlic, cook 8-10 minutes or until very soft.
  2. Add chicken stock, tomatoes, corn, chilies, and cumin, bring to boil, reduce to simmer, and cook 5-8 minutes to blend flavors.
  3. Add chicken and simmer about 3 minutes to cook meat.
  4. Stir in cilantro and lime juice.
  5. Correct seasonings.
