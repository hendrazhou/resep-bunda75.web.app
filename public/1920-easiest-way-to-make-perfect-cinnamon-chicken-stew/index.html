The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon chicken stew:
  1. Take 2 bone- in chicken breast
  2. Prepare 4 large carrots chopped
  3. Prepare 5 small to medium potatoes cjopped
  4. Take 1 large can of crushed tomatoes
  5. Get 4 cloves chopped garlic
  6. Make ready 1 large yellow onion chopped
  7. Prepare 1/2 C light brown sugar
  8. Get 4 Tbsp olive oil
  9. Make ready 1/4 c Adobo seasoning
  10. Get 3 C water
  11. Take 4 Tbsp cinnamon

Steps to make Cinnamon chicken stew:
  1. Mix chopped garlic, 2 Tbsp olive oil, brown sugar, cinnamon and adobo and cleaned chicken breast in a ziploc bag and mix.
  2. Make sure chicken is completely covered.
  3. Refrigerate for 1 hour or more.
  4. Heat the rest of olive oil in a pot.
  5. When done marinating, add chicken to the pot.
  6. Brown chicken on all sides.
  7. After browning chicken add chopped onions until translucent (slightly see through).
  8. Add water or broth to pot.
  9. Make sure you scrap the goodness on the bottom of the pan for epic flavor.
  10. Add crushed tomatoes to pot.
  11. After cooking for 1/2 hour add potatoes and carrots.
  12. 1 hour into cooking remove chicken to take away the bones.
  13. Shred chicken and add back to pot.
  14. Cook until potatoes and carrots are to your liking and chicken is cooked through. I cooked for another 40 minutes.
  15. Voila! done
