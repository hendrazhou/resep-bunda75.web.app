The ingredients needed to make Guava Juice:
  1. Prepare 3-4 fresh guavas (medium sized)
  2. Take 1/4 cup sugar
  3. Make ready To taste salt
  4. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper
  5. Get 1 cup cold water (or as required)
  6. Get 1 pinch black salt (optional)

Instructions to make Guava Juice:
  1. Rinse the guavas in water well and chop them into small pieces.
  2. Transfer them to a blender. Add in sugar, salt, black pepper and water. Blend the mixture well, such that it's consistency is smooth. Don't blend it for too long as it will be difficult to strain the seeds later on.
  3. Use a strainer to strain the juice into a bowl and throw away the seeds left in the strainer.
  4. Add the black salt and mix well.
  5. Pour the juice in serving glasses and serve immediately.
