The ingredients needed to make Vanilla tutti frutti cake:
  1. Get 1 cup All purpose flour
  2. Take 3/4 cup Sugar powder
  3. Take 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  4. Get 1 tsp Baking powder
  5. Prepare as required Tutti frutti
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup Butter
  7. Get 1/4 cup oil
  8. Prepare 2 eggs

Steps to make Vanilla tutti frutti cake:
  1. Beat butter,oil and sugar.
  2. Add baking powder,maida,eggs
  3. Add eggs,maida,baking powder and vanilla extract,beat well
  4. Pour batter in a baking container.add tutti frutti.Tap container,glaze with milk.
  5. Bake in preheated microwave at 200℃.for 30 minutes.Check with toothpick.Let it cool then de mould.
  6. When cools completely slice and serve.
