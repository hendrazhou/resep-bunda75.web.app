Instructions to make Raw Key lime matcha cheesecake:
  1. Soak cashews in boiling hot water, while that sets blend the crust ingredients in a food processor.
  2. Press the crust into a pie pan, drain the cashew and blend them with the rest of the filling ingredients except for matcha.
  3. Take out half of the blended filling and place it in a bowl, and add matcha to the rest and blend.
  4. Now, pour the plain filling on the pie crust, then the matcha filling, and blend them together with a chopstick to create flower shapes.
  5. Freeze the cheesecake for at least 4 hours, take it out, and slice it, then place it in the fridge, and enjoy it whenever.
