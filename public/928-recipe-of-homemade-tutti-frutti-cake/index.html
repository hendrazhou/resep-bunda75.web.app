  4. Get 1/2 cup milk powder
  5. Make ready 1 cup milk
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanila essences
  7. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  8. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  9. Take 1 cup tutti fruti

Instructions to make Tutti-frutti cake:
  1. Mix well maida, baking powder, baking soda and sieve it.
  2. Grease and dust the baking tin with oil & maida.
  3. Preheat the oven at 180* for 10 minutes.
  4. Beat the butter and powder sugar with hand blender until it soften & become fluffy.
  5. Add milk powder & beat again.add essences & beat well.
  6. Now slowly mix in the flour mixture & mix well with a spatula.
  7. Add milk & mix well.Add tutti fruti and mix well.
  8. If the batter is thik add more 1or 2 tbsp of milk & mix well.
  9. Pour the batter in the baking tin & bake it for 30-35 min till the knife come clean.
  10. Now cake is ready.take them out of the oven.
  11. Once cool down it is ready to serve with a cup of tea.
  12. Happy baking😊
