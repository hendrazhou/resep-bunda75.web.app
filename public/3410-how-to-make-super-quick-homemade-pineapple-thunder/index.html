The ingredients needed to make Pineapple Thunder:
  1. Make ready 2 cups Fresh pineapple juice
  2. Get 2 cups Any cola drink 1 cup vanilla icecream
  3. Prepare Crushed ice
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp Sugar syrup
  5. Take Icing sugar
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  7. Make ready Pineapple slices to decorate

Steps to make Pineapple Thunder:
  1. Blend and strain the juice of pineapple.
  2. Add sugar syrup into the juice.Add vanilla ice cream. Churn it.
  3. At the serving time dip the rim of glasses in the icing sugar to coat it.
  4. Half fill the glasses with crushed ice.
  5. Pour juice mixed with ice cream and sugar syrup into glasses.
  6. Now fill them with cola drink.
  7. Sprinkle black pepper. Decorate with a slice of pineapple
  8. Serve chilled.
